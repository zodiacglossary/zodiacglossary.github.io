tny.t ḫny

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/14/2023, 3:09:34 PM
Primary meaning part of mayhem
Literal translation part of mayhem
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
part of mayhem astrological concept

6th astrological place; alt. name for "House of misfortune"

tꜣ tny.t šry mw sbkꜣ | tꜣ tny.t ḫny nꜣ tbt.w

‘The part of the son: Aquarius and Mercury | The part of Mayhem: Pisces’

— O.Neugeb. 3 16-17

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117
