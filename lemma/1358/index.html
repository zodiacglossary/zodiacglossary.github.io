horoscopo

Latin verb
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:51:39 AM
Primary meaning to be on the ascendant
Literal translation to be on the ascendant
Variants
horoscopo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to be on the ascendant astrological concept

Si [...] horoscopante Marte Venus cum Mercurio in occasu fuerit constituta [...]

‘If Venus and Mars are located on the descendant while Mars is on the ascendant [...]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.31.17

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.153
