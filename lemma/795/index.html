δεκανός
dekanos

Greek noun

Last edited: 8/1/2023, 2:28:28 PM
Primary meaning decan
Literal translation one of ten
Variants
δεκανός dekanos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
decan astral concept

δε̣κανῶι δευτέρωι καλουμένωι Σενταχορ

‘(The Sun was found) in the second decan (of Aries), which is called Sentachor’

— P.Lond. I 130 66–68

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)

τῶν δεκανὸς βʹ Κατ/ κουατ/

‘(The dodecatemorion resulted) where the 2nd decan is, Kat kouat’

— P.Lond. 98 57–58

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95 CE
