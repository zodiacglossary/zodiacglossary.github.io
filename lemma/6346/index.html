तुला
tulā

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 9/30/2025, 2:28:40 PM
Primary meaning Libra
Literal translation scales, balance, weight
Variants
तुल tula
Edited by
Libra zodiac sign

वीथ्यां तुलापण्यधरो मनुष्यः स्थितः स नाभीकटिवस्तिदेशः।

vīthyāṃ tulāpaṇyadharo manuṣyaḥ sthitaḥ sa nābhīkaṭivastideśaḥ|

‘The man holding merchandise in his scales in the market (Libra), is located at place of the navel, hips and groins.’

— Vṛddhayavanajātaka 1.10ab

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: ca. 300–325 CE

Mak, Bill M. 2018. “The First Two Chapters of Mīnarāja’s Vṛddhayavanajātaka.” Zinbun 48: 1–31.
