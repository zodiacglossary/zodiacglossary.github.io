GISKIM
ittu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:30:12 PM
Primary meaning omen sign
Literal translation sign
Variants
GISKIM ittu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
omen sign astrological concept

(9') GISKIM.BI ZU.ŠE₃ 59.8.58.16.17.46.40 a-na 6.4.30 IL₂-ma (10') 5.59.20 ip-pal-ka

‘In order to know its sign: you ‘raise’ (0);59,8,58,16,17,46,40 to 6,4;30, then (10’) it gives you 5,59;20.’

— BM 36712 (BMAPT No. 102) obv. 9'-10'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer 518-519
