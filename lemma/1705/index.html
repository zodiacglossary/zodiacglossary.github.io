EN.NUN ša₂ gi-ne₂-e
naṣāru ša ginê

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/18/2024, 9:46:56 AM
Primary meaning astronomical diary
Literal translation regular watch
Variants
EN.NUN ša₂ gi-ne₂-e naṣāru ša ginê
Edited by
Alessia Pilloni
regular watch astronomical genre

term for astronomical diaries

EN.NUN ša₂ gi-ne₂-e TA GAN EN TIL ŠE MU.12.[KAM₂ mu₂-ma-kuš] ša₂ mar₂-tak-šat-su <LUGAL> MU-šu₂ na-bu-u₂

‘Diary from month IX to the end of month XII, year 12 [of Umakuš,] who is called king Artaxerxes.’

— BM 46229 (ADRT I No. -346) r. 35

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 347 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 152-153
