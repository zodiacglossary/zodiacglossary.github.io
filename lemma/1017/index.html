benevolus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/1/2023, 4:11:56 PM
Primary meaning benefic
Literal translation benevolent
Variants
benevolus
benivolus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
benefic astrological concept

Quemcumque enim locum benivolae stellae respexerint, [...] omnia [...] feliciter proueniunt

‘For when the benefic planets are in aspect [...] all the things [...] turn out well’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.20.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.66
