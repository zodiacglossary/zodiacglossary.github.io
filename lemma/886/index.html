statio

Latin noun

Last edited: 8/13/2024, 10:44:00 AM
Primary meaning position
Literal translation standing
Variants
statio
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
position astronomical term, astrological concept

praedicimus etiam quando retrogradus ad stationem pristinam revertatur

‘We even predict when he will retrograde and when return to his former position’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.4.6

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.12
