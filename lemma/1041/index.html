middle watch of the night

iti.GAN 1 | [UD.27(?) m]i-šil GE6 lu2.TUR | [a-lid]

‘Month IX 1, | [on the 27th(?) day], middle of night, the child | [was born].’

— BM 77265 (BH 24) 2-4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 1st century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: SE 229 IX.27(?) = -82 Dec.20)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 129
