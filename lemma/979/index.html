locus parentum

Latin noun

Last edited: 9/19/2023, 2:24:41 PM
Primary meaning place of parents
Literal translation place of parents
Variants
locus parentum
parentum locus
Edited by

place of parents astrological place

4th astrological place

parentum in quarto

‘the [place] of the parents [is] in the fourth [sign]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.14.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.58
