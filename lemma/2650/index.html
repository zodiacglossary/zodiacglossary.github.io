λίψ
lips

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 11:06:02 AM
Primary meaning south-west wind
Literal translation Lybian
Variants
λίψ lips
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
south-west wind type of wind

ἐπεὶ βαθμῷ ιʹ, ἀνέμῳ Λίβει, δε- κανῷ γʹ

‘in setp 10, wind Lips, de- can 3.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 ii. 10-11

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 424-425
