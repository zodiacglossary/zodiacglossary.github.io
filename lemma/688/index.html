ḏmꜥ n wnm-wnw.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 10/31/2023, 10:30:56 PM
Primary meaning Book of the astronomers
Literal translation Book of the astronomers
Variants
dmꜥ n wnm-wnw.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Book of the astronomers noun

Category of instructional texts

...] ỉrm nꜣ ḏmꜥ.w (n) [nꜣ wnm]-wnw.t r ḥꜣṱ (n) pr-ꜥꜣ twtw r-r=w ḏr=w

‘...] with the Books of the astronomers while the heart of pharaoh was content with them all.’

— P.Carlsberg XI 10 1-2

Genre: literary text
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd Cent. CE

Quack, J. F. and K. Ryholt, 2019, Petese Interpreting Astrology by Imhotep for King Nechepsos. Copenhagen, 2019, 161-183. 162
