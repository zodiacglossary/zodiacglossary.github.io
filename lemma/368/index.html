SI MAŠ₂
qaran suḫurmāši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:28:48 PM
Primary meaning β Capricorni
Literal translation the horn of the goat-fish
Variants
SI MAŠ₂ qaran suḫurmāši
SI ša₂ MAŠ₂ qarnu ša suḫurmāši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
β Capricorni star: normal star

Jones (2004) c26

GI₆ 23 ina ZALAG₂ GU₄.UD SIG SI MAŠ₂ 2 KUŠ₃ 8 SI

‘Night of (day) 23, last part of the night, Mercury was 2 cubits 8 fingers below the horn of the goat-fish.’

— BM 46229 (ADRT I No. -346) obv. 28

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 347 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 146-147
