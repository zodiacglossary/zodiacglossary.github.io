pedes anteriores tauri

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:52:03 AM
Primary meaning the fore-feet of the Bull
Literal translation fore-feet of the bull
Variants
pedes anteriores tauri
anteriores pedes tauri
tauri anteriores pedes
tauri pedes anteriores
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the fore-feet of the Bull animal part

16°–17° Taurus

XVI. et XVII. in anterioribus pedibus Tauri ordinantur

‘[degrees] 16 and 17 are appointed to the fore-feet of the Bull’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
