iura

Latin noun

Last edited: 11/4/2024, 8:56:51 AM
Primary meaning decanship
Literal translation rights
Variants
iura
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
decanship astrological concept

fontibus aeternis gaudens urnaque fluenti iura sui Librae permittit prima regenda

‘He that rejoices in the never-ending stream that pours from his urn transfers control of the first rights over himself to the Balance’

— Manilius, Astronomica 4.354 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 248-249
