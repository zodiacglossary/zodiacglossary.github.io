ⲥⲟⲩⲛⳗⲁⳗⲉ
sounčače

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 6/24/2024, 7:37:55 AM
Primary meaning hostile planet
Literal translation hostile star
Variants

hostile planet astrological concept

Planet in unfavourable position

ⲁϥⳓⲱⲡⲉ ⳋⲣⲡⳉⲱⲧ ⲻⲥⲟⲩⲛⳗⲁⳗⲉ ⲛ ⲡϥⲟⲟⲩ ⲻⲙⲓⲥⲧϥ ...

afšôpe šrpxôt – counčače n pfoou – mistf ...

‘If Jupiter was a hostile star on his his day of birth ...’

— P.Lond. 98 127

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95

J. Černý, P.E. Kahle, and R.A. Parker, 1957, The Old Coptic Horoscope, Journal of Egyptian Archaeology 43, 86-100. 98
