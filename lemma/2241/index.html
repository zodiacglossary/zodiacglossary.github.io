stella praeceps

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/29/2024, 9:00:15 AM
Primary meaning shooting star
Literal translation headlong star
Variants
stella praeceps
praeceps stella
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
shooting star astronomical phenomenon

et tenuem longis iaculantur tractibus ignem praecipites stellae passimque volare videntur

‘There are also shooting stars, which hurl long trails of slender fire and are seen flying everywhere’

— Manilius, Astronomica 1.849+847

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 72-73

〈stella prosiliens〉 ‘shooting star’
