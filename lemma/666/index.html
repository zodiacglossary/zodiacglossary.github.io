τόπος ἀργός
topos argos

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/12/2023, 8:48:40 AM
Primary meaning idle place
Literal translation idle place
Variants
τόπος ἀργός topos argos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
idle place astrological place

ἔσχον ἀρχὴν μὲν τοῦ θʹ τόπου· τουτέστι τοῦ καλουμένου θεοῦ· τέλος δὲ τοῦ ηʹ ὃν καὶ τόπον ἀργὸν καλοῦσι

‘(By these calculations) I obtained the beginning of the 9th place—that is, the one called God—and the end of the 8th, which they also call the Idle place’

— CCAG VIII.2 p. 68

Genre: horoscope
