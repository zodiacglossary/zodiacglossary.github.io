Αἰγίοχος
Aigiochos

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/4/2025, 3:45:35 AM
Primary meaning Jupiter
Literal translation Aegis-bearer
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Jupiter planet

Αἰγίοχος δὲ Καρκίνου ἐν δεκαπέντε

‘And the Aegis-bearer (Jupiter) (has its exaltation) in fifteen (degrees) of Cancer’

— Dorotheus 1.2.2–3

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
