autumnus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:13:58 AM
Primary meaning of autumn
Literal translation of autumn
Variants
autumnus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
of autumn season, time interval

quattuor in partes scribuntur sidera terna; hiberna aestivis, autumni verna repugnant.

‘The four divisions of the year are each allotted three signs; winter’s are at war with summer’s, the vernal with those of autumn.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.268 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 102-103
