πολυπλασιάζω
poluplasiazō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 11:05:23 AM
Primary meaning to multiply
Literal translation to multiply
Variants
πολυπλασιάζω poluplasiazō
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to multiply mathematical operation, arithmetical operation, multiplicative operation

..[ π]ολυπλασία[σον] [ἐπὶ τὰς ἤτοι] πρὸ μεσημ- βριας ἢ μετὰ μεσημβρί[αν] ὥρ(ας) κ(αὶ) τὸν γινόμεν(ον) ἀριθμ(ὸν) [ἐ]κ τοῦ πολυπλασιασμοῦ π(αρά)- βαλε π(αρὰ) τ(ὸν) ιεʹ.

‘... multiply [by the] hours [whether] before noon or after noon, and the number resulting from the multiplication divide by 15.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 4-9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 26
