κύκλος
kuklos

Greek noun

Last edited: 3/25/2025, 11:19:55 AM
Primary meaning circle
Literal translation circle
Variants
κύκλος kuklos
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
circle geometrical shape

Τῶν δὲ ἐν τῇ σφαίρᾳ κύκλων οἱ μὲν εἰσι παράλληλοι, οἱ δὲ λοξοί, οἱ δὲ διὰ τῶν πόλων.

‘Of the circles on the sphere, some are parallel, some are oblique, and some are [passing] through the poles.’

— Geminus 5.1

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 21
