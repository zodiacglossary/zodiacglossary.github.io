venter aquarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:52:50 AM
Primary meaning the belly of the Waterbearer
Literal translation belly of the waterbearer
Variants
venter aquarii
aquarii venter
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the belly of the Waterbearer animal part

20°–21° Aquarius

Duae aliae ponuntur in ventre

‘Two other [following] degrees are [placed] in the belly [of Aquarius]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.11

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
