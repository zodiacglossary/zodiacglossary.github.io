λατρώδης
latrōdēs

Greek adjective

Last edited: 1/20/2025, 9:52:01 AM
Primary meaning servile
Literal translation servile
Variants

Edited by

servile zodiac sign quality

Κριός ἐστιν (…) ζῴδιον (…) λατρῶδες

Genre: astrological treatise
