engonasin

Latin noun
quoted from Greek

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 12:39:44 PM
Primary meaning the Kneeling one
Literal translation on knees
Variants
engonasin
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Kneeling one constellation

modern Hercules

In extremis partibus Piscium oritur Ingeniculus qui a Graecis Engonasin dicitur.

‘In the last degrees of Pisces rises the Kneeling one who is called ἐν γόνασιν [i.e. on knees] by the Greeks.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.17.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.312
