MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ MUL₂.MUL₂
kakkabu arkû ša zappi

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:11:56 PM
Primary meaning the rear star of the Pleiades
Literal translation the rear star of the Stars
Variants
MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ MUL₂.MUL₂ kakkabu arkû ša zappi
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
27 Tauri star: normal star

Jones (2004) a1. Rarely attested, identified by Jones (2004)

16 BAR 3 SIG MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ MUL₂.MUL₂ 1 1/2 KUŠ₃

‘(Year) 16, month I, (day) 3, it (Venus) was 1 1/2 cubits below the rear star of the bristle.’

— BM 36751+ (ADRT V No. 61) rev. iii 11

Genre: astronomical diaries and related texts: planetary excerpt
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 280-320 BCE

Hunger, H. 2001. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. V. Lunar and Planetary Texts. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wis­senschaften. 230-231
