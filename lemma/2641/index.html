καταλήγω
katalēgō

Greek verb

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:33:09 AM
Primary meaning to be located
Literal translation to end (up)
Variants
καταλήγω katalēgō
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to be located planetary position, verb of position, spatial expression

[...] ἀπὸ τῆς μ[. .]ρης δωδεκατημο- ρίου καταλήγ[ει] Ἰχθ[ύ]σι

‘[...] from the dodekatemorion fell in Pisces’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 i. 10-11

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 420-421
