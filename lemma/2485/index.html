ἀβελιακός
abeliakos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 9/12/2024, 1:42:36 PM
Primary meaning solar
Literal translation solar
Variants
ἀβελιακός abeliakos rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
solar luminary or planetary quality

ἀβελιακόν· ἡλιακόν. Παμφύλιοι

‘abeliakos: solar. Pamphylians.’

— Hesychius α 104

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2018. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 1 (A - Delta), ed. I.C. Cunningham. Berlin, Boston: Walter de Gruyter. 7

See also

〈phoebeus〉 ‘of the Sun’
