mul.LUGAL
šarru

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 3/24/2025, 9:08:37 AM
Primary meaning King-star
Literal translation King-star
Variants
mul.LUGAL šarru
mul2.LUGAL šarru
LUGAL šarru in astronomical diaries
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Marvin Schreiber
King-star constellation: 3 x 12 constellations

iti.GAN 2 mul2.Ṣal-bat-a-nu 1.10 mul2.GIR2.TAB 35 mul2.[LU]GAL

‘Month IX: 2 Mars, 1,10 'The Scorpion', 35 'The [Ki]ng'.’

— BM 34713 (LBAT 1499) obv. 9

Genre: schematic astronomy: 'three stars each' + rising times
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 4th-2nd cent BCE

Horowitz, W., 2014. The Three Stars Each: The Astrolabes and Related Texts (Archiv für Orientforschung, Beiheft 33). Horn/Wien: Berger & Söhne/Institut für Orientalistik der Universität Wien. 126

α Leonis (Regulus) star: normal star, star

Jones (2004): c15

⸢ina ZALAG₂⸣ dil-bat e LUGAL 2 SI dil-bat 4 SI ana ŠU₂ LAL

‘Last part of the night, Venus was 2 fingers above the king (star), Venus being 4 fingers back to the west.’

— BM 33850 (ADRT III No. -162) obv. 13

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 163 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 24-25
