εὐμετάβολος
eumetabolos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/2/2024, 3:29:42 PM
Primary meaning variable
Literal translation easily changeable
Variants
εὐμετάβολος eumetabolos
Edited by

variable zodiac sign quality

Κριός ἐστιν (…) ζῴδιον (…) εὐμετάβολον

‘Aries is a (…) variable zodiac sign’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.1

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
