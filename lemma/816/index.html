NIM.GIR2
birqu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/2/2024, 4:58:19 PM
Primary meaning lightning
Literal translation lightning
Variants
NIM.GIR2 birqu
GIR2 birqu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
lightning meteorological phenomenon, celestial phenomenon

d.GU4.UD u AN ina ŠU2 ina mul2.ŠU.GI GUB.MEŠ-ma ŠEG3 u ILLU NIM.GIR2 u GU3 d.IŠKUR

‘(When) Mercury and Mars are standing in the west, in the Old-Man-star: rain and flood, lightning and thunder.’

— AO 6449 (TCL 6 19 = TU 19) rev. 15

Genre: astrological weather prediction
Provenance: Uruk
Date: 226 BCE (12 V Seleucid Era 84)

Hunger, H. 1976. Astrologische Wettervorhersagen. Zeitschrift fur Assyriologie 66: 234–260 249
