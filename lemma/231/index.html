rꜥ-ḥtp

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:38:41 PM
Primary meaning descendant
Literal translation setting
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
descendant astrological concept

pꜣy-ms=w r pꜣ nṯr-t(w)ꜣ n rꜥ-ḥtp ỉw=f ꜥšꜣ wꜥw ỉrm s-ḥm.t

‘The one who was born when Venus was in the descendant, he wỉll be in a lot of strife with a woman.’

— P.Berlin 8345

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: 1st cent. CE to 2nd cent. CE

Quack, J. F., 2008, Texte aus Ägypten: Demotische, magische und divinatorische Texte. In B. Janowski and B. Wilhelm (eds), Omina, Orakel, Rituale und Beschwörungen. Texte aus der Umwelt des Alten Testaments, Neue Folge 4. Gütersloh: Gütersloher Verlagshaus.
