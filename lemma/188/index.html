Dûzu (month IV) month, time interval

iti.ŠU mul.SIPA.ZI.AN.NA d.NIN.ŠUBUR SUKKAL.MAḪ

‘Dûzu, constellation of the True Shepherd of Anu (Orion) Ninšubur, the great Visir’

— KAV 218 a i 38-39

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Nineveh

Casaburi, M. C. 2003. Tre-stelle-per-ciascun (-mese): l'Astrolabio B: edizione filologica. Annali/Supplemento 33

¶ ina iti.ŠU BAR2 GAL UŠ₂-ma DUMU-šú AŠ.TE DAB ŠE.GIŠ.I₃ ⸢u?⸣ […]

‘If in Dûzu the harvest a great king will die and his son will take the throne, Sesame [...]’

— Sm.1281 + Rm-II.132 4'

Genre: celestial divination: enūma anu enlil
Provenance: Nineveh

Gehlken, E. 2008. Die Adad-Tafeln der Omenserie Enuma Anu Enlil; T. 2: die ersten beiden Donnertafeln (EAE 42 und EAE 43). Zeitschrift für Orient-Archäologie, 1, 256-314. 266
