ina NIM ŠU₂
ina ... rabû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:24:48 AM
Primary meaning Morning Last
Literal translation to set in the East
Variants
ina NIM ŠU₂ ina x rabû Akkadian reading of NIM uncertain (x=elê?)
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Morning Last synodic phenomenon

Said of Mercury and Venus (inner planets)

7 dil-bat ina NIM ina SAG LU ŠU₂

‘(Month XII2), (day) 7, Venus sets in the East in the beginning of Aries’

— BM 32333+32420 (ADRT I No. -375C) rev. 6

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 376 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 99
