ἀποσαλεύω
aposaleuō

Greek verb

Last edited: 5/5/2025, 3:59:37 PM
Primary meaning to be at anchor offshore
Literal translation to be at anchor offshore
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to be at anchor offshore astrological concept

ὁμοροοῦν λέγεται ζῴδιον τῇ Σελήνῃ τὸ ἐπαναφερόμενον ἐφ’ ὃ φέρεται· τὸ γὰρ αὐτὸ καὶ παράκολλον καλεῖται· ἀποσαλεῦον δὲ τὸ μηδὲ ὁροούμενον ὑπὸ τῆς Σελήνης ζῴδιον

‘A zodiac sign is said to “be a neighbor” to the Moon that is in a following position (epanapheromai) to the (sign) in which (the Moon) is: the same (sign) is called “attached”; the zodiac sign that has no contiguity(?) with the Moon is “anchored offshore”’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?
