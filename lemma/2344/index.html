stadium

Latin noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:07:55 PM
Primary meaning stade
Literal translation stade
Variants
stadium
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
stade unit of celestial position

= 2 arc minutes

Nunc age, quot stadiis et quanto tempore surgant sidera, quotque cadant, animo cognosce sagaci

‘Now learn with a perceptive mind how many stades and how much time the stars take to rise and set’

— Manilius, Astronomica 3.275 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 184-185
