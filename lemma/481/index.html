ἀναποδισμός
anapodismos

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/12/2024, 3:53:26 PM
Primary meaning retrograde motion
Literal translation return
Variants
ἀναποδισμός anapodismos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
retrograde motion astronomical term

[Φαέ]θ̣ω̣ν ὁ τοῦ Διὸς ἐν Καρκίνωι [μοι]ρ̣ῶ̣ν ι̣θ̣ʹ ἐ̣ν̣ ἀναποδισμῶι ὁ̣ρ̣ί̣ο̣ι̣[ς] Κ̣ρ̣[όν]ο̣υ̣.

‘The shining one, the (star) of Zeus (Jupiter) (was) in Cancer, 19 degrees, in retrograde, terms of Kronos (Saturn).’

— P.Oxy. 307 8–10

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 46 CE

(GH 46)
