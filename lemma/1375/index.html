locus mariti

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:01:36 PM
Primary meaning lot of the husband
Literal translation place of the husband
Variants
locus mariti
mariti locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of the husband astrological lot

Si vero in genitura mulieris locum mariti volueris invenire, et sit diurna genitura, a Marte ad Venerem ———

‘If you want to find the lot of the husband in a woman's chart, if it is a day-chart, from Mars to Venus ——’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.32

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.183

See also

〈κλῆρος γάμου〉 klēros gamou ‘lot of marriage’
