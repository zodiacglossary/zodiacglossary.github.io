societas iuncta

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:42:47 PM
Primary meaning aspect
Literal translation joined society
Variants
societas iuncta
iuncta societas
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
aspect astrological concept

nunc iunctas stellarum societates manifesta sermonis interpretatione signabimus

‘now we want to delineate the aspects of the planets with clear interpretations’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.22.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.114

See also

〈coniunctio〉 ‘aspect’

〈copulatio〉 ‘aspect’

〈commixtio〉 ‘conjunction, stellium’

〈mixtura〉 ‘configuration of planets’
