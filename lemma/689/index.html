twtw n s-ḥm.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 7:56:09 AM
Primary meaning feminine zodiac sign
Literal translation female image
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
feminine zodiac sign astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

[ṯꜣy pꜣ swsw] mḥ-12 twtw (n) s-ḥm.t nꜣw smḥ

‘[From the] 12th [degree], it means female zodiac signs to the left;’

— P.Carlsberg XI 10 12

Genre: literary text
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd Cent. CE

Quack, J. F. and K. Ryholt, 2019, Petese Interpreting Astrology by Imhotep for King Nechepsos. Copenhagen, 2019, 161-183. 165
