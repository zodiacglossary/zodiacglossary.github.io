θερινός
therinos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 3:49:51 PM
Primary meaning of summer
Literal translation of summer
Variants
θερινός therinos
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
of summer calendrical concept, season

Τροπὴ δέ θερινὴ γίνεται περὶ τὴν τῶν καυμάτων ἐπίτασιν ἐν Καρκίνου μιᾷ μοίρᾳ.

‘And summer solstice takes place around the intensification of the heat, (when the sun is) in the first degree of Cancer.’

— Geminus 1.9

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 2

See also
