TAB
ṭepû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 3/22/2024, 3:03:49 PM
Primary meaning to add
Literal translation to add
Variants
TAB teṭeppe you add
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to add mathematical operation, arithmetical operation, additive operation

(6) KI BAR.NUN ša₂ l[u-maš] (7) ša₂-šu₂ ki TAB TAB ki LA₂ LA₂ ana muḫ-ḫi a-ḫa-miš₂ GAR.GAR-ma ½-šu₂-nu GIŠ-ma ana BAR.NUN ⸢GAR⸣-[an]

‘(6) You add it with the corresponding ṣiliptu-coefficient for the zod[iac] if it is increasing, subtract if it is decreasing. You accumulate (them) together, compute half of it and put it down as the ṣiliptu-coefficient.’

— BM 35399 (BMAPT No. 61A) Obv. 6-7

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 396
