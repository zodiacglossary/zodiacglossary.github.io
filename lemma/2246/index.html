λεπτὸν τρίτον
lepton triton

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:09:47 PM
Primary meaning third-order minute
Literal translation third minute
Variants
λεπτὸν τρίτον lepton triton
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
third-order minute unit of celestial position

ἐπεῖχε Καρκίνου μοίρας ἓξ καὶ ἔ̣τι λεπτὰ τρίτα δέκ̣[α]· ἃ δὴ μέρος μοίρης ἐστὶν δισμυροχιλεξακοσιοστόν

‘(Jupiter) occupied six degrees of Cancer and further ten third(-order) minutes, which are a twenty-six-thousandth of a degree’

— P.Lond. I 130 v 110–115

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
