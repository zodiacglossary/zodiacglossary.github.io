ὑπουργός
hupourgos

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/11/2023, 11:04:53 AM
Primary meaning assistant
Literal translation assistant
Variants
ὑπουργός hupourgos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
assistant astrological concept

βοηθοὶ δὲ λέγονται ἀστέρες οἱ συλλαμβανόμενοι ἢ ἐπιμαρτυροῦντες ἢ μεσεμβολοῦντες

‘Collaborators are the (stars) that travel together (are in conjunction?), and assistants are those that meet each other (in adjacent signs?)’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?
