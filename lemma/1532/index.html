cor arietis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:37:32 AM
Primary meaning the heart of the Ram
Literal translation heart of the ram
Variants
cor arietis
arietis cor
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the heart of the Ram animal part

16°–17° Aries

XVI. et XVII. in corde,

‘[the degrees] 16 and 17 are in the heart [of Aries]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.287
