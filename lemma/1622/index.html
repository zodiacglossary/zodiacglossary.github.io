pes sinister virginis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:31:25 PM
Primary meaning the left foot of the Virgin
Literal translation left foot of the virgin
Variants
sinister pes virginis
virginis sinister pes
pes sinister virginis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the left foot of the Virgin animal part

26°–27° Virgo

XXVI. et XXVII. in sinistro collocantur pede

‘[the degrees] 26 and 27 are located in the left foot [of the Virgin]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.6

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.290
