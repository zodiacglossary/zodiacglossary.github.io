Τήβηθ
Tēbēth

Greek noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:26:27 AM
Primary meaning Peritios
Literal translation Peritios
Variants
Τήβηθ Tēbēth rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Peritios month

month IV in the Macedonian calendar

Τήβηθ· Περίτιος μήν

‘Tēbēth: the month Peritios.’

— Hesychius τ 737

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2009. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 4 (Tau - Omega), eds. P.A. Hansen and I.C. Cunningham. Berlin, New York: Walter de Gruyter. 41
