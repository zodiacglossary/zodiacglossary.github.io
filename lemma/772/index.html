eš-šu
eššu

Akkadian adjective

Last edited: 7/24/2023, 7:44:13 PM
Primary meaning new
Literal translation new
Variants
eš-še-tu₂ eššetu fem. form
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
new

Refers to the (future) Goal Year in Goal Year procedures

TA BAR ša₂ MU-ka eš-še-tu₂ 18 GUR-ma KI.LA₂ u₄-mu u GI₆ ša₂ BAR ša₂ 18-ka tam-mar

‘From month I of your new year you turn back 18 and you see the duration ("weight") of day and night of month I of your (year) 18.’

— AO 6455 (TU 11) rev. 5

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Brack-Bernsen, Lis, and Hunger, Hermann. 2002. TU 11. A Collection of Rules for the Prediction of Lunar Phases and of Month Lengths. SCIAMVS 3: 3–90.
