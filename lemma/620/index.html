Στίλβων
Stilbōn

Greek adjective

Last edited: 6/26/2024, 4:18:40 PM
Primary meaning Mercury
Literal translation shining
Variants
Στίλβων Stilbōn
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Mercury planet

Στίλβων Ἀπόλλωνος

‘The shining one (Mercury), (the star of) Apollo’

— IGLS I 36 (GH –61)

Genre: horoscope

Στίλβων· ὁ τοῦ Ἑρμοῦ ἀστήρ

‘Stilbōn: the star of Hermes.’

— Hesychius σ 1861

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2005. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 3 (Pi - Sigma), ed. P.A. Hansen. Berlin, New York: Walter de Gruyter. 350
