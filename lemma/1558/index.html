cervix tauri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:33:28 AM
Primary meaning the neck of the Bull
Literal translation neck of the bull
Variants
cervix tauri
tauri cervix
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the neck of the Bull animal part

6°–7° Taurus

VI. et VII. in cervice

‘[the degrees] 6° and 7° are in the neck [of Taurus]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
