pectus arietis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:27:18 PM
Primary meaning the chest of the Ram
Literal translation chest of the ram
Variants
pectus arietis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the chest of the Ram animal part

11–12° Aries

XI. vero et XII. in pectore

‘11–12° [of Aries] are in the chest’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.287
