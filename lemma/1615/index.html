manus virginis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:17:54 PM
Primary meaning the hands of the Virgin
Literal translation hands of the virgin
Variants
manus virginis
virginis manus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the hands of the Virgin animal part

6°–7° Virgo

VI. vero et VII. in manibus

‘[the degrees] 6 and 7 are in the hands [of the Virgin]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.6

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.290
