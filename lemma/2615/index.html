καθ’ Ἑλήνου
kath’ Helēnou

Greek adverb

Last edited: 2/26/2025, 1:39:30 PM
Primary meaning according to the Greeks
Literal translation according to a Greek
Variants
καθ’ Ἑλήνου kath’ Helēnou
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
according to the Greeks calendrical concept

of hours of the day

[...] ὥρας εʹ ἡμέρας καθ’ Ἑλήνου

‘hour 5(?) of the day according to the Greeks’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4239 2

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 130 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 253 + II: 376-377

See also

〈ὥρα〉 hōra ‘hour’

〈κατ’ Αἰγυπτίων〉 kat’ Aiguptiōn ‘according to the Egyptians’
