Βόρειος Λίνος
Boreios Linos

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/26/2025, 12:17:01 PM
Primary meaning Northern Cord
Literal translation Northern Cord
Variants
Βόρειος Λίνος Boreios Linos
Βόρειος Λήνος Boreios Lēnos
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Northern Cord part of constellation

part of Cords; corresponding to ο+π+η+ρ Piscium when defined as 4 stars (Ptolemy); likely corresponding to the same 4 stars + α Piscium (Knot) when defined as 5 stars (Geminus)

Οἱ δὲ ἀπὸ τῶν οὐραίων μερῶν τῶν Ἰχθύων κατὰ τὸ ἑξῆς κείμενοι ἀστέρες Λίνοι προσαγορεύεται· εἰσί δὲ ἐν μὲν τῷ νοτίῳ λίνῳ ἀστέρες θʹ, ἐν δὲ τῷ βορείῳ λίνῳ ἀστέρες εʹ· ὁ δὲ ἐν ἄκρῳ τῷ λίνῳ κείμενος λαμπρὸς ἀστὴρ Σύνδεσμος προσαγορεύτεται.

‘The stars lying from the tail parts of Pisces in a row are called Cords; in the southern cord there are 9 stars, in the northern cord there are 5 stars; the bright star lying on the tip of the cord is called Knot.’

— Geminus 3.7

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 19
