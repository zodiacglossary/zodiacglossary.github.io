ἀπόκρουσις
apokrousis

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/1/2024, 11:40:04 AM
Primary meaning point opposite syzygy
Literal translation breaking
Variants
ἀπόκρουσις apokrousis
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
point opposite syzygy astrological concept

ἡ προγεναμένη ἀπόκρο̣υ̣σις ἐ̣γ̣έ̣νετο Διδύμοις μυρ(ῶν) βʹ λε(πτῶν) μθʹ

‘The preceding “breaking” (i.e., point opposite the prenatal syzygy?) was in Gemini 2 degrees 49 minutes’

— PSI I 23a 17–18

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Antinoopolis
Date: 338 CE

(GH 338)
