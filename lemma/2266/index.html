σαρός
saros

Greek noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:27:02 AM
Primary meaning 3,600
Literal translation 3,600
Variants
σαρός saros rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
3,600 mathematical quantity: number

σαρός· ἀριθμός τις παρὰ Βαβυλωνίοις

‘saros: a certain number, according to the Babylonians.’

— Hesychius σ 226

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2005. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 3 (Pi - Sigma), ed. P.A. Hansen. Berlin, New York: Walter de Gruyter. 269
