venter leonis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:47:45 PM
Primary meaning the belly of the Lion
Literal translation belly of the lion
Variants
venter leonis
leonis venter
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the belly of the Lion animal part

18°–19° Leo

duae sequentes in ventre

‘the two following [degrees] are in the belly [of the Lion]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
