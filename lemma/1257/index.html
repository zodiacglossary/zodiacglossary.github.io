συναφή
sunaphē

Greek noun

Last edited: 10/9/2023, 10:09:00 AM
Primary meaning contact
Literal translation contact
Variants
συναφή sunaphē
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
contact astrological concept

ἡ δὲ Ἀφροδίτη ὡρονομοῦσα καὶ Ζεὺς ἀγαθοδαιμονῶν καὶ τὴν συναφὴν τῆς Σελήνης ἐκδεχόμενος ἐδήλουν τὴν ἡμερότητα

‘Venus apportioning the hour (being in the Ascendant) and Jupiter being in the (lot of) the Good Divinity and receiving contact with the Moon indicated the tameness (of the animal concerned in the horoscope)’

— CCAG VI p. 66

Genre: horoscope

(GH L 483)
