MUL₂ DILI ša₂ KUN.ME-šu₂
kakkabu ēdu ša zibbātišu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/10/2023, 11:50:18 AM
Primary meaning β Leonis
Literal translation single star from his tail
Variants
MUL₂ DILI ša₂ KUN.ME-šu₂ kakkabu ēdu ša zibbātišu

β Leonis star: ziqpu star

MUL₂ DILI ša₂ KUN.ME-šu₂ [...]

‘The single star from his tail: [...]’

— BM 36628+ obv. iii 12'

Genre: astral compendium
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 450-50 BCE

Steele, J. 2015, A Late Babylonian Compendium of Calendrical and Stellar Astrology, Journal of Cuneiform Studies 67, 187-215 196, 202

1 2/3 MA.NA MIN 10 UŠ i-na KI.MIN 18 lim DANNA ina KI.MIN | TA MUL 2 ša₂ GIŠ.KUN-šu₂ EN MUL DILI ša₂ KUN-šu₂

‘1 2/3 minas ditto (weight), 10 UŠ on ditto (the ground), 18 thousand bēru in ditto (heaven), | from the 2 stars of its rump to the single star of its tail.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) rev. 17-18

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 219
