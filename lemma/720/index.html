Akkadian noun

Last edited: 5/11/2025, 4:12:50 PM
Primary meaning computed table
Literal translation construction
Variants

computed table astronomical genre, artefact of astral science

te-er-si-tu₄ ša₂ m.ki-din-nu

‘Computed table according to Kidinnu.’

— BM 45849+ (ACT No. 123a) rev. 22

Genre: mathematical astronomy: synodic table
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 2nd century BCE

Neugebauer, Otto, 1955, Astronomical Cuneiform Texts
