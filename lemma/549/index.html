ꜥ.wy.w nty ỉr md.t nfr.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 6:42:37 PM
Primary meaning houses of beneficence
Literal translation houses of beneficence
Variants
ꜥ.wy.w nty ỉr md.t nfr.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
houses of beneficence astrological concept

Designation for the triplicities

pꜣ ꜥ.wy n ṯsy n ḥr-pꜣ-kꜣ tꜣ ỉh̭y.t [n swsw 20 pꜣy⸗f ꜥ.wy n] ⌈hby pꜣ ỉsw n⌉ swsw ⌈20⌉ nꜣ ḥ[t]r(.w) ⟨tꜣ ỉh̭y.t ⟩ pꜣ-twtw-n-pꜣ-mw nꜣy⸗f ꜥ.wy.w n ỉr md.t nfr.t ⌈nꜣ.w⌉ [...

‘The exaltation of Saturn: Libra [in 20 degrees; its ⌈depression: Aries in 20⌉ degrees; Gemini, ⟨Libra⟩, and Aquarius are his houses of beneficence.’

— P.Carlsberg 81 Frag. 31 ii 16-17

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

A. Winkler, 2016, Some Astrologers and Their Handbooks in Demotic Egyptian, in J.M. Steele (ed.), The Circulation of Astronomical Knowledge in the Ancient World (Leiden: Brill), 245-286 256
