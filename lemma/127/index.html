ꜥ.wy sḥn-ꜥnḫ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 10/21/2024, 7:49:35 AM
Primary meaning House of the provisions of life
Literal translation House of the provisions of life
Variants
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
House of the provisions of life astrological house

[pꜣy-ms=w r pꜣ-nṯr]-twꜣ n ꜥ.wy (n) sḥn-ꜥnḫ pꜣy=f ꜥnḫ |

‘[The one who was born when Ven]us was in the House of provisions of life, his life will |’

— P.Berlin 8345 x+1, 9

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: 1st cent. CE to 2nd cent. CE

Quack, J. F., 2008, Texte aus Ägypten: Demotische, magische und divinatorische Texte. In B. Janowski and B. Wilhelm (eds), Omina, Orakel, Rituale und Beschwörungen. Texte aus der Umwelt des Alten Testaments, Neue Folge 4. Gütersloh: Gütersloher Verlagshaus.
