ṣipru

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:46:23 AM
Primary meaning coma
Variants
ṣip-ru ṣipru
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
crest common noun
coma (of a comet)

DIŠ MUL4 ša2 ina IGI-šu2 ṣip-ru ina EGIR-šu2 KUN GAR-[nu] | IGI-ma AN-e ZALAG2-ir ki-ma ṣal-lu-mu-u2 | ki-ma me-šiḫ MUL.MEŠ

‘If a star that has a coma in front and a tail behind | appears and it lightens up the sky like a comet, | like the flashing of stars.’

— K 250+ (CT 26 40-41) iv 13-15

Genre: star list
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: 7th cent BCE

Koch-Westenholz, U., 1995. Mesopotamian Astrology. An Introduction to Babylonian and Assyrian Celestial Divination. Copenhagen: Museum Tusculanum Press. 194
