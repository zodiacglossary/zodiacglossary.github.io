DIRI
malû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:58:59 AM
Primary meaning to be/become full
Literal translation to be/become full
Variants
ma-lu-uš-šu₂ malûššu Locative of inf. G + poss. suff. 3 m. sg.
DIRI-šu₂ malûššu Locative of inf. G + poss. suff. 3 m. sg.
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to be/become full mathematical expression

in mathematical astronomy (Moon system A column G): summation of arithmetical sequence

(3) ana tar-ṣi 2.13.20 LA₂-u₂ 2.40 GAR-an mim-ma ša₂ al-la 2.13.20 LA₂-[u₂ LA₂-u₂ EN 2.10.40] (4) LA₂-u₂ A.RA₂ 3.22.30 DU ma-lu-uš-šu₂ 17.46.40 GAR-an ana muḫ-ḫi a-[ḫa-miš₂ GAR.GAR-ma] (5) KI ⸢2.40 TAB-ma GAR⸣-an

‘(3) Opposite 2,13;20, decreasing, you put 2,40. Whatever (the amount) by which it is less than 2,13;20, decreas[ing, until 2,10;40], (4) decreasing, you multiply by 3;22,30; while it fills up you put down (0);17,46,40, you accumulate it together, and (5) you add it with 2,40, and you put it down.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. i 3-5

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 362-363
