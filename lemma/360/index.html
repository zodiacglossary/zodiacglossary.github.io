Egyptian noun

ryr.t

Edited by
Andreas Winkler
The constellation sow constellation

mỉ ỉr=s m-ḳd n pꜣ ḫpš m-sꜣ tꜣ ryr.t

‘Cause that she makes her travel to Ursa major and the Sow.’

— GEMF 16 xxi 38

Genre: magical text
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 2nd cent. CE

Faraone, Ch. and Torallas Tovar, S. 2022, Greek and Egyptian Magical Formularies: Text and Translation, vol. I (Berkeley CA)
