धन्विन्
dhanvin

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 9/30/2025, 2:44:23 PM
Primary meaning Sagittarius
Literal translation bow-bearer, archer
Edited by
Sagittarius zodiac sign

धन्वी मनुष्यो हयपश्चिमार्धस्तमाहुरूरू भुवनप्रणेतुः।

dhanvī manuṣyo hayapaścimārdhas tam āhur ūrū bhuvanapraṇetuḥ|

‘A man carrying a bow with the rear half of a horse [is the ninth sign]. They say this is the thigh of the Maker of the World.’

— Vṛddhayavanajātaka 1.12ab

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: ca. 300–325 CE

Mak, Bill M. 2018. “The First Two Chapters of Mīnarāja’s Vṛddhayavanajātaka.” Zinbun 48: 1–31.
