x A.RA2 y DU
x ana y alāku

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 11/4/2023, 9:03:33 PM
Primary meaning to multiply x times y
Literal translation to go x times y
Variants
x A.RA2 y DU x ana y alāku
x GAM y DU x ana y alāku
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to multiply mathematical operation

BE-ma ME ana GI₆ DIRI u₄-mu A.RA₂ 4 tal-lak

‘If the day(light) exceeds the night you multiply the day(light) by 4.’

— AO 6455 (TU 11) rev. 14

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Brack-Bernsen, Lis, and Hunger, Hermann. 2002. TU 11. A Collection of Rules for the Prediction of Lunar Phases and of Month Lengths. SCIAMVS 3: 3–90.

10 ABSIN0 3.20 ša₂ al 10 ABSIN0 DIRI A.RA₂ 40 DU TA 3.20 LA₂

‘10 Virgo 3,20. (The amount) by which it exceeds 10 Virgo you multiply by (0);40, subtract from 3,20.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) obv. ia 7

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.

(25') ana 1 UŠ KI ša₂ al 13 (26') ⸢ABSIN0 DIRI⸣ GAM 15 DU KI 1.58.45.42 TAB ana NIM lu ana SIG GAR

‘For 1 UŠ by which the position exceeds 13 (26’) Virgo you multiply by (0);15, you add it to 1,58;45,42, you put it down as the ‘height’ or the ‘depth’.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) obv. i 25'-26'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
