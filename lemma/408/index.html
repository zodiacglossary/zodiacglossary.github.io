Wagon-of-Heaven-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to Ursa Minor.

DIŠ mul.MAR.GID2.DA.AN.NA d.Dam-ki-an-na

‘¶ The Wagon of Heaven, Damkianna.’

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
