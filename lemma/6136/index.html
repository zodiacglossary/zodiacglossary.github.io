χρυσῷ ὁμοία
chrusō homoia

Greek adjective

Last edited: 6/15/2025, 3:08:32 PM
Primary meaning golden
Literal translation gold-like
Variants
χρυσῷ ὁμοία chrusō homoia
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
golden lunar quality

ἡ δὲ θεὰ καὶ σελασφόρος Σελήνη (…) χρυσῷ ὁμοία

‘The goddess and light-bearing Moon (…) being golden (in color) …’

— P.Lond. 98 iv 73–86

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95 CE

(GH 95)
