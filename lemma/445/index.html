MUL₂ IGI ša₂ ALLA ša₂ SI
kakkabu maḫrû ša allutti ša iltāni

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:18:25 PM
Primary meaning η Cancri
Literal translation the front star of the crab to the north
Variants
MUL₂ IGI ša₂ ALLA ša₂ SI kakkabu maḫrû ša allutti ša iltāni
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
η Cancri star: normal star

Jones (2004) a2. Rarely used.

GE₆ 27 ina ZALAG₂ sin e MUL₂-IGI-ša₂-ALLA-ša₂-SI 1 KUŠ₃ sin 1/2 KUŠ₃ ana ŠU₂ LAL

‘Night of (day) 27, last part of the night, Sin was 1 cubit above the front star of the crab to the north, Sin being 1/2 cubit back to the west.’

— BM 43025+ (ADRT III No. -77A) rev. 14

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 78 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 494-495
