Edited by
Capricorn

मृगार्धपूर्वो मकरोऽम्बुमध्ये जानुप्रदेशं तमुशन्ति धातुः।

mṛgārdhapūrvo makaro ’mbumadhye jānupradeśaṃ tam uśanti dhātuḥ|

‘The tenth [sign] is a Makara with the front half of a deer, [and the rest] in the midst of water— they call it the knee-area of the Creator.’

— Vṛddhayavanajātaka 1.13ab

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: ca. 300–325 CE

Mak, Bill M. 2018. “The First Two Chapters of Mīnarāja’s Vṛddhayavanajātaka.” Zinbun 48: 1–31.
