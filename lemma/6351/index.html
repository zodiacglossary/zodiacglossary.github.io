कुम्भ
kumbha

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 9/30/2025, 3:00:44 PM
Primary meaning Aquarius
Literal translation pot
Edited by
Bill Mak
Aquarius zodiac sign

स्कन्धे तु रिक्तः पुरुषस्य कुम्भो जङ्घोरुमेकादशमाहुराद्याः।

skandhe tu riktaḥ puruṣasya kumbho jaṅghorum ekādaśam āhur ādyāḥ|

‘An emptied pot on the shoulder of a man. The ancients call this the eleventh [sign, which is] the shanks and thighs.’

— Vṛddhayavanajātaka 1.14ab

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: ca. 300–325 CE

Mak, Bill M. 2018. “The First Two Chapters of Mīnarāja’s Vṛddhayavanajātaka.” Zinbun 48: 1–31. 15
