ὡρονόμος
hōronomos

Greek noun

Last edited: 3/24/2025, 7:26:26 AM
Primary meaning Ascendant
Literal translation hour-apportioner
Variants
ὡρονόμος hōronomos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Ascendant cardinal point

ὅς κεν ἔχῃσι δωδέκατον μετὰ χῶρον ἀφ’ ὡρονόμοιο Σελήνην

‘whoever has the Moon in the twelfth place from the Ascendant’

— Dorotheus 5.16.1–40

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?

p. 393 Pingree
