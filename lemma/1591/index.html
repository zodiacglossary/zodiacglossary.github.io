dorsum cancri

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:55:49 AM
Primary meaning the back of the Crab
Literal translation back of the crab
Variants
dorsum cancri
cancri dorsum
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the back of the Crab animal part

11°–12° Cancer

XI. et XII. in dorso

‘[the degrees] 11 and 12 are in the back [of the Crab]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
