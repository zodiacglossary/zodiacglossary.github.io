E
elû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 2/25/2024, 6:14:43 PM
Primary meaning to rise
Literal translation to rise
Variants
E elû
E₁₁ elû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to rise astronomical event

only in the expression "ana ME E" = "to rise acronychally"

See also

〈E₁₁〉 šūlû ‘to subtract’

〈E₃〉 aṣû ‘to rise’

〈זָרַח〉 zāraḥ ‘to rise’
