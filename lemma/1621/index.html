pes dexter virginis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:30:34 PM
Primary meaning the right foot of the Virgin
Literal translation right foot of the virgin
Variants
pes dexter virginis
virginis pes dexter
dexter pes virginis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the right foot of the Virgin animal part

18°–19° Virgo

tres vero sequentes in dextro collocantur pede

‘the three following [degrees] are located in the right foot’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.6

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.290
