manus dextra capricorni

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:08:17 PM
Primary meaning the right hand of Capricorn
Literal translation right hand of the goat-horned
Variants
manus dextra capricorni
dextra manus capricorni
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the right hand of Capricorn animal part

16°–18° Capricorn

in dextra vero manu sequentes tres per ordinem collocantur

‘in the right hand [of Capricorn] however are located the following three [degrees] in order’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
