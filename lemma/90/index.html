Šulpae(a)

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:17:45 AM
Primary meaning Jupiter
Literal translation The Resplendent Youth
Variants
d.Šul-pa-e3(-a) Šulpae(a)
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Jupiter planet

d.Be-let DINGIR.MEŠ ana d.Šul-pa-e3-a iṭ-ḫi

‘Belet-ili came close to Jupiter’

— AO 6455 (TCL VI 11 = TU 11) 3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Uruk
Date: late 3rd cent BCE

Brack-Bernsen, Lis, and Hunger, Hermann. 2002. TU 11. A Collection of Rules for the Prediction of Lunar Phases and of Month Lengths. SCIAMVS 3: 3–90.
