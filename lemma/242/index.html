MUL₂ IGI ša SAG ḪUN
kakkabu maḫrû ša rēš agri

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:19:19 PM
Primary meaning β Arietis
Literal translation front star of the head of the hired man
Variants
MUL₂ IGI ša SAG ḪUN kakkabu maḫrû ša rēš agri
MUL₂ IGI ša₂ SAG ḪUN.GA₂ kakkabu maḫrû ša rēš agri
MUL₂ IGI ša₂ SAG LU kakkabu maḫrû ša rēš agri
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
β Arietis star: normal star

Jones (2004) c2. In astronomical diaries and related texts

GI₆ 6 SAG GI₆ sin SIG MUL₂ IGI ša₂ SAG ḪUN 4 KUŠ₃ sin 1/2 KUŠ₃ ana NIM ⸢DIB⸣

‘Night of (day) 6, beginning of the night, Sin was 4 cubits below front star of the head of the hired man, Sin having passed 1/2 cubit to the east.’

— BM 46030 (ADRT II No. -249B) obv. 10'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 250 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 44-45
