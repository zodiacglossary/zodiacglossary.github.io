נוניא
nwnyʾ

Aramaic noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:12:57 AM
Primary meaning Pisces
Literal translation the fishes
Variants
נוניא nwnyʾ
Edited by

Pisces zodiac sign

דו[לא] ב3////// וב3[/////// וב3///////]/ נו[ניא ב3///////// וב3¬]

dw[lʾ] b26 wb2[7 wb28] nw[nyʾ b29 wb30]

‘Aquarius, in 26 and in 2[7 and in 28] Pi[sces and 29 and in 30]’

— 4Q318 vii 4, 9, viii 5.

H. Jacobus, 2015, Zodiac Calendars in the Dead Sea Scrolls and Their Reception 46
