ꜥḥꜥ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 6:34:42 PM
Primary meaning station
Literal translation standing
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
station synodic phenomenon

7 sw 1 ḏlꜣ.t 7 0 [5]⌈7⌉ ꜥḥꜥ mḥ-1

‘7 day 1 Scorpio 7 0 [5]⌈7⌉ First station’

— P.Monts.Roca. inv. 314 ii x+9

Genre: sign entry table
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 2nd cent. CE

M. Escolano-Poveda, 2018/2019, Astronomica Monserratensia I: A Demotic monthly almanac with synodic phenomena (P.Monts.Roca. inv. 314), Enchoria 36, 1-36. 4;6
