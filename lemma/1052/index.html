SAG GE6
rēš mūši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/30/2023, 1:32:23 PM
Primary meaning beginning of the night (in the first half of a month)
Literal translation beginning of the night
Variants
SAG GE6 rēš mūši
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
beginning of the night (in the first half of a month) time interval

A time interval attested in horoscopes and astronomical diaries. Always followed by a lunar position. The counterpart is ina ZALAG2.

iti.ŠE 30 GE6 6 SAG GE6 30 | ina IGI ŠUR GIGIR ša2 SI 1 KUŠ3 | 6 ina ZALAG2 lu2.TUR a-lid

‘Month XII 30, night of the 6th, beginning of night, the moon | was 1 cubit west of the northern ... of the Chariot (β Tauri), | On the 6th in the last part of night, the child was born.’

— BM 35516 (Rochberg Horoscopes No. 18) 2-4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 142 BCE

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88, 1–164. 109

See also
