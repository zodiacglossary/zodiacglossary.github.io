Araḫsamna (month VIII) month, month name, month VIII, time interval

¶ iti.APIN giš.AL.LA₂.BI giš.APIN.NA

‘Month VIII: the hoe, the plough’

— KAV 218 a ii 39-40

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: Neo-Assyrian
