hydra

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 7:44:52 AM
Primary meaning the Water-snake
Literal translation water-snake
Variants
hydra
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Water-snake constellation

modern Hydra

extremamque secans Hydram mediumque sub austris Centaurum adverso concurrit rursus in axe

‘[The equinoctial colure] cleaves the extremity of the Water-snake and the middle of the southern Centaur, and again converges upon the opposing circle at the pole;’

— Manilius, Astronomica 1.612 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 52-53
