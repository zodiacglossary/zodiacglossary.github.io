ꜥm

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 2/25/2024, 10:16:02 PM
Primary meaning to swallow
Literal translation to swallow
Variants
ꜥm
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
to swallow celestial phenomenon

Refers to a lunar eclipse

ỉ.ỉr=y sḏm=s n Ṱbn pꜣy=(y) dmỉ ḏd ꜥm tꜣ p.t pꜣ ỉt[n]

‘I have heard in Teben my hometown that the sky has swallowed the disc’

— P.Berol. 13588 iii 1

Genre: literary text
Provenance: Abusir el-Meleq
Date: 305 BCE - 30 BCE

W. Erichsen, 1956, Eine neue demotische Erzählung, Akademie der Wissenschaften und der Literatur, Abhandlungen der geistes- und sozialwissenschaftlichen Klasse, 1956 (2), Mainz - Wiesbaden , 49-81
