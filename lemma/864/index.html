Mars planet

id est si Luna facit candidos, Mars rubeos, Saturnus nigros, cur omnes in Aethiopia nigri, in Germania candidi, in Thracia rubei procreantur

‘that is, if the Moon makes people fair-skinned, Mars red, Saturn black, why is the whole population of Ethiopia black, of Germany blond, of Thrace red-haired?’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.2.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.6
