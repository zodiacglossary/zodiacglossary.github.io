Mars planet, luminary

MUL₄ sar-ru [...] : MUL₄ ṣal-bat-a-nu 7 MU-šú

‘The false star [...] : Mars its 7 names’

— K 2067 (CT 26 45)

Genre: star list
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: 7th century BCE

Reiner, E., Civil, M. 1974. The Series HAR-ra = hubullu: Tablets XX-XXIV (Materials for the Sumerian Lexicon XI). Pontificium Institutum Biblicum: Rome. 17

Crab constellation

In commentary from Nineveh

LUL.LA : AL.LUL

‘The false star is the Crab.’

— K 4293 III, 7-7c

Genre: celestial divination: enūma anu enlil
Provenance: Nineveh

Reiner, E., Pingree, D. 1998. Babylonian Planetary Omens: Part Three. 110-111
