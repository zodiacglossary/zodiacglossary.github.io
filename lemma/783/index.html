κλῆρος τοῦ δαίμονος
klēros tou daimonos

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 11:53:21 AM
Primary meaning lot of the daimon
Literal translation lot of the daimon
Variants
κλῆρος τοῦ δαίμονος klēros tou daimonos
κλῆρος δαίμονος klēros daimonos P. Oxy. LXI 4277
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
lot of the daimon astrological lot

[ὁ κλ]ῆρος ὁ τοῦ δαίμονος εὑρέθη Αἰγογέρῳ μοιρῶν κζʹ

‘The lot of the daimon was found in 27 degrees of Capricorn’

— P.Princ. II 75 4

Genre: horoscope
Date: 138–161 CE

(GH 138/161)
