Βίου
Biou

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/12/2025, 6:39:06 AM
Primary meaning Decan 1 of Pisces
Variants
Βίου Biou
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 1 of Pisces decan

οἱ δὲ τρεῖς αὐτοῦ δεκανοί· ὁ πρῶτος Βίου, ὁ δεύτερος Χονταρέ, ὁ τρίτος Πτιβιοῦ

‘The three decans of (Pisces) are: the first, Biou; the second, Chontare; the third, Ptibiou’

— Hephaestion 1.1.225

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
