šitqulu
LAL₂

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 6/10/2024, 7:48:43 AM
Primary meaning to be in or near conjunction?
Literal translation to be in balance
Variants
LAL₂ šitqulu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to be in or near conjunction? spatial expression, astronomical term

GE₆ ⸢14⸣ [x]+1 GE₆ DIR muš SAG GE₆ sin ina IGI SI₄ 1/2 KUŠ₃ ana SI-šu₂ SIG LAL₂

‘Night of (day) 14, GE₆ (= sunset to moonrise) was [x]+1 (UŠ), measured (despite) clouds; beginning of the night, Sin was 1/2 cubit in front of Lisi (= α Sco), it was balanced to its lower horn.’

— BM 32889+ (ADRT II No. -246) obv. 5

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 247 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.
