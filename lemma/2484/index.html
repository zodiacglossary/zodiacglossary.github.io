Ἀβέλιος
Abelios

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/12/2024, 2:22:50 PM
Primary meaning Sun
Literal translation Sun
Variants
Ἀβέλιος Abelios rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Sun luminary, planet, deity

ἀβέλιον· ἥλιον. Κρῆτες

‘Abelios: Sun. Cretans.’

— Hesychius α 105

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2018. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 1 (A - Delta), ed. I.C. Cunningham. Berlin, Boston: Walter de Gruyter. 7

The form is given by Hesychius in the accusative.
