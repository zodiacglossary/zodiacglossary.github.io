swsw

Egyptian noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 8/25/2025, 8:02:18 AM
Primary meaning degree
Literal translation degree
Variants
swsw
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
degree unit of celestial position

Loan word from Akkadian šuššān

n-ṯꜣy pꜣ gn⟨ḥ⟩ḏ 6 šꜥ ỉḫ.t 26 r swsw 110

‘From Cancer 6° to Libra 26° is 110 degrees’

— ANAshm.Mus.D.O. 483 5

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 3rd cent. BCE to 3rd cent. CE

Ossendrijver, M. and A. Winkler, 2018. Chaldeans on the Nile: Two Egyptian Procedure Texts with Babylonian System A1 and A2 for Mercury, in Scaffolding of Our Thoughts: Essays on Assyriology and the History of Science in Honor of Francesca Rochberg, eds. J. Crisostomo et al. (Leiden: Brill, 2018), 282-418 386 - 387
