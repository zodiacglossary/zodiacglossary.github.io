ara

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/31/2025, 3:44:22 PM
Primary meaning the Altar
Literal translation altar
Variants
ara
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Altar constellation

modern Ara

In I. Scorpii parte oritur Ara.

‘At 1° Scorpio rises the Altar.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.13.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.307
