pedes aquarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:55:15 AM
Primary meaning the feet of the Waterbearer
Literal translation feet of the waterbearer
Variants
pedes aquarii
aquarii pedes
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the feet of the Waterbearer animal part

22°–24° Aquarius

tres vero sequentes usque ad extremes porriguntur pedes

‘the three following [degrees] however go down to the bottoms of the feet [of Aquarius]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.11

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
