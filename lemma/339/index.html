πρῶτον τετράγωνον
prōton tetragōnon

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/30/2025, 1:22:41 PM
Primary meaning first square
Literal translation first quadrangle
Variants
πρῶτον τετράγωνον prōton tetragōnon
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
first square astrological concept, aspect

τὸ πρῶτον τρίγωνον καὶ τετράγωνον καὶ ἑξάγωνον λέγεται τὰ εὐώνυμα †ἑξῆς ἡ Σελήνη πρῶτον συνάπτει τινὶ τῶν ἀστέρων

‘The “first triangle” and “(first) quadrangle” and “(first) hexagon” are called the left(-aspect) ones, starting from when(?) the Moon first makes contact with any of the planets’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?

CCAG VIII.4 227
