mšꜥ

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:06:57 PM
Primary meaning to move
Literal translation to go, journey, move
Variants
ⲙⲟⲟϣⲉ
mšꜥ
mšy
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
going noun
pace noun
journey noun

sẖ ḥm-nṯr n spd.t ỉmy-r(ꜣ) mšꜥ n ỉꜥḥ wꜥb n p(ꜣ) 5 sbꜣ ꜥnḫ.w

‘Written by the prophet of Sothis, overseer of the moon's movement, and wrist of the five living stars.’

— Graf.Phil. 421 14

Genre: inscription
Provenance: Philae
Date: 3rd cent. CE

Griffith, F.Ll., 1935-1937, Catalogue of the Demotic Graffiti of the Dodecaschoenus, Oxford. 26-31
