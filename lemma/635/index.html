šmw

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/28/2023, 6:58:55 PM
Primary meaning Shemu season
Literal translation Harvest season
Variants
šmw
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Shemu season calendrical concept

tpy šmw pꜣ knḥḏ | ỉbd-2 šmw pꜣ mꜣy ỉbd-3 šmw tꜣ rpy.t | ỉbd-4 šmw tꜣ ꜣḫ.t

‘Month 1 of Shemu: Cancer; | month 2 of Shemu: Leo; Month 3 of Shemu: Virgo; | Month 4 of Shemu: Libra.’

— O.Strasb.Dem. 521 8-10

Genre: astronomical list
Provenance: Thebes (?)
Date: 300 BCE - 300 CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 121-122 121
