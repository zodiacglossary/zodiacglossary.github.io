cervix capricorni

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:33:17 AM
Primary meaning the neck of Capricorn
Literal translation neck of the goat-horned
Variants
cervix capricorni
capricorni cervix
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the neck of Capricorn animal part

12°–13° Capricorn

XII. et XIII. in cervice

‘[degrees] 7 and 8 are in the neck [of Capricorn]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
