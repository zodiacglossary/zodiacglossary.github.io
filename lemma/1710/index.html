ina ŠU₂ ŠU₂
ina rabê rabû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:10:23 AM
Primary meaning evening last
Literal translation to set in the west
Variants
ina ŠU₂ ŠU₂ ina rabê rabû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Evening Last synodic phenomenon

verbal expression in astronomical diaries

in 20 GU₄.UD ina ŠU₂ ina MAŠ₂ ŠU₂

‘Around (day) 20, Mercury set in the west in Capricorn.’

— BM 34728+ (ADRT II No. -254) obv. 11'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 255 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 25
