natura sagittarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/10/2024, 7:30:27 AM
Primary meaning the genitals of the Archer
Literal translation nature of the archer
Variants
natura sagittarii
sagittarii natura
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the genitals of the Archer animal part

26°–27° Sagittarius

XXVI. vero et XXVII. in natura Sagittarii collocantur

‘[the degrees] 26 and 27 however are located in the genitals of the Archer’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
