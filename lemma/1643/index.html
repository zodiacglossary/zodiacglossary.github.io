facies sagittarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:47:41 AM
Primary meaning the face of the Archer
Literal translation face of the archer
Variants
facies sagittarii
sagittarii facies
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the face of the Archer animal part

3–5° Sagittarius

tres vero sequentes per ordinem ponuntur in facie,

‘the three following [degrees] are in order placed in the face [of the Archer]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
