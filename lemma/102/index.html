Nisannu (month I) month, time interval

[EN.NUN ša₂ gi-ne2]-e ša2 TA BAR EN TIL DU6 MU 1 ME 50.KAM2 man LUGAL

‘[Diary] from month I to the end of month VII, year 150, king Antiochus’

— BM 34140 l.e. 1

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: -161

Hunger & Sachs ADRT 1

¶ iti.BAR2.ZAG.GAR mul.AŠ.IKU BAR2.AN.NA

‘Nisannu, constellation of 1 iku(Field), the pedestal’

— K 2920+ o. 17'

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Assur

Casaburi, M. C. 2003. Tre-stelle-per-ciascun (-mese): l'Astrolabio B: edizione filologica. Annali/Supplemento, p.29.
