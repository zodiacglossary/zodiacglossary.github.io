pectus leonis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:27:24 PM
Primary meaning the chest of the Lion
Literal translation chest of the lion
Variants
pectus leonis
leonis pectus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the chest of the Lion animal part

23°–24° Leo

in pectore XXIII. et XXIV. quaerimus

‘in the chest [of the Lion] we get the degrees 23 and 24’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.290
