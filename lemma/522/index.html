mḥ-2.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:08:42 PM
Primary meaning waning (of the Moon)
Literal translation second
Variants
mḥ-2.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
waning (of the Moon) time interval

ḥsb.t 24(.t) ỉbd-4 šmw sw 21 (wnw.t) 8.t n hrw | pꜣ rꜥ mꜣy 21 sbkꜣ | ỉꜥḥ gnḥḏ 6 (ḥr)-tšy kꜣ 12 sbkꜣ | ḥsb.t 1 mḥ-2.t wꜣḥ

‘Regnal year 24, Mesore 21, 8th (hour) of the day | the Sun in Leo 21°: (terms of) Mercury| the Moon in Cancer 6°: (terms of) Mars; Taurus 12°: (terms of) Mercury; Numbering year 1, waning: (it) adds.’

— O. Athribis 17-36-5/1741 1-4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Athribis
Date: 6 BCE

Escolano-Poveda, 2022, Astrologica Athribitana: Four Demotic-Hieratic Horoscopes from Athribis (O. Athribis 17-36-5/1741 and ANAsh. Mus.D.O.633 Reedited), Journal for the History of Astronomy, 53.1, 49-82. 57
