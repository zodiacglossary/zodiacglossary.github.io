tam-ṭi-tu₄
tamṭītu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 3/22/2024, 3:10:31 PM
Primary meaning deficit
Literal translation deficit
Variants
tam-ṭi-tu₄ tamṭītu
LA₂ tamṭītu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
deficit mathematical quantity, arithmetical quantity, subtractive quantity

Rarely attested in mathematical astronomy

(33) 4 a-na 30 ŠEŠ.MEŠ SE₃-ma IGI 30 : 2 (34) 4 A.RA₂ 2 8 ⸢8⸣ A.RA₂ 4 tam-ṭi-tu₄ DU-ma (35) 32

‘(33) You divide 4 into 30 parts: the reciprocal of 30 is (0);2, (34) 4 times 2 is (0);8. You multiply (0);8 by 4, the deficit, it is (35) (0);32.’

— BM 36722 (BMAPT No. 52) obv. i 33-35

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: Seleucid

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer

subtraction mathematical quantity, arithmetical quantity, subtractive quantity
