καιρικὴ ὥρα
kairikē hōra

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:24:11 AM
Primary meaning seasonal hour
Literal translation seasonal hour
Variants
καιρικὴ ὥρα kairikē hōra
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
seasonal hour time interval, unit of time

τὰς δο[θεί-] σας κ(αι)ρικὰς ἡμερινὰ[ς] ὥρ(ας) [ἀ]ναλῦσαι ε[ἰς] τὰς ἰσημ[ερινάς]

‘to convert the given seasonal hours of day into equinoctial (hours)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 Fr. 1, 15-17

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 26

See also

〈ὥρα〉 hōra ‘hour’

〈ἰσημερινὴ ὥρα〉 isēmerinē hōra ‘equinoctial hour’
