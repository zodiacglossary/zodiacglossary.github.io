facies arietis

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:52:42 AM
Primary meaning the face of the Ram
Literal translation face of the ram
Variants
facies arietis
arietis facies
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the face of the Ram (6–7° Aries) animal part

VI. vero et VII. in facie

‘[degrees] 6 and 7 are in the face [of Aries]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.287
