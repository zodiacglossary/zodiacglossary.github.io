zodiac astronomical term

Zodiacus orbis, in quo duodecim signa infixa sunt

‘The circle of the zodiac in which the twelve signs are fixed’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.1.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.41
