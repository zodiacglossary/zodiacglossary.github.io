MUL₂ ar₂ ša SAG ḪUN
kakkabu arkû ša rēš agri

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 9/9/2025, 11:38:06 AM
Primary meaning α Arietis
Literal translation rear star of the head of the Hired Man
Variants
MUL₂ ar₂ ša SAG ḪUN kakkabu arkû ša rēš agri
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
α Arietis star: normal star

Jones (2004) c3. In astronomical diaries and related texts

GI₆ 23 ina ZALAG₂ sin SIG MUL₂ ar₂ ša SAG ḪUN 5 KUŠ₃

‘Night of the 23rd, last part of the night ('at dawn'), Sin (Moon) was 5 cubits below the Rear Star of the Head of the Hired Man.’

— BM 41477 (ADRT II No. -194A) obv. 12'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 195 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.
