Θώθ
Thōth

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 4:02:39 PM
Primary meaning Thoth (month I)
Literal translation Thoth
Variants
Θώθ Thōth
Θωύθ Thōuth
Edited by

Thoth (month I) month, time interval

month I in the Alexandrian and Egyptian calendar
