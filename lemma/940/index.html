genitura nocturna

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:51:00 AM
Primary meaning nocturnal nativity
Literal translation nightly birth
Variants
genitura nocturna
nocturna genitura
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
nocturnal nativity astrological concept

Nam si deficientem Lunam deficiens Venus exceperit aut Mars in nocturna genitura, tunc prospera omnia deficiens Luna felicitatesque decernit

‘If Venus and Mars aspect the waning moon in a nocturnal chart, the waning Moon indicates all kinds of prosperity and good fortune’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.7.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.49
