manus gemini

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:42:02 AM
Primary meaning the hands of the Twin
Literal translation hands of the twin
Variants
manus gemini
gemini manus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the hands of the northern Twin

11°–12° Gemini

XI. et XII. in manibus

‘[the degrees] 11 and 12 [are] in the hands [of the northern twin]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
