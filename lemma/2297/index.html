auribus pareo

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:12:02 AM
Primary meaning to hear
Literal translation to obey with the ears
Variants
auribus pareo
pareo auribus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to hear astrological concept

nec non Arcitenens magno parere Leoni auribus

‘The Archer moreover has grown used to waiting on the mighty Lion with his ears’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.504 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 122-123
