attendo

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:10:05 AM
Primary meaning to hear
Literal translation to attend
Variants
attendo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to hear astrological concept

relation between zodiac signs

Pisces ad Scorpion acrem derexere aciem cupiuntque attendere Taurum.

‘The Fishes have fixed their glance upon the fierce Scorpion and crave to listen to the Bull.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.513 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 122-123
