ὁρικός
horikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 7/11/2023, 10:32:40 AM
Primary meaning of terms
Literal translation of boundaries
Variants
ὁρικός horikos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
of terms astrological concept

ἐχθροὶ γάρ εἰσιν οἱ τῶν ἀντικειμένων μοιρῶν ὁρικοὶ κύριοι

‘For the masters of the terms of the degrees that are in opposition are hostile’

— Vettius Valens 3.5.3

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
