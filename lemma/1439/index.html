Crook's Handle star star: ziqpu star

θ and υ Aurigae. Part of the constellation "Crook".

[...] 10 UŠ i-na KI.MIN 18 lim DANNA ina KI.MIN | TA mul.GAM₃ EN mul.KIŠIB-GAM₃

‘[...] 10 UŠ on ditto (the ground), 54 thousand bēru in ditto (heaven), | from the Crook to the Crook's Handle.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) rev. 3-4

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 219
