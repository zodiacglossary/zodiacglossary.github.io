pedes gemini

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:43:02 AM
Primary meaning the feet of the Twin
Literal translation feet of the twin
Variants
pedes gemini
gemini pedes
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the feet of the northern Twin animal part

13°–15° Gemini

XIII. et XIV. et XV. in pedibus

‘[the degrees] 13 and 14 and 15 are in the feet [of the northern twin]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288

the feet of the southern Twin animal part

30° Gemini

in pedibus sola XXX.

‘in the feet [of the southern twin is located] only the 30th degree’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
