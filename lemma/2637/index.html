εὑρίσκω
heuriskō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/3/2024, 12:55:08 PM
Primary meaning to find
Literal translation to find
Variants
εὑρίσκω heuriskō
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to find

Στίλβων ὁ τοῦ Ἑρμο[ῦ] ἀστ[ήρ, ὁ] τὴ[ν] ζώ- νην τοῦ ἀέρος λελογχώς εὑρέθη [τὴ]ν κα- τὰ μῆκος κείνησιν ποιούμενος ἐ[ν] ζῳδίῳ [...] Αἰγόκερῳ

‘Stilbon, the star of Mercury, which has been allotted the belt of the air, has been found making its longitudinal motion in the [...] sign Capricorn’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 i. 2-5

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 420-421
