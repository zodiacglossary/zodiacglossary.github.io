faetho

Latin noun
quoted from Greek

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:49:05 AM
Primary meaning Jupiter
Variants
faetho
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Jupiter planet

quem nos Iovem vocamus, Aegypti Faethontem vocant

‘the one we call Jupiter the Egyptians call Faetho’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.2.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.42
