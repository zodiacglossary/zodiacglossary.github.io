μεσουράνημα
mesouranēma

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/3/2024, 3:30:18 PM
Primary meaning upper midheaven
Literal translation midheaven
Variants
μεσουράνημα mesouranēma
μησ mēs abbreviation (P. Oxy. LXI 4257)
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Kierán Meinhardt
upper midheaven astrological concept

ὡροσκόπος Σκ[ορπίῳ μεσουράνημα .[

‘Ascendant in Scorpio. Midheaven in ...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4238 7-8

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 117 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 252-253 + II: 374-375

Παῦνι ιγʹ εἰς ιδʹ, ὥρᾳ γʹ νυκτός. Ὥρα Ἐγόκερῳ. Μησ Ταύρῳ. Ζεὺς Κριῷ. (...)

‘Pauni 13 till 14, hour 3 of night. Ascendant in Capricorn. Midheaven in Taurus. Jupiter in Aries. (...)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4257 1-2

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 279 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 267 + II: 394-395
