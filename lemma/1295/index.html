KI.TA
šaplâtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/29/2024, 10:20:13 AM
Primary meaning lower part
Literal translation lower part
Variants
KI.TA šaplâtu
šap-lat šaplâtu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
lower part spatial concept

i-nu-šu2 30 šap-lat SI GIR2.TAB

‘At that time, the moon was below the Horn of the Scorpion.’

— AB 251 (BH 2) obv. 3

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon?
Date: 3rd century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: [Darius II Year 14].I.14 = -409 April 29)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 56

netherworld spatial concept
