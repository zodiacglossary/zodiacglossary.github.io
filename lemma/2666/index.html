κατὰ μέτοχον
kata metochon

Greek preposition

Last edited: 11/19/2024, 3:42:18 PM
Primary meaning shared with
Literal translation shared with
Variants
κατὰ μέτοχον kata metochon
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
shared with astrological concept

of triplicities

τριγώνῳ σελήν[η]ς [κ]ατὰ μέτοχον Κρό[νο]υ

‘triangle of the moon shared with Saturn’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 fr. 1, col. i, 8-9

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd-3rd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 284-286 + II: 420-427
