noceo

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 8:53:50 AM
Primary meaning to be harmful
Literal translation to be harmful
Variants
noceo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to be harmful astrological concept

of astrological influence

Lanigeri pars quarta nocet nec sexta salubris;

‘The fourth degree of the Ram inflicts harm, and the sixth is also unwholesome;’

— Manilius, Astronomica 4.444

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 256-257
