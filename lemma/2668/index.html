βόρειος
boreios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 4/8/2025, 1:47:58 PM
Primary meaning northern
Literal translation of the north wind
Variants
βόρειος boreios
βο bo abbreviation
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Kierán Meinhardt
northern cardinal direction

σελήνη οὖσα ἐν Ἰχ[θύσι τὰ βόρια ἢ καὶ συν[ νος ἀντὶ τοῦ ἀνατ[

‘The moon being in Pisces(?) [ the north or even at the node(?) [ ... against(?) the eastern(?)[’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4140 3-5

Genre: astronomical list
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 100-101+ II: 24-25

Λέων | βο | [... Πάρθεν(ος) | βο | γ [...

‘Leo | northern | [... Virgo | northern | 3 [...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4222 fr. 1, 4-5

Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 238-239 + II: 336-337
