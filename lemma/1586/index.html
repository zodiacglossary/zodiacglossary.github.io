geniculae gemini

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:53:29 AM
Primary meaning the knees of the Twin
Literal translation knees of the twin
Variants
geniculae gemini
gemini geniculae
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the knees of the Twin animal part

27°–29° Gemini

in geniculis autem XXVII. et XXVIII. et XXIX

‘in the knees [of the southern twin] however are [the degrees] 27 and 28 and 29’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
