GU
Gula

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/25/2025, 2:38:11 PM
Primary meaning Aquarius
Literal translation Great One
Variants
GU gula
GU.LA gula
mul.GU.LA gula
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Aquarius zodiac sign

(18) ša₂ GENNA TA 10 A EN 30 GU TUR-tu₂ (19) TA 30 GU EN 10 A GAL-tu₂

‘For Saturn: from 10 Leo until 30 Aquarius the small one. From 30 Aquarius until 10 Leo the large one.’

— AO 6477 (BMAPT No. 42) obv. 18-19

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
