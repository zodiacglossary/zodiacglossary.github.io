venter arietis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:47:28 PM
Primary meaning the belly of the Ram
Literal translation belly of the ram
Variants
venter arietis
arietis venter
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the belly of the Ram animal part

23°–25° Aries

in ventre vero ΧΧΠΙ. et XXIV. et XXV.

‘in the belly [of Aries] are [the degrees] 23, 24, and 25’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.287
