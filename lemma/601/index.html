mul.DAR.LUGAL
tarlugallu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:59:05 AM
Primary meaning Rooster-star
Literal translation Rooster-star
Variants
mul.DAR.LUGAL tarlugallu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Rooster-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to Lepus.

DIŠ MUL ša2 EGIR-šu2 GUB-zu mul.DAR.LUGAL

‘¶ The star which stands behind it: the Rooster.’

— Rm 2, 174+313 i 13'

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: ca. 911-612 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 39-40
