aurem intendo

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:11:40 AM
Primary meaning to hear
Literal translation to stretch the ear towards
Variants
aurem intendo
intendo aurem
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to hear astrological concept

relation between zodiac signs

at nudus Geminis intendit Aquarius aurem sublimemque colit Cancrum spectatque reducta tela Sagittiferi.

‘And now to the Twins does the Waterman incline his ear, he naked too; he worships the Crab on high and contemplates the drawn bow of the Archer.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.511 ff

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 122-123
