cor leonis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:37:43 AM
Primary meaning the heart of the Lion
Literal translation heart of the lion
Variants
cor leonis
leonis cor
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the heart of the Lion animal part

tres aliae sequentes in ore sunt Leonis positae, in corde vero totidem

‘the other three following [degrees] are located in the mouth of the Lion, in the heart there are as many degrees as that’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
