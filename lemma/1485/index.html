arcturus

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 12/1/2023, 3:58:10 PM
Primary meaning Arcturus
Literal translation bear's tail
Variants
arcturus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Arcturus star

α Bootis

In Sagittarii signo in parte V. oritur Arcturus.

‘At 5° of the sign of Sagittarius rises Arcturus.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.14.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.308
