qa-tu-u₂
qatû

Akkadian adjective

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:42:55 PM
Primary meaning final
Literal translation final
Variants
qa-tu-u₂ qatû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
final mathematical expression

(27') si-man qa-tu-u₂ ana DU₃-ka a-šar TAB KI si-man-ni-ka TAB a-šar LA₂-u₂ TA si-man-[ni-ka] (28') LA₂ ana si-man qa-tu-u₂ GAR

‘(27’) In order for you to ‘construct’ the final time (interval) (K): where it is additive you add it (C′ ) to your time (interval) (G + J), where it is subtractive you (28’) deduct it from [your] time (interval), you put it down as the final time (interval).’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) obv. ii 27'-28'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
