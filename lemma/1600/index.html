caput leonis

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:58:35 AM
Primary meaning the head of the Lion
Literal translation head of the lion
Variants
caput leonis
leonis caput
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the head of the Lion animal part

1°–2° Leo

I. et II. partes in Leonis sunt capite collocatae,

‘the degrees 1 and 2 of Leo are located in the head’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
