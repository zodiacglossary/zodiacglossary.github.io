akukūtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/10/2024, 7:41:56 AM
Primary meaning Aurora Borealis
Variants
a-ku-ku-tu4 akukūtu
a-ku6-ku6-tu4 akukūtu
ḫa-ku6-ku6-tu4 akukūtu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Aurora Borealis meteorological phenomenon

GE₆ 29 a-ku₆-ku₆-<<ku₆>>-tu₄ ina ŠU₂ KUR 2 ⸢DANNA⸣ [...]

‘Night of the 29th, red glow flared up in the west; 2 bēru [...]’

— VAT 4956 (ADRT I No. -567) rev. 10'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: ?
Date: 568 BC

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.

flame common noun

poetic synonym for fire
