ꜣḫ.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 6/30/2023, 11:13:46 AM
Primary meaning Akhet season
Literal translation Flooding period
Variants
ꜣḫ.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Akhet season calendrical concept

Corresponds to the flooding season.

tpy ꜣḫ.t ḏlꜣ(.t) | ỉbd-2 ꜣḫ.t pꜣ-[nty-ꜣtḥ] ỉbd-3 ꜣḫ.t pꜣ-ḥr-ꜥnḫ | ỉbd-4 ꜣḫ.t pꜣ-twtw-n-pꜣ-mw

‘(Month) 1 of Akhet: Scorpio; | month 2 of Akhet: ⌈Sagittarius⌉; month 3 of Akhet: Capricorn | month 4 of Akhet: Aquarius’

— O.Strasb.Dem. 521 10-12

Genre: astronomical list
Provenance: Thebes (?)
Date: 300 BCE - 300 CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 121-122 121
