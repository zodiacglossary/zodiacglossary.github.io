גדיא
gdyʾ

Aramaic noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:35:03 AM
Primary meaning Capricorn
Literal translation the kid
Variants
גדיא gdyʾ
Edited by

Capricorn zodiac sign

‏[ב¬/]///////// וב3 קש[תא ב3// וב3/// ג]דיא [ב3//// וב3/////]

[b1]9 wb20 qš[tʾ b22 wb23 g]dyʾ [b24 wb25]

‘in [1]9 and in 20, and < in 21 > Sagitt[arius, in 22 and in 23 Cap]ricorn, [in 24 and in 25]’

— 4Q318 iv 9, 8, viii 4.

H. Jacobus, 2015, Zodiac Calendars in the Dead Sea Scrolls and Their Reception 46
