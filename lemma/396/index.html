dỉ.t šm

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:01:25 PM
Primary meaning to give sign
Literal translation to give sign
Variants
dỉ.t šm
ⲧⲓ Ⳅⲉⲙ ϯ ϣⲉⲙ Old Coptic
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
to give sign astrological concept

Refers to planet giving the native a forecast; constructed with dative.

ⲁϥϬⲱⲡⲉ ⲟⲩⲛⲺ ⲥⲟⲩⲛⲟⲩϥⲉ ⲧⲓ ⲛⲉϥ Ⳅⲉⲙ ...

‘If he had a beneficent star giving him a sign ...’

— P.Lond. 98

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95

J. Černý, P.E. Kahle, and R.A. Parker, 1957, The Old Coptic Horoscope, Journal of Egyptian Archaeology 43, 86-100. 88; 98
