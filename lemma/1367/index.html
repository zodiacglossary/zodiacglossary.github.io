locus patris

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:01:54 PM
Primary meaning lot of the father
Literal translation place of the father
Variants
locus patris
patris locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of the father astrological lot

Si locum patris partili volueris ratione colligere, et sit diurna genitura, conputes a parte Solis usque ad partem Saturni omnem partium numerum [...]

‘If you want to compute the lot of the father in a diurnal chart, count from the degree of the Sun until the degree of Saturn the number of all degrees’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.175
