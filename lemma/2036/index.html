limes brumalis

Latin noun

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 7:51:29 AM
Primary meaning tropic of Capricorn
Literal translation winter-solstitial border
Variants
limes brumalis
brumalis limes
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
tropic of Capricorn spatial concept

proximus hunc ultra brumalis nomine limes ultima designat fugientis limina solis

‘Next after this a boundary named the winter tropic marks the furthest station of the retreating Sun’

— Manilius, Astronomica 1.582 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 50-51

See also

〈circulus brumalis〉 ‘tropic of Capricorn’

〈χειμερινὸς τροπικὸς κύκλος〉 cheimerinos tropikos kuklos ‘winter tropic’
