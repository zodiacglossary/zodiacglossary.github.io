12°–14° Leo decan

In Leonis signo quae sint partes plenae, quae vacuae, hoc demonstramus exemplo. Sunt autem plenae partes XXI, vacuae VIIII. I locus partes VII Craumonis [idest afruicois/afriuocis] II locus partes IV vacuus III locus partes III Sic IV locus partes VI vacuus V locus partes X Futile [idest eisie]

‘In the sign of Leo there are some full degrees and some empty. This we will show by example. And there are 21° full degrees and 9° empty. 1. 7° Craumonis [i.e. Afruicois/Afriuocis] 2. 4° empty 3. 3° Sic 4. 6° empty 5. 10° Futile [i.e. Eisie]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.22.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.268
