διαυγάζω
diaugazō

Greek verb

Last edited: 10/9/2023, 10:17:25 AM
Primary meaning to result
Literal translation to shine through
Variants
διαυγάζω diaugazō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to result astrological concept

οὗ τὸ δωδεκατημόριον διηύγαζεν Σκορπίου περὶ τὸν πρῶτον σφόνδυλον

‘Its dodekatemorion shone through (i.e., resulted) in Scoprio around the first joint’

— P.Lond. I 130 iii 68–72

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
