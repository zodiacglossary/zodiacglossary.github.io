ἀγαθός
agathos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 10/7/2025, 8:16:45 AM
Primary meaning benefic
Literal translation good
Variants
ἀγαθός agathos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
benefic astrological concept

οἱ δὲ ἀγαθοὶ ἐπιθεωρήσαντες σίνος ποιοῦσι καὶ πάθος.

‘but when the good planets are looking on, they cause injury and suffering.’

— Hephaestion 1.1

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
