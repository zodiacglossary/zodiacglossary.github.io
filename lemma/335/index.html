ἀρχέτυπος
archetupos

Greek noun

Last edited: 10/31/2023, 11:05:57 AM
Primary meaning lot of fortune
Literal translation exemplar
Variants
ἀρχέτυπος archetupos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
exemplar common noun
lot of fortune astrological concept

ἀρχέτυπος λέγεται ὁ καλούμενος κλῆρος τύχης, τὰ δὲ τούτου τετράγωνα ὑπόστασις

‘The “exemplar” is called the so-called “lot of fortune,” and its quadrangles are the “substance”’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology

CCAG VIII.4 227
