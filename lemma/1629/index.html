pars dextra iugi librae

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:26:03 PM
Primary meaning the right side of the Scales' beam
Literal translation right part of the Scales' beam
Variants
pars dextra iugi
dextra pars iugi
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the right side of the Scales' beam animal part

13°–15° Libra

tres vero sequentes in dextra parte iugi invenimus

‘we find the three following [degrees] in the right side of the Scales' beam’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.7

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.290

See also
