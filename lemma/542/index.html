full month astronomical term

30-day month

GUB.ME u GUR ŠAR₂.ŠAR₂

‘You compute the confirmed (months) and the returned (= hollow months).’

— BM 41004 rev. 19

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon

Brack-Bernsen & Hunger 2005/6, AfO 51, 96-107
