os piscis

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/31/2025, 3:32:52 PM
Primary meaning the mouth of the Fish
Literal translation mouth of the fish
Variants
os piscis
piscis os
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the mouth of the southern Fish animal part

1°–2° Pisces

I. itaque et II. partes in australis Piscis ore collocantur

‘therefore degrees 1 and 2 are located in the mouth of the southern Fish’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.293
