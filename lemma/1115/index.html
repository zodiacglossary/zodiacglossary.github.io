ἐνιαυτός
eniautos

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 2:44:07 PM
Primary meaning year
Literal translation anniversary
Variants
ἐνιαυτός eniautos
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
year calendrical concept, unit of time

Ὁ δὲ ἥλιος ἐνιαυτῷ διαπορεύεται τὸν ζῳδιακὸν κύκλον· (...)

‘The sun goes through the zodiac in a year: (...)’

— Geminus 1.7

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 2
