ἀναβιβάζω
anabibazō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/17/2024, 1:54:21 PM
Primary meaning to ascend
Literal translation to ascend
Variants
ἀναβιβάζω anabibazō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to ascend verb

ἡ δὲ θεὰ καὶ σελασφόρος Σελήνη (...) ἐπὶ τῆς ῥάχεως ἀναβιβάζουσα τοῦ Ταύρου.

‘The goddess, the light-bearing Moon (...) was ascending upon the backbone of the Bull (Taurus).’

— P.Lond. I 130 73–88

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)

Πυρόε[ι]ς δ’ ὁ τοῦ Ἄρεως ἀστὴ̣ρ Ὑδρο̣χόου μοίρας ἀν̣εβίβαζεν δε[κα]ὲξ εἰκοστόν.

‘The fiery one, the star of Ares (Mars) was ascending sixteen degrees (and) one twentieth of Aquarius.’

— P.Lond. I 130 126–130

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
