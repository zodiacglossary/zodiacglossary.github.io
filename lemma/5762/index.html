τῶν ἀπ’ ἄρκτων τοῦ κέντρου βʹ ὁ προηγούμενος
tōn ap arktōn tou kentrou 2 ho proēgoumenos

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:21:19 AM
Primary meaning 45 Ophiuchi
Literal translation The most advanced of the 2 stars to the north of the sting
Variants

45 Ophiuchi star

τῶν ἀπ’ ἄρκτων τοῦ κέντρου βʹ ὁ προηγούμενος

‘The most advanced of the 2 stars to the north of the sting [45 Ophiuchi]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 8

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

Ptolemy. 1903. Claudii Ptolemaei opera quae exstant omnia volumen I, Syntaxis Mathematica Pars II, Libros VII-XIII continens, ed. J.L. Heiberg. Leipzig: B.G. Teubner. 84–129
