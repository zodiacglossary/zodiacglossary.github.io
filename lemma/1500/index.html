praesum

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 10:37:47 AM
Primary meaning to be located
Literal translation to be present
Variants
praesum
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to be located verb of position

In parte XII. Geminorum quicumque habuerit horoscopum, Marte praesente, punctu teli morietur [...]

‘He who has the ascendant at 12° Gemini with Mars present will die by the tip of a spear [...]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.21.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 5.324
