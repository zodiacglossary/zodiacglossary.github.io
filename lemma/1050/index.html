GAL-tu₂
rabītu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/12/2023, 5:15:51 PM
Primary meaning maximum
Literal translation large one
Variants
GAL-tu₂ rabītu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
maximum mathematical quantity

(18) ša₂ GENNA TA 10 A EN 30 GU TUR-tu₂ (19) TA 30 GU EN 10 A GAL-tu₂

‘(18) For Saturn. From 10 Leo until 30 Aquarius the small one; (19) from 30 Aquarius until 10 Leo the large one.’

— AO 6477 (BMAPT No. 42)

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 250-150 BCE

Ossendrijver 2012 No. 42 obv. 18-19
