autumnus

Latin noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:04:31 AM
Primary meaning autumn
Literal translation autumn
Variants
autumnus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
autumn season, time interval

una dies sub utroque aequat sibi tempore noctem, dum Libra atque Aries autumnum verque figurant;

‘One day alone at either season makes equal to itself the night, while the Scales and Ram are shaping autumn and spring;’

— Manilius, Astronomica 3.671 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 216-217
