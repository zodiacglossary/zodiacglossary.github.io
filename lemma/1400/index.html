sepultura

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:41:44 PM
Primary meaning lot of burial
Literal translation burial
Variants
sepultura
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of burial astrological lot

Si sepulturae ——— <sepulturae> exitum monstrat

‘If [you want to calculate] the lot of burial ——— the lot of burial shows the end of life’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.58

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.190
