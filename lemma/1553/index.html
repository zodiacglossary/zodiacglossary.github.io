armus sinister arietis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/1/2023, 4:01:10 PM
Primary meaning the left shoulder of the Ram
Literal translation left shoulder of the ram
Variants
armus sinister arietis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the left shoulder of the Ram animal part

20°–22° Aries

XX. et XXI. et XXII. in anno sinistro

‘[degrees] 20, 21, and 22 are in the left shoulder [of Aries]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.287
