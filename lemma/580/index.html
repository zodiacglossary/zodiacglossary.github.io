ꜥ.wy n ỉr md.t nfr.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 6:45:09 PM
Primary meaning triplicity
Literal translation house of being beneficent
Variants
ꜥ.wy n ỉr md.t nfr.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
triplicity astrological concept

pꜣ ꜥ.wy n ṯsy n ḥr-pꜣ-kꜣ tꜣ ỉh̭y.t [n swsw 20 pꜣy⸗f ꜥ.wy n] ⌈hby pꜣ ỉsw n⌉ swsw 20 | nꜣ ḥ[t]r(.w) ⟨tꜣ ỉh̭y.t ⟩ pꜣ-twtw-n-pꜣ-mw nꜣy⸗f ꜥ.wy.w n ỉr md.t nfr.t ⌈nꜣ.w⌉

‘The exaltation of Saturn: Libra [in 20 degrees; its] ⌈depression: Aries⌉ in 20 degrees;| Gemini, ⟨Lỉbra⟩, and Aquarius are its houses of being beneficent.’

— P.Carlsberg 81 Frag. 31 ii 16-17

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

Unpublished
