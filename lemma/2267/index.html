ἀρῶς
arōs

Greek noun
loaned from Akkadian

Variants

? mathematical quantity: number

possibly 3,600, if equated with σαρός

ἀρῶς· ἀριθμοῦ ὄνομα, παρὰ Πέρσαις

‘arōs: name of a number, according to the Persians.’

— Hesychius α 7607

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE
