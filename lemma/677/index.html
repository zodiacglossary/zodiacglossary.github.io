SAG.KI GU₄
pūt alpi

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:41:53 PM
Primary meaning trapezoid
Literal translation head of ox
Variants
SAG.KI GU₄ pūt alpi
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
trapezoid mathematical figure

1-en SAG.KI GU₄ ša₂ 12 SAG [GAL]

‘One trapezoid of which the large side is (0);12.’

— BM 34081+ (BMAPT No. 18) obv. i 20'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2016, Ancient Babylonian astronomers calculated Jupiters position from the area under a time-velocity graph, Science, 351, 482-484
