ἀνατέλλω
anatellō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/2/2024, 3:46:52 PM
Primary meaning to rise
Literal translation to rise
Variants
ἀνατέλλω anatellō
ἀντέλλω antellō
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
to rise astronomical event

Ἀπορεῖται δή, πῶς καὶ ἐν ταῖς μακροτάταις καὶ ἐν ταῖς βραχυτάταις νύκτεσιν ἡμικύκλιον τοῦ ζῳδιακοῦ κύκλου καὶ ἀνατέλλει καὶ δύνει.

‘The question is then, how both in the longest and in the shortest nights a semicircle of the zodiacal circle both rises and sets.’

— Geminus 7.14

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 44
