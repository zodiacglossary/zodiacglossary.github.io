Ἤπη
Ēpē

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/5/2025, 1:24:44 AM
Primary meaning Decan 2 of Leo
Variants
Ἤπη Ēpē
Ἡπή Hēpē variant reading in MS P
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 2 of Leo decan

οἱ δὲ δεκανοὶ αὐτοῦ τρεῖς· Χαρχνοῦμις ὁ πρῶτος, Ἤπη ὁ δεύτερος, Φούπη ὁ τρίτος

‘The decans of (Leo) are three: Charchnoumis is the first, Ēpē the second, Phoupē the third’

— Hephaestion 1.1.89

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
