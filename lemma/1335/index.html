societas aequata radiationis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:42:41 PM
Primary meaning conjunction
Literal translation equal society of radiation
Variants
societas aequata radiationis
aequata radiationis societas
aequata societas radiationis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
conjunction

Haec etiam in feminarum genituris Venus et Saturnus simul positi, aequata ra<dia>tionis societate decernunt.

‘Venus and Saturn decree this when located in conjunction in nativities of women.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.22.13

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.118
