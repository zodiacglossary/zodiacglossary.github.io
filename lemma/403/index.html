ULU₃
šūtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:48:18 PM
Primary meaning south
Literal translation south
Variants
ULU₃ šūtu
IM.ULU₃ šūtu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
south cardinal direction

[GE₆ 19] DIR AN ZA ULU₃ GIN ina ZALAG₂ sin ar₂ MAŠ.MAŠ ar₂ 1 KUŠ₃ sin 5 KUŠ₃ ana ULU₃ SIG

‘[Night of (day) 19,] clouds were in the sky, the south wind blew; last part of the night, Sin was 1 cubit behind the rear twin, Sin being 5 cubits low to the south.’

— BM 35015+ (ADRT II No. -164B) obv. 7'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 165 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 494-495
