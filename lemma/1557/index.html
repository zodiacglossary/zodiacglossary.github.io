cornua tauri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:38:11 AM
Primary meaning the horns of the Bull
Literal translation horns of the bull
Variants
cornua tauri
tauri cornua
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the horns of the Bull animal part

1°–2° Taurus

I. et II. partes in Tauri cornibus sunt constitutae

‘the 1st and 2nd degree of Taurus are located in its horns’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288

See also
