ἀκμή
akmē

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/16/2024, 10:55:22 AM
Primary meaning peak
Literal translation peak
Variants
ἀκμή akmē
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
peak term of motion

of motion

...] καὶ ἀκμὴ κα[ὶ] ἄνεσις τῆς φ[ορᾶς ...

‘...] both peak and relaxation of the m[otion’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4144 10

Genre: astronomical treatise
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd-3rd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 108-109 + II: 40-41

See also

〈GAL-tu₂〉 rabītu ‘maximum’
