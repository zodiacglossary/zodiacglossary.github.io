Molobobar

loaned from Akkadian

Primary meaning Jupiter

Variants

μολοβόβαρ· ὁ τοῦ Δίος ἀστήρ, παρὰ Χαλδαίοις

‘Molobobar: the star of Zeus, according to the Chaldeans.’

— Hesychius μ 1568

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE
