Χονταχρέ
Chontachre

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 10:59:10 AM
Primary meaning Decan 2 of Aries
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 2 of Aries decan

καὶ ἔστιν ὁ μὲν πρῶτος Χονταρέ, ὁ δὲ δεύτερος Χονταχρέ, ὁ τρίτος Σικέτ

‘The first (decan of Aries) is Chontare, the second Chontachre, the third Siket’

— Hephaestion 1.1.12

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
