ṯsy

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:48:39 AM
Primary meaning to be exalted
Literal translation to rise
Variants
ṯsy
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
to be exalted astrological concept

Verb used of exaltation

[ḥr-pꜣ-šty tꜣ] qnḥy.(t) mtw⸗f ṯsy ẖn⸗s | pꜣ gnhḏ n swsw [1]5 pꜣy⸗f ꜥ.wy n hby pꜣ-ḥr-ꜥnḫ [n swsw 15 pꜣ ỉsw pꜣ mꜣy pꜣ-nty-ꜣtḥ]

‘[Jupiter, the] dignity in which it is exalted: Cancer in 15 degrees, its depression Capricorn [in 15 degrees; Aries, Leo, Sagittarius]’

— P.Carlsberg 81 Frag. 31 ii 17-18

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

A. Winkler, 2016, Some Astrologers and Their Handbooks in Demotic Egyptian, in J.M. Steele (ed.), The Circulation of Astronomical Knowledge in the Ancient World (Leiden: Brill), 245-286 257
