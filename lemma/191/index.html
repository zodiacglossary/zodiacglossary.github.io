λαμπήνη
lampēnē

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/11/2023, 2:56:35 PM
Primary meaning exaltation
Literal translation covered chariot
Variants
λαμπήνη lampēnē
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
covered chariot common noun
exaltation astrological concept

ἐν ἰδίαις λαμπήναις λέγονται οἱ ἀστέρες ὅταν ἐπὶ τῶν ἰδίων ὑψωμάτων τύχωσι

‘The planets are said to be “in their own covered chariots” whenever they are at their own exaltations’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?

CCAG VIII.4 227
