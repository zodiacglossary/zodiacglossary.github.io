septentriones

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:41:47 PM
Primary meaning Ursa Major
Literal translation the seven plough-oxen
Variants
septentriones
septentrio singular
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Ursa Major constellation

cur septentriones numquam caeli rotata vertigo ad occidentem transferat orientique restituat

‘why the spinning of the sky never carries Ursa Major over to the west and back to the east’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.0.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.3
