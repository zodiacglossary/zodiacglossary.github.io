συγχρηματίζω
sunchrēmatizō

Greek verb

Last edited: 7/12/2023, 9:30:08 AM
Primary meaning to be jointly active
Literal translation to do business together
Variants
συγχρηματίζω sunchrēmatizō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to do business together verb
to be jointly active verb

χρηματίζοντες ⟨ἀστέρες⟩ λέγονται οἱ ἐπὶ δυνάμεως ἐπίκεντροι ἢ ἐπίκληροι καὶ οἱ ἐπὶ φάσεως ἢ οἱ τρίγωνοι Ἡλίου γινόμενοι. συγχρηματίζοντες δὲ οἱ κοινῶς ἔγκεντροι ἢ ἔγκληροι καὶ οἱ ἐπὶ φάσεως. ἀχρημάτιστοι δέ εἰσιν οἱ μὴ οὕτως ἔχοντες

‘Stars that are active are called those in force (and) in cardinal points, or “heirs” (or, in lots), and those in phase, or those in trine with the Sun. (Stars) that are jointly active are those generally at cardinal points, or “heirs,” and those in phase. Inactive are those that are not in this disposition’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?
