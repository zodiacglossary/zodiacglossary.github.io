ḫꜥ ỉmnṱ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/25/2025, 7:54:34 AM
Primary meaning evening first
Literal translation western rising
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
evening first synodic phenomenon

Sbk | pꜣy=f ḫꜥ ỉmnṱ 1513 | tꜣy=f rnp.t ꜥꜣ.t 480

‘Mercury | its western rising 1513 | its great year 480’

— ANAShm.Mus.D.O. 483 1-3

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 3rd cent. BCE to 3rd cent. CE

Ossendrijver, M. and A. Winkler, 2018. Chaldeans on the Nile: Two Egyptian Procedure Texts with Babylonian System A1 and A2 for Mercury, in Scaffolding of Our Thoughts: Essays on Assyriology and the History of Science in Honor of Francesca Rochberg, eds. J. Crisostomo et al. (Leiden: Brill, 2018), 282-418
