ἀναβιβάζων
anabibazōn

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/12/2024, 3:24:56 PM
Primary meaning ascending node
Literal translation ascending
Variants
ἀναβιβάζων anabibazōn
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
ascending node astrological concept

ἔστω ὑποδείγματος χάριν Ἥλιος Ταύρου μοίρᾳ αʹ, Σελήνη Διδύμων μοίρᾳ ιϛʹ, ὡροσκόπος Ταύρου μοίρᾳ ιδʹ, ἡ σύνοδος περὶ μοίρας κζʹ τοῦ Κριοῦ, ἀναβιβάζων Αἰγοκέρωτος μοίρᾳ κεʹ

‘As an example, let the Sun be in degree 1 of Taurus, the Moon in degree 16 of Gemini, the Ascendant in degree 14 of Taurus, the conjunction (prenatal syzygy) at about 27 degrees of Aries, the ascending (node) in degree 25 of Capricorn.’

— Vettius Valens 3.13.20

Genre: horoscope
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 74,IV)
