Ulūlu (month VI) month, month name, time interval

¶ iti.KIN mul.PAN d.INANNA ELAM.MA.ki.KE₄ <DUMU.MUNUS AN.NA.KE₄>

‘Month VI, constellation of the Bow, "Elamite" Inanna,’

— KAV 218 a ii 16

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: Neo-Assyrian

Casaburi, M. C. 2003. Tre-stelle-per-ciascun (-mese): l'Astrolabio B: edizione filologica. Annali/Supplemento 37
