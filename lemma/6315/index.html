σελαγίζω
selagizō

Greek verb

Last edited: 8/4/2025, 7:29:01 AM
Primary meaning to shine
Literal translation to shine
Variants
σελαγίζω selagizō
Edited by

to shine

ἐπ[ὶ] τῆς λαγόνος τοῦ Κριοῦ σελαγίζων

‘(the Sun) was shining on the Flank of the Ram (Aries)’

— P.Lond. I 130 63–5

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE
