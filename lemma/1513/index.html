Rare term in astronomical texts. For discussion see Sachs and Neugebauer, 1956 ‘A procedure text concerning solar and lunar motion: BM 36712’, JCS 10, 131–136

NINDA₂-at AN-u₂

‘Measure(?) of heaven’

— BM 36712 (BMAPT No. 102) obv. 2'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE
