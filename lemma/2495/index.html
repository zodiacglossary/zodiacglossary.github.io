reduco

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:44:07 AM
Primary meaning to make rise
Literal translation to bring back
Variants
reduco
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to make rise astronomical event

et quota de bis sex astris pars quaeque reducat

‘and which degree of the zodiac brings each of them back above the horizon’

— Manilius, Astronomica 5.29

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 302-303

See also

〈orior〉 ‘to rise’
