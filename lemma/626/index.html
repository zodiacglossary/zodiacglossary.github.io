d.Šullat2 u d.Ḫaniš2
Šullat u Ḫaniš

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/2/2024, 4:44:07 PM
Primary meaning Šullat-and-Ḫaniš-stars
Literal translation Šullat and Ḫaniš
Variants
d.Šullat2 u d.Ḫaniš2 Šullat u Ḫaniš
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Šullat-and-Ḫaniš-stars constellation

Approx. corresponding to μ Centauri (?), and ε or ν Centauri (?).

DIŠ 2 MUL.MEŠ ša2 EGIR-šu2 GUB.MEŠ2-zu d.Šullat2 u d.Ḫaniš2 d.UTU u d.IŠKUR

‘¶ The two stars which stand behind it: Šullat and Ḫaniš, Šamaš and Adad.’

— BM 86378 ii 25

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 44
