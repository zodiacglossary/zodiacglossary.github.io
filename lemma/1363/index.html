teneo

Latin verb

Last edited: 9/10/2024, 10:58:51 AM
Primary meaning to be located
Literal translation to hold
Variants
teneo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to be located verb of position

Si <Venus in Tauro fuerit> constituta, et eas Tauri partes teneat, in quibus sunt Pleiades positae [...]

‘If Venus is in Taurus and occupies those degrees of Taurus in which the Pleiades are located [...]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.31.60

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.164

to belong to astrological concept

of zodiac signs to regions of earth

Hesperiam sua Libra tenet

‘Italy belongs to the Balance’

— Manilius, Astronomica 4.773

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 284-285
