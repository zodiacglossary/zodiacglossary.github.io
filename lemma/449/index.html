15.nt

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:01:21 PM
Primary meaning full moon (day)
Literal translation 15th (day)
Variants
15.nt
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
full moon astronomical event

ḫr-ỉr=k=f n-ṯꜣy-n pꜣ hrw | n mḥ-4 n pꜣ wrš šꜥ pꜣ hrw n mḥ-15 nty-ỉw 15.nt pꜣy ỉw ỉꜥḥ mḥ wḏꜣ.t

‘You always do it from the | 4th day of the lunar month until day 15, which is the full-moon day, when the Moon fills the Udjat-eye.’

— GEMF 16 294-295

Genre: magical text
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 2nd cent. CE

Faraone, Ch. and Torallas Tovar, S. 2022, Greek and Egyptian Magical Formularies: Text and Translation, vol. I (Berkeley CA) 174

acronychal event astronomical event

8 sw 28 ḏlꜣ.t - ⌈2 0⌉ 1⌈6⌉ 15.nt

‘8 day 28 Scorpio - ⌈2 0⌉ 1⌈6⌉ acronychal’

— P.Monts.Roca. inv. 314 ii x+10

Genre: sign entry table
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 2nd cent. CE

M. Escolano-Poveda, 2018/2019, Astronomica Monserratensia I: A Demotic monthly almanac with synodic phenomena (P.Monts.Roca. inv. 314), Enchoria 36, 1-36. 4; 6.
