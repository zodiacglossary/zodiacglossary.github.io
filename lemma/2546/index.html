אֵתָנִים
ʾēt̲ānîm

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 4:11:30 PM
Primary meaning Etanim (month VII)
Variants
אֵתָנִים ʾēt̲ānîm
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Etanim (month VII) month name, time interval

וַיִּקָּ֨הֲל֜וּ אֶל־הַמֶּ֤לֶךְ שְׁלֹמֹה֙ כׇּל־אִ֣ישׁ יִשְׂרָאֵ֔ל בְּיֶ֥רַח הָאֵתָנִ֖ים בֶּחָ֑ג ה֖וּא הַחֹ֥דֶשׁ הַשְּׁבִיעִֽי׃

wayyiqqāhălû ʾel-hammelek̲ šəlōmōh kol-ʾîš yiśrāʾēl bəyeraḥ hāʾēt̲ānîm beḥāg̲ hûʾ haḥōd̲eš haššəb̲îʿî.

‘The entire body of Israel gathered before King Solomon at the Feast [of Booths], in the month of Ethanim—that is, the seventh month.’

— 1 Kings 8 2

Genre: religious text
