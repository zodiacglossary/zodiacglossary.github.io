radiis adversus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:34:25 PM
Primary meaning in opposition
Literal translation opposite in rays
Variants
radiis adversus
adversus radiis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
in opposition aspect

Si vero Sol et Luna luminum suorum fuerint radiis adversi, tunc vita parentum ———

‘If however sun and moon with their lights are in opposition, then the life of the parents ———’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.25.6

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.129
