d.Iš8-tar2
Ištar

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:08:20 AM
Primary meaning Venus
Literal translation Ištar
Variants
Iš8-tar2 Ištar Transliterations of this name vary in different editions: : aš11/eš4/eš18/iš8-dar/tar2, U.DAR..
d.Iš8-tar2 Ištar
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Ištar deity
Venus planet

DIŠ Iš₈-tar2 ina iti.še-er-BURU14 ina KUR 20 KUR-ma ⸢TUR3⸣ ⸢NIGIN2⸣ KUR ELAM.MA.ki me-sir IGI

‘If Venus in the month of the Harvest Furrow rises at sunrise and is surrounded by a halo: Elam will experience hemming in.’

— K 229+ rev. 24

Genre: celestial divination: enūma anu enlil 59-60
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: 7th century BCE

Reiner, E. & Pingree, D., 1998. Babylonian Planetary Omens: Part Three (Cuneiform Monographs 11). Groningen: Styx. 178
