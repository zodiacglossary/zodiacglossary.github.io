ἀπλανής
aplanēs

Greek adjective

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 8:07:01 AM
Primary meaning fixed
Literal translation unwandering
Variants
ἀπλανής aplanēs
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
fixed astronomical term

said of stars

συνεγγίζει δ’ αὐτῷ ἀπλανὴς ἀστὴρ ὁ μεταξὺ τῶν χηλῶν τοῦ Σκορπίου μεγέθους δʹ ἐπέχων κατὰ μῆκος Σκορπίου δʹ μϛʹ

‘And coming near it (the Sun) is the fixed star between the claws of Scorpius, of magnitude 4, being at a longitude of 4;46 in Scorpio’

— CCAG IV p. 106

Genre: horoscope

(GH L 497)
