MURUB₄-tu₂
qablītu

Akkadian adjective

Last edited: 9/13/2023, 10:57:15 AM
Primary meaning middle one
Literal translation middle one
Variants
MURUB₄-tu₂ qablītu
Edited by

Second of the three watches of the night.
