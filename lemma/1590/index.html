os capricorni

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:24:44 PM
Primary meaning the mouth of Capricorn
Literal translation mouth of the goat-horned
Variants
os capricorni
capricorni os
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the mouth of Capricorn animal part

8°–10° Capricorn

tres sequentes per ordinem in ore Capricorni constituuntur.

‘the three following [degrees] are located in order in the mouth of Capricorn’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
