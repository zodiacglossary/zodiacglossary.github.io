DUG.A.NAG

watering place, drinking place noun
water clock(?) astronomical instrument, artefact of astral science

e-nu-ma ina bi-ib-lu UD-mu er-pu GAL₂-ka li-ti-ik-šu₂ DUG maš-qu-u

‘Should it happen to you that at the disappearance of the moon the weather should be cloudy, the water-clock(?) should be the means of computing it’

— K 2848 65

Genre: handbook
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: Neo-Assyrian

Oppenheim, A. L. 1974. A Babylonian Diviner’s Manual. Journal of Near Eastern Studies, 33(2), 197–220. 200
