עַיִשׁ
ʿayiš

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 4/26/2025, 9:20:16 AM
Primary meaning Aldebaran? Ursa Major? Leo?
Literal translation ?
Variants
עַיִשׁ ʿayiš Job 38:12
עָשׁ ʿāš Job 9:9
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Aldebaran? Ursa Major? Leo? constellation, star

עֹֽשֶׂה־עָ֭שׁ כְּסִ֥יל וְכִימָ֗ה וְחַדְרֵ֥י תֵמָֽן׃

ʿōśeh-ʿāš kəsîl wək̲îmāh wəḥad̲rê t̲ēmān.

‘Who made the Bear(?) and Orion, Pleiades, and the chambers of the south wind;’

— Job 9 9

Genre: religious text

הֲתֹצִ֣יא מַזָּר֣וֹת בְּעִתּ֑וֹ וְ֝עַ֗יִשׁ עַל־בָּנֶ֥יהָ תַנְחֵֽם׃

hăt̲ōṣîʾ mazzārôt̲ bəʿittô wəʿayiš ʿal-bānêhā t̲anḥēm.

‘Can you lead out Mazzaroth(?) in its season, Conduct the Bear with her sons?’

— Job 38 32

Genre: religious text

Identified as "most probable of all" with Leo by Halpern (2002: 260). Halpern, Baruch. 2002. ‘Assyrian and pre-Socratic Astronomies and the Location of the Book of Job’. In Kein Land für sich allein: Studien zum Kulturkontakt in Kanaan, Israel/Palästina und Ebirnâri für Manfred Weippert zum 65. Geburtstag, edited by Ulrich Hübner and Ernst Axel Knauf, 255–64. Orbis biblicus et orientalis 186. Göttingen: Vandenhoeck & Ruprecht. https://www.zora.uzh.ch/id/eprint/141560.
