דָּרוֹם
dārôm

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 5/5/2025, 3:58:25 PM
Primary meaning south
Literal translation south
Variants
דָּרוֹם dārôm
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
south cardinal direction

הוֹלֵךְ֙ אֶל־דָּר֔וֹם וְסוֹבֵ֖ב אֶל־צָפ֑וֹן סוֹבֵ֤ב ׀ סֹבֵב֙ הוֹלֵ֣ךְ הָר֔וּחַ וְעַל־סְבִיבֹתָ֖יו שָׁ֥ב הָרֽוּחַ׃

hôlēk̲ ʾel-dārôm wəsôb̲ēb̲ ʾel-ṣāp̲ôn sôb̲ēb̲  sōb̲ēb̲ hôlēk̲ hārûaḥ wəʿal-səb̲îb̲ōt̲āyw šāb̲ hārûaḥ.

‘Southward blowing, Turning northward, Ever turning blows the wind; On its rounds the wind returns.’

— Ecclesiastes 1 6

Genre: religious text
