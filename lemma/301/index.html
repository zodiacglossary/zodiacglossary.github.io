AN.GE6
attalû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:19:06 AM
Primary meaning eclipse
Literal translation darkening of sky
Variants

eclipse astronomical phenomenon, lunar phenomenon, solar phenomenon

⸢ina⸣ 18 MU.MEŠ 38 ITI AN.GE6 [sin]

‘In 18 years 38 months of an eclipse of [Sin]’

— BM 55555 (BMAPT No. 95) rev. i 6'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE
