ni-ip-ṣu
nipṣu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/30/2023, 1:31:51 PM
Primary meaning ?
Literal translation ?
Variants
ni-ip-ṣu nipṣu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
A type of position in the zodiac that involves two planets. planetary position

Only attested so far in a text containing astrologcial weather forecasts.

ina ni-ip-ṣu ša2 d.SAG.ME.GAR ina mul2.UR.A AN ina mul2.GU ŠEG3 MAḪ | ina mul2.KUN u3 mul2.ABSIN KIMIN

‘At the nipṣu of Jupiter in Leo, and Mars in Aquarius: heavy rain. In Pisces and Virgo: dito.’

— AO 6449 (TCL 6 19 = TU 19) rev. 1-2

Genre: astrological weather prediction
Provenance: Uruk
Date: 226 BCE (12 V Seleucid Era 84)

Hunger, H. 1976. Astrologische Wettervorhersagen. Zeitschrift fur Assyriologie 66: 234–260 248
