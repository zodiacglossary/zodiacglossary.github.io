fauces piscis

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/31/2025, 3:32:18 PM
Primary meaning the throat of the Fish
Literal translation throat of the fish
Variants
fauces piscis
piscis fauces
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the throat of the southern Fish animal part

6°–7° Pisces

in faucibus duae

‘in the throat [of the southern Fish] are the two [following degrees]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.293

See also
