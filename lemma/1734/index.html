rainbow meteorological phenomenon

TIR.AN ina KUR GIB

‘A rainbow stretched in the east.’

— BM 36729 (ADRT I No. -343) rev. 17'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: -343 (Artaxerxes III year 15)

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 156
