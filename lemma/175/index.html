ἰσχύω
ischuō

Greek verb

Last edited: 8/30/2023, 9:20:06 AM
Primary meaning to be strong
Literal translation to be strong
Variants
ἰσχύω ischuō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to be strong verb

ἰσχύοντες λέγονται ἀστέρες οἱ ἐπὶ τῶν ἐπικαίρων τῆς γενέσεως τόπων τύχοντες· τουτέστι ἐπίκεντροι· καὶ οἱ ἐπὶ φάσεως καὶ οἱ ἐπίκληροι καὶ οἱ ἐν ταῖς συνόδοις ἀκριβῶς ὄντες

‘Those planets are said to be strong that are in the opportune places in a nativity—that is, at cardinal points—and those in phase and “heirs” (or, in lots) and those precisely in conjunction’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?

CCAG VIII.4 226
