στερεός
stereos

Greek none

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 8:05:43 AM
Primary meaning fixed
Literal translation solid
Variants
στερεός stereos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
fixed zodiac sign quality, astrological concept

ζῳδίωι Ἀφροδίτης ὑψώματι ἰδίωι ὁρίοις Ἑρμοῦ θηλυκῶι καὶ στερεῶι ζῳδίωι

‘(The Moon was) in (Taurus), a zodiac sign of Venus, its own exaltation, terms of Mercury, a female and solid zodiac sign’

— P.Lond. I 130 80–85

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
