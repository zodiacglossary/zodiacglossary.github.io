šny

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 6/30/2023, 10:32:06 AM
Primary meaning to make a (divinatory) consultation
Literal translation to ask
Variants
šny
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
to make a (divinatory) consultation verb

pꜣ gꜣy (n) šny m-sꜣ ⌈pꜣ⌉ ꜥ.wy (n) [...] ⌈nty-ỉw=w ỉr=f (n) pꜣ ⌈rmṯ⌉ mtw=w r ms.ṱ=f šꜥ-ỉr=w pꜣ šn pꜣ twtw nty-ỉw ỉꜥḥ ẖn=f

‘The manner of enquiring after the house of [...] which they will make for the man who will be born. One always examines the zodiac sign in which the Moon is.’

— P.Carlsberg XI 10 11

Genre: li
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd Cent. CE

Quack, J. F. and K. Ryholt, 2019, Petese Interpreting Astrology by Imhotep for King Nechepsos. Copenhagen, 2019, 161-183. 165
