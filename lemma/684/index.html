ἀφαιρετικός
aphairetikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 7/9/2023, 9:57:05 AM
Primary meaning in retrograde
Literal translation removing
Variants
ἀφαιρετικός aphairetikos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
in retrograde planetary motion

Κρόνον Κρειῷ ι̣αʹ λε̣[π](τῶν) λ̣β̣ʹ ἀκρώνυκτον ἀφαιρετι[κό]ς

‘Saturn in 11 (degrees) 32 minutes of Aries, acronychal, in retrograde’

— P.Oxy. 1476 5–6

(GH 260)

See also

〈retrogradus〉 ‘retrograde’
