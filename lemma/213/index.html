IGI ša₂ KUR
nanmurtu ša šadê?

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:37:28 PM
Primary meaning Morning First
Literal translation eastern appearance
Variants
IGI ša₂ KUR nanmurtu ša šadê?
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Morning First synodic phenomenon

(1) ⸢ša₂ d⸣.GU₄.UD IGI ša₂ KUR a-na IGI ša₂ KUR (2) TA 1 A EN 16 MAŠ₂ 1.46 TAB

‘(1) For Mercury. Eastern appearance until eastern appearance (2) from 1 Leo until 16 Capricorn you add 1,46.’

— AO 6477 (BMAPT No. 42) obv. 1-2

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer
