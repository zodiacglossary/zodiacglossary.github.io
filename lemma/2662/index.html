κληρόω
klēroō

Greek verb

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:25:31 AM
Primary meaning to allot
Literal translation to allot
Variants
κληρόω klēroō
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to allot astrological concept

Πυρόεις ὁ τοῦ] Ἄρεος [ἀ]στὴρ ὁ τ[ὴν ζώνην τοῦ π]όλου κλ[η]ρωθεὶς ψ[

‘Pyroeis] the star of Mars which has been allotted the [belt of the] heavens,’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4283 4-5

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 3rd-4th century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 432-433

See also

〈κλῆρος〉 klēros ‘lot’
