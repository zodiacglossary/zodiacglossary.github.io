ὀλιγόγονος
oligogonos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 1/6/2025, 3:46:11 PM
Primary meaning unprolific
Literal translation producing few offspring
Variants
ὀλιγόγονος oligogonos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
unprolific zodiac sign quality

Κριός ἐστιν (…) ζῴδιον (…) ὀλιγόγονον

‘Aries is an (…) unprolific zodiac sign’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.1

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
