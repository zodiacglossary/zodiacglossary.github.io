mul.LU.LIM
lulīmu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:09:59 AM
Primary meaning Stag-star
Literal translation Stag-star
Variants
mul.LU.LIM lulīmu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Stag-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to the eastern part of Andromeda.

DIŠ MUL ša2 EGIR-šu2 GUB-zu mul.lu-lim SUKAL MUL.MUL

‘¶ The star which stands behind it: the Stag, the minister of the Stars.’

— BM 86378 i 31

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 36

See also
