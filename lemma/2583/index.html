תורא
twrʾ

Aramaic noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:37:25 AM
Primary meaning Taurus
Literal translation the ox
Variants
תורא twrʾ
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Taurus zodiac sign

אדר ב/ זב// דכרא ב/// וב//// תורא ב//[/// וב////// וב/////// תאומיא]

ʾdr b1 wb2 dkrʾ b3 wb4 twrʾ b[5 wb6 wb7 tʾwmyʾ]

‘Adar. In 1 and 2 Aries, in 3 and 4 Taurus, in 5 [and in 6 and in 7 Gemini]’

— 4Q318 vii 5, viii 1

H. Jacobus, 2015, Zodiac Calendars in the Dead Sea Scrolls and Their Reception 46
