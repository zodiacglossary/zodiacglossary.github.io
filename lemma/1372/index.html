divido

Latin verb

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:41:49 AM
Primary meaning to divide
Literal translation to divide
Variants
divido
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to divide mathematical operation

et omnium partium summa collecta, ab horoscope inchoans totas partes sequentibus divides signis

‘and when the sum of all degrees is collected, divide all degrees by the following signs, starting from the ascendant’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.21

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.180

See also

〈BAR〉 zâzu ‘to divide’

〈παραβάλλω〉 paraballō ‘to divide’
