mul.giš.GAN2.UR3
maškakātu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:10:17 AM
Primary meaning Harrow-star
Literal translation Harrow-star
Variants
mul.giš.GAN2.UR3 maškakātu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Harrow-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to the eastern part of Vela.

DIŠ MUL ša2 ina A2-šu2 GUB-zu mul.giš.GAN2.UR3 giš.TUKUL ša2 d.A-E2 ša2 ina lib3-bi-šu2 ZU.AB i-bar-ru-u2

‘¶ The star which stands at its side: the Harrow, the weapon of Mār-bīti, by means of which he sees the subterranean waters.’

— BM 86378 ii 23-24

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 43-44
