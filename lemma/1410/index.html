MUL ša₂ maš-a-ti
kakkabu ša₂ māšāti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 9:48:09 AM
Primary meaning Star from the Doublets
Literal translation Star from the Doublets
Variants
MUL ša₂ maš-a-ti kakkabu ša₂ māšāti
MUL2 ša maš-ša2-a-tu2 kakkabu ša₂ māšāti
MUL2 UTU-a-ta kakkabu šamašāta
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Star from the Doublets star: ziqpu star

β Herculis

2 1/2 MA.NA MIN 1/2 DANNA i-na KI.MIN 27 lim DANNA ina [AN-e] | TA mul.GAM-ti EN MUL ša₂ maš-a-ti

‘2 1/2 minas ditto (weight), 1/2 bēru on ditto (the ground), 27 thousand bēru in [heaven], | from the Circle to the Star from the Doublets.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) obv. 9'-10'

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 218
