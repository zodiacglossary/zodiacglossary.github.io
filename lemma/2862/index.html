Παφία
Paphia

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/6/2025, 6:03:18 AM
Primary meaning Venus
Literal translation Paphian one
Variants
Παφία Paphia
Παφίη Paphiē poetic form
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Venus planet

ἢ Διὸς ἢ Παφίης ἐν σχήμασι μαρτυρεόντων ἢ καὶ συνεσσομένων

‘With Jupiter or the Paphian (Venus) witnessing in configurations or even being together (in the same sign)’

— Dorotheus 5.6.1–7.2

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
