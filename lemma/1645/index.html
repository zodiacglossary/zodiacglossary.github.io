cervix sagittarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:33:24 AM
Primary meaning the neck of the Archer
Literal translation neck of the archer
Variants
cervix sagittarii
sagittarii cervix
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the neck of the Archer animal part

8°–10° Sagittarius

tres sequentes in ordine in Sagittarii cervice ponentur

‘the three following [degrees] in order will be placed in the neck of the Archer’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
