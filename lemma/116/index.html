Ajjaru (month II) month, month name, month II, time interval

¶ iti.GU₄ MUL.MUL d.IMIN.BI DINGIR GAL.GAL.E.NE ki.PAD.RA₂ GU₄<MEŠ> SI.SA₂.NE

‘Ajaru, constellation of the Pleiades, Sebettu The great gods opening of the earth, oxen are headed’

— KAV 218 a i 12-14

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Nineveh
