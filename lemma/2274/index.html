ψηφίζω
psēphizō

Greek verb

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:30:17 AM
Primary meaning to calculate
Literal translation to calculate
Variants
ψηφίζω psēphizō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to calculate mathematical operation

ἐπιγνόντες τὴν τῶν ἑπτὰ θεῶν κείνησιν τὰ ὅλα σ̣[υ]νέχουσάν τε καὶ διοικοῦσαν ἀφ[θ]ό̣νως ἡμεῖν διὰ κανόνων αἰωνί̣ων ἀπέλειπ̣ον τὴν περὶ αὐτῶν γνῶσιν· ὅθεν ἕκαστον ψηφίσας ἀκριβῶς τέταχά σοι

‘(The ancient Egyptians,) having recognized the motion of the seven (planetary) gods, which comprises and dispenses all things, have generously left us with the knowledge about them through perpetual tables: having precisely calculated each (planet’s position) by those means, I have set them out for you’

— P.Lond. I 130 6–19

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
