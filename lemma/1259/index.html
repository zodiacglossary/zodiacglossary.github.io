ἐκλάμπω
eklampō

Greek verb

Last edited: 10/9/2023, 10:14:19 AM
Primary meaning to result
Literal translation to shine forth
Variants
ἐκλάμπω eklampō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to result astrological concept

ἧς τὸ δωδεκατημόριον Σκορπίου πάλιν περὶ τὸν αὐτὸν τόπον ἐξέλαμπεν̣

‘Its dodekatemorion shone forth (i.e., resulted) in Scorpio again around the same place’

— P.Lond. I 130 iv 91–95

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
