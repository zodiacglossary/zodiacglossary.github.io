ὑπεροχή
huperochē

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 11:09:06 AM
Primary meaning difference
Literal translation excess
Variants
ὑπεροχή huperochē
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
difference mathematical entity, mathematical quantity

ἔστιν οὖν ἡ τούτ(ων) τ(ῶν) μή[κων] ὑπεροχὴ ἐν μο(ίραις) κδʹ ιʹ

‘Hence the difference between those longitudes in degrees is 24;10’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 Fr. 2, col. ii, l. 18-19

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 30

See also

〈taš-pil-tu₂〉 tašpiltu ‘difference’

〈αὐξομείωσις〉 auxomeiōsis ‘difference’
