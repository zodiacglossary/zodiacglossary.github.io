facies gemini

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:40:09 AM
Primary meaning the face of the Twin
Literal translation face of the twin
Variants
facies gemini
gemini facies
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the face of the northern Twin animal part

3°–5° Gemini

III. vero et IV. et V. in eiusdem Gemini facie sunt

‘[the degrees] 3 and 4 and 5 however are in the face of that same [northern] twin’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288

the face of the southern Twin animal part

21°–23° Gemini

in facie vero XXI. et XXII. et XXIII.

‘in the face [of the southern twin are the degrees] 21 and 22 and 23’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
