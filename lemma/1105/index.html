vocalis

Latin adjective

Last edited: 10/17/2024, 8:59:48 AM
Primary meaning voiced
Literal translation voiced
Variants
vocalis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
voiced astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

the signs Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Virgo, Aquarius

facit etiam frequenter oratores et advocates, praesertim si in hoc loco vel in suis signis vel in ceteris vocalibus signis fuerit inventus.

‘Often he [Mercury] makes orators and lawyers, especially if in this house he is in his own sign or in other voiced signs.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 3.7.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.155

See also

〈signum modicae vocis〉 ‘semi-voiced sign’

〈mutus〉 ‘unvoiced’

〈εὔφωνος〉 euphōnos ‘good-voiced’
