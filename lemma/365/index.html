KUR₄
ba'ālu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:25:47 PM
Primary meaning to be bright
Literal translation to be bright
Variants
KUR₄ ba'ālu
KUR ba'ālu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to be bright verb of visual perception

ŠE 1 23 KUR₄ ina šamaš₂ GUB IGI

‘Month XII, (day) 1 (which followed day 30 of the preceding month), (sunset to moonset) was 23 (UŠ); it was bright, it could be seen while Šamaš stood there.’

— BM 33837 (ADRT II No. -232) rev. 25

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 233 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 114-115

9 GU₄.UD ina NIM ina MAŠ.MAŠ-IGI KUR NIM-a 18.30 na-su in 5 IGI

‘(Day) 9, Mercury’s morning rising in Gemini; it was bright (and) high, its presence (NA) was 18;30; (ideal) appearance on (day) 5.’

— BM 45608 (ADRT II -209D) rev. 5

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 210 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 180-181
