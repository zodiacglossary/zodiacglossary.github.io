sbꜣ(.t)

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:41:14 PM
Primary meaning teaching
Literal translation teaching
Variants
sbꜣ(.t)
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
teaching noun

Can refer to the content of an astrological manual

šꜥ ntf=⌈f ṯk⌉ꜥšꜣ.w (n) tꜣy=f sbꜣ(.t) ẖr md.t [nb.t nty pḥ] r tꜣ p.t

‘He (Imhotep) explains numerous drawings (diagrams) in his teaching concerning [every] matter [that pertains] to the sky.’

— P.Carlsberg XI 10 11

Genre: literary text
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd Cent. CE

Quack, J. F. and K. Ryholt, 2019, Petese Interpreting Astrology by Imhotep for King Nechepsos. Copenhagen, 2019, 161-183. 164
