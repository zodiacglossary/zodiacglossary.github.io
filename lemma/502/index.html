ἀνατολικὸν κέντρον
anatolikon kentron

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/22/2025, 10:43:52 AM
Primary meaning Ascendant
Literal translation rising center
Variants
ἀνατολικὸν κέντρον anatolikon kentron
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Ascendant cardinal point

αὕτη μὲν οὖν ἐν Αἰγοκέρωτι ἐγένετο κατὰ τὸ ἀνατολικὸν κέντρον.

‘This (eclipse) happened in Capricorn at the rising center (Ascendant).’

— Marinus, Life of Proclus §37

Genre: horoscope
Date: 5th century CE

(GH L 412)
