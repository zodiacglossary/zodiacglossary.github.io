nnm

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 8:54:59 AM
Primary meaning to move irregularly
Literal translation to err
Variants
nmm
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
to move irregularly verb of motion

mꜣꜣ mꜣꜣ.wt nb(.wt) m p.t m tꜣ wbꜣ-ỉb mꜣꜣ ḫꜣbꜣs.w n nnm n.im=sn

‘The one who observes all that is observable in the sky and on earth, who is taught in observing the stars among which there is no erring.’

— JE 38545 1

Genre: autobiographical inscription
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 330-30 BCE

Altmann-Wendling, Victoria and Marco Stockhusen 2020. Die Statue eines ägyptische Priesters und Astronomen (JE 38545). In Färber, Roland and Rita Gautschy (eds), Zeit in den Kulturen des Altertums: antike Chronologie im Spiegel der Quellen, 103-115. Wien; Köln; Weimar: Böhlau 104
