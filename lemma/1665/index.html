cauda capricorni

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:32:12 AM
Primary meaning the tail of Capricorn
Literal translation tail of the goat-horned
Variants
cauda capricorni
capricorni cauda
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the tail of Capricorn animal part

28°–30° Capricorn

tres posteriores per ordinem is collocantur in cauda

‘the three latter [degrees] in order are located in the tail [of Capricorn]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
