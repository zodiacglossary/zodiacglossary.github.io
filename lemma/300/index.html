hrw

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:02:46 PM
Primary meaning day
Literal translation day
Variants
hrw
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
day time interval

ẖsb.t 43 | tpy ꜣḫ.t sw 16 pꜣ ṯꜣy-wnw.t 8.t n | pꜣ hrw

‘Regnal year 43 | first month of Akhet, 8th hour of | the day’

— O.Neugb. 1 1-2

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. AD

O. Neugebauer, “Demotic Horoscopes”, JAOS 63 (1943), 115–127. 116-117
