αὐθημέρᾳ
authēmerai

Greek adverb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:12:44 AM
Primary meaning on that day
Literal translation on that day
Variants
αὐθημέρᾳ authēmerai
αὐθημερί authēmeri
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
on that day temporal expression

Ἄρης αὐθημέρᾳ Ἰχθύσι

‘Mars, that day in Pisces’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4266 6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 312

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. 404

〈ἡμέρα〉 hēmera ‘day’
