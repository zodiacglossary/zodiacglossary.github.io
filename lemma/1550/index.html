MUL
kakkabu

Akkadian noun

Primary meaning star
Literal translation star
Variants
MUL kakkabu

star astronomical term, celestial object

DIŠ 1 MUL MU2-uḫ : U4 1-KAM ina ŠA3 <<ina ŠA3>> d.UDU.IDIM.MEŠ IGI-ma : ša2-niš MUL2 <ana> ŠA3-šu2 | KU4-ma

‘¶ "If one star rises" (means): on the 1st day among «among» the planets it appears; alternatively (it means): a star enters inside it.’

— AO 6464 (TCL VI 17 = TU 17) rev. 18-19

Genre: commentary
Provenance: Uruk
Date: late 4th century BCE

Hunger, H. 1995. Ein Kommentar zu Mond-Omina. In: Vom Alten Orient zum Alten Testament, Festschrift für Wolfram Freiherrn von Soden zum 85. Geburtstag am 19. Juni 1993, eds.W. Dietrich and O. Loretz. Butzon & Kevelaer: 105-118.
