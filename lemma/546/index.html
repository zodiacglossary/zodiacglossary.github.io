foundation noun

rḫ pꜣ snṱ n tꜣ p.t

‘Know the foundation of the sky’

— P.BM. EA 10805 xxv 12

Genre: literary text
Provenance: Akhmim
Date: Ptolemaic

S.R.K. Glanville, 1955, The Instructions of Onchsheshonqy, London.
