MUL ne₂-bu-u₂ ša₂ GABA-šu₂
kakkabu nebû ša irtišu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/2/2024, 4:53:15 PM
Primary meaning Bright Star of its Breast
Literal translation Bright Star of its Breast
Variants
MUL ne₂-bu-u₂ ša₂ GABA-šu₂ kakkabu nebû ša irtišu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Bright Star of its Breast star: ziqpu star

α Cygni. "Its Breast" refers to the Demon-with-the-gaping-mouth-star.

3 1/3 MA.NA MIN 2/3 DANNA i-na KI.MIN 36 lim DANNA ina KI.MIN | TA MUL ne₂-bi-i ša₂ GABA-šu₂ EN mul.kin-⸢ṣi⸣

‘3 1/3 minas ditto (weight), 2/3 bēru on ditto (the ground), 36 thousand bēru in ditto (heaven), from the Bright Star of its Breast to the Knee.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) obv. 21'-22'

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 218
