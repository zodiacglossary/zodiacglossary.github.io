τῆς ἑπομένης πλευρᾶς τῶν βʹ ὁ βορειότερος
tēs hepomenēs pleuras tōn 2 ho boreioteros

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:20:32 AM
Primary meaning 82 Virginis
Literal translation The northernmost of the 2 stars on the rear side
Variants

‘The northernmost of the 2 stars on the rear side [82 Virginis]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 7

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
