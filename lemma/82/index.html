Dilbat

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/11/2023, 6:33:14 AM
Primary meaning Venus
Variants
d.Dil-bat Dilbat
mul.Dil-bat Dilbat
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Venus planet

aš2-šu2 d.GU4.UD u d.Dil-bat KI 30 2-ta MUNUS.MEŠ

‘Because Mercury and Venus (stand) with the Moon: two females (will be born).’

— BM 35402 (LBAT 1593) obv. 10'-11'

Genre: horoscopic astrology
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 4th-2nd century BCE

Reiner, E. 2000. Early Zodiologia and Related Matters. In: Wisdom, Gods and Literature, Studies in Assyriology in Honour of W. G. Lambert, eds. Andrew R. George and I. L. Finkel. Winona Lake, Indiana: Eisenbauns: 421-427. 422
