trigonum martis

Latin noun

Last edited: 10/17/2024, 8:57:52 AM
Primary meaning Mars' triangle
Literal translation Mars' triangle
Variants
trigonum martis
martis trigonum
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Mars' triangle astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

the watery signs Cancer, Scorpio and Pisces

Si Luna et Mars in septimo sint ab horoscope loco constituti, id est in occasu, et sint in eo signo constituti, in quo Martis invenitur trigonum, id est aut in Cancro aut in Scorpione aut in Piscibus, [...]

‘If Moon and Mars are located in the 7th place, i.e. the descendant, and they are located in the sign in which is found Mars' triangle, i.e. in Cancer or in Scorpio or in Pisces’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.31.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.149
