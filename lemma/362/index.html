sꜣḥ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:47:46 PM
Primary meaning Orion
Literal translation Orion
Variants
sꜣḥ
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Orion constellation

tw=w šm pꜣy=k by ỉrm swḥ r-tw wsỉr pꜣy ỉrm nꜣ sỉw.w nty šms n spd.t

‘Cause that your ba-sould goes with Orion, who is Osiris, and the stars, which follow Sothis.’

— P.Rhind I vi 5d

Genre: religious text
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. BCE

G. Moller, 1913, Die beiden Totenpapyrus Rhind des Museum su Edinburg (Leipzig)
