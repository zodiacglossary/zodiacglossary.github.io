ᵈUTU.E₃.A
ṣīt Šamši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/1/2023, 9:37:09 AM
Primary meaning East
Literal translation sunrise
Variants
ᵈUTU.E₃.A ṣīt Šamši
UTU.E₃.A ṣīt Šamši
UTU.E₃ ṣīt Šamši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
East cardinal direction

primarily in texts predating ca. 400 BCE, rare in astronomical diaries

ina ZALAG₂ ina ᵈUTU.E₃.A ṣal-⸢lum⸣-[mu-u₂ ...]

‘Last part of the night, a com[et] in the east [...]’

— Rm 694+ (ADRT II No. -234A) rev. 3'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 235 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 96-97
