previous month was hollow

The number 30 following the month name in horoscopes indicates that the previous month had 29 days, or was hollow.

[it]i.NE 30 GE6 4 SAG GE6 30 | SIG SA4 ša2 ABSIN 1 5/6 KUŠ3 30 1/2 KUŠ3 | ana NIM DIB U4.4.KAM LU2 a-lid

‘[Mon]th V 30, night of the 4th, beginning of night, the moon was | below the bright star of the Furrow (= a Virginis) by 1 5/6 cubits, the moon passed 1/2 cubit | to the east. On the 4th day the child was born.’

— BM 47642 (BH 13) 2-4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 3rd(-2nd?) century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: S.E. 88 V.4 = -223 July 29)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 89
