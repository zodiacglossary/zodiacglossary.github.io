οἴαξ
oiax

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:20:20 AM
Primary meaning ascendant
Literal translation rudder
Variants
οἴαξ oiax
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
ascendant astrological concept, cardinal point

οἴαξ ὁ [κ]αλούμενος ὡ[ροσκό]πος, [. . . . . . .] κυβερ[ν]ήτης κατὰ τ[. .] [. . .]ει[. . . .]αρ[. . .]

‘The rudder called 'ascendant' ... steersman ...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 i. 17-18

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 420-421

See also

〈ὥρα〉 hōra ‘hour’

〈ἀνατολικὸν κέντρον〉 anatolikon kentron ‘Ascendant’
