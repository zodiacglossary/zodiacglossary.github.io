Addaru (month XII) lunar month, month name, month XII, time interval

iti.ŠE še-um in-ne-eṣ-ṣi-id maš-ka-na-a-tu₄ EDIN i-ma-al-la-a

‘Month XII: barley is harvested; barns in the plain are filled’

— KAV 218 14

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Assur
Date: Neo-Assyrian

Casaburi, M. C. 2003. Tre-stelle-per-ciascun (-mese): l'Astrolabio B: edizione filologica. Annali/Supplemento 50
