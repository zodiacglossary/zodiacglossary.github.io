Φαίνων
Phainōn

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/14/2025, 5:59:29 AM
Primary meaning Saturn
Literal translation Shining one
Variants
Φαίνων Phainōn
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Saturn planet

Φαίνων γε μένεσσιν ἐρύκει δερκόμενος, μαλερὴν δὲ περὶ φλόγα Θοῦρος ὀρίνει

‘The Shining one (Saturn) wards off raging fire (from houses) with its strength when in aspect, but the Furious one (Mars) provokes it’

— Dorotheus 5.6.1–7.2

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
