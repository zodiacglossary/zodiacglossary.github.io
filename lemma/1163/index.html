stationem facio

Latin verb

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 9:23:00 AM
Primary meaning to become stationary
Literal translation to make a station
Variants
stationem facio
facio stationem
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to become stationary astronomical event, synodic phenomenon

Si vero per noctem minuta luminibus Saturno retrograde vel stationem facienti se Luna coniunxerit, magnarum infelicitatum decernit <incommoda>;

‘If however in a night chart the waning Moon aspects Saturn while in retrograde or stationary, it indicates major misfortune’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.16.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.236
