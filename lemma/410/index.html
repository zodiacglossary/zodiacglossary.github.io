MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ ALLA ša₂ ULU₃
kakkabu arkû ša allutti ša šūti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:09:23 PM
Primary meaning δ Cancri
Literal translation the rear star of the crab to the south
Variants
MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ ALLA ša₂ ULU₃ kakkabu arkû ša allutti ša šūti
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
δ Cancri star: normal star

Jones (2004) c13

GE₆ 22 ina ZALAG₂ sin ar₂ MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ ALLA ša₂ ULU₃ 2 1/2 KUŠ₃ sin 1 KUŠ₃ ana ULU₃ SIG

‘Night of (day) 22, last part of the night, Sin was 2 1/2 cubits behind the rear star of the crab to the south, Sin being 1 cubit low to the south.’

— BM 32839+ (ADRT III No. -141F) obv. 6'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 142 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 124-125
