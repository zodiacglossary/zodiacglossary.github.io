MAŠ.MAŠ IGI
māšu maḫrû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:25:40 PM
Primary meaning α Geminorum
Literal translation front twin
Variants
MAŠ.MAŠ IGI māšu maḫrû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
α Geminorum star: normal star

Jones (2004) c11

GI₆ 15 ina [ZALAG₂] ⸢sin⸣ ina IGI MAŠ.MAŠ IGI 2 KUŠ₃

‘Night of (day) 15, last [part of the night], Sin was 2 cubits in front of the front twin (star).’

— BM 34362+ (ADRT II -197B) obv. 15

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 198 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 250-251
