ἀφαιρέτης
aphairetēs

Greek noun

Last edited: 10/14/2024, 6:30:58 AM
Primary meaning in retrograde
Literal translation remover
Variants
ἀφαιρέτης aphairetēs
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
in retrograde planetary motion

Κρόνος Κριοῦ κδʹ κηʹ (...) ἀπὸ πρώτου τοῦ στηριγμοῦ, ἀφαιρέτης, ἑσπέριος φαινόμενος

‘Saturn, in Aries 24;28 (...) appearing in the evening after the first station, in retrograde’

— CCAG IV p. 107

Genre: horoscope
