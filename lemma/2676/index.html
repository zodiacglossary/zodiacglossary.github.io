ἀναλύω
analuō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/12/2024, 3:41:24 PM
Primary meaning to convert
Literal translation to unloose
Variants
ἀναλύω analuō
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to convert mathematical operation

(...) τὰς δο[θεί-] σας κ(αι)ρικὰς ἡμερινὰ[ς] ὥρ(ας) [ἀ]ναλῦσαι ε[ἰς] τὰς ἰσημ[ερινάς,] (...)

‘(...) to convert the given seasonal hours of day into equinoctial (hours), (...)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 fr. 1, 15-17

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 4th century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 102-105 + II: 26-35
