pr.t mḥ-1.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:35:29 AM
Primary meaning first visibility
Literal translation first appearance
Variants
pr.t mḥ-1.t
pr mḥ-1
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
first visibility (of planet) synodic phenomenon

2 sw 2⌈5 mꜣy - 21 0 21 pr mḥ-1⌉

‘2 day 2⌈5 Leo - 21 0 21 first appearance⌉’

— P.Monts.Roca. inv. 314 ii x+15

Genre: sign entry table
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 2nd cent. CE

M. Escolano-Poveda, 2018/2019, Astronomica Monserratensia I: A Demotic monthly almanac with synodic phenomena (P.Monts.Roca. inv. 314), Enchoria 36, 1-36. 4; 6
