Δύστρος
Dustros

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:43:46 PM
Primary meaning Dustros
Literal translation Dustros
Variants
Δύστρος Dustros
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Dustros month, time interval

month V in the Macedonian calendar

Δύστρος· ὑπὸ Μακεδόνων μήν

‘Dustros: a month among the Macedonians.’

— Hesychius δ 2675

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2018. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 1 (A - Delta), ed. I.C. Cunningham. Berlin, Boston: Walter de Gruyter. 656
