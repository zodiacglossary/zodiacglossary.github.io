šūḫuzu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:36:24 AM
Primary meaning to let proceed
Variants
tu-šaḫ-ḫa-az tušaḫḫaz causative Š stem of aḫāzu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to let proceed mathematical operation

used in mathematical astronomy in computations of motion

(22) ina 5 DANNA si-man 5 UŠ (23) ᵈsin ana ᵈUTU.ŠU₂.A tu-šaḫ-ḫa-az

‘(22) In 5 bēru time (23) you let Sin proceed 5 UŠ to the west.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. ii 22-23

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
