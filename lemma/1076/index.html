δίσωμος
disōmos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 8:02:00 AM
Primary meaning double-bodied
Literal translation two-bodied
Variants
δίσωμος disōmos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
double-bodied astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

ὡσαύτως δὲ καὶ ἐνθάδε ἐν δισώμοις οἱ ἀστέρες ἔτυχον

‘And so too here did the planets happen to be in two-bodied (zodiac signs).’

— Vettius Valens 7.6.44

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 134,VI)
