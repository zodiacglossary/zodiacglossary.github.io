šur GIGIR ša₂ ULU₃
šur narkabti ša šūti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:36:49 PM
Primary meaning ζ Tauri
Literal translation the southern rein of the chariot
Variants
šur GIGIR ša₂ ULU₃ šur narkabti ša šūti šur is probably st. constr. of šur'u "rein"
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
ζ Tauri star: normal star

Jones (2004) c7. Translation rein given by CAD šur'u (also Jones 2004) but not in ADRT

(6) 12 SAG GE₆ sin (7) SIG šur GIGIR ša₂ ULU₃ 1 KUŠ₃ TUR₃ NU KAD₂

‘(Day) 12, beginning of the night, Sin was 1 cubit below the southern rein of the chariot, surrounded by a halo which was not closed.’

— BM 46229 (ADRT I No. -346) obv. 6-7

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 347 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 142-143
