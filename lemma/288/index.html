pr nṯr.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:17:56 PM

Variants
pr nṯr.t

House of the goddess astrological concept

10th astrological place; alternative name for Upper midheaven

tꜣ tny.t nṯr nꜣ ḥtr(.w) | pr nṯr.t pꜣ gnḥḏ

‘The part of god: Gemini | The domain of the goddess: Cancer’

— O.Neugeb. 3 20-21

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st century CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117
