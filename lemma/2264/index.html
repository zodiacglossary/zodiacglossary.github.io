μισσυνή
missunē

Greek noun

Last edited: 6/27/2024, 2:48:20 PM
Primary meaning sharpness
Literal translation sharpness
Variants
μισσυνή missunē rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
sharpness mathematical property

used for acute angles

μισσυνή· ἡ ὀξύτης παρὰ Χαλδαίοις

‘Missunē: sharpness according to the Chaldeans.’

— Hesychius μ 1467

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2020. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 2 (a. Epsilon – Iota, b. Kappa – Omicron), ed. I.C. Cunningham. Berlin, Boston: Walter de Gruyter. 840
