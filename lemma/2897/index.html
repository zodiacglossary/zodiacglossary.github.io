φαῦλος
phaulos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/24/2025, 6:55:26 AM
Primary meaning malefic
Literal translation bad
Variants
φαῦλος phaulos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
malefic astrological concept

The Sun, when injured by bad ones (malefics), and the Foam-born (Venus) (…) bring pain to a husband

‘The Sun, when injured by bad ones (malefics), and the Foam-born (Venus) (…) bring pain to a husband’

— Dorotheus 5.16.1–40

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
