mul.a-si-du
asīdu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/2/2024, 4:53:43 PM
Primary meaning Heel star
Literal translation Heel star
Variants
mul.a-si-du asīdu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Heel star star: ziqpu star, astronomical phenomenon

λ Andromedae. Part of the constellation "Demon with the Gaping Mouth".

[...] ⸢MA⸣.NA MIN 10 UŠ i-na KI.MIN 18 lim DANNA ina KI.MIN | TA mul.a-si-di EN 4 ša₂ mul.LU.[LIM]

‘[...] minas ditto (weight), 10 UŠ on ditto (the ground), 18 thousand bēru in ditto (heaven), | from the Heel to the 4 (stars) of the Stag.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) obv. 25'-26'

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 218
