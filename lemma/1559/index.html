facies tauri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:47:49 AM
Primary meaning the face of the Bull
Literal translation face of the bull
Variants
facies tauri
tauri facies
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the face of the Bull animal part

3°–5° Taurus

III. vero et IV. et V. in omni facie

‘the 3rd, 4th and 5th degrees are in the face [of Taurus]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
