GIR₃ ar₂ ša₂ A
šēpu arkītu ša nēši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:35:22 PM
Primary meaning β Virginis
Literal translation the rear foot of the lion
Variants
GIR₃ ar₂ ša₂ A šēpu arkītu ša nēši
GIR₃ ar₂-tu₂ ša₂ A šēpu arkītu ša nēši
GIR₃ ar₂-tu₂ ša₂ UR.A šēpu arkītu ša nēši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
β Virginis star: normal star

Jones (2004) c18

GI₆ 9 SAG GI₆ sin ina IGI GIR₃ ar₂ ša₂ A 2 KUŠ₃

‘Night of (day) 9, beginning of the night, Sin was 2 cubits in front of the rear foot of the lion.’

— BM 45614 (ADRT III No. -165A) obv. 11

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 166 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 6-7
