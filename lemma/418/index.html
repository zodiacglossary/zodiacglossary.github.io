RIN₂ ša₂ SI
zibānītu ša iltāni

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:39:39 PM
Primary meaning β Librae
Literal translation the northern (part of) the scales
Variants
RIN₂ ša₂ SI zibānītu ša iltāni
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
β Librae star: normal star

Jones (2004) c22

ina ZALAG₂ GU₄.UD SIG RIN₂ ša₂ SI 2 SI

‘Last part of the night, Mercury was 2 fingers below the northern (part of) the scales.’

— BM 34609 (ADRT III No. -144) rev. 4

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 145 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 92-93
