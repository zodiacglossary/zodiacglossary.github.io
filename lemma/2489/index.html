μονομοιρία
monomoiria

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/20/2025, 9:13:17 AM
Primary meaning monomoiria
Literal translation single-degree
Variants
μονομοιρία monomoiria
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
monomoiria astrological concept, spatial concept

Ἑρμῆς Τοξότῃ μοιρῶν ι̅γ̣̅ λεπτῶ̣ν ν̅γ̅, οἶκος Διός, ὁ̣ρ̣ίοις Ἀφ[ρο]δίτης, μον[ο]μοιρίας Ἄ̣ρεως

‘Mercury (was) in Sagittarius, at 13 degrees 53 minutes, a house of Jupiter, terms of Venus, monomoiria of (i.e., single degree assigned to) Mars’

— P.Berlin 9825 vi 1–3

Genre: horoscope
Date: 319 CE

D. Greenbaum and A. Jones, P.Berl. 9825: An elaborate horoscope for 319 CE and its significance for Greek astronomical and astrological practice (ISAW Papers 12 [2017])

See also

〈στοιχεῖον〉 stoicheion ‘monomoiria-degree’

〈μοῖρα〉 moira ‘degree’
