אֱלוּל
ʾĕlûl

Hebrew noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 4:10:48 PM
Primary meaning Elul (month VI)
Variants
אֱלוּל ʾĕlûl
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Elul (month VI) month name, time interval

וַתִּשְׁלַם֙ הַֽחוֹמָ֔ה בְּעֶשְׂרִ֥ים וַחֲמִשָּׁ֖ה לֶאֱל֑וּל לַחֲמִשִּׁ֥ים וּשְׁנַ֖יִם יֽוֹם׃ {פ}

wattišlam haḥômāh bəʿeśrîm waḥămiššāh leʾĕlûl laḥămiššîm ûšənayim yôm.

‘The wall was finished on the twenty-fifth of Elul, after fifty-two days.’

— Nehemiah 6 15

Genre: religious text
