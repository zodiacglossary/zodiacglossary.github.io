πάρυγρος
parugros

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:32:46 AM
Primary meaning damp
Literal translation somewhat wet
Variants
πάρυγρος parugros
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
damp zodiac sign quality

of Aquarius

τ[ὸ] δὲ δῦνον [ε]ὑρέθη [τὴν κατὰ μῆ]κος κείνη- σιν ποιούμενον ἐν ζῳ[δίῳ στερεῷ] ἀρρενι- κ]ῷ παρύ[γρ]ῳ Ὑδρ[οχ]όῳ

‘The setting point has been found making its longitudinal motion in the solid, masculine, damp sign Aquarius’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 i. 28-30

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 422-423
