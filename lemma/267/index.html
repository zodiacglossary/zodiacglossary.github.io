Upper midheaven astrological concept

10th astrological place

pꜣ šy p.t pꜣ-nty-ꜣtḥ pꜣ šy dwꜣ.t ḥtr.w

‘The Lake of the Sky: Sagittarius | The Lake of the netherworld: Gemini’

— O.Neugeb. 2 6-7

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent.CE
