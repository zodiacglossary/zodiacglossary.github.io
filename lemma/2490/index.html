λειτουργός
leitourgos

Greek noun

Last edited: 9/16/2024, 1:01:30 PM
Primary meaning sub-decan
Literal translation minister
Variants
λειτουργός leitourgos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
sub-decan astrological concept

λειτουρ̣[γ]ο̣ὶ̣ σ̣ε̣μ̣ν̣ο̣ὶ̣ τρεῖς π̣ε̣ρίεισιν ἕνα | ὡρονό̣[μον] κρατερόν· περὶ γὰρ τὸ ζῴδιον οὗτοι | ἐννέα λειτουργοὶ [ἀ]ν̣τολίην ἔλαχον

‘Three august ministers (i.e., subdecans) accompany one mighty hour-apportioner (i.e., decan): for throughout the zodiac sign these nine ministers have their rising’

— Anoubion F1b 10–12

P. Schubert, Anoubion. Poème astrologique. Témoinages et fragments (Paris, 2015)
