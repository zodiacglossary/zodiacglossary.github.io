LA₂
maṭû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:10:16 AM
Primary meaning to be lacking
Literal translation to be lacking
Variants
LA₂ maṭû
ma-ṭu-u₂ maṭû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to decrease mathematical expression

in mathematical astronomy, said of number sequences

(6) [ana tar-ṣi] (7) 1.58.31.6.40 LA₂-u₂ 4.46.42.⸢57⸣.46.40 GAR-an ša₂ al-la 1.58.31.⸢6⸣.[40] (8) LA₂-u₂ LA₂-u₂ EN 1.58.13.20 ma-ṭu-u₂ A.RA₂ 8.20 DU KI 4.46.42.57.4⸢6.40⸣ ( 9) TAB-ma GAR-an

‘(6) [Opposite] (7) 1,58;31,6,40, decreasing, you put 4,46;42,57,46,40. Whatever (the amount) by which it is less than 1,58;31,6, [40], (8) decreasing, until 1,58;13,20, decreasing, you multiply by 8;20, add with 4,46;42,57,46,40, and (9) put down.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. i 6-9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
