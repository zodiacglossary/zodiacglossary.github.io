sfaera barbarica

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 9:17:39 AM
Primary meaning fixed stars outside the zodiac
Literal translation barbaric sphere
Variants
sfaera barbarica
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
fixed stars outside the zodiac astrological concept

Quae omnia tunc explicabimus, cum ad interpretationem venerimus sfaerae barbaricae;

‘All this we will explain when we come to the interpretation of the sfaera barbarica;’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.17.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.238

See also

〈παρανατέλλω〉 paranatellō ‘to rise besides’
