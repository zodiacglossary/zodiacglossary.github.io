pars novissima

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/24/2024, 8:00:08 AM
Primary meaning the latest degree
Literal translation the newest part
Variants
pars novissima
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the latest degree astrological concept

of a sign

Illud etiam sciendum est, quod in omnibus signis, quotiens Luna novissimas possederit partes, ad nullum feratur;

‘It is necessary to know that in all signs in which he possesses the last degrees, he moves towards nothing [i. e. has no effect]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.25.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.277
