URU₃
naṣāru

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 5/19/2025, 9:33:57 AM
Primary meaning to watch
Literal translation to watch
Variants
URU₃ naṣāru
PAP naṣāru In astronomical diaries and horoscopes.
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Marvin Schreiber
to watch verb of visual perception

28 AN.MI šamaš₂ ki PAP NU IGI

‘(Day) 28, eclipse of Šamaš, when I watched I did not see it.’

— BM 34609 (ADRT III No. -144) obv. 10'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 145 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.

28 AN.GE6 20 ina ABSIN | ki PAP NU IGI

‘On the 28th (of month V), an eclipse of the sun in Virgo; | when watched for (it) was not observed.’

— BM 33018 (BH 21) rev. 4-5

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 2nd century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: S.E. 187 VI.22 = -124 Oct. 1)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 117
