ὁ ἐν τῷ ζʹ σπονδύλῳ τῷ παρὰ τὸ κέντρον
ho en tōi 6 spondulōi tōi para to kentron

Greek noun

Last edited: 6/2/2025, 7:40:37 AM
Primary meaning κ Scorpionis
Literal translation The star in the 7th joint, the joint next to the sting
Variants

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
