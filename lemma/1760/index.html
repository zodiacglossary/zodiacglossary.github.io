ina KUR ŠU₂
ina ... rabû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:24:40 AM
Primary meaning to set at the morning last
Literal translation to set in the east
Variants
ina KUR ŠU₂ ina ... rabû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to set at the morning last synodic phenomenon

Said of Mercury and Venus (inner planets)

⸢16 ŠU⸣ 12 ina KUR 19.26.40 ALLA ŠU₂

‘(Year) ⸢16 , (month) IV⸣, (day) 12, in the east, 19;26,40 Cancer, it sets.’

— BM 36723 (ACT No. 300a) rev. 9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: synodic table
Provenance: Babylon
Date: Ca. 320 BCE

Neugebauer, Otto, 1955, Astronomical Cuneiform Texts
