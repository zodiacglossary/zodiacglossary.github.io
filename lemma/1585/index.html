venter gemini

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:47:41 PM
Primary meaning the belly of the Twin
Literal translation belly of the twin
Variants
venter gemini
gemini venter
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the belly of the southern Twin animal part

24°–26° Gemini

sub ventre vero XXIV. XXV. et XXVI.

‘under the belly [of the southern twin are counted the degrees] 24 and 25 and 26’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
