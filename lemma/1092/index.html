ME ana ŠU2 šamaš2
ūmu ana rabî Šamaš

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/9/2023, 7:18:59 PM
Primary meaning time until sunset
Literal translation day until sunset
Variants
ME ana ŠU2 šamaš2 ūmu ana rabî Šamaš
ŠU2 šamaš2 ?
ŠU2 ?
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Marvin Schreiber
time until sunset time interval

interval from a moment after noon and until sunset

U4 8 ša2 30 DANNA ME ana ŠU2 šamaš2 a-lid [...]

‘The 8th day, at one-half bēru before sunset, he was born [...].’

— BM 47721 (BH 6) rev. 3

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 3rd century BCE

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88, 1–164. 69

28 19 ŠU2 šamaš2

‘(day) 28, 19 (UŠ before) sunset’

— BM 45849+ (ACT No. 123a) obv. 15 col. M (= time of New Moon)

Genre: mathematical astronomy: synodic table
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 2nd century BCE

Neugebauer ACT

28 1.4 ŠU2

‘(day) 28, 1,4 (UŠ before) the setting (of the sun)’

— BM 34066+ (ACT No. 120) obv. 4 col. M (= time of New Moon)

Genre: mathematical astronomy: synodic table
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 2nd century BCE

Neugebauer, Otto, 1955, Astronomical Cuneiform Texts
