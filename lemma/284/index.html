tny.t šry

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/14/2023, 3:09:27 PM
Primary meaning part of the son
Literal translation part of the son
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
part of the son astrological concept

5th astrological place; alt. name for "House of fortune"

pꜣ tny.t ỉt pꜣ-ḥr-ꜥnḫ| pꜣ tny.t šry pꜣ mw sbkꜣ

‘The part of the father: Capricorn | The part of the son: Aquarius and Mercury’

— O.Neugeb. 3 15-16

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117

See also
