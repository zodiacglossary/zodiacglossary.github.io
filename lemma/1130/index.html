TIL-tu4
gamartu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/24/2024, 7:51:48 AM
Primary meaning totality (of an eclipse)
Literal translation totality
Variants
TIL-tu4 gamartu
TIL-ti3 gamartu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
totality (of an eclipse) astronomical event

iti.APIN GE6 13 AN.GE6 30 | TIL-ti3 ina MUL2.MUL2 GAR-an

‘Month VIII, night of the 13th, a lunar eclipse. | Totality occurred in Taurus’

— BM 33382 (BH 4) rev. 3-4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 3rd century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: S.E. 24 V.19 = -287 Sept. 1)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 61
