sw wnwn

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:44:35 PM
Primary meaning decan
Literal translation running star
Variants
sw wnwn
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
decan star, decan

epithet of the decans

nꜣ sḫny.w n pꜣ rꜥ ỉw=f ꜥḥꜥ ỉrm wꜥ ẖn pꜣ 36 sw wnwn

‘The influences of the sun when it stands together with one of the 36 running stars’

— PSI inv. D 39

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

A. Winkler, 2016, Some Astrologers and Their Handbooks in Demotic Egyptian, in J.M. Steele (ed.), The Circulation of Astronomical Knowledge in the Ancient World (Leiden: Brill), 245-286 250 n. 27
