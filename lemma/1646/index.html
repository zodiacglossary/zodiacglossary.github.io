manus sinistra sagittarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:53:31 AM
Primary meaning the left hand of the Archer
Literal translation left hand of the archer
Variants
manus sinistra sagittarii
sinistra manus sagittarii
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the left hand of the Archer animal part

11°–12° Sagittarius

duae vero sequentes in sinistra manu

‘the two following [degrees] however are located in the left hand [of the Archer]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
