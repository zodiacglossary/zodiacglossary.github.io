שֶׁמֶשׁ
šemeš

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 9/24/2024, 8:03:21 AM
Primary meaning Sun
Literal translation Sun
Variants
שֶׁמֶשׁ šemeš
שָׁמֶשׁ šāmeš in pausa
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Sun luminary, planet

וְזָרַ֥ח הַשֶּׁ֖מֶשׁ וּבָ֣א הַשָּׁ֑מֶשׁ וְאֶ֨ל־מְקוֹמ֔וֹ שׁוֹאֵ֛ף זוֹרֵ֥חַֽ ה֖וּא שָֽׁם׃

Wəzāraḥ haššemeš ûb̲āʾ haššāmeš wəʾel-məqômô šôʾēp̲ zôrēaḥ hûʾ šām.

‘Also, the Sun hath risen, and the Sun hath gone in, and unto its place panting it is rising there.’

— Ecclesiastes 1,5

Genre: religious text
