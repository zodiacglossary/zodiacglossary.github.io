DIRI
atāru

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 3/22/2024, 3:40:43 PM
Primary meaning to exceed
Literal translation to exceed
Variants
DIRI atāru
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to exceed mathematical expression, comparative operation, mathematical operation

(23) mim-ma ša₂ al-la 1.59.12.35.33.20 (24) TAB-u₂ DIRI EN 1.59.30.22.13.20 TAB-u₂ A.RA₂ 2 DU TA 4.56.35.33.⸢20⸣ (25) E₁₁ GAR

‘(23) Whatever (the amount) by which it exceeds 1,59;12,35,33,20, (24), increasing, until 1,59;30,22,13,20, increasing, you multiply by 2, deduct from 4,56;35,33,20, (25) put down.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. i 23-25

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
