ἀμετάβολος
ametabolos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:02:29 AM
Primary meaning invariable
Literal translation invariable
Variants
ἀμετάβολος ametabolos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
invariable zodiac sign quality

ἔστι δὲ τὸ ζῴδιον (…) ἀμετάβολον

‘The zodiac sign (Taurus) is (…) invariable’

— Vettius Valens `.2.14

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
