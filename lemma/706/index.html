ἀφίστημι
aphistēmi

Greek verb

Last edited: 7/9/2023, 5:35:40 PM
Primary meaning to be distant from
Literal translation to stand apart from
Variants
ἀφίστημι aphistēmi
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to be distant from astral concept

κεκλήρωται ἡ Σελήνη τὴν δυάδα ἐπεὶ ἀφέστηκε Κρόνου βʹ

‘The Moon is allotted the second (interval) since it was 2 (zodiac signs) distant from Saturn.’

— Vettius Valens 5.7.21

Genre: horoscope
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 37)
