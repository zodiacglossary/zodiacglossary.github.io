KUR
kašādu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 10/19/2023, 8:59:30 AM
Primary meaning to reach
Literal translation to reach
Variants
KUR kašādu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Marvin Schreiber
to reach verb of motion

GU4 ina 27(?) MAŠ.MAŠ KUR-ad2

‘Mercury reached 27° Gemini.’

— W 20030/10 (BH 16) rev. 6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Uruk
Date: 2nd century BCE

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88, 1–164. 100

meš-ḫi ša₂ KUR-ad₂.MEŠ ša₂ ᵈUDU.IDIM.MEŠ ša₂ MU.1-me.29 m.Se-lu-ku LUGAL

‘Predictions of the reachings of the planets of year 1 hundred 29, king Seleucus.’

— BM 33873 (ADRT VII No. 158) rev. 7'

Genre: astronomical diaries and related texts: normal star almanac
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 183 BCE

Hunger, H. 2014. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. VII: Almanacs and Normal Star Almanacs. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.

to conquer verb of motion
