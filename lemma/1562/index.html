humeri tauri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:52:02 AM
Primary meaning the shoulders of the Bull
Literal translation shoulders of the bull
Variants
humeri tauri
tauri humeri
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the shoulders of the Bull animal part

13°–15° Taurus

XIII. vero et XIV. et XV. in humeris

‘the 13th, 14th and 15th degree however is in the shoulders [of Taurus]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
