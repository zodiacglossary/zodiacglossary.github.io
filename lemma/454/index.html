zi-im ša₂ ŠA₃ ALLA
zīm ša libbi allutti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/5/2025, 2:06:50 PM
Primary meaning Praesepe (Beehive Cluster)
Literal translation the glow inside the crab
Variants
zi-im ša₂ ŠA₃ ALLA zīm ša libbi allutti
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Praesepe (Beehive Cluster) star: normal star

Jones (2004) a4. Rarely used

USAN GENNA ana ⸢ŠU₂⸣ ina LA₂-šu₂ SIG zi-im ša₂ ŠA₃ ALLA 2 SI LAL₂

‘(Night of day 25 ...) first part of the night, Saturn, while moving back to the west, was balanced 2 fingers below the Glow inside the Crab.’

— Rm 792 + (ADRT III No. -322D) rev. 11

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: -323 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 214-215
