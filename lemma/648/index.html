συνάπτω
sunaptō

Greek verb

Last edited: 7/12/2023, 9:30:29 AM
Primary meaning to make contact
Literal translation to join
Variants
συνάπτω sunaptō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to make contact astrological concept

τὸ δὲ τὴν Σελήνην ἀπερρευκέναι τῆς Ἀφροδίτης καὶ τῷ Διὶ συνάπτειν τὰς τύχας συνῆπτε τοῦ τε ἀποστείλαντος καὶ τοῦ ὑποδεξαμένου

‘The fact that the Moon had left contact with Venus and was making contact with Jupiter placed the fortunes of the person who sent and the person who received (a gift, the occasion of this catarchic horoscope)’

— CCAG VI p. 66

Genre: horoscope

(GH L 486)
