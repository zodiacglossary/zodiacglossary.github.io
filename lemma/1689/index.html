renes leonis

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:57:42 AM
Primary meaning the kidneys of the Lion
Literal translation kidneys of the lion
Variants
renes leonis
leonis renes
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the kidneys of the Lion animal part

29° Leo

XXIX. in renibus

‘[the degree] 29 is in the kidneys [of Leo]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.290
