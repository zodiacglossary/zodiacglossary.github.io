ἀφαιρέω
aphaireō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/3/2024, 12:14:39 PM
Primary meaning to diminish
Literal translation to take away
Variants
ἀφαιρέω aphaireō
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to diminish (?) arithmetical operation

[...] ἀφαιροῦσα τῷ φ[ ]αθουσα τοῖς ἀριθ[μοῖς

‘[...] diminishing the ... and ...ing the numbers’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4245

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 382

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society.

to subtract subtractive operation, arithmetical operation

ἀφελ(ὼν) [ἀ(πὸ) τ(ῶν) ὁ]μαλ(ῶν) τὰ διαφορὰ (ἑξηκοστὰ) τὰ π(αρα)κείμενα [τῇ ἡλιακῇ] μο(ίρᾳ) ἐν τῷ ἐπ' ὀρθῆς τῆς σφαί- [ρας κα]νονίῳ

‘subtracting [from the] non-uniform ones the sixtieths corresponding [to the solar] degree in the table of sphaera recta, ...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 Fr. 3, col. i, l. 10-13

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 32-35
