ἀρρενικός
arrenikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 2:54:21 PM
Primary meaning masculine
Literal translation masculine
Variants
ἀρρενικός arrenikos
ἀρσενικός arsenikos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
masculine astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

ζῳδίῳ Ἄ̣ρεως ὁρίοις Ἑρμ̣οῦ στοιχείῳ Διὸς ἀρρενικῷ καὶ π̣ρ̣ὸς βορρῶι ζῳδίῳ

‘(In Aries,) the zodiac sign of Mars, terms of Mercury, “element” of Jupiter, a masculine and northerly zodiac sign’

— P.Lond. I 130 59–63

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)

ἐν Ζυγῷ ζῳδίῳ ἀρ̣σενικῷ

‘in Libra, a masculine zodiac sign’

— P.Oxy. 235 8

Genre: horoscope

(GH 15/22)
