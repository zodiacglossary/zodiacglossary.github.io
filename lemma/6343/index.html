कर्किन्
karkin

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 9/30/2025, 2:13:19 PM
Primary meaning Cancer
Literal translation crab
Variants
कर्कि karki
कर्क karka
कर्कट karkaṭa
Edited by
Cancer zodiac sign

कर्की कुलीराकृतिरम्बसंस्थो वक्षःप्रदेशो विहितश् चतुर्थः।

karkī kulīrākṛtir ambasaṃstho vakṣaḥpradeśo vihitaś caturthaḥ|

‘The fourth [sign] (Cancer), whose form is that of a crab staying in water, is designated as the chest-area.’

— Vṛddhayavanajātaka 1.7ab

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: ca. 300–325 CE

Mak, Bill M. 2018. “The First Two Chapters of Mīnarāja’s Vṛddhayavanajātaka.” Zinbun 48: 1–31. 15
