IGI ša₂ ŠU₂
nanmurtu ša rabê

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 8/30/2023, 9:20:40 AM
Primary meaning Evening First
Literal translation western appearance
Variants
IGI ša₂ ŠU₂ nanmurtu ša rabê
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Evening First synodic phenomenon
western appearance synodic phenomenon

ša₂ GU₄.UD IGI ša₂ ŠU₂ ana IGI ša₂ ŠU₂ TA 6 ALLA EN 26 RIN₂ 2.40 TAB

‘For Mercury: western appearance to western appearance from 6 Cancer until 26 Libra you add 2,40.’

— AO 6477 (BMAPT No. 42) obv. 11

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer
