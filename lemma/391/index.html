ỉr ḏḏy

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 10/31/2023, 10:35:04 PM
Primary meaning to be hostile
Literal translation to be hostile
Variants
ḏḏy
ϫⲁϫⲉ Old Coptic
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
to be hostile (towards another planet) astrological concept

A planet can in certain configurations become at enmity towards another planet irrespective of quality as either beneficent or maleficent

[ⲁϥⳓⲱ ⲉⲟ]ⲩⲛⲧϥ ⲡⲣⲉ ⲻⲥⲟⲩϧⲁⲛ ⲥⲟⲩⲕⲏ ⲻⲧⲟⲩⲁⲣⳗⲁⳗⲉ ⲻⲡⲓⲥ[ⲟⲩ ...]

‘[if] he [had] the sun as a friendly star and Mercury, they were hostile to this st[ar ...’

— P.Lond. 98 118

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95

J. Černý, P.E. Kahle, and R.A. Parker, 1957, The Old Coptic Horoscope, Journal of Egyptian Archaeology 43, 86-100. 87; 97
