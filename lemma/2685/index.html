ἀνωφερής
anōpherēs

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/28/2024, 1:45:59 PM
Primary meaning inclined upward
Literal translation inclined upward
Variants
ἀνωφερής anōpherēs
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
inclined upward zodiac sign quality

Κριός ἐστιν (…) ζῴδιον (…) ἀνωφερές

‘Aries is (…) an upward-inclined zodiac sign’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.1

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
