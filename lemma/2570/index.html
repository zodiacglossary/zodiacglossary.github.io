צָהֳרַיִם
ṣohŏrayim

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:43:02 AM
Primary meaning noon
Literal translation two noons
Variants
צָהֳרַיִם ṣohŏrayim
צָהֳרָ֑יִם ṣohŏrāyim in pausa
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
noon point in time

וְהָיָ֣ה ׀ בַּיּ֣וֹם הַה֗וּא נְאֻם֙ אֲדֹנָ֣י יְהוִ֔ה וְהֵבֵאתִ֥י הַשֶּׁ֖מֶשׁ בַּֽצָּהֳרָ֑יִם וְהַחֲשַׁכְתִּ֥י לָאָ֖רֶץ בְּי֥וֹם אֽוֹר׃

wəhāyāh bayyôm hahûʾ nəʾum ʾăd̲ōnāi YHWH wəhēb̲ēʾt̲î haššemeš baṣṣohŏrāyim wəhaḥăšak̲tî lāʾāreṣ bəyôm ʾôr.

‘And in that day —declares the Sovereign GOD— I will make the sun set at noon, I will darken the earth on a sunny day.’

— Amos 8 9

Genre: religious text
