ḥtp ỉꜣbṱ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/25/2025, 7:54:16 AM
Primary meaning morning last
Literal translation eastern setting
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
morning last synodic phenomenon

pꜣy=f ḥtp ỉꜣbṱ 1223 tꜣy=f rnp.t ꜥꜣ.t 388

‘Its eastern setting 1223; its great year 388’

— ANAshm.D.O. 525+ 9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Egypt

Ossendrijver, M. and A. Winkler, 2018. Chaldeans on the Nile: Two Egyptian Procedure Texts with Babylonian System A1 and A2 for Mercury, in Scaffolding of Our Thoughts: Essays on Assyriology and the History of Science in Honor of Francesca Rochberg, eds. J. Crisostomo et al. (Leiden: Brill, 2018), 282-418 388
