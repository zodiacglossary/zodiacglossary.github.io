ⲉⲣ ⲕⲁⲕⲉ
er kake

Egyptian verb

Last edited: 6/24/2024, 7:36:20 AM
Primary meaning to be eclipsed
Literal translation to become dark
Variants
ⲣ̅ ⲕⲁⲕⲉ
ⲉⲣ ⲕⲁⲕⲉ
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
to be eclipsed astronomical event

ϩⲛ̅ ⲥⲟⲩ ⲙⲛ̅ⲧⲁϥⲧⲉ ⲙ | ⲫⲁⲙⲉⲛⲱⲑ ⲧⲏⲥ | ⲧⲉⲧⲣⲁⲧⲏⲥ ⲓⲛⲇⲓⲕ/ | ⲁⲡⲣⲉⲣ̅ⲕⲁⲕⲉ ⲛ̅ϫ̅ⲡ | ϥⲧⲟ ⲙ̅ⲡ̅ϩⲟⲟⲩ

hn sou mntafte m | phamenôtʰ tês | tetratês indik/ | aperkake nčp | fto mphoou

‘On the fourteenth day | of Phamenoth on the fourth (year of the) indiction, the sun was eclipsed during the | fourth hour of the day’

— O.Tur.Copt. 38 1-5

Genre: letter
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 7th cent. CE

J. Ray and G. Gilmore, 2006, A Fixed Point in Coptic Chronology: The Solar Eclipse of 10 March, 601, Zeitschrift für Papyrologie und Epigraphik 158, 190-192. 190
