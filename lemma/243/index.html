4.AM₃ IGI.ME ša₂ PA ša₂ ana ZA maš-lu
4 maḫrûtu ša Pabilsag ša ana ZA mašlū

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:05:18 PM
Primary meaning 4 stars in constellation Pabilsag
Literal translation the 4 front (ones) of Pabilsag which are similar to the (sign) ZA
Variants
4.AM₃ IGI.ME ša₂ PA ša₂ ana ZA maš-lu 4 maḫrûtu ša Pabilsag ša ana ZA mašlū
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
4 stars in constellation Pabilsag (Sagittarius) star: normal star

Jones (2004): a8. 4 unidentified stars in Sagittarius; in astronomical diaries and related texts

(18') MU.1-me-1-šu-6.KAM m.A-lek-sa-an-dar LUGAL BAR in EN 10 AN ana NIM ki UŠ-u₂ 1 KUŠ₃ ar₂ 4.AM₃ IGI.ME (19') ša₂ PA ša₂ ana ZA maš-lu UŠ

‘(18') Year 1 hundred 66, Alexander was king. Month I, around until (day) 10, when Mars became stationary to the East, it became stationary 1 cubit behind the four front (ones) of Pabilsag which are similar to the (sign) ZA.’

— BM 35317 (ADRT VI No. 88) obv. 18'-19'

Genre: goal year text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 67 BCE

Hunger, H. 2006. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. VI: Goal-Year Texts. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 346-347
