conversio

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:37:25 AM
Primary meaning revolution
Literal translation revolution
Variants
conversio
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
revolution astronomical term

quantis etiam conversionibus maior [...] perficeretur annus

‘which number of revolutions it takes to make the greater year’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.0.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.2
