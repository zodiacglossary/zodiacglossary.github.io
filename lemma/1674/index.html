aqua aquarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:46:13 AM
Primary meaning the water of the Waterbearer
Literal translation water of the waterbearer
Variants
aqua aquarii
aquarii aqua
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the water of the Waterbearer animal part

13°–15° Aquarius

Tres quae post has sunt Aquarii in aqua sunt collocatae

‘The three [degrees] which are after these are located in the water of Aquarius’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.11

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
