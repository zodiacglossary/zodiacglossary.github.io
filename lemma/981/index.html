locus valitudinis

Latin noun

Last edited: 9/19/2023, 2:10:52 PM
Primary meaning place of health
Literal translation place of health
Variants
locus valitudinis
valitudinis locus
locus valetudinis
valetudinis locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
place of health astrological place

6th astrological place

valitudinis in sexto

‘the [place] of health [is] in the sixth [sign]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.14.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.58
