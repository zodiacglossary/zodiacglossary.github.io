πανσέληνος
panselēnos

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/29/2025, 3:23:30 PM
Primary meaning full moon
Literal translation full moon
Variants
πανσέληνος panselēnos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
full moon lunar phase

ἡ προγενομένη πανσέληνος ἐγένετο τῷ αὐτῷ μηνὶ Παῦνι κεʹ εἰς κϛʹ ὥρ(ᾳ) βʹ νυκ(τός), (...)

‘The preceding full moon took place in the same month, Pauni 25 till 26, hour 2 of night, (...)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4282 1-6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 3rd-4th century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 288 + II: 430-431
