Ἀφόσο
Aphoso

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/5/2025, 2:28:00 AM
Primary meaning Decan 3 of Virgo
Variants
Ἀφόσο Aphoso
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 3 of Virgo decan

οἱ δὲ τρεῖς αὐτοῦ δεκανοί· Τὼμ ὁ πρῶτος, Οὐεστεβκὼτ ὁ δεύτερος, Ἀφόσο ὁ τρίτος

‘The three decans of (Virgo) are: Tōm, the first; Ouestebkōt, the second; Aphoso, the third’

— Hephaestion 1.1.108

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
