humilior

Latin verb

Last edited: 1/12/2024, 11:30:31 AM
Primary meaning to be in a falling place
Literal translation to be humbled
Variants
humilior
humilitor Firmicus Maternus 4.19.12
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to be in a falling place astrological concept

in hoc signo, in quo humiliatur atque deicitur

‘in this sign, in which it is in fall’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.29.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.82
