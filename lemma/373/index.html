mul.UR.GI7
kalbu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:09:16 AM
Primary meaning Dog-star
Literal translation Dog-star
Variants
mul.UR.GI7 kalbu
mul2.UR.GI7 kalbu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Dog-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to the southern part of Hercules.

DIŠ MUL ša2 ina IGI mul.UZ3 GUB-zu mul.UR.GI7

‘¶ The star which stands in front of the Goat: the Dog.’

— BM 86378 i 25

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 35
