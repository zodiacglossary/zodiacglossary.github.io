GUB
izuzzu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 8/20/2023, 3:10:37 PM
Primary meaning to be at the solstice
Literal translation to stand
Variants
GUB izziz
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to be at the solstice astronomical event

with Sun as subject

25 šamaš₂ GUB NU PAP

‘(Day) 25, Šamaš was at the solstice; I did not watch.’

— BM 45763 (ADRT II -256) obv. 17'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 257 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 12-13

to be present in the sky astronomical event

GE₆ 17 DIR AN ZA ina ZALAG₂ sin ar₂ GENNA 5/6 KUŠ₃ ana ⸢NIM⸣ GUB ar₂ MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ SUḪUR.MAŠ₂ 1 KUŠ₃ 8 u

‘Night of (day) 17, clouds were in the sky; last part of the night, Sin stood 5/6 cubit behind Kayyamanu to the east, 1 cubit 8 fingers behind the Rear star of the Goat-Fish.’

— BM 32889+ (ADRT II No. -246) obv. 16

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 247 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.

to stand
