Βελέβατος· ὁ τοῦ πῦρος ἀστήρ. Βαβυλώνιοι

‘Belebatos: the star of fire. Babylonians.’

— Hesychius β 479

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE
