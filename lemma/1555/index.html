pedes arietis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:27:47 PM
Primary meaning the feet of the Ram
Literal translation feet of the ram
Variants
pedes arietis
arietis pedes
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the feet of the Ram animal part

26°–27° Aries

novissimi autem pedes XXVI. et XXVII. habent partem,

‘the newest feet [of the Ram] have the 26th and 27th degree’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.287
