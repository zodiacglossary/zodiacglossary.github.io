genitura diurna

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:50:55 AM
Primary meaning diurnal nativity
Literal translation daily birth
Variants
genitura diurna
diurna genitura
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
diurnal nativity astrological concept

non ideo in diurnis genituris in oportunis locis positi [...]

‘if placed in a diurnal nativity in no good places [...]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.7.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.49
