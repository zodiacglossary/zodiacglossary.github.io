οἰκητήριον
oikētērion

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/19/2025, 7:23:07 AM
Primary meaning house
Literal translation dwelling
Variants
οἰκητήριον oikētērion
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
house astrological concept

τῶν] οἰκητ̣[ηρίων κ]αὶ ὑψωμάτων τάξις ἐστὶ[ν τοι]αύτη

‘of the houses and exaltations (of the planets) the arrangement is such (as described above)’

— P.Mich. III 149 xvii 11–13

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st-2nd century CE
