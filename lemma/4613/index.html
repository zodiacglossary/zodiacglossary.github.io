ἀγαθοποιός
agathopoios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 3/10/2025, 1:45:06 AM
Primary meaning benefic
Literal translation doing good
Variants
ἀγαθοποιός agathopoios
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
benefic astrological concept

τῶν ἀγαθοποιῶν ἀποστρόφων ὄντων

‘if the benefics are turned away (not in aspect)’

— Anon., On Cardinal Points §83

Genre: astrological treatise

Pingree, D. (1976) Dorothei Sidonii Carmen Astrologicum 367
