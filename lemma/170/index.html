ḫꜥ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 7/21/2025, 7:38:28 AM
Primary meaning appearance
Literal translation appearance
Variants
ḫꜥ
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
appearance astronomical event

nth rḫ n šꜥ.w n pꜣ 5 sw ꜥnḫ.w ḥnꜥ gm pꜣ wš n ỉr ꜥb.t n p(ꜣ) rꜥ ỉꜥḥ

‘... who knows the appearance of the 5 living stars and find the time of obscuration of the sun and the moon’

— Graf.Phil. 410 7-8

Genre: inscription
Provenance: Philae
Date: 3rd cent. CE

Griffith, F.Ll., Catalogue of the Demotic Graffiti of the Dodecaschoenus (Oxford, 1935 - 1937) 26-31

to appear verb
