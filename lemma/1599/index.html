cauda cancri

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:58:02 AM
Primary meaning the tail of the Crab
Literal translation tail of the crab
Variants
cauda cancri
cancri cauda
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the tail of the Crab animal part

30° Cancer

in cauda sola XXX.

‘in the tail [of the Crab] only the 30th [degree is located]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
