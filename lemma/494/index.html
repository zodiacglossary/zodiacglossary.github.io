mul.Ḫe2-gal2-a-a-u
Ḫegalāju

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/2/2024, 4:40:20 PM
Primary meaning Abundant-One-star
Literal translation Abundant-One-star
Variants
mul.Ḫe2-gal2-a-a-u2 Ḫegalāju
mul.Ḫe2-gal2-a-a Ḫegalāju
mul2.Ḫe2-gal2-la-a-a Ḫegalāju
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Abundant-One-star star

Approx. corresponding to β Comae Berenices (?).

DIŠ MUL ša2 ina IGI-šu2 GUB-zu mul.Ḫe2-gal2-a-a SUKAL d.Nin-lil2

‘¶ The star which stands in front of it: the Abundant One, the minister of Ninlil.’

— BM 86378 i 13

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 32
