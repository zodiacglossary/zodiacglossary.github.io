cauda sagittarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:32:30 AM
Primary meaning the tail of the Archer
Literal translation tail of the archer
Variants
cauda sagittarii
sagittarii cauda
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the tail of the Archer animal part

30° Sagittarius

XXX. sola pars collocatur in cauda.

‘only the 30th degree is located in the tail [of the Archer]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
