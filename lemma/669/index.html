στοιχεῖον
stoicheion

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/12/2023, 9:31:15 AM
Primary meaning monomoiria-degree
Literal translation element
Variants
στοιχεῖον stoicheion
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
monomoiria-degree astrological concept

uncertain

ζῳδίῳ Ἄ̣ρεως ὁρίοις Ἑρμ̣οῦ στοιχείῳ Διὸς ἀρρενικῷ καὶ π̣ρ̣ὸς βορρῶι ζῳδίῳ

‘(In Aries,) the zodiac sign of Mars, terms of Mercury, “element” of Jupiter, a masculine and northerly zodiac sign’

— P.Lond. I 130 59–63

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
