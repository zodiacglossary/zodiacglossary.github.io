מִזְרָח
mizrāḥ

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:40:22 AM
Primary meaning east
Literal translation rising-place
Variants
מִזְרָח mizrāḥ
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
east cardinal direction

אַל־תִּירָ֖א כִּ֣י אִתְּךָ־אָ֑נִי מִמִּזְרָח֙ אָבִ֣יא זַרְעֶ֔ךָ וּמִֽמַּעֲרָ֖ב אֲקַבְּצֶֽךָּ׃

ʾal-tîrāʾ kî ʾittək̲ā-ʾānî mimmizrāḥ ʾāb̲îʾ zarʿek̲ā ûmimmaʿărāb̲ ʾăqabbəṣekkā.

‘Fear not, for I am with you: I will bring your folk from the East, Will gather you out of the West;’

— Isaiah 43 5

Genre: religious text
