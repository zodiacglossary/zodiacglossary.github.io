Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:20:19 AM
Primary meaning NGC 2632 M44
Literal translation The middle of the nebulous mass in the chest, called Praesepe
Variants

‘The middle of the nebulous mass in the chest, called Praesepe [NGC 2632 M44]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 7

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

Ptolemy. 1903. Claudii Ptolemaei opera quae exstant omnia volumen I, Syntaxis Mathematica Pars II, Libros VII-XIII continens, ed. J.L. Heiberg. Leipzig: B.G. Teubner. 84–129
