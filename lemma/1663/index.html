venter capricorni

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:47:34 PM
Primary meaning the belly of Capricorn
Literal translation belly of the goat-horned
Variants
venter capricorni
capricorni venter
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the belly of Capricorn animal part

21°–23° Capricorn

tres sequentes in ventre,

‘the three following [degrees] are in the belly [of Capricorn]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
