Ἀγαθὸς Δαίμων
Agathos Daimōn

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/22/2025, 6:15:18 AM
Primary meaning Lot (or Place) of Agathos Daimōn
Literal translation Good Deity
Variants
Ἀγαθὸς Δαίμων Agathos Daimōn
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Lot of Agathos Daimōn astrological lot

ὁ π̣ρ̣[ῶτος κλῆρος τῆς γ]ε̣νέσεως ὅ ἐστιν Ἀγαθὸς Δαίμω̣ν̣ [ἐν Ταύρωι μοιρῶν . . λε]π̣τῶν λδʹ οἴκωι Ἀφροδείτης ὑψώ[ματι Σελήνης ὁρίοις] Ἑρμοῦ καὶ Ἄρεως.

‘The first lot of the nativity, that is, Agathos Daimōn, is in Taurus, [..] degrees, 34 minutes, house of Venus, exaltation of the Moon, terms of Mercury and Mars.’

— P.Lond. 98 72–74

Genre: horoscope
Date: 95 CE

GH 95

Place of Agathos Daimon astrological place

Ἀγαθοῦ δαίμονος τόπος· πολλὰ σχήματα.

‘The place of Agathos Daimōn: many configurations.’

— Vettius Valens 2.6

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 134,VI)
