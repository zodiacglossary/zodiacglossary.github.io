MUL₂ IGI ša₂ še-pit MAŠ.MAŠ
kakkabu maḫrû ša šepīt māši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/25/2025, 2:26:37 PM
Primary meaning Propus (η Geminorum)
Literal translation the front star of the twins' feet
Variants
MUL₂ IGI ša₂ še-pit MAŠ.MAŠ kakkabu maḫrû ša šepīt māši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Propus (η Geminorum) star: normal star

Jones (2004) c8

GE₆ 8 SAG GE₆ sin ina IGI MUL₂ IGI ša₂ še-pit MAŠ.MAŠ 1 1/2 KUŠ₃

‘Night of the 8th, beginning of the night, Sin was 1 1/2 cubits in front of the front star of the twins' feet.’

— BM 46229 (ADRT I No. -346) rev. 2

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 347 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.
