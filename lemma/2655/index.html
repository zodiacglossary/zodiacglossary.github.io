τρίτος κλῆρος
tritos klēros

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/18/2024, 9:17:18 AM
Primary meaning third lot
Literal translation third lot
Variants
τρίτος κλῆρος tritos klēros
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
third lot astrological lot

= lot of eros?

τρί[τος κλῆρος ὁ ἔρωτος εὑρέθη τὴν [κατὰ μῆκος κείνησιν ποιούμενος ἐν ζῳ[δί]ῳ θηλυ[κῷ ἀνθρςποειδεῖ Παρθένῳ [...]

‘The third [lot, of eros(?), has been found] [making] its [longitudinal motion] in the feminine, [anthropomorphic] sign Virgo [...]’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 ii. 34 - iii.2

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 424-427
