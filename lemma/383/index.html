MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ še-pit MAŠ.MAŠ
kakkabu arkû ša šepīt māši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:10:38 PM
Primary meaning μ Geminorum
Literal translation the rear star of the twins' feet
Variants
MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ še-pit MAŠ.MAŠ kakkabu arkû ša šepīt māši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
μ Geminorum star: normal star

Jones (2004) c9

(28) GE₆ 23 USAN AN e MUL₂ ar₂ (29) [ša₂ še]-⸢pit⸣ MAŠ.MAŠ 20 SI

‘Night of the 23rd, first part of the night, Mars was 20 fingers above the rear star of the twins' feet.’

— BM 46229 (ADRT I No. -346) rev. 28-29

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 347 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 150-151
