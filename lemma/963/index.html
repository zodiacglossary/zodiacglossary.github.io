mobilis

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:21:24 PM
Primary meaning mutable
Literal translation mobile
Variants
mobilis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
mutable astrological concept

Pisces sunt signum femininum, duplex, humidum, aquosum, biforme, fecundum, squamosum, maculosum, incuruum, mutum, mobile

‘Pisces are a feminine sign, dual, damp, watery, double-bodied, fertile, scaly, spotted, curved, silent, mutable’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.10.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.53
