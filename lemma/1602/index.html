facies et nares leonis

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:49:09 AM
Primary meaning the face and nose of the Lion
Literal translation face and nose of the lion
Variants
facies et nares leonis
leonis facies et nares
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the face and nose of the Lion animal part

3°–5° Leo

tres sequentes in omni facie pariter et in naribus

‘the three following [degrees] are equally in the whole face and the nose [of the Lion]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
