ŠID
minûtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:25:10 AM
Primary meaning number
Literal translation number
Variants
ŠID minûtu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
number mathematical quantity

29.31.50.19.11.4.56 ŠID AB₂ KUR ina ZI MURUB₄ AB₂ a-na AB₂

‘29;31,50,19,11,4,56, the number (of days) of the month for (the Moon) reaching (the Sun) with the mean displacement, month by month.’

— BM 36705 (BMAPT No. 82) obv. 15'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer 470-471
