ταπείνωμα
tapeinōma

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/7/2025, 11:49:30 AM
Primary meaning depression
Literal translation lowliness
Variants
ταπείνωμα tapeinōma
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
depression astrological concept

ἔστι δὲ ἡ ἀρχὴ τοῦ ζῳδιακοῦ κύκλου ὁ Κριός, ἀρσενικόν, ἰσημερινόν, τροπικόν, ἐαρινόν, οἶκος Ἄρεως, ὕψωμα Ἡλίου περὶ μοίρας ιθʹ, ταπείνωμα Κρόνου περὶ μοίρας κʹ

‘The beginning of the zodiac circle is Aries: male, equinoctial, tropic, springtime, house of Mars, exaltation of the Sun around 19 degrees, depression of Saturn around 20 degrees’

— Paul of Alexandria 2 (p. 2 Boer)

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 4th century CE
