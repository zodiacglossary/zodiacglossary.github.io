renes aquarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:54:36 AM
Primary meaning the kidneys of the Waterbearer
Literal translation kidneys of the waterbearer
Variants
renes aquarii
aquarii renes
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the kidneys of the Waterbearer animal part

25°–27° Aquarius

aliae tres in renibus,

‘the other three [following degrees] are in the kidneys [of Aquarius]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.11

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
