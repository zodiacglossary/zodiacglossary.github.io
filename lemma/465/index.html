mul.ANŠE.KUR.RA
sīsû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:59:45 AM
Primary meaning Horse-star
Literal translation Horse-star
Variants
mul.ANŠE.KUR.RA sīsû
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Horse-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to α, β, γ, δ Cassiopeiae.

DIŠ MUL ša2 ina GUB3-šu2 GUB-zu mul.ANŠE.KUR.RA

‘¶ The star which stands on its left: the Horse.’

— BM 86378 i 30

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 36
