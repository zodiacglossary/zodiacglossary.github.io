ūmū mitḫarūtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 10:44:07 AM
Primary meaning corresponding days
Variants
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
corresponding days calendrical concept

EN.NUN DU3 mu-ši | EREN2 KASKAL šu-šu-rum | EREN2 URU ka-ša-du | ka-šad URU u EREN2 šu-šur KASKAL | u4-mu mit-ḫa-ru-tu4 SIG5.MEŠ

‘The watches of the entire night: | for dispatching the army on a campaign, | for the army to conquer a city, | for defeating a city and an army, for proceeding a campaign, | the corresponding days are favorable.’

— K 2847 xiii II 78-82

Genre: celestial divination
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: 7th century BCE

Oppenheim, A. L. 1974. A Babylonian Diviner’s Manual. Journal of Near Eastern Studies, 33(2), 197–220. 201
