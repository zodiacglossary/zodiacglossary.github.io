šꜣw

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 10/21/2024, 7:45:34 AM
Primary meaning Lot of Daimon
Literal translation determination
Variants
šꜣw
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Lot of Daimon astrological concept, astrological lot

(rꜥ)-ḫꜥ mw 22 (rꜥ)-ḥtp mꜣy 22 (šy-n-tꜣ)-p.t ḏlꜣ.t 20 |(šy-n-tꜣ)-dwꜣ.t kꜣ 20 šꜣw tbt(.w) {30} sšr rpy.t 30 špšy.t | ḥr-ꜥnḫ 14 wrꜣ(.t) gnhd ⌈14⌉ ꜥnḫ pꜣ-nty-ꜣtḥ | mwt ḥtr(.w) ḥꜥ(.w) rpy.t (y)ꜥb(.t) tbt(.w)

‘Ascendant: Aquarius 22°; Descendant: Leo 22°; (Lake of the) Sky: Scorpio 20°| (Lake of the) Netherworld: Taurus 20° (Lot of the) Daimon: Pisces {30}; (Lot of the) Evil Daimon: Virgo 30°; (Lot of) Fortune: |Capricorn 14°; (Lot of) Misfortune: Cancer ⌈14°⌉; (Lot of) Life: Sagittarius 12. (Lot of) Death: Gemini; (Lot of) Flesh: Virgo; (Lot of) Illness: Pisces;’

— ANAshm.Mus.D.O. 641 9-12

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Athribis
Date: 48 BCE

A. Winkler, 2022, The First Zodiac Sign and the Daimon: The Advent of an Astrological Tradition and Seven Elaborate Horoscopes, Studien our Alt'ägyptischen Culture 51: 267-319 302
