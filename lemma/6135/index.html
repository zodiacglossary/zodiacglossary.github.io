ὀξυγώνιος
oxugōnios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 6/15/2025, 3:06:37 PM
Primary meaning cresecent
Literal translation sharp-cornered
Variants
ὀξυγώνιος oxugōnios
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
crescent lunar phase

ἡ δὲ θεὰ καὶ σελασφόρος Σελήνη (…) ὀξυγώνιος

‘The goddess and light-bearing Moon (…) being in crescent (phase) …’

— P.Lond. 98 iv 73–76

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95 CE

(GH 95)
