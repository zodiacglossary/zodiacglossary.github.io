twtw n hwṱ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 7:57:09 AM
Primary meaning masculine zodiac sign
Literal translation male image
Variants
twtw n hwṱ
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
masculine zodiac sign astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

ỉn ỉw=f ḥr wnmy n pꜣ twtw ⌈ṯꜣy⌉ [pꜣ sw]sw ⌈mḥ⌉-1 šꜥ mḥ-12 twtw n ḥwṱ ⌈n⌉ wmnmy

‘If it is on the right of the zodiac sign> from the 1st until the 12th degree, that means male zodiac sign on the right;’

— P.Carlsberg XI 10 12

Genre: literary text
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd Cent. CE

Quack, J. F. and K. Ryholt, 2019, Petese Interpreting Astrology by Imhotep for King Nechepsos. Copenhagen, 2019, 161-183. 165
