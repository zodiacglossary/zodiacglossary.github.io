ὕψωμα
hupsōma

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/7/2025, 11:49:44 AM
Primary meaning exaltation
Literal translation elevation
Variants
ὕψωμα hupsōma
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
exaltation astrological concept

τρίτον ζῴδιον Δίδυμοι, ἀρσενικόν, δίσωμον, ἐαρινόν, ἀνθρωποειδές, οἶκος Ἑρμοῦ, ὕψωμα οὐδενός, κοίλωμα οὐδενός

‘The third zodiac-sign is Gemini: male, two-bodied, springtime, human-formed, house of Mercury, exaltation of none, depression of none’

— Paul of Alexandria 2 (p. 4 Boer)

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 4th century CE
