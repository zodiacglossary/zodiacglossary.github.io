altitudo

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/1/2023, 3:39:54 PM
Primary meaning exaltation
Literal translation altitude
Variants
altitudo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
exaltation astrological concept

In altitudinibus itaque suis gaudent[es] constitutae

‘When planets are in their own exaltations we say that they rejoice’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.3.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.43
