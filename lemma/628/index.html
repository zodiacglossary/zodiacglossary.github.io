d.Šar2-ur4 u d.Šar2-gaz
Šarur u Šargaz

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/20/2023, 11:02:40 AM
Primary meaning Šarur-and-Šargaz-stars
Literal translation Šarur-and-Šargaz-stars
Variants
d.Šar2-ur4 u d.Šar2-gaz Šarur u Šargaz
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Šarur-and-Šargaz-stars constellation

approx. corresponding to λ and υ scorpii.

DIŠ 2 MUL.MEŠ ša2 ina zi-qit mul.GIR2.TAB GUB.MEŠ-zu d.Šar2-ur4 u d.Šar2-gaz

‘¶ The two stars which stand in the sting of the Scorpion: Šarur and Šargaz.’

— BM 86378 ii 31-32

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 45-46
