ἐναντίωσις
enantiōsis

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:36:56 AM
Primary meaning opposition
Literal translation opposition
Variants
ἐναντίωσις enantiōsis
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
opposition astrological concept

ζητήσαντες καθὼς προεκτεθείμεθα εὕρομεν τὴν ἐναντίωσιν Ἄρεως καὶ Ἀφροδίτης χρηματίζουσαν οὕτως κατὰ τὸ ζʹ κλίμα καὶ τὴν τετράγωνον πλευρὰν Κρόνου

‘By searching in the manner in which we have set out, we found the opposition of Mars and Venus thus active according to the 7th clime, and the square side (i.e., quadrangle) of Saturn’

— Vettius Valens 7.6.30

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 124)
