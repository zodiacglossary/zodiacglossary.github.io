σύνδεσμος
sundesmos

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/14/2025, 6:03:45 AM
Primary meaning lunar node
Literal translation binding
Variants
σύνδεσμος sundesmos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
lunar node astrological concept

συνδέσμῳ δ’ ἀνάγοντι Σεληναίης παρεούσης

‘When the Moon is present in the ascending lunar node’

— Dorotheus 5.10.43

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
