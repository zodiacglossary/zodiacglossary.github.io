ἑξαγωνίζω
hexagōnizō

Greek verb

Last edited: 10/27/2025, 6:56:06 AM
Primary meaning to be in sextile
Literal translation to be in sextile
Variants
ἑξαγωνίζω hexagōnizō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to be in sextile aspect

αὐτὸς ὁ τοῦ Κρόνου σύμφωνον ἔχῃ συσχηματισμὸν πρὸς τὸν Ἥλιον ἤτοι συνὼν ἢ ἑξαγωνίζων

‘(If) the (star) of Kronos (Saturn) itself has a harmonious configuration with the Sun, that is, conjunction or sextile aspect …’

— Hephaestion 2.4.8

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
See also

〈ἑξάγωνος〉 hexagōnos ‘sextile’

〈exagonum〉 ‘sextile’
