ꜥ.wy špšy.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 6:48:01 PM
Primary meaning House of fortune
Literal translation House of fortune
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
House of fortune astrological house

5th astrological place

pꜣy-ms=w r swgꜣ (n) ꜥ.wy (n) špšy.t ỉw=f [r ỉr]\ nfr r šy

‘The one who was born when Mercury was in the House of fortune, he will be | lucky as too fate.’

— P.Berlin 8345 iv 10-11

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: 1st cent. CE to 2nd cent. CE

Quack, J. F., 2008, Texte aus Ägypten: Demotische, magische und divinatorische Texte. In B. Janowski and B. Wilhelm (eds), Omina, Orakel, Rituale und Beschwörungen. Texte aus der Umwelt des Alten Testaments, Neue Folge 4. Gütersloh: Gütersloher Verlagshaus.
