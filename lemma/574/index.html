BAL
palû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/24/2024, 9:03:14 AM
Primary meaning period
Literal translation turn
Variants
BAL palû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
period astronomical quantity

ša₂ AN 4.44 MU.MEŠ ⸢2.13 x?⸣ IGI.MEŠ 2.31 BAL.MEŠ 15.6 KI DU

‘For Mars. 4,44 years, 2,[1]3 ...? appearances, 2,31 turns, the position proceeds 15,6,(0).’

— BM 34676 (BMAPT No. 13) rev. 33'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 232-233
