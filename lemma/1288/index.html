IGI
pānu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/30/2023, 1:32:02 PM
Primary meaning front
Literal translation front
Variants
IGI pānu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
front expression of relative position

30 TA MURUB4 ana NIM pa-ni-šu2 GAR.MEŠ

‘The moon sets its front from the middle toward positive latitude.’

— MLC 2190 (BH 10) obv. 4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Uruk
Date: 3rd century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: S.E. 77 III.4 = -234 June 2/3)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88, 1–164. 83
