SIG
šapālu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 3:55:53 PM
Primary meaning to be below the ecliptic
Literal translation to be low
Variants
SIG šapālu
BUR3 šapālu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to be below the ecliptic verb of motion

ina 9 MU.MEŠ NIM ina 9 MU.MEŠ i-šap-pil

‘In 9 years it goes up, in 9 years it goes down.’

— BM 41004 obv. 10

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon

Brack-Bernsen & Hunger 2005/6, AfO 51, 96-107

to be low verb of position
to descend verb of motion
to become low verb of motion
See also

〈SIG〉 šuplu ‘distance below ecliptic’

〈KI.TA〉 šaplâtu ‘lower part’
