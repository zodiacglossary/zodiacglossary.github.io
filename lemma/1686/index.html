cervix piscis

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 8:00:42 AM
Primary meaning the neck of the Fish
Literal translation neck of the fish
Variants
cervix piscis
piscis cervix
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the neck of the northern Fish animal part

23°–25° Pisces

in cervice vero tres aliae quae sequuntur

‘in the neck [of the northern Fish] however are the three other [degrees] which follow’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.293
