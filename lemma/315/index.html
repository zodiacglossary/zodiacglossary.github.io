SA₄ ša₂ ABSIN
nebû ša šer'i

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/24/2025, 3:18:57 PM
Primary meaning Spica (α Virginis)
Literal translation the bright one of the furrow
Variants
SA₄ ša₂ ABSIN nebû ša šer'i
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Spica (α Virginis) star: normal star

Jones (2004) c20

GE₆ 5 ina ZALAG₂ MUL₂.BABBAR ar₂ SA₄ ša₂ [ABSIN]

‘Night of (day) 5, last part of the night, Jupiter was behind the bright one of the [Furrow].’

— BM 40099 (ADRT I No. -332B) obv. 7'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 333 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 172-173
