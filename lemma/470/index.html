Deleter-star star

Approx. corresponding to β Andromedae.

DIŠ MUL SA5 ne2-bu-u2 ša2 ina BIR mul.LU.LIM GUB-zu mul.ZU2.MUŠ.I3.KU2.E

‘¶ The bright red star which stands in the kidney of the Stag: The Deleter.’

— BM 86378 i 34-35

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 36-37
