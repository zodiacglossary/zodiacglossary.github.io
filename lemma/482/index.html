pr.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:18:30 PM
Primary meaning appearance
Literal translation going forth
Variants
pr.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
appearance astronomical event

mtw=k ꜥš nꜣy r-r=f n sp 7 n hrw 7 wbꜣ tꜣ pr(.t) n pꜣ rꜥ

‘And you recite this towards him seven times for seven days opposite the appearance of the Sun.’

— GEMF 16 364

Genre: magical text
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 2nd cent. CE

Faraone, Ch. and Torallas Tovar, S. 2022, Greek and Egyptian Magical Formularies: Text and Translation, vol. I (Berkeley CA) 182

See also

〈IGI〉 nanmurtu ‘first appearance’
