ἄκρον
akron

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/12/2024, 3:20:06 PM
Primary meaning end of an hour
Literal translation highest point
Variants
ἄκρον akron
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
end of an hour

ὥρ(ᾳ) δʹ περὶ ἄκρο[ν.]

‘hour 4 about the end.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4248 2

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 229 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 261 + II: 386-387
