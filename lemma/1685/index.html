pectus piscis

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/10/2024, 7:46:43 AM
Primary meaning the chest of the Fish
Literal translation chest of the fish
Variants
pectus piscis
piscis pectus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the chest of the northern Fish animal part

20°–22° Pisces

tres vero sequentes in pectore

‘the three following [degrees] however are in the chest [of the northern Fish]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.293
