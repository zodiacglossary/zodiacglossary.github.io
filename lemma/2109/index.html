ὀλιγανάφορος
oliganaphoros

Greek adjective

Last edited: 10/14/2024, 6:28:04 AM
Primary meaning of short rising time
Literal translation short-rising
Variants
ὀλιγανάφορος oliganaphoros
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
of short rising time zodiac sign quality,

τὸ δὲ τάχιον αὐτὸν διαδεχθῆναι παρεσκεύασαν ὁ Ἄρης καὶ ὡροσκόπος καὶ ἡ Σελήνη ἐν τροπικῷ ζῳδίῳ καὶ ὀλιγαναφόροις τετυχηκότες

‘The fact that he was succeeded quickly was provided by Mars and the Ascendant and the Moon, which were in a tropical zodiac sign and ones of short rising time.’

— CCAG I p. 107

Genre: horoscope

(GH L 487)
