νυκτερινός
nukterinos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 12:02:47 PM
Primary meaning of night
Literal translation of night
Variants
νυκτερινός nukterinos
νυκτερ nukter abbreviation
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
of night temporal expression

ὥρ(ᾳ) νυκτερ(ινῇ) ζʹ

‘hour 7 of night’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4275 3

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 508 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 281-282 + II: 416-417

See also

〈nocturnus〉 ‘nocturnal’

〈νύξ〉 nux ‘night’
