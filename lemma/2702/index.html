κανόνιον
kanonion

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/18/2025, 6:09:59 PM
Primary meaning astronomical table
Literal translation small bar
Variants
κανόνιον kanonion
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
astronomical table textual layout, artefact of astral science, astronomical genre

ἀφελ(ὼν) [ἀ(πὸ) τ(ῶν) ὁ]μαλ(ῶν) τὰ διαφορὰ (ἑξηκοστὰ) τὰ π(αρα)κείμενα [τῇ ἡλιακῇ] μο(ίρᾳ) ἐν τῷ ἐπ' ὀρθῆς τῆς σφαί- [ρας κα]νονίῳ

‘subtracting [from the] non-uniform ones the sixtieths corresponding [to the solar] degree in the table of sphaera recta, ...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 Fr. 3, col. i, l. 10-13

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 32-35
