עֵת
ʿēt̲

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:42:39 AM
Primary meaning time
Literal translation time
Variants
עֵת ʿēt̲
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
time temporal expression

כִּ֣י לְכׇל־חֵ֔פֶץ יֵ֖שׁ עֵ֣ת וּמִשְׁפָּ֑ט כִּֽי־רָעַ֥ת הָאָדָ֖ם רַבָּ֥ה עָלָֽיו׃

kî lək̲ol-ḥēp̲eṣ yēš ʿēt̲ ûmišpāṭ kî-rāʿat̲ hāʾād̲ām rabbāh ʿālāyw.

‘For there is a time for every experience, including the doom; for a man’s calamity overwhelms him.’

— Ecclesiastes 8 6

Genre: religious text
