Nin-nisig-and-Erragal-stars constellation

ε and ζ Lyrae.

DIŠ 2 MUL.MEŠ ša2 EGIR-šu2 GUB.MEŠ-zu d.Nin-SAR u d.Er3-ra-gal

‘¶ The two stars which stand behind it: Nin-nisig and Erragal.’

— BM 86378 i 27

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE
