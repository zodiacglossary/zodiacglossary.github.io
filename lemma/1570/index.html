mul.GABA GIR2.TAB
irat zuqaqīpi

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/18/2024, 9:44:27 AM
Primary meaning α Scorpii
Literal translation Breast-of-the-Scorpion star
Variants
mul.GABA GIR2.TAB irat zuqaqīpi
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
α Scorpii (Antares) star

The star is also known as the Lisi-star.

DIŠ mul.GABA GIR2.TAB u mul.UZ3 KUR.MEŠ-ma mul.ŠU.GI u mul.SIPA.ZI.AN.NA ŠU2.MEŠ

‘¶ The Breast of the Scorpion and the Goat rise, and the Old Man and the True Shepherd of Anu set.’

— BM 86378 iii 26

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 56
