Κάλπις
Kalpis

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/25/2025, 3:45:32 PM
Primary meaning Pitcher
Literal translation Pitcher
Variants
Κάλπις Kalpis
Κάλπεις Kalpeis
Κάλπη Kalpē
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Pitcher part of constellation

part of Aquarius; likely corresponding to γ+ζ+η+π Aquarii when defined as 4 stars (Geminus)

Οἱ δὲ ὲν ἄκρᾳ τῇ δεξιᾷ χειρὶ κείμενοι τοῦ Ὑδροχόου δʹ ἀστέρες Κάλπις καλοῦνται.

‘The 4 stars lying on the tip of the right hand of Aquarius are called Pitcher.’

— Geminus 3.6

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 18-19

Some manuscripts have the variant Κάλπεις or Κάλπη.
See also

〈Ὑδροχόος〉 Hudrochoos ‘Aquarius’
