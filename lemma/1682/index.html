collum piscis

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/31/2025, 3:31:26 PM
Primary meaning the neck of the Fish
Literal translation neck of the fish
Variants
collum piscis
piscis collum
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the neck of the southern Fish animal part

8°–10° Pisces

tres quae sequuntur in ordine collocantur in collo

‘the three [degrees] which follow in order are located in the neck [of the southern Fish]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.293
