18°–21° Aries decan

Aries. In hoc signo, idest in Ariete, quae sint partes plenae, quae vacuae, speciali significatione monstrabimus. Itaque in Ariete plenae partes XII, vacuae partes XVIII. I locus partes III vacuus II locus partes V Senator [idest asiccan] III locus partes IX vacuus IV locus partes IV Senacher [idest sentafora] V locus partes V vacuus VI locus partes IV Sentacher [uaucate idest asentacer]

‘Aries: In that sign, i. e. Aries, some degrees are full and some are empty. We will show this using a special figure. Therefore there are 12° full and 18° empty in Aries. 1. 3° empty 2. 5° Senator [i.e. Asiccan] 3. 9° empty 4. 4° Senacher [i.e. Sentafora] 5. 5° empty 6. 4° Sentacher [Uaucate i.e. Asentacer]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.22.8

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.266-267
