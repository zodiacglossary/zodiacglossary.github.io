Νισᾶν
Nisan

Greek noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:52:10 PM
Primary meaning Artemisios
Literal translation Artemisios
Variants
Νισᾶν Nisan rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Artemisios month, time interval

month VII in the Macedonian calendar

Νισᾶν· ὁ Ἀρτεμίσιος μήν. καὶ † Ἰλίου πόλις

‘Nisan: the month Artemisios. And Heliopolis.’

— Hesychius ν 587

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2020. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 2 (a. Epsilon – Iota, b. Kappa – Omicron), ed. I.C. Cunningham. Berlin, Boston: Walter de Gruyter. 891
