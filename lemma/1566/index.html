ἐπιμαρτύρησις
epimarturēsis

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/24/2023, 11:39:54 AM
Primary meaning aspect
Literal translation bearing witness
Variants
ἐπιμαρτύρησις epimarturēsis
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
aspect astrological concept

εἰ δὲ καὶ πλείοσι τὴν συναφὴν ἐπεποίητο ἤτοι κατὰ κόλλησιν ἢ καὶ ἐπιμαρτύρησιν, καὶ πλείονας ἂν ἀδελφοὺς εἶχεν

‘If (the Moon) had made contact with more (planets), that is, in terms of attachment (conjunction) or also aspect, he would also have had more brothers.’

— Hephaestion 2.18.42

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: V CE
See also

〈μαρτυρέω〉 martureō ‘to be in aspect’

〈ἐπιμάρτυρος〉 epimarturos ‘in aspect’
