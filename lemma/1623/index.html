caput librae

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:30:39 AM
Primary meaning the head of the Scales
Literal translation head of the scales
Variants
caput librae
librae caput
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the head of the Scales animal part

1°–2° Libra

I. et II. partes collocantur in capite

‘degrees 1 and 2 are located in the head [of the Scales]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.7

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.290
