κλῆρος τύχης
klēros tuchēs

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:06:37 AM
Primary meaning lot of fortune
Literal translation lot of fortune
Variants
κλῆρος τύχης klēros tuchēs
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of fortune astrological lot

κλ[ῆ]ρος τύχης ὁ πρῶτος κλῆρος ὁ καλούμενος τύ- χης εὑρέθη τὴν κατὰ μῆκος [κείνησιν] ποιούμενος ἐν ζῳδί[ῳ θ]η[λ]υκῷ ἀνθρωποειδεῖ Παρθένω [...]

‘Lot of fortune. The first lot, called of fortune, has been found making its longitudinal [motion] in the feminine, anthropomorphic sign Virgo [...]’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 ii. 12-16

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 424-425
