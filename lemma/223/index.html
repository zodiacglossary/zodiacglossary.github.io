mꜣꜥ ḫꜥ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/25/2025, 7:59:15 AM
Primary meaning step
Literal translation part of rising
Variants
mꜣꜥ ḫꜥ
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
step (morning first) astronomical term

(n)-ṯꜣy pꜣ gn⟨ḥ⟩ḏ 6 šꜥ ỉḫ.t 26 r swsw 110 ỉw=f mšꜥ | tny ỉwṱ 20 r mꜣꜥ ḫꜥ 3 r swsw 1 ỉw=k ỉr 3 {100} sp 110 r 330

‘From Cancer 6° to Libra 26° is 110 degrees, proceeding at the rate of 0;20, as a part of rising, 3 (of them) make 1 degree. You shall multiply 3 times 110, it makes 330.’

— ANAShm.Mus.D.O. 483

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 3rd cent. BCE to 3rd cent. CE

Ossendrijver, M. and A. Winkler, 2018. Chaldeans on the Nile: Two Egyptian Procedure Texts with Babylonian System A1 and A2 for Mercury, in Scaffolding of Our Thoughts: Essays on Assyriology and the History of Science in Honor of Francesca Rochberg, eds. J. Crisostomo et al. (Leiden: Brill, 2018), 282-418 386
