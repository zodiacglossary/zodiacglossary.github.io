Χονταρέ
Chontare

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/5/2025, 3:15:14 AM
Primary meaning Decan 3 of Libra
Variants
Χονταρέ Chontare
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 3 of Libra decan

οἱ δὲ δεκανοὶ τρεῖς· ὁ πρῶτος Σουχωέ, ὁ δεύτερος Πτηχούτ, ὁ τρίτος Χονταρέ

‘The decans are three: the first, Souchōe, the second, Ptēchout, the third, Chontare’

— Hephaestion 1.1.127

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
