culmen mundi

Latin noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:15:11 AM
Primary meaning upper midheaven
Literal translation culmination of the world
Variants
culmen mundi
mundi culmen
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
upper midheaven cardinal point

quod summo premitur devexum culmine mundi donec ad occasus veniat, puerilibus annis succedit teneramque regit sub sede iuventam.

‘The slope which sinks down from the summit of the sky till it reaches the Occident succeeds to the years of childhood and includes in its province control of tender youth.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.846 ff

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 148-149
