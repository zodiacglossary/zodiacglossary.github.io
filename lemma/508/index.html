ἀναφορικός
anaphorikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/17/2024, 1:56:28 PM
Primary meaning of rising times
Literal translation of rising times
Variants
ἀναφορικός anaphorikos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
of rising times astronomical term

τὸν δὲ ὡρονόμον ὡροσκόπον προσδεῖ δι’ ὅλην τὴν [ἀν]αλογίαν ἐκ τοῦ ἀναφορικοῦ πρὸς κλεψύδραν· ἐκπείπτει ἐν Καρκίνωι περὶ μοιρῶν κεʹ.

‘It is also necessary (to calculate) the hour apportioning Ascendant from the (table) of rising times in complete proportion by water clock: the result is in Cancer, about 25 degrees.’

— P.Lond. 98 59–61

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95 CE

(GH 95)
