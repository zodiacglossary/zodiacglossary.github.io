κλῆρος γάμου
klēros gamou

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 9:25:35 AM
Primary meaning lot of marriage
Literal translation lot of marriage
Variants

lot of marriage astrological lot

κλῆρος γάμος Σκορπίῳ

‘Lot of marriage in Scorpio.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4270 14

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 350

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 276-277 + II: 410-411

"Dorotheus II 2 (ed. Pingree, p. 199) gives instructions for finding the Lot of Marriage: the elongation of Venus from Saturn is to be added to the ascendant (for diurnal nativities of men and, apparently, nocturnal nativities of women) or subtracted from the ascendant (for diurnal nativities of women and nocturnal nativities of men)." (Jones 1999: II 276)
