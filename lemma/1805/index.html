appareo

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:05:57 AM
Primary meaning to become visible
Literal translation to appear
Variants
appareo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to become visible verb of visual perception

“Quid ergo? Non aliquando etiam apparentibus stellis et nocte tranquilla fulgurat?”

‘“But well! Does it not sometimes flash lightning, even when the stars are visible and the night is clear?”’

— Seneca, Natural Questions 2.26.8

Genre: philosophy
Date: 62/63 CE

Seneca. Natural Questions, Volume I: Books 1-3. Translated by Thomas H. Corcoran. Loeb Classical Library 450. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1971. 142-143
