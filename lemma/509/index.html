ἀνοικοδεσπότητος
anoikodespotētos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/17/2024, 4:10:47 PM
Primary meaning without a housemaster
Literal translation without a housemaster
Variants
ἀνοικοδεσπότητος anoikodespotētos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
without a housemaster astrological concept

ἀνοικοδεσπότητος ἡ γένεσις διὰ τὸ ὑπὸ δύσιν ἐμπεπτωκέναι Ἀφροδίτην τὴν κυρίαν τῶν ὁρίων τῆς Σελήνης.

‘The nativity is without a housemaster because Venus, the lord of the terms of the Moon, has fallen below the Descendant.’

— Vettius Valens 3.5.8

Genre: horoscope
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 75)
