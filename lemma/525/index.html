astronomical meaning

d.sin ana d.UTU.šu2.a la-IGI

‘The Moon is in front of the Sun by 16(15!);42’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. ii 24

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE
