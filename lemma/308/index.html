MAŠ.MAŠ ar₂
māšu arkû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:25:11 PM
Primary meaning β Geminorum
Literal translation rear twin
Variants
MAŠ.MAŠ ar₂ māšu arkû
mul.MAŠ.TAB.BA EGIR-u₂ māšu arkû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Marvin Schreiber
β Geminorum star: normal star, star: ziqpu star

Jones (2004) c12 (normal star)

[... GI₆ 19] DIR AN ZA ULU₃ GIN ina ZALAG₂ sin ar₂ MAŠ.MAŠ ar₂ 1 KUŠ₃ sin 5 KUŠ₃ ana ULU₃ SIG

‘[... Night of (day) 19], clouds were in the sky, the south wind blew; last part of the night, Sin was 1 cubit behind the rear twin (star), Sin being 5 cubits low to the south.’

— BM 35015+ (ADRT II No. -164B) obv. 7'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 165 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 494-495
