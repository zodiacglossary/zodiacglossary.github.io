potestas

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:32:44 PM
Primary meaning rulership
Literal translation power
Variants
potestas
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
rulership astrological concept

Est itaque Solis Leo et in hoc signo habet domicilium et potestatem; Lunae Cancer.

‘The house of the Sun is Leo, and in this sign he has his home and his power; and that of the Moon is Cancer.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.2.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.42
