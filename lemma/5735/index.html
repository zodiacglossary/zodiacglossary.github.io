τὸ ἑπόμενον καὶ στενότατον πέρας τῆς Πλειάδος
to hepomenon kai stenotaton peras tēs Pleiados

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:20:51 AM
Primary meaning 27 Tauri
Literal translation The rearmost and narrowest end of the Pleiades
Variants
τὸ ἑπόμενον καὶ στενότατον πέρας τῆς Πλειάδος to hepomenon kai stenotaton peras tēs Pleiados

27 Tauri star

τὸ ἑπόμενον καὶ στενότατον πέρας τῆς Πλειάδος

‘The rearmost and narrowest end of the Pleiades [27 Tauri]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 7

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

Ptolemy. 1903. Claudii Ptolemaei opera quae exstant omnia volumen I, Syntaxis Mathematica Pars II, Libros VII-XIII continens, ed. J.L. Heiberg. Leipzig: B.G. Teubner. 84–129
