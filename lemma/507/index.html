MUL.MEŠ šu-ut d.En-lil2
kakkabū šūt Enlil

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:09:08 AM
Primary meaning Stars of Enlil
Literal translation Stars of Enlil
Variants
MUL.MEŠ šu-ut d.En-lil2 kakkabū šūt Enlil
MUL.MEŠ šu-ut 50 kakkabū šūt Enlil
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Stars of Enlil group of stars and constellations

One of the three stellar divisions of the sky.

33 MUL.MEŠ šu-ut d.En-lil2

‘33 stars of Enlil.’

— BM 86378 i 39

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 38

[ki-ma] MUL.MEŠ šu-ut 50 ug-da-me-ru-ni

‘When the stars of Enlil have been finished, (...).’

— ND 5497/22 i 16'

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: Kalhu
Date: ca. 800-600 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 37
