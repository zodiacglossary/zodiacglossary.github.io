geminatus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 8:03:16 AM
Primary meaning double-bodied
Literal translation doubled
Variants
geminatus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
double-bodied astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

the signs Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces

nunc binis insiste; dabunt geminata potentis per socium effectus.

‘Now turn to the double signs; being doubled they will exert influences the power of which is tempered by a partner.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.159 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 94-95
