ἀπόστροφος
apostrophos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/9/2023, 2:53:14 PM
Primary meaning not in aspect
Literal translation turned away
Variants
ἀπόστροφος apostrophos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
not in aspect

καὶ ὁ κατ’ ἐπέμβασιν Ἄρης καὶ ἡ Σελήνη Αἰγοκέρωτι ἀπόστροφοι

‘Both the one at entry (into rule of a time-period), Mars, and the Moon in Capricorn were not in aspect’

— Vettius Valens 5.4.22

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH pp. 116–117)
