SI GIR2.TAB
qaran zuqaqīpi

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:38:14 AM
Primary meaning horn of the scorpion
Literal translation horn of the scorpion
Variants
SI GIR2.TAB qaran zuqaqīpi
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
horn of the scorpion constellation

Part of the constellation Scorpius resp. the zodiacal sign Scorpio. "Horn", i.e. the pincer of the scorpion.

i-nu-šu2 30 šap-lat SI GIR2.TAB

‘At that time, the moon was below the Horn of the Scorpion.’

— AB 251 (BH 2) obv. 3

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon?
Date: 3rd century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: [Darius II Year 14].I.14 = -409 April 29)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 56
