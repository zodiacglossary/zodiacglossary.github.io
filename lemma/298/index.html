ITI
arḫu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:22:37 PM
Primary meaning month
Literal translation month
Variants
ITI arḫu
AB₂ arḫu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
month unit of time

dil-bat ina RIN₂ ina MURUB₄ ITI ina GIR₂

‘Venus was in Libra, in the middle of the month in Scorpio.’

— BM 33837 (ADRT II No. -232) obv. 12

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 233 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 104-105

36 taš AB₂ ana AB₂

‘(0);36 is the difference month to month.’

— BCM A.1845-1982.2+ab (BMAPT No. 97) obv. ii 11'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
