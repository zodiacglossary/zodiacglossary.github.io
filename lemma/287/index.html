tny.t nṯr

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/14/2023, 3:08:47 PM
Primary meaning part of god
Literal translation part of god
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
part of god astrological concept

9th astrological place; alternative to "House of god"

ꜥ.wy (n) sḥn-mwt pꜣ kꜣ ḥr-tšy | tꜣ tny.t nṯr nꜣ ḥtr.w

‘The House of provisions of death: Taurus and Mars | The part of god: Gemini’

— O.Neugeb. 3 19-20

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117
