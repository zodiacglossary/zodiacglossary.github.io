ana ar₂-šu₂
ana arkišu

Akkadian adverb

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:50:33 AM
Primary meaning in retrograde direction
Literal translation backward
Variants
ana ar₂-šu₂ ana arkišu
ana EGIR-šu₂ ana arkišu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
in retrograde direction spatial expression, planetary motion, direction of motion

(9') [TA UŠ] (10') 4 KI ana ar₂-šu₂ LA₂-is-ma ana ME E

‘(9') [From the station] (10') the position (of Jupiter) moves backward 4 (UŠ), then it rises to daylight (Acronychal Rising).’

— BM 34081+ (BMAPT No. 18) Obv. i 9'-10'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2016, Ancient Babylonian astronomers calculated Jupiters position from the area under a time-velocity graph, Science, 351, 482-484 248-249
