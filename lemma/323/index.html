DANNA
bēru

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:08:58 PM
Primary meaning bēru
Literal translation ?
Variants
DANNA bēru
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
bēru unit of celestial position

1 bēru = 30 UŠ

a-na 1 DANNA 8 u NIM u SIG

‘For 1 bēru (of distance along the ecliptic) it (Jupiter) ascends and descends 8 fingers.’

— BM 37266 (BMAPT No. 47) obv. 4

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 334

bēru unit of time

1 bēru = 30 UŠ

1 DANNA ME NIM-a AN.MI ᵈ[šamaš₂ ša₂ DIB]

‘(At) 1 bēru after sunrise eclipse of [Šamaš which was omitted.]’

— BM 40099 (ADRT I No. -332B) rev. 10

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 333 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.

bēru unit of length

1 bēru = 30 UŠ
