γεώδης
geōdēs

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:07:34 AM
Primary meaning earthly
Literal translation earthy
Variants
γεώδης geōdēs
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
earthly zodiac sign quality

ἔστι δὲ τὸ ζῴδιον (…) γεῶδες

‘The zodiac sign (Taurus) is (…) earthly’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.14

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
