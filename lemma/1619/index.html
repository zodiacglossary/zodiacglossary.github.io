caput scorpii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:30:53 AM
Primary meaning the head of the Scorpion
Literal translation head of the scorpion
Variants
caput scorpii
scorpii caput
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the head of the Scorpion animal part

1°–2° Scorpio

I. et II. partes in capite Scorpii collocantur,

‘degrees 1 and 2 are located in the Scorpion's head’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.8

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
