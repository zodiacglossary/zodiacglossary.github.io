DIM₄
sanāqu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:44:11 PM
Primary meaning to come near
Literal translation to come near
Variants
DIM₄ sanāqu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to come near verb of motion

(30) GE₆ 2 ina ZALAG₂ dele-bat SIG GIR₃ ar₂ ša₂ A (31) ⸢2⸣ SI DIM₄

‘(30) Night of (day) 2, last part of the night, Venus came near 2 fingers below the rear foot of the lion.’

— BM 35070+ (ADRT III No. -132B) obv. 30-31

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 133 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 214-215
