Στωχνῆνε
Stōchnēne

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/6/2025, 6:08:02 AM
Primary meaning Decan 1 of Scorpio
Variants
Στωχνῆνε Stōchnēne
Στοχνηυέ Stochnēue variant reading in MS P
Στπχνηέ Stpchnēe variant reading in Ep. 4
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 1 of Scorpio decan

οἱ δὲ δεκανοὶ τρεῖς· ὁ πρῶτος Στωχνῆε, ὁ δὲ δεύτερος Σεσμέ, ὁ τρίτος Σισιεμέ

‘The decans are three: the first is Stōchnēne, the second Sesme, the third Sisieme’

— Hephaestion 1.1.147

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
