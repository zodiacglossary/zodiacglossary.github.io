Ἐνυάλιος
Enualios

Greek noun

Last edited: 10/13/2025, 8:36:07 AM
Primary meaning Mars
Literal translation Warlike
Variants
Ἐνυάλιος Enualios
Edited by

Mars planet

ἢν δ’ ἂρ Ἐνυαλίῳ συνέῃ Κρόνος

‘If Saturn is together with the Warlike one (Mars)’

— Dorotheus 2.18.2–3

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
