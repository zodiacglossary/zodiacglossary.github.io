συνανατέλλω
sunanatellō

Greek verb

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 3:48:16 PM
Primary meaning to rise simultaneously
Literal translation to rise
Variants
συνανατέλλω sunanatellō
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
to rise simultaneously astronomical event

Εἰ γὰρ δύναμίν τινα προσεφέρετο ὁ Κύων, ἔδει κατὰ τὴν ἀληθινὴν ἐπιτολὴν τῶν καυμάτων γίνεσθαι ἐπίτασιν· τότε γὰρ ἅμα συνανατέλλει τῷ ἡλίῳ.

‘For if the Dog Star would be contributing any power, increase of the burning heat would have occurred at the true heliacal rising: for then it rises simultaneously with the sun.’

— Geminus 17.39

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 91
