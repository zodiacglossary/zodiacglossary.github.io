limpidus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:00:13 PM
Primary meaning bright
Literal translation bright
Variants
limpidus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
bright adjective, term of visual perception

In Leonis parte II. stella limpida invenitur.

‘A bright star is found at 2° Leo.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.31.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.357
