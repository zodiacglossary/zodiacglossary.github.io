LA₂
muṭṭû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 3/22/2024, 3:06:32 PM
Primary meaning to subtract
Literal translation to diminish
Variants
LA₂ muṭṭû used with preposition ana
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to subtract mathematical operation, subtractive operation, arithmetical operation

preferred for identity-conserving subtractions

(5) [IGI.DU₈.A ša₂ d].dil-bat 8 MU.MEŠ a-na ar₂-ki-ka GUR-ar₂ (6) [xxxxx] 4 u₄-mu.MEŠ tu-maṭ-ṭa IGI-mar

‘(5) [The appearances ... of] Venus: you turn back 8 years behind you, (6) [...] you diminish (the date) by 4 days, you see it (Venus).’

— BM 45728 obv. 6

Genre: goal year procedure
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 450-50 BCE

Britton chk
