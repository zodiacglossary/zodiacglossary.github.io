ἴδιος
idios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/2/2024, 9:32:17 AM
Primary meaning one's own
Literal translation one's own
Variants
ἴδιος idios
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
one's own astrological concept

e. g. of terms

Κρόνος] ἀνατολικ(ὸς) ἑῷος [ἐν Λέοντι μοί(ρᾳ) . .ʹ, ζῳδίῳ] ἡλίου, ὁρίοις ἰδ[ίοις

‘Saturn] rising in the east [in Leo xx°, house] of the sun, his own terms.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4236 8-9

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 63 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 250-251 + II: 372-373
