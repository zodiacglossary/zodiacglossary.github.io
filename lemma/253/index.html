ἐπιτολή
epitolē

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 3:01:53 PM
Primary meaning heliacal rising
Literal translation rising
Variants
ἐπιτολή epitolē
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
heliacal rising astronomical event

Μεγάλη δέ ἐστι διαφορὰ ἀνατολῆς καὶ ἐπιτολῆς. Ἀνατολὴ μὲν γάρ ἐστιν ἡ προειρημένη, ἐπιτολὴ δὲ ἡ γινομένη πρὸς τὸν ὁρίζοντα φάσις μετὰ τῆς πρὸς τὸν ἥλιον ἀποστάσεως ἀπολαμβανομένη.

‘And there is a big difference between a (diurnal) rising and a (heliacal) rising. For a (diurnal) rising is the aforementioned, but a (heliacal) rising is the appearance happening at the horizon that is being taken together with the distance to the sun.’

— Geminus 13.3

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 68
