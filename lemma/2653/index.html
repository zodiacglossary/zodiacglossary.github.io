δεύτερος κλῆρος
deuteros klēros

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:30:31 AM
Primary meaning lot of the daimon
Literal translation second lot
Variants
δεύτερος κλῆρος deuteros klēros
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of the daimon astrological lot

κ[λῆρος δαίμ]ονος ὁ δεύτερος [κλῆρος ὁ καλούμενος δαί- μων εὑρέθη τὴν [κατὰ μῆκος κείνησιν ποιούμεν[ος ἐν ζῳδίῳ στερέῳ βασιλι- κῷ Λέοντι [...]

‘Lot of daimon. The second lot, [called] daimon, has been found making [its longitudinal motion] [in the solid], royal [sign] Leo [...]’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 ii. 23-27

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 424-425

See also

〈κλῆρος τοῦ δαίμονος〉 klēros tou daimonos ‘lot of the daimon’
