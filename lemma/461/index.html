qup-pu IGI-u₂ ša₂ GU
quppu maḫrû ša Gula

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:30:00 PM
Primary meaning a group of stars in Aquarius
Literal translation the front basket of the great one
Variants
qup-pu IGI-u₂ ša₂ GU quppu maḫrû ša Gula
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
a group of stars in Aquarius star: normal star

Jones (2004) a11. Perhaps φ, χ, ψ1 , ψ2 , ψ3 Aqr (Jones 2004); rarely used

ana tar-ṣa qup-pu IGI-u₂ ša₂ GU KA×MI

‘Opposite the front basket of the great one it was eclipsed.’

— BM 33024+ (ADRT III No. -123A) rev. 15'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 124 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 284-285
