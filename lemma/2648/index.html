τὸ ὑπὸ γῆν
to hupo gēn

Greek noun

Last edited: 3/31/2025, 1:51:30 PM
Primary meaning lower midheaven
Literal translation the one below earth
Variants
τὸ ὑπὸ γῆν to hupo gēn
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lower midheaven astrological place, cardinal point

τὸ δὲ ὑπὸ γῆν εὑρέθη τὴν κατὰ μῆκο[ς] κείνησιν ποιούμενον ἐν ζῳδίῳ [...] [...] Σκορπίῳ

‘The lower midheaven has been found making its longitudinal motion in the [...] sign Scorpio’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 ii. 4-6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 422-423
