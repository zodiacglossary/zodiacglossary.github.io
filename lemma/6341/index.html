वृष
vṛṣa

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 9/30/2025, 2:14:11 PM
Primary meaning Taurus
Literal translation bull
Edited by
Taurus zodiac sign

वृषाकृतिस्तु प्रथितो द्वितीयः सवक्त्रकण्ठायतनं विधातुः।

vṛṣākṛtis tu prathito dvitīyaḥ savaktrakaṇṭhāyatanaṃ vidhātuḥ|

‘The second [sign] (Taurus), whose form is that of a bull, is said to be the area of the face and neck of the Creator.’

— Vṛddhayavanajātaka 1.5ab

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: ca. 300–325 CE

Mak, Bill M. 2018. “The First Two Chapters of Mīnarāja’s Vṛddhayavanajātaka.” Zinbun 48: 1–31. 15
