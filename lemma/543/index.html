GUR
turru

Akkadian adjective

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:44:53 PM
Primary meaning hollow month
Literal translation returned
Variants
GUR turru
tur-ru turru
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
hollow month astronomical term

GUB.ME u GUR ŠAR₂.ŠAR₂

‘You compute the confirmed (months) and the returned (months).’

— BM 41004 rev. 19

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon

Brack-Bernsen & Hunger 2005/6, AfO 51, 96-107

(15) kun-nu u [nu]-bal-kut ME ana ME kun-nu (16) la nu-bal-kut 1-en ME LA₂ tur-ru la nu-bal-kut ME ana ME tur-ru u nu-bal-kut 1-en ME TAB

‘(15) (The month) is confirmed and (the day) is transgressed: the day (corresponds) to the (same) day. (The month) is confirmed (16), (the day) is not transgressed: you subtract one day. (The month) is returned and (the day) is not transgressed: the day (corresponds) to the (same) day. (The month) is returned and (the day) is transgressed: you add one day.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. ii 15-16

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
