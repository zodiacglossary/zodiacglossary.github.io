diurnus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 8:55:33 AM
Primary meaning diurnal
Literal translation diurnal
Variants
diurnus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
diurnal astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

the sign Aries, Cancer, Leo, Scorpius, Sagittarius, Pisces

Nec te praetereat nocturna diurnaque signa quae sint perspicere et propria deducere lege

‘Fail not to perceive and from true rule deduce what signs are nocturnal, and what diurnal’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.203 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 98-99
