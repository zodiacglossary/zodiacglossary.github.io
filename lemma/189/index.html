abu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/12/2024, 2:38:59 PM
Primary meaning Abu (month V)
Variants
iti.NE.IZI.GAR abu Month of the installation of braziers

NE abu
Edited by
Alessia Pilloni
Abu (month V) month, month name, time interval

iti.NE šu-ku-du d.nin-urta KI.NE.MEŠ ut-tap-pa-ḫa di-pa-ru a-na d.a-nun-na-ke₄ in-na-aš₂-ši

‘Month V, the arrow, Ninurta, the braziers are lightened, the torch is lifted to the Annunaki’

— KAV 218 a ii 8-10

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: Neo-Assyrian

Casaburi, M. C. 2003. Tre-stelle-per-ciascun (-mese): l'Astrolabio B: edizione filologica. Annali/Supplemento 36
