RIN₂ ša₂ ULU₃
zibānītu ša šūti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:40:01 PM
Primary meaning α Librae
Literal translation the southern (part of) the scales
Variants
RIN₂ ša₂ ULU₃ zibānītu ša šūti
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
α Librae star: normal star

Jones (2004) c21

GI₆ 4 SAG GI₆ sin ina IGI RIN₂ ša₂ ULU₃ 2 1/2 KUŠ₃

‘Night of the 4th, beginning of the night, the moon was 2 1/2 cubits in front of the southern (part of) the scales.’

— BM 36388+ (ADRT I No. -332A) obv. 3

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 333 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 168-169
