aculeus scorpii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/1/2023, 3:36:35 PM
Primary meaning the sting of the Scorpion
Literal translation sting of the scorpion
Variants
aculeus scorpii
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the sting of the Scorpion animal part

26°–30° Scorpio

in aculeo vero eius qui ad septentrionem versus extenditur, posteriores quinque partes collocantur.

‘in the sting of him [i. e. Scorpius] who is turned towards Ursa Major the last five degrees belong’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.8

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
