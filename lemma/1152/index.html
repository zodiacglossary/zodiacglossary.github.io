locus primus

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:02:01 PM
Primary meaning important place
Literal translation first place
Variants
locus primus
primus locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
important place astrological concept

in a nativity

facit [...] efficaces tamen et omnia complentes [...] praesertim si in primis geniturae locis fuerint constituti

‘it makes them [...] successful and efficient, especially when they are in important places in the chart’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.202
