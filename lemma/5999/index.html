Greek noun

Last edited: 5/19/2025, 6:55:04 AM

Variants

term-ruler astrological concept

ὁ ὁριοκράτωρ τοῦ δύνοντος κακοποιὸς ὤν

‘The term-ruler of the Descendant, if it is a malefic …’
