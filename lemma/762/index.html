Παχών
Pachōn

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:55:14 PM
Primary meaning Pachon (month IX)
Literal translation Pachon
Variants
Παχών Pachōn
Παχώ Pachō abbreviation
Παχ Pach abbreviation
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Pachon (month IX) month, time interval

month IX in the Alexandrian and Egyptian calendar

...] Παχών | ιε β . [...

‘...] Pachon | 15 2 x [...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4154 5

Genre: mathematical astronomy: epoch table
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd/3rd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 126-128 + II: 70-71

...] ζ μθ λ .η λδ Παχω [...

‘...] 7 49 30 x8 34 Pacho(n) [...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4212 9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: five-day almanac
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: after 218 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 226 + II: 314-315

A horizontal bar is written above and to the right of the ω to indicate that it is an abbreviation.

...Π]αχ [...] ε .θ νδ [...

‘...P]ach(on) [...] 5 x9 54 [...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4212 10

Genre: mathematical astronomy: five-day almanac
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: after 218 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 226 + II: 314-315

The χ is written in superscript.
