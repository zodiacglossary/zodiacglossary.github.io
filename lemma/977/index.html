locus pecuniae

Latin noun

Last edited: 9/19/2023, 2:22:49 PM
Primary meaning place of money
Literal translation place of money
Variants
locus pecuniae
pecuniae locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
place of money astrological place

2nd astrological place

spei vel pecuniae in secundo horoscopi signo

‘of Expectation of Inheritance and Wealth in the second sign from the ascendant’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.14.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.58
