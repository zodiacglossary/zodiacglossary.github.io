ŠAR₂.ŠAR₂
šutābulu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 8/22/2023, 8:18:32 AM
Primary meaning to compute
Literal translation to compute
Variants
ŠAR₂.ŠAR₂ šutābulu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to compute mathematical operation

(4') ša₂ lu-maš (5') gab-bi ki-i KA ME u ZI ŠAR₂.ŠAR₂

‘For all zodiacal signs, you compute according to the day and the displacement.’

— BM 34081+ (BMAPT No. 18) obv. i' 4'-5'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE

M. Ossendrijver, 2017, New results on a Babylonian scheme for Jupiter's motion along the zodiac, Journal of Near Eastern Studies 76, 231–247 239

to evaluate mathematical operation
