simānu

Akkadian noun

Literal translation Month of the brick

Simānu (month III) month, month name, time interval

iti.SIG₄ is le-e a-gi d.a-nim

‘Month III, the jaw of the Bull of Heaven, Anu's tiara’

— KAV 218 a i 32

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: Neo-Assyrian

Casaburi, M. C. 2003. Tre-stelle-per-ciascun (-mese): l'Astrolabio B: edizione filologica. Annali/Supplemento 33
