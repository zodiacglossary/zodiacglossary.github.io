ὁ περὶ δούλων τόπος
ho peri doulōn topos

Greek noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 2:45:07 PM
Primary meaning the place concerning slaves
Literal translation the place concerning slaves
Variants
ὁ περὶ δούλων τόπος ho peri doulōn topos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
the place concerning slaves astrological place

ἐν δὲ τῷ αὐτῷ ἔτει καὶ παλλακίδας ἠλευθέρωσε διὰ τὸ εἰς τὸν περὶ δούλων τόπον Δία ὄντα παρειληφέναι ἀπὸ Ἀφροδίτης

‘In the same year he also freed concubines on account of Jupiter, being in the place concerning slaves, receiving (as chronokrator) from Venus’

— Vettius Valens 5.6.85

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 111,IV)
