NIM
?

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 12/11/2024, 9:56:55 AM
Primary meaning east
Literal translation rising
Variants
NIM ṣītu (or a derivation)?
GIŠ.NIM ṣītu (or a derivation)? means "east"; rare variant attested in early Seleucid astronomical procedure text BM 41004 ("atypical astronomical Text E"). Akkadian reading unclear (see discussion in Neugebauer & Sachs 1967, 213)
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
east cardinal direction

ina 30 MU.MEŠ 7.20 KI-šu₂ ana GIŠ.NIM DIB

‘In 30 years its (Saturn's) position passes 7;20 to the east.’

— BM 41004 rev. 15

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon

Brack-Bernsen & Hunger 2005/6, AfO 51, 96-107
