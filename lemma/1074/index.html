διανοητικὸς τόπος
dianoētikos topos

Greek noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 2:12:11 PM
Primary meaning place of intellect
Literal translation intellectual place
Variants
διανοητικὸς τόπος dianoētikos topos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
place of intellect astrological place

καὶ γὰρ ὁ κύριος τοῦ δαίμονος καὶ τοῦ διανοητικοῦ τόπου Ἑρμῆς ἑαυτῷ ἠναντιώθη· τουτέστι τοῖς Διδύμοις

‘For the lord of the (lot of the) Daimon and of the place of intellect, Mercury, was in opposition to itself, that is, in Gemini.’

— Vettius Valens 5.6.125

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 123,I)
