pꜣ 5 sw ꜥnḫ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:33:07 PM
Primary meaning planets
Literal translation the five living stars
Variants
pꜣ 5 sw ꜥnḫ
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
planets noun

sẖ ḥm-nṯr n spd.t ỉmy-r(ꜣ) mšꜥ n ỉꜥḥ wꜥb n p(ꜣ) 5 sbꜣ ꜥnḫ.w

‘Written by the prophet of Sothis, overseer of the moon's movement, and wrist of the five living stars.’

— Graf.Phil. 421 14

Genre: inscription
Provenance: Philae
Date: 3rd cent. CE

Griffith, F.Ll., Catalogue of the Demotic Graffiti of the Dodecaschoenus (Oxford, 1935 - 1937) 26-31
