σελασφόρος
selasphoros

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/4/2024, 11:07:39 AM
Primary meaning light-bearing
Literal translation light-bearing
Variants
σελασφόρος selasphoros
σελασφ selasph abbreviation
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
light-bearing an epithet of the moon(god), term of visual perception

[...] ἡ δὲ θεὰ καὶ σελασφ(όρος) . . ἦν] ἐν ζῳδίῳ θηλυκῷ δισώμῳ

‘The goddess and light-bearing (moon?) [was] in a feminine bicorporal sign’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4245 7-8

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 218 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 258-259 + II: 382-383

See also P. Lond. I 130 (NH 81)
See also

〈Σελήνη〉 Selēnē ‘Moon’
