ina ZALAG2
ina namāri

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/30/2023, 1:30:57 PM
Primary meaning last part of the night (in the second half of a month)
Literal translation at dawn
Variants
ina ZALAG2 ina namāri
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
last part of the night (in the second half of a month) time interval

A time interval attested in horoscopes and astronomical diaries. Mostly followed by a lunar position. The counterpart is SAG GE6.

GE₆ 23 ina ZALAG₂ 30 ar₂ AN 1 KUŠ₃ ana NIM GUB

‘Night of the 23rd, last part of the night, the moon stood 1 cubit behind Mars to the east.’

— BM 32887 obv. 15'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 142 BCE (-141)

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.
