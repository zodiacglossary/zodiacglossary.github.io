MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ ALLA ša₂ SI
kakkabu arkû ša allutti ša iltāni

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:07:41 PM
Primary meaning γ Cancri
Literal translation the rear star of the crab to the north
Variants
MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ ALLA ša₂ SI kakkabu arkû ša allutti ša iltāni
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
γ Cancri star: normal star

rarely used. Jones (2004): a5

[ina] ⸢ZALAG₂⸣ sin ar₂ MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ ALLA ša₂ SI 1 KUŠ₃

‘Last part of the ni]ght, Sin was 1 cubit behind the rear star of the crab to the north.’

— BM 45782 (ADRT II No. -166A) obv. 5

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 167 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 484-485
