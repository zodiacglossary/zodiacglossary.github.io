E ME
?

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 1/26/2024, 12:20:19 PM
Primary meaning acronychal rising
Literal translation rising (to) daylight
Variants
E ME ? Attested in procedure texts.
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
acronychal rising synodic phenomenon

(16') TA E ME ana UŠ ar₂-tu₂ (17') 13.15 KI GUR

‘(16') From rising (to) daylight to second station (17') the position turns back 13;15.’

— BM 34081+ (BMAPT No. 18) rev. ii 16'-17'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
