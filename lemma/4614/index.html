ὁράω
horaō

Greek verb

Last edited: 3/17/2025, 6:06:19 AM
Primary meaning to see
Literal translation to see
Variants
ὁράω horaō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to see astrological concept

ἐπ̣[ὶ] δὲ ζ̣ῳ̣δίων ἃ μέν ἐσ[τι]ν̣ ἀκ[ο]ύ̣ο̣ντα ἀλλήλων, ἃ δὲ ὁρῶντα ἄλληλα, ἃ δὲ αἰσθανόμενα ἀλλήλων

‘As for the zodiac-signs, some are hearers of each other, some are seers of each other, some are sensers of each other.’

— P.Mich. III 149 xii 11–13

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st-2nd century CE
