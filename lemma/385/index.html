παρανατολή
paranatolē

Greek noun

Last edited: 6/10/2024, 8:03:36 AM
Primary meaning adjacent rising
Literal translation rising
Variants
παρανατολή paranatolē
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
adjacent rising astronomical event

Διὸ τάς τε ἀνατολὰς καὶ παρανατολὰς τῆς κατὰ μῆκος κινήσεως καὶ τὰς ἀπὸ τῶν πρεσβυτέρων ἀποδιδομένας ἐκλύτους καὶ ῥᾳθύμους αἰτίας τῆς ὑπολείψεως λεγομένης παραιτεῖται.

‘For this reason he (i.e. Eudemus) rejects the risings and adjacent risings as (effects) of movement in longitude and the explanations rendered by the elders as weak and facile causes of the direct motion.’

— Theon of Smyrna, On Mathematics Useful for the Understanding of Plato 41

Genre: handbook
Date: 2nd century CE

Theon of Smyrna. 1892. Théon de Smyrne, philosophe platonicien. Exposition des connaissances utiles pour la lecture de Platon, traduite pour la première fois du grec en français, ed. J. Dupuis. Paris: Librairie Hachette. 324, lines 3-6
