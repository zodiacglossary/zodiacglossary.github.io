-
naparšudu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 11/9/2023, 2:37:41 PM
Primary meaning to escape
Literal translation to escape
Variants
i-par-šid ipparšid 3 sg. pret G
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to escape astronomical term

said of the Moon in relation to eclipse in one procedure text

(6) ḪAB-rat al MURUB₄ KASKAL.2 ⸢SIG?⸣ (7) ᵈsin i-par-šid-ma šal-šu₂ (8) ḪAB-rat sin al ⸢MURUB₄⸣

‘(6) The disk is 2 below the middle of the path, (7) the Moon escapes, and a third (8) of the disk the Moon [is above] the middle .’

— BM 35739 (BMAPT No. 99) side X ii' 6-8

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 400-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer 513
