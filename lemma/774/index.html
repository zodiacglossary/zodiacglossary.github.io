width mathematical expression

Used for the unequal widths of a trapezoid and other figures.

(4') DU₃.DU₃.BI ša₂ SAG.KI GU₄ SAG-⸢su⸣ [xx] (5') ana muḫ-ḫi a-ḫa-miš₂ TAB-ma ½-šu₂ GIŠ-⸢ma⸣

‘(4') Its procedure: the [...] width(s) of the trapezoid (5') you add (“accumulate”) together, you compute half of it, and ...’

— BM 34757 side Y 4'-5'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon?
Date: ca. 350-100 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2016, Ancient Babylonian astronomers calculated Jupiters position from the area under a time-velocity graph, Science, 351, 482-484
