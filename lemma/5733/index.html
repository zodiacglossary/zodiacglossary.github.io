τοῦ ὑπὸ τὸν ἡγούμενον Ἰχθὺν τετραπλεύρου τῶν βορείων βʹ ὁ ἡγούμενος
tou hupo ton hēgoumenon Ichthun tetrapleurou tōn boreiōn 2 ho hēgoumenos

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:20:23 AM
Primary meaning 27 Piscium
Literal translation The quadrilateral under the advance fish: the more advanced of the 2 northern stars
Variants

τοῦ ὑπὸ τὸν ἡγούμενον Ἰχθὺν τετραπλεύρου τῶν βορείων βʹ ὁ ἡγούμενος

‘The quadrilateral under the advance fish: the more advanced of the 2 northern stars [27 Piscium]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 8

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

Ptolemy. 1903. Claudii Ptolemaei opera quae exstant omnia volumen I, Syntaxis Mathematica Pars II, Libros VII-XIII continens, ed. J.L. Heiberg. Leipzig: B.G. Teubner. 84–129
