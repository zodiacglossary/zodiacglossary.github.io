KUR ša₂ NIM
napāḫu/nipḫu ša x

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/11/2023, 2:46:48 PM
Primary meaning Morning First
Literal translation eastern rising
Variants
KUR ša₂ NIM napāḫu/nipḫu ša x Akkadian reading of NIM uncertain (elî?)
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Morning First synodic phenomenon

[M]U ana MU ŠU₂ ša₂ ŠU₂ u KUR ša₂ NIM

‘Year by year, western setting and eastern rising.’

— BM 36321 (BMAPT No. 5) obv. 4

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
