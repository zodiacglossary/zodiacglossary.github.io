ME
ūmu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:28:41 PM
Primary meaning day
Literal translation day
Variants
ME ūmu
U₄ ūmu
u₄-mu ūmu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
day unit of time

30 ME ina IGI-at ŠU₂-šu₂ ša₂ ME 5 ZI-ma ŠU₂

‘30 days before its setting it (Saturn) moves (0);5 per day, then it sets.’

— AO 6477 (BMAPT No. 42) rev. 4

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 250-150 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer

mean tithi unit of time

1 "day" = 1/30 mean synodic month = mean tithi

daytime time interval

time from sunrise to sunset

time since sunrise time interval

ina 18 ME TA MAR ana KUR ZALAG₂

‘In 18 (UŠ) of daytime it (solar eclipse) cleared from west to east.’

— Rm 712 (ADRT II No. -241) obv.? 6'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 242 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 76-77
