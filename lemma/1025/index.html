chronocrator

Latin noun
calqued from Greek

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:33:45 AM
Primary meaning time lord
Literal translation time-ruler
Variants
chronocrator
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
time lord astrological concept

planet that rules a specific time period

Chronocratorem dixerunt Graeci temporum dominum;

‘The Greeks call the Ruler of Time Chronocrator’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.26.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.75
