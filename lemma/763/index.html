Παῦνι
Pauni

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 11:09:45 AM
Primary meaning Pauni (month X)
Literal translation Pauni
Variants
Παῦνι Pauni
Παοῖνι Paoini
Παῦν Paun abbreviation
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Pauni (month X) month, time interval

month X in the Alexandrian and Egyptian calendar

...] Παῦνι

‘...] Pauni’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4210 fr. 1, V: 11

Genre: mathematical astronomy: five-day almanac
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: after 302 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 224-225 + II: 308-311

Γένεσις Ἀπίας. (ἔτους) αʹ Κάρου καὶ Καρίνου καὶ Νουμεριανοῦ, Παοῖνι κεʹ εἰς κϛʹ, νοικτὸς ὅρᾳ δʹ.

‘Nativity of Apia. Year 1 of Karos and Karinos and Noumerianos, Pauni 25th until 26th, hour 4 of the night.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4258

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: after 283 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 267 + II: 396-397
