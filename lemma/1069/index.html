aquosus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 7:42:00 AM
Primary meaning watery
Literal translation watery
Variants
aquosus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
watery astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

the signs Cancer, Scorpius, Pisces

iu aquosis constitutus naufragio submergit;

‘in watery signs he perishes in a boat;’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 3.4.20

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.120

See also

〈trigonum martis〉 ‘Mars' triangle’
