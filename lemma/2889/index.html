Decan 2 of Taurus decan

δεκανῶι δευτέρωι καλουμένῳ Αρωθ

‘In the second decan (of Taurus), called Arōth’

— P.Lond. I 130 iv 89–91

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
