brachia cancri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/1/2023, 4:16:38 PM
Primary meaning the arms of the Crab
Literal translation arms of the crab
Variants
brachia cancri
cancri brachia
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the arms of the Crab animal part

16°–18° Cancer

tres sequentes in ambobus Cancri brachiis invenimus

‘we find the three following degrees in both claws of the Crab’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
