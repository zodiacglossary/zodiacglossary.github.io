πικός
pikos

Greek noun

Last edited: 9/12/2024, 1:12:31 PM
Primary meaning pointed
Literal translation pointed
Variants
πικός pikos rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
pointed mathematical property

πικόν· πικρόν. Χαλδαῖοι

‘pikon: pointed. Chaldeans.’

— Hesychius π 2278

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2005. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 3 (Pi - Sigma), ed. P.A. Hansen. Berlin, New York: Walter de Gruyter. 111

NB. The form is given by Hesychius in the accusative.
See also
