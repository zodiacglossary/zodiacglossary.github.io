δυσμικός
dusmikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/2/2024, 8:32:37 AM
Primary meaning western
Literal translation of the setting
Variants
δυσμικός dusmikos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
western cardinal direction, expression of relative position

ὁ δ(ιὰ) Ῥώμης μεσημβρινὸς δυσμικώτερός ἐσ- τι τοῦ δ(ι') Ἀλεξανδρείας μεσημβρινοῦ

‘the meridian through Rome is west of the meridian through Alexandria’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 Fr. 2, col. ii., 13-15

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 30
