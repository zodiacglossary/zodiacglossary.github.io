septentrionalis

Latin adjective

Last edited: 2/13/2024, 12:44:22 PM
Primary meaning northern
Literal translation belonging to the seven oxen
Variants
septentrionalis
septemtrionalis e.g. Sen. Q. N. 5.16.6
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
northern cardinal direction

In parte XX. Librae quicumque habuerint horoscopum, in septentrionali scilicet parte, erunt medici famosa artis nobilitate perspicui.

‘Whoever has the ascendant at 20° Libra, i. e. in the northern degree, will be a famous medic, famous for the excellence of their skill.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.25.7

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.334
