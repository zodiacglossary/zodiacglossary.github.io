vago

Latin verb

Last edited: 10/25/2024, 12:47:27 PM
Primary meaning to wander
Literal translation to wander
Variants
vago
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to wander planetary motion, verb of motion

caeloque rediret ac per descensum medios percurreret ignes Saturni, Iovis et Martis Solisque, sub illis post Venerem et Maia natum te, Luna, vagantem.

‘Here would another leave the celestial sphere and make his descent through the intervening fires of Saturn, Jupiter, Mars, and the Sun, and you, O Moon, beneath them, whose wandering path follows Venus and Maia’s son.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 5.4 ff

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 300-301
