the Arrow constellation

Sagitta

In signo Librae parte VIII. oritur Sagitta.

‘At 8° of the sign of Libra rises the Arrow.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.12.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.306
