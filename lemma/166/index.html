TAB
ṭīpu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 3/22/2024, 3:05:32 PM
Primary meaning addition
Literal translation addition
Variants
TAB ṭīpu
ṭi-pu ṭīpu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
addition mathematical quantity, additive quantity, arithmetical quantity

(8) 5 TAB ša₂ zib (9) 5 TAB ša₂ ḪUN bi-rit TAB ana TAB ia-a-nu

‘(8) The addition for Pisces is 5, (9) the addition for Aries is 5, the difference from addition to addition is nothing.’

— BM 36722 (BMAPT No. 52) rev. i 8-9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
