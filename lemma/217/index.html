IGI
nanmurtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 12/11/2024, 9:50:53 AM
Primary meaning first appearance
Literal translation appearance
Variants
IGI nanmurtu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
first appearance synodic phenomenon

said of planets and stars

IGI ana IGI TA 25 MAŠ.MAŠ EN 30 GIR₂.TAB 30 TAB

‘Appearance to appearance: from 25 Gemini until 30 Scorpio you add 30.’

— BM 34081+ (BMAPT No. 18) obv. i 8

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
