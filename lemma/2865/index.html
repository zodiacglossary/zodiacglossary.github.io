Σισιεμέ
Sisieme

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/6/2025, 6:10:46 AM
Primary meaning Decan 3 of Scorpio
Variants
Σισιεμέ Sisieme
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 3 of Scorpio decan

οἱ δὲ δεκανοὶ τρεῖς· ὁ πρῶτος Στωχνῆε, ὁ δὲ δεύτερος Σεσμέ, ὁ τρίτος Σισιεμέ

‘The decans are three: the first is Stōchnēne, the second Sesme, the third Sisieme’

— Hephaestion 1.1.147

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
