εἴδωλον
eidōlon

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/11/2025, 3:34:55 PM
Primary meaning constellation
Literal translation image
Variants
εἴδωλον eidōlon
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
constellation astral concept, celestial object

ἀστέρα τὸν ἐν τ̣ῆι Χ̣λαμύδι καλο̣ύ̣μ̣ενο̣ν Γανυμήδην ὁ̣μ̣ωνύμως τῶι ὅλωι εἰδώλωι

‘The star in the Cloak, called Ganymede, homonymous with the entire constellation’

— P.Lond. I 130 vi 132–137

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)

zodiac sign astral concept, spatial concept

εἰδώλοις δ’ ἐν ὑγροῖσι Κρόνον καὶ φοίνιον Ἄρην ξυνὴν ἐξανύοντας ὁδὸν Τιτὰν ὅτε λεύσσῃ

‘When the Titan (the Sun) sees (is in aspect with) Saturn and bloody Mars as they complete a common path in watery zodiac signs’

— Pseudo-Manetho, Apotelesmatika 6.362–363

Genre: astrological treatise
