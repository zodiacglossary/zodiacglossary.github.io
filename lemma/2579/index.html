χερσαῖος
chersaios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:39:22 AM
Primary meaning of dry land
Literal translation of dry land
Variants
χερσαῖος chersaios
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
of dry land zodiac sign quality

Κριός ἐστιν οἶκος Ἄρεως, ζῴδιον ἀρρενικόν, τροπικόν, χερσαῖον

‘Aries is a house of Mars, a masculine zodiac sign, tropical, of dry land’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.1

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
