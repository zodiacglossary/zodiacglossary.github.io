di-ru
dīru

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/17/2024, 7:20:28 AM
Primary meaning intercalary (month)
Literal translation intercalary (month)
Variants
di-ri dīru
di-ir-ri dīru
di-ir dīru
Edited by
Alessia Pilloni
intercalary (month) time interval

MU.AN.NA ki-in-ma di-ri-ša šu-ul-lim

‘Establish the (length of the) year and complete its intercalary (months).’

— K 2848 71

Genre: handbook
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: Neo-Assyrian

Oppenheim, A. L. 1974. A Babylonian Diviner’s Manual. Journal of Near Eastern Studies, 33(2), 197–220. 200
