constellatio

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 11:09:08 AM
Primary meaning conjunction
Literal translation constellation
Variants
constellatio
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
conjunction aspect

hapax in Firmicus Maternus

Sit iniquus, sit perfidus, sit malivolus, sit irreligiosus, quia haec vitia mala, Mercuri cum Marte perfecit constellatio!

‘If he is unjust, treacherous, spiteful, impious, it is because these vices are brought about by the conjunction of Mercury with Mars!’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.2.6

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.7
