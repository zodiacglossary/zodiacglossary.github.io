mul.UR.BAR.RA
barbaru

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 8/30/2023, 9:28:35 AM
Primary meaning Wolf-star
Literal translation Wolf-star
Variants
mul.UR.BAR.RA barbaru
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Wolf-star star

Approx. corresponding to α Trianguli.

DIŠ ⌈mul.UR⌉.BAR.RA ⌈giš.NINDA2 ša2⌉ mul.[API]N

‘¶ The Wolf, the seed-funnel of the Plough.’

— VAT 9429 i 2

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: Assur
Date: 8th-7th cent. BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 30
