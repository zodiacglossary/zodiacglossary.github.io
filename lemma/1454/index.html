testimonio sublevo

Latin verb

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:46:15 PM
Primary meaning to be in favorable aspect
Literal translation to raise with testimony
Variants
testimonio sublevo
sublevo testimonio
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to be in favorable aspect astrological concept

Omnia enim pro naturae suae potestate perficiunt, si nonagesimam partem aut malivolae stellae minaci radiatione respexerint, aut benivolae prospero testimonio sublevarint.

‘They indicate all according to the power of their own nature, either if malefics aspect the 90th degree unfavorably or if benefics aspect it favorably.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.2.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.285
