pergo

Latin verb

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:30:30 PM
Primary meaning to move
Literal translation to go
Variants
pergo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to move verb of motion

Per cetera vero signa pergens bonos actus bonas occupationes et bonae felicitatis decernit ornatum

‘Going through the other signs he indicates good deeds and occupations, fame and increase in income’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 5.3.23

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.37
