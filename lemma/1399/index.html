locus servorum

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:02:09 PM
Primary meaning lot of slaves
Literal translation place of slaves
Variants
locus servorum
servorum locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of slaves astrological lot

Si servorum locum partili volueris ratione colligere, a Mercurio semper conputabis Lunam, et tantundem ab horoscopo

‘If you want to find the lot of slaves by degree, you will always count from Mercury up to the Moon and [add] this amount to the ascendant’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.57

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.189

See also

〈ὁ περὶ δούλων τόπος〉 ho peri doulōn topos ‘the place concerning slaves’
