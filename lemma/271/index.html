EN.NUN.USAN
barārītu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 3/20/2024, 8:54:51 PM
Primary meaning Evening watch
Literal translation Evening watch

evening watch time interval

First of the three watches of the night.

mul2.UD.KA.DUḪ.A a-dir 30 ina EN.NUN.USAN AN.GE6 GAR-ma

‘The Panther is dark: the Moon makes an eclipse in the evening watch.’

— AO 6455 (TCL VI 11 = TU 11) obv. 1

Genre: collection of astrological and astronomical rules
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Brack-Bernsen, Lis, and Hunger, Hermann. 2002. TU 11. A Collection of Rules for the Prediction of Lunar Phases and of Month Lengths. SCIAMVS 3: 3–90. 7

GE6 9 USAN2 lu2.TUR GUD(?) x x

‘Night of the 9th, evening watch, the child ... .’

— BM 36796 (BH 15) 4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 2nd century BCE (Date of birth in horoscope: S.E. 109 XI.9 = -201 Feb.4)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88, 1–164. 97
