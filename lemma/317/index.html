ꜥ.wy (n) ṯsy

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/19/2023, 9:43:28 AM
Primary meaning exaltation
Literal translation house of lifting
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
exaltation astrological concept

wpy tꜣ ḳnḥy.t n pꜣ rꜥ ỉꜥḥ pꜣ 5 sw ꜥnḫ nꜣ ꜥ.wy.w nty mr[=w n-ỉm=w] ⌈nꜣy=w⌉ ꜥ.wy.w n ṯsy tꜣ ḳnḥy.t n pꜣ rꜥ pꜣ mꜣe pꜣy=f ꜥ.wy n ṯsy pꜣ ỉsw n s[wsw] 19

‘Dividing the dignities of the Sun, the Moon, and the five living stars; the horses that they prefer among them, their exaltations: the dignity of the Sun: Leo; its exaltation: Aries in 19°’

— P.Carlsberg 81

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

Winkler, A. 2016. Some Astrologers and their Handbooks in Demotic Egyptian. In The Circulation of Astronomical Knowledge in the Ancient World, ed. J. M. Steele. Leiden: Brill: ### 256
