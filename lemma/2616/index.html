ὡροσκοπέω
hōroskopeō

Greek verb

Last edited: 11/4/2024, 10:58:43 AM
Primary meaning to be in the ascendant
Literal translation to be in the ascendant
Variants
ὡροσκοπέω hōroskopeō
Edited by

to be in the ascendant astrological concept

ὡροσκοπεῖ Ζυγ[ός

‘Ascendant Libra’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4240 9

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 150 CE
