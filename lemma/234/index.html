ŠU₂ ša₂ KUR
rabû ša napāḫi/nipḫi

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:12:17 AM
Primary meaning Morning Last
Literal translation eastern setting
Variants
ŠU₂ ša₂ KUR rabû ša napāḫi/nipḫi
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Morning Last synodic phenomenon

for Mercury, Venus

(1) ŠU₂ ša₂ KUR a-na ŠU₂ ša₂ KUR (2) zib.ME ḪUN 3.45.20 TAB (3) MUL₂ MAŠ ALLA 3.29.40 TAB (4) A ABSIN 3.28.50 TAB (5) RIN₂ GIR₂.TAB PA 3.38.30 TAB (6) ⸢MAŠ₂ GU⸣ 3.35.10 TAB

‘(1) Eastern setting to eastern setting: (2) Pisces Aries you add 3,45,20; (3) Taurus Gemini Cancer you add 3,29,40, (4) Leo Virgo you add 3,28,50, (5) Libra Scorpio Sagittarius you add 3,38,30, (6) Capricorn, Aquarius you add 3,35,10.’

— BM 35495 (BMAPT No. 7) rev. vi 1-6

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 219-223
