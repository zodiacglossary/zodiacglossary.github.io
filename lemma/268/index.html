Tašrītu (month VII) month, month name, lunar month

iti.DU₆ ni-i-ru d.en-lil₂ šu-ba-a-tu u₂-tal₂-la-la

‘Month VII , the yoke, Enlil, the pedestals are being purified.’

— KAV 218 a ii 30-31

Genre: astrolabe
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: Neo-Assyrian

Casaburi, M. C. 2003. Tre-stelle-per-ciascun (-mese): l'Astrolabio B: edizione filologica. Annali/Supplemento 38

beginning noun
(a festival) noun

Solis cotidiani cursus annuique reditus

‘the daily and the annual path of the Sun’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.0.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.
