dorsum scorpii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:42:59 AM
Primary meaning the back of the Scorpion
Literal translation back of the scorpion
Variants
dorsum scorpii
scorpii dorsum
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the back of the Scorpion animal part

11°–12° Scorpio

has quae sequuntur duae ponuntur in dorso

‘the two following [degrees] are placed in the back [of the Scorpion]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.8

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
