mul.PA.BIL.SAG
Pabilsag

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/12/2023, 4:00:54 PM
Primary meaning Sagittarius
Literal translation Pabilsag
Variants
mul.PA.BIL.SAG Pabilsag
PA Pabilsag
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Sagittarius zodiac sign

(9') ina GIR₂ u PA 1 ⸢TAB⸣ (10') ša₂ al 30 PA DIRI (11') A.RA₂ 1.30 D[U]

‘In Scorpio and Sagittarius you add 1,(0), (the amount) by which it exceeds 30 Sagittarius you multiply by 1;30.’

— BM 33048 (BMAPT No. 43) Rev.(?) i' 9'-11'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
