aequans

Latin adjective

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:04:48 AM
Primary meaning equinoctial
Literal translation equating
Variants
aequans
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
equinoctial astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

the signs Aries and Libra

Proxima in effectum et similis referentia motus esse ferunt luces aequantia signa tenebris.

‘Close to these in their influence and displaying variation similar to each other are, they say, the signs which level the hours of light and darkness.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 3.644 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 214-215
