UŠ ar₂-tu₂
nēmettu arkītu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:39:45 PM
Primary meaning Second Station
Literal translation second station
Variants
UŠ ar₂-tu₂ nēmettu arkītu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Second Station synodic phenomenon

TA UŠ ar₂-tu₂ ana ŠU₂ 17.45 KI DU ŠU₂

‘From the second station to the setting it (Jupiter) proceeds (by) 17;45, (then) it sets.’

— BM 34081+ (BMAPT No. 18) obv. i 11

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
