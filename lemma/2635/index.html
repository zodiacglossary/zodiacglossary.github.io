ἀκριβής
akribēs

Greek adverb

Last edited: 12/12/2024, 2:50:41 PM
Primary meaning accurate
Literal translation accurate
Variants
ἀκριβής akribēs
ἀκρειβής akreibēs
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
accurate mathematical expression

(...) ψηφισθείς ἀκρει- [βῶ]ς εὑρέ[θη] ἐν ζῳδίῳ ἀρρενικῷ (...)

‘(...) being computed accurate- ly it was found in a masculine sign (...)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4276 ii, 5-6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd-3rd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 282-283 + II: 418-419
