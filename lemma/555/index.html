mul.AŠ.IKU
ikû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/25/2023, 2:40:20 PM
Primary meaning Field-star / Pisces
Literal translation Field-star
Variants
mul.AŠ.IKU ikû
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Field-star constellation

Approx. correspponding to α, β, γ Pegasi and α Andromedae.

DIŠ mul.AŠ.IKU šu-bat d.E2-a a-lik IGI MUL.MEŠ šu-ut d.A-nim

‘¶ The Field, the seat of Ea, who goes in front of the stars of Anu.’

— BM 86378 i 40

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 38

Pisces zodiac sign

A name for Pisces attested (mostly) in the Micro-zodiac series.
