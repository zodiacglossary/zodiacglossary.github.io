4 ša₂ mul.LU.LIM
4 ša lulīmu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:44:24 PM
Primary meaning 4 of the Stag star
Literal translation 4 of the Stag star
Variants
4 ša₂ mul.LU.LIM 4 ša lulīmu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
4 of the Stag star star: ziqpu star

α, β, γ, δ Cassiopeiae

[...] ⸢MA⸣.NA MIN 1/2 DANNA i-na KI.MIN 27 lim DANNA [...] | TA 4 ša₂ mul.LU.LIM EN MUL um-mu-lu-[ti]

‘[...] minas ditto (weight) 1/2 bēru on ditto (the ground) 27 thousand bēru [...], | from the 4 (stars) of the Stag to the Dusky Stars.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) obv. 27'-28'

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 218
