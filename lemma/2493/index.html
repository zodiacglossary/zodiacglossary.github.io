ales

Latin noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 9:01:35 AM
Primary meaning the Swan
Literal translation bird
Variants
ales
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Swan constellation

modern Cygnus

et Iuppiter alite tectus

‘and Jupiter in swan’s disguise’

— Manilius, Astronomica 5.25

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 302-303
