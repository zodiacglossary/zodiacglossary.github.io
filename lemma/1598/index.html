pedes extremi cancri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:28:12 PM
Primary meaning the outer legs of the Crab
Literal translation outer legs of the Crab
Variants
pedes extremi cancri
cancri pedes extremi
extremi pedes cancri
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the outer legs of the Crab animal part

28°–29° Cancer

in extremis vero pedibus XXVIII. et XXIX. sunt,

‘in the outer(?) legs [of the Crab] are [the degrees] 28 and 29’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
