νεομηνία
neomēnia

Greek noun

Last edited: 8/18/2025, 7:21:43 AM
Primary meaning first day of the month
Literal translation new moon
Variants
νεομηνία neomēnia
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
beginning of the month point in time

κατ’ ἀρχαίους δὲ Παχων νεομηνίᾳ εἰς τὴν δευτέραν

‘According to (the calendar of) the ancients, (the nativity was) in Pachon on the first day of the month, (the night leading) into the second’

— P.Lond. I 130 45–48

Genre: horoscope
Date: 81 CE

(GH 81)
