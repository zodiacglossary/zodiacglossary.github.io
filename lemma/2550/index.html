כִּסְלֵו
kislēw

Hebrew noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:47:38 PM
Primary meaning Kislew (month IX)
Variants
כִּסְלֵו
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Kislew (month IX) month name, time interval, month

דִּבְרֵ֥י נְחֶמְיָ֖ה בֶּן־חֲכַלְיָ֑ה וַיְהִ֤י בְחֹֽדֶשׁ־כִּסְלֵו֙ שְׁנַ֣ת עֶשְׂרִ֔ים וַאֲנִ֥י הָיִ֖יתִי בְּשׁוּשַׁ֥ן הַבִּירָֽה׃

dib̲rê nəḥemyāh ben-ḥăk̲alyāh wayhî b̲əḥōd̲eš-kislēw šənat̲ ʿeśrîm waʾănî hāyît̲î bəšûšan habbîrāh.

‘The narrative of Nehemiah son of Hacaliah: In the month of Kislev of the twentieth year, when I was in the fortress of Shushan,’

— Nehemiah 1 1

Genre: religious text
