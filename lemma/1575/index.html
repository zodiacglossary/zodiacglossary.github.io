natura tauri

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/1/2024, 7:50:32 AM
Primary meaning the genitals of the Bull
Literal translation nature of the bull
Variants
natura tauri
tauri natura
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the genitals of the Bull animal part

26°–27° Taurus

XXVI. vero et XXVII. in natura sunt Tauri

‘[the degrees] 26 and 27 however are in the genitals of the Bull’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
