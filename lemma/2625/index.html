κατ’ Αἰγυπτίων
kat’ Aiguptiōn

Greek adverb

Last edited: 2/26/2025, 1:39:04 PM
Primary meaning according to the Egyptians
Literal translation according to the Egyptians
Variants
κατὰ τοὺς Αἰγυπτίους kata tous Aiguptious
κατ’ Αἰγυπτίων kat’ Aiguptiōn P. Oxy. LXI 4251
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
according to the Egyptians calendrical concept

in the measuring of hours

κατὰ τοὺς Αἰ[γυπτίους ὥρᾳ γʹ νυκ[τός.

‘according to the Egyptians ... hour 3 of night.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4249 3-4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 172 (?) CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 262-263 + II: 386-387

See also

〈καθ’ Ἑλήνου〉 kath’ Helēnou ‘according to the Greeks’
