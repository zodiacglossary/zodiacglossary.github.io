locus peregrinationis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:01:58 PM
Primary meaning lot of sojourn
Literal translation place of sojourn
Variants
locus peregrinationis
peregrinationis locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of sojourn astrological lot

Si peregrinationis locum partiliter volueris invenire, a Sole semper conputabis ad Martem

‘If you want to find the lot of sojourn by degrees, count from the Sun up to Mars’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.49

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.188
