splendor

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:43:43 PM
Primary meaning brightness
Literal translation brightness
Variants
splendor
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
brightness noun

Quinta pars Leonis habet stellam lucido splendore fulgentem.

‘The fifth degree of Leo has a star shining with brightness full of light.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.2.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.69
