sw nfr

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:43:57 PM
Primary meaning benefic planet
Literal translation good star
Variants
ⲥⲟⲩⲛⲟⲩϥⲉ Old Coptic
sw nfr
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
benefic planet astrological concept

Describes the inherent quality of a planet

⌈wpy⌉ nꜣ sw.w bꜣn.w | ỉrm nꜣ sw.w nfr.w ḥr-pꜣ-kꜣ ḥr-tšy pꜣ-rꜥ nꜣ sw.w bꜣn.w nꜣ.w ḥr-pꜣ-šty nṯr-tꜣy sbkꜣ nꜣ sw.w nfr.w nꜣ.w

‘Listing the maleficent stars | and the beneficent stars: Saturn, Mars, and the Sun are the maleficent stars; Jupiter, Venus, and Mercury are the beneficent stars.’

— P.Carlsberg 81

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

A. Winkler, 2016, Some Astrologers and Their Handbooks in Demotic Egyptian, in J.M. Steele (ed.), The Circulation of Astronomical Knowledge in the Ancient World (Leiden: Brill), 245-286 258
