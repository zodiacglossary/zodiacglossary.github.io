τοῦ ἐν τῷ γʹ σπονδύλῳ διπλοῦ ὁ βόρειος
tou en tōi 3 spondulōi diplou ho boreios

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:20:55 AM
Primary meaning ζ¹ Scorpionis
Literal translation The northern star of the double-star in the 3rd joint
Variants
