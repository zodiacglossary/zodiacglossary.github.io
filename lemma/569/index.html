τριζῳδία
trizōidia

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/9/2023, 7:32:51 PM
Primary meaning group of three zodiac signs
Literal translation triple figure
Variants
τριζῳδία trizōidia
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
group of three zodiac signs astrological concept

ἐν ταύτῃ δὲ τῇ τριζῳδίᾳ ἀπαρτίζεται ἡ τῆς ἐαρινῆς ὥρας τροπή, ἥτις καλεῖται ἀήρ

‘In this group of three zodiac signs (Aries, Taurus, Gemini) the turning of springtime is completed, and it is called “Air”’

— Paul of Alexandria 2 (p. 4 Boer)

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 4th cent. CE
See also

〈ζῴδιον〉 zōidion ‘zodiac sign’
