προσνεύω
prosneuō

Greek verb

Last edited: 7/12/2023, 11:05:58 AM
Primary meaning to incline towards
Literal translation to bend forward
Variants
προσνεύω prosneuō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to bend forward verb
to incline toward verb

προσνεύων λέγεται ὁ ἀκολουθῶν τῷ προερχομένῳ

‘The (planet) following is said to be “inclining towards” the (planet) preceding it.’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?

CCAG VIII.4 226
