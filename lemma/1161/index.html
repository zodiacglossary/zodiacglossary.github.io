mitigo

Latin verb

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:21:10 PM
Primary meaning to mitigate
Literal translation to mitigate
Variants
mitigo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to mitigate verb, astrological concept

lessen an astrological indication

<si> enim Iuppiter et Venus istas omnes coniunctiones vel defluctiones, per quas misera hominibus decernuntur exitia, bona radiatione respexerint, decretorum malorum <et> miserae calamitatis exitia mitigantur.

‘for if Jupiter and Venus aspect all those configurations and separations well which indicate miserable outcomes for natives, the outcome of miserable catastrophe is lessened’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.16.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.234
