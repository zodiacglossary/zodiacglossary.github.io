bright adjective

Claras stellas et augusta maiestatis radiatione fulgentes in signis omnibus invenimus, sed regales in quattuor, in Leone scilicet in Scorpione in Aquario et in Tauro.

‘Bright stars and ones which glitter with majestic grandeur we find in every sign, but kingly [stars] only in four [signs]: in Leo as in Scorpio, in Aquarius and in Taurus’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.2.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.69
