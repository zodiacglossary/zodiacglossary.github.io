τῆς νοτίου πλευρᾶς ὁ προηγούμενος
tēs notiou pleuras ho proēgoumenos

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:21:10 AM
Primary meaning 59 Sagittarii
Literal translation The advance star on the southern side
Variants

59 Sagittarii star

τῆς νοτίου πλευρᾶς ὁ προηγούμενος

‘The advance star on the southern side [59 Sagittarii]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 8

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

Ptolemy. 1903. Claudii Ptolemaei opera quae exstant omnia volumen I, Syntaxis Mathematica Pars II, Libros VII-XIII continens, ed. J.L. Heiberg. Leipzig: B.G. Teubner. 84–129

30 Piscium star

τῆς νοτίου πλευρᾶς ὁ προηγούμενος

‘The more advanced star on the southern side [30 Piscium]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 8

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

Ptolemy. 1903. Claudii Ptolemaei opera quae exstant omnia volumen I, Syntaxis Mathematica Pars II, Libros VII-XIII continens, ed. J.L. Heiberg. Leipzig: B.G. Teubner. 84–129
