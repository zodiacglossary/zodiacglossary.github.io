ỉḫy.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:42:21 AM
Primary meaning Libra
Literal translation horizon
Variants
ꜣḫ.t
ỉḫy.t
ỉḫe
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Libra zodiac sign

ꜣbd-4 šmw tꜣ ỉḫy

‘Mesore: Libra’

— O.Strasb.Dem. 521 10

Genre: astronomical list
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 300 BCE - 300 CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 121-122 121

Horizon zodiac sign

mh̭y tꜣ.wy rn⸗f ⌈ḏd⌉ tꜣ ỉh̭e tꜣ.wy ḏr⸗w tꜣy

‘The balance of the Two Lands is his name because it is the horizon of every land’

— P.Berol. 13603 iv 21

Genre: theological treatise
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 1st cent. BCE

W. Erichsen - S. Schott, Fragmente memphitischer Theologie in demotischer Schrift (Pap. demot. Berlin 13603), Abhandlungen der Akademie der Wissenschaften und der Literatur Mainz, Geistes- und sozialwiss. Kl., Jg. 1954, Nr. 7, Wiesbaden 1954
