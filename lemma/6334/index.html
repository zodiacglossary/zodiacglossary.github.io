ὀψέ
opse

Greek adverb

Last edited: 8/18/2025, 7:12:56 AM
Primary meaning late
Literal translation late
Variants
ὀψέ opse
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
late adverb of time

Τυβι ὀψὲ εἰς ιβʹ

‘(Nativity…) in (the month of) Tybi, late (in the day) on the 12th’

— PSI I 22a 4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Antinoopolis
Date: 338 CE
