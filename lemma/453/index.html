MUL₂ IGI ša₂ ALLA ša₂ ULU₃
kakkabu maḫrû ša allutti ša šūti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:18:48 PM
Primary meaning θ Cancri
Literal translation the front star of the crab to the south
Variants
MUL₂ IGI ša₂ ALLA ša₂ ULU₃ kakkabu maḫrû ša allutti ša šūti
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
θ Cancri star: normal star

Jones(2004) a3. Rarely used

(10') [GE₆ 23] (11') ina ZALAG₂ sin ina IGI MUL₂ IGI ša₂ ALLA ša₂ ⸢ULU₃⸣ 1 KUŠ₃

‘(10') [Night of (day) 23], last part of the night, Sin was 1 cubit in front of the front star of the crab to the south.’

— BM 32843 (ADRT III No. -133A) obv. 10'-11'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 134 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 198-199
