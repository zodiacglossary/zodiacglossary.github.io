ꜥ.wy mwt

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/19/2023, 9:37:56 AM
Primary meaning House of death
Literal translation House of death
Variants
ꜥ.wy (n) sḥn-mwt In Thebes
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
House of death astrological house

7th astrological place

pꜣy-ms=w r pꜣ-nṯr-dwꜣ n ꜥ.wy n mwt ỉw=f ꜥšꜣ ...

‘He who was born when Venus was in the house of death, he will be plentiful of ...’

— P.Berlin 8345 ii 10

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: 1st cent. CE to 2nd cent. CE

Quack, J. F., 2008, Texte aus Ägypten: Demotische, magische und divinatorische Texte. In B. Janowski and B. Wilhelm (eds), Omina, Orakel, Rituale und Beschwörungen. Texte aus der Umwelt des Alten Testaments, Neue Folge 4. Gütersloh: Gütersloher Verlagshaus.

ꜥ.wy (n) sḥn-mwt pꜣ kꜣ ḥr-tšy

‘House of provisions of death: Taurus and Mars’

— O.Neugeb. 3 19

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117
