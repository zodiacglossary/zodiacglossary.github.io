γεωργικός
geōrgikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:07:00 AM
Primary meaning agricultural
Literal translation agricultural
Variants
γεωργικός geōrgikos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
agricultural zodiac sign quality

ἔστι δὲ τὸ ζῴδιον (…) γεωργικόν

‘The zodiac sign (Taurus) is (…) agricultural’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.14

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
