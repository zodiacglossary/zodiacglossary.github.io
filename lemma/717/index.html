rnp.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:36:10 PM
Primary meaning year
Literal translation year
Variants
rnp.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
year calendrical concept

tꜣy⸗s rnp.t mḥ 19 rnp.t n ꜥb tꜣy n ḥꜣ.ṱ⸗s pḥ.ṱ⸗s ỉr=s nmy ỉ.ỉr-ḥr [...

‘Her 19th year: It is a year of miasma at the beginning and the end. She will be a flaming star before [...’

— P.Carlsberg XI 22 5

Genre: celestial divination
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 1st to 2nd century CE

Quack, J.F, 2019, Prognose über Desaster-Jahre, in K. Ryholt and J.F. Quack, eds, Demotic Literary Texts from Tebtunis and Beyond; Copenhagen, 273-281. 279
