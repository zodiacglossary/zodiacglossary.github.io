Point of quadruplicity astrological concept

Point of quadruplicity; listed as middle, western, and eastern swšp

rꜥ-ḫꜥ ỉḫy.t | rꜥ-ḥtp ỉsw | šy p.t pꜣ gnḥḏ | šy dwꜣ.t pꜣ ḥr-ꜥnḫ ḥr-pꜣ-kꜣ | pꜣ swšp n mtr [nꜣ] ḥtr.w | pꜣ swšp ỉꜣbṱ nꜣ (?) tbt.w| pꜣ swšp ỉmnṱ tꜣ rpy.t

‘Ascendant: Libra | Descendant: Aries | Lake of the Sky: Cancer | Lake of the Netherworld: Capricorn |the middle swšp: Gemini | the eastern swšp: pisces | the western swšp: Virgo’

— O.Neugeb. 3 4-10

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117
