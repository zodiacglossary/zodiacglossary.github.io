MURUB4 GE6
qabal mūši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:25:37 PM
Primary meaning middle watch of the night
Literal translation middle of the night
Variants
MURUB4 GE6 qabal mūši
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
middle watch of the night time interval

AB 1 GE6 9 MURUB4 G[E6] |lu2.TUR a-l[id]

‘Month X 1, night of the 9th, middle of nig[ht,] | the child was bo[rn].’

— BM 34003 (LBAT 1470) (BH 23) 2-3

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 1st century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: S.E. 223 X.9 = -87 Jan.5)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 127

See also

〈EN.NUN.MURUB4.BA〉 qablītu ‘Middle watch of the night’
