MAŠ.MAŠ ša₂ SIPA
māšu ša rē'i

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:26:00 PM
Primary meaning γ Geminorum
Literal translation the twins’ star of the shepherd
Variants
MAŠ.MAŠ ša₂ SIPA māšu ša rē'i
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
γ Geminorum star: normal star

Jones (2004) c10

(18) ⸢GE₆⸣ [6] (19) SAG GE₆ sin e MAŠ.MAŠ ša₂ SIPA 20 SI

‘(18) Ni[ght of (day) 6,] (19) beginning of the night, Sin was 20 fingers above the twins’ star of the shepherd.’

— BM 46229 (ADRT I No. -346) rev. 18-19

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 347 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.
