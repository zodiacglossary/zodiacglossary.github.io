ἀπόκλισις
apoklisis

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/9/2023, 5:36:55 PM
Primary meaning declining place
Literal translation decline
Variants
ἀπόκλισις apoklisis
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
declining place astrological concept

καὶ γὰρ ὁ κλῆρος ἐν ἀποκλίσει εὑρέθη καὶ ὁ κύριος τοῦ τριγώνου τῆς Σελήνης Ἄρης

‘For the lot (of Fortune) was found in a declining place, and the lord of the triangle of the Moon was Ares.’

— Vettius Valens 4.8.22

Genre: horoscope
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 75)
