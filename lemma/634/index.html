pr.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:31:40 PM
Primary meaning Peret season
Literal translation Season of coming forth
Variants
pr.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Peret season calendrical concept

συμπαθεῖς εἰσιν οἱ κατ’ ἴσον ἀπέχοντες κατ’ ἐπιθεώρησιν ἀλλήλων σύμφωνοι· ἀσυμπαθεῖς δὲ οἱ κατὰ μηδένα τρόπον ἐπιθεωροῦντες ἀλλήλους· ὅπερ γίνεται ἐκ τῆς τῶν ἀσυνδέτων ζῳδίων ἀποστροφίας· ἔστιν δὲ ἀσύνδετα τὰ παρ’ ἑκάτερα καὶ τὰ διὰ ϛʹ καὶ ηʹ ζῳδίων ἀριθμούμενα

‘Sympathetic (planets) are those that are an equal distance apart and harmonious in their aspect with each other, and unsympathetic are those that are not in any kind of aspect with each other, which results from the aversion of unconnected zodiac signs. Unconnected (zodiac signs) are those that are counted one after another and six and eight apart.’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?

CCAG VIII.4 226

ỉbd-2 pr.t pꜣ ỉsw ỉbd-3 pr.t pꜣ kꜣ

‘Month 2 of the Peret season: Aries; Month 3 of the Peret season: Taurus’

— O.Strasb.Dem. 521 7

Genre: astronomical list
Provenance: Thebes (?)
Date: 300 BCE - 300 CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 121-122 121
