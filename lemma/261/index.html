ꜥ.wy wry.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 6:48:39 PM
Primary meaning House of misfortune
Literal translation House of the great one
Variants
wly.t Fayyumic
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
House of misfortune astrological house

6th astrological place

pꜣy-ms=w r pꜣ nṯr-t(w)ꜣ n ꜥ.wy n wly.t | syt bꜣn ꜥšꜣy r ḫpr n=f

‘He who was born when Venus was in the House of misfortune, much ill fame will happen to him’

— P.Berlin 8345 ii 1-2

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: 1st cent. CE to 2nd cent. CE

Quack, J. F., 2008, Texte aus Ägypten: Demotische, magische und divinatorische Texte. In B. Janowski and B. Wilhelm (eds), Omina, Orakel, Rituale und Beschwörungen. Texte aus der Umwelt des Alten Testaments, Neue Folge 4. Gütersloh: Gütersloher Verlagshaus.
