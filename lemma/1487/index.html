ofiuchus

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 10/7/2025, 8:29:32 AM
Primary meaning the Serpent-holder
Literal translation serpent-holder
Variants
ofiuchus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Serpent-holder constellation

In primis partibus Capricorni oritur Ofiuchus.

‘In the first degrees of Capricorn rises the Serpent-holder.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.15.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.309
