προστάσσων
prostassōn

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 11:58:22 AM
Primary meaning commanding
Literal translation commanding
Variants
προστάσσων prostassōn
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
commanding zodiac sign quality

τοῦτο δὴ τὸ κατὰ τὸν Κριὸν δωδεκατημόριον (…) προσαγορεύουσιν (…) προστάσσον, φωνῆεν (…)

‘This twelfth-part at Aries (…) they call (…) commanding, vocal (…)’

— Hephaestion 1.1.3

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
See also
