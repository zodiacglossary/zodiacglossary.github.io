θέρος
theros

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 2:56:44 PM
Primary meaning summer
Literal translation summer
Variants
θέρος theros
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
summer calendrical concept, season

Διαιρεῖται δὲ ὁ ἐνιαύσιος χρόνος εἰς μέρη δʹ, ἔαρ θέρος φθινόπωρον καὶ χειμῶνα.

‘The time of a year is divided into 4 parts: spring, summer, autumn and winter.’

— Geminus 1.9

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 2
