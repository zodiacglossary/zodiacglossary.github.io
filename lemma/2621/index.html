προπορεύομαι
proporeuomai

Greek verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:02:12 AM
Primary meaning to move ahead
Literal translation to travel ahead
Variants
προπορεύομαι proporeuomai
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to move ahead verb of motion

[...] προπορε[υό]μενος τ[οῦ Κ]ρόνου

‘advancing ahead of Saturn’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4245 27

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 218 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 258-259 + II: 382-383
