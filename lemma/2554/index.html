טֵבֵת
ṭēb̲ēt̲

Hebrew noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 12/2/2024, 8:35:34 AM
Primary meaning Ṭebētu
Variants
טֵבֵת ṭēb̲ēt̲
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Ṭēb̲ēt̲ (month X) month name

וַתִּלָּקַ֨ח אֶסְתֵּ֜ר אֶל־הַמֶּ֤לֶךְ אֲחַשְׁוֵרוֹשׁ֙ אֶל־בֵּ֣ית מַלְכוּת֔וֹ בַּחֹ֥דֶשׁ הָעֲשִׂירִ֖י הוּא־חֹ֣דֶשׁ טֵבֵ֑ת בִּשְׁנַת־שֶׁ֖בַע לְמַלְכוּתֽוֹ׃

wattillāqaḥ ʾestēr ʾel-hammelek̲ ʾăḥašwērôš ʾel-bêt̲ malk̲ût̲ô baḥōd̲eš hāʿăśîrî hûʾ-ḥōd̲eš ṭēb̲ēt̲ bišnat̲-šeb̲aʿ ləmalk̲ût̲ô.

‘Esther was taken to King Ahasuerus, in his royal palace, in the tenth month, which is the month of Tebeth, in the seventh year of his reign.’

— Esther 2 16

Genre: religious text
