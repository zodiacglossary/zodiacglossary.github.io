templum

Latin noun

Last edited: 10/21/2024, 7:54:35 AM
Primary meaning astrological place
Literal translation temple
Variants
templum
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
astrological place astrological concept

chiefly Manilius

tali sub sorte notandae templorum tibi sunt vires: quae pervolat omnis astrorum series

‘This is the system by which you must mark the powers of the temples: through them revolves the entire procession of the zodiac’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.959 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 158-159
