adhaeresco

Latin verb

Last edited: 12/2/2024, 9:06:25 AM
Primary meaning to approach?
Literal translation to grow attached to
Variants
adhaeresco
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to approach? verb of motion

Si [...] Saturnus <vero> et Mars ita sint positi ut unus Solis alter Lunae radiis adhaerescat [...]

‘However, if Saturn and Mars are placed such that either one comes into aspect with the Sun, the other with the Moon [...]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 7.2.18

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.215

See also

〈applico〉 ‘to move into conjunction’

〈ἐπισεύω〉 episeuō ‘to approach’
