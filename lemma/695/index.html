Great-Twins-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to α and β Geminorum.

DIŠ mul.⌈MAŠ.TAB.BA⌉.GAL.GAL d.Lugal-ir9-ra d.Mes-lam-ta-⌈e3-a⌉

‘¶ The Great Twins, Lugalirra and Meslamtaea.’

— VAT 9429 i 4

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: Assur
Date: 8th-7th cent. BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 30

Twins star star: ziqpu star

α and β Geminorum

[...] 1 DANNA i-na KI.MIN 54 lim DANNA ina KI.MIN | TA mul.KIŠIB-GAM₃ EN mul.MAŠ.TAB.BA

‘[...] 1 bēru on ditto (the ground), 18 thousand bēru in ditto (heaven), | from the Crook's Handle to the Twins.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) rev. 5-6

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 219
