σπόριμος
sporimos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/19/2024, 3:54:19 PM
Primary meaning of conception
Literal translation of conception
Variants
σπόριμος sporimos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
of conception astrological concept

καὶ πρὸς τὸ κλίματος τ[ῆς] Αἰγύπτου καὶ κατὰ σύμφωνον τοῦ [σπ]ορίμου τόπου ψηφισθεὶς ἀκρει[βῶ]ς

‘and for the klima of Egypt and in accordance with the place of conception accurately computed’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4276 col. ii, l. 3-6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd-3rd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 282-283 + II: 418-419

Reading [σπ]ορίμου by Zellmann-Rohrer (forthcoming). Jones (1999) reads [γν]ορίμου.
See also

〈sol conceptionalis〉 ‘the Sun at the time of conception’
