ἐπιδέκατον
epidekaton

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/11/2023, 8:46:08 AM
Primary meaning right quartile aspect
Literal translation tenth part
Variants
ἐπιδέκατον epidekaton
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
ratio of 11/10 common noun
tenth part common noun
right quartile aspect

ἐπιδέκατον λέγεται τὸ δεξιὸν τετράγωνον

‘“Tenth part” is called the right quadrangle’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
Date: 1st cent. BCE/CE?
