Choiak (month IV) month, time interval

month IV in the Alexandrian and Egyptian calendar

...] (ἔτος) β | Χοίακ | κ κ[...

‘...] year 2 | Choiak | 20 2[x ...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4160 38

Genre: mathematical astronomy: epoch table
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: after 79 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 145-148 + II: 88-91
