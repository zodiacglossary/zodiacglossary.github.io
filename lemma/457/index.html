4.AM₃ ar₂ ša₂ PA ša₂ ana ZA maš-lu
4 arkûtu ša Pabilsag ša ana ZA mašlū

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:04:50 PM
Primary meaning four stars in Sagittarius
Literal translation the four rear ones of Pabilsag which are similar to the sign ZA
Variants
4.AM₃ ar₂ ša₂ PA ša₂ ana ZA maš-lu 4 arkûtu ša Pabilsag ša ana ZA mašlū
4.AM₃ ar₂.MEŠ ša₂ PA ša₂ ana ZA maš-lu 4 arkûtu ša Pabilsag ša ana ZA mašlū
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
four stars in Sagittarius star: normal star

Jones (2004) a9. Rarely used; perhaps = σ , φ, ζ , and τ Sagittarii (Jones 2004)

[ GENNA ...] KUŠ₃ ar₂ 4.AM₃ ar₂.MEŠ ša₂ PA ša₂ ana ZA maš-lu UŠ

‘[Saturn] became stationary [...] cubits behind the four rear ones of Pabilsag which are similar to the ZA sign.’

— BM 45617+ (ADRT II -190B) rev. 16'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 191 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 310-311
