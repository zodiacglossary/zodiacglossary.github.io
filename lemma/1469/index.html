lepus

Latin noun

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 8:40:44 AM
Primary meaning the Hare
Literal translation hare
Variants
lepus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Hare constellation

modern Lepus

In Geminorum parte VII. oritur Lepus.

‘At 7° Gemini rises the Hare.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.8.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.301
