Σιόαν
Sioan

Greek noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:59:59 PM
Primary meaning Panemos
Literal translation Panemos
Variants
Σιόαν Sioan rare
Σιούαν Siouan rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Panemos month, time interval

month IX in the Macedonian calendar

Σιόαν· οὕτω καλοῦσι Χαλδαῖοι τὸν Πάνημον μῆνα.

‘Sioan: that is how the Chaldeans call the month Panemos.’

— Hesychius σ 701

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2005. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 3 (Pi - Sigma), ed. P.A. Hansen. Berlin, New York: Walter de Gruyter. 294
