puppis

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/31/2025, 2:29:39 PM
Primary meaning poop-deck
Literal translation poop-deck
Variants
puppis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
poop-deck part of constellation

of Argo (modern Puppis)

nunc quoque vicinam puppi, ceu naviget, Argo a dextri lateris ducit regione per astra.

‘even now draws Argo by the poop to its side, as though still on the seas, through the stars on its right.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 5.36 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 302-303
