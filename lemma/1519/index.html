mul.4 ša₂ GABA mul.UR.A
4 kakkabū ša irat nēši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:43:58 PM
Primary meaning 4 stars of the breast of the Lion star
Literal translation 4 stars of the breast of the Lion star
Variants
mul.4 ša₂ GABA mul.UR.A 4 kakkabū ša irat nēši
mul.4 ša₂ GABA-šu₂ 4 kakkabū ša irtišu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
4 stars of the breast of the Lion star star: ziqpu star

α, γ, ζ, η Leonis

1 2/3 MA.NA MIN 10 UŠ i-na KI.MIN 18 lim DANNA ina KI.MIN | TA 2 MUL.MEŠ ša₂ SAG.DU mul.UR.GU.LA EN MUL 4 ša₂ GABA-šu₂

‘1 2/3 minas ditto (weight), 10 UŠ on ditto (the ground), 18 thousand bēru in ditto (heaven), | from the 2 stars of the head of the Lion to the 4 stars of its (the Lion's) breast.’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) rev. 13-14

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 219
