amicus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:02:15 AM
Primary meaning friendly
Literal translation friendly
Variants
amicus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
friendly astrological concept

crebrius adversis odium est, cognata quadratis corpora censentur signis et amica trigonis.

‘Between opposite signs exists mostly hatred; and to quadrate signs are mostly ascribed ties of kinship, ties of friendship to trigonal signs.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.652 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 132-133
