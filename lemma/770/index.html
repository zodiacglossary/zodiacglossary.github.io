KI GIR3.II
qaqqar šēpē

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 5/5/2025, 3:59:48 PM
Primary meaning region of the feet
Literal translation region of the feet
Variants
KI GIR3.II qaqqar šēpē
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
region of the feet astrological concept

The third of three regions of a zodiacal sign.

⸢KI mul.GU⸣.[LA] | KI SAG-šu2 30 [MU] |KI MURUB4-šu2 ⸢15⸣ [MU?] | KI GIR3.II-šu2 7 MU 6 [ITI]

‘Region of Aqua[rius]. | Region of its head: 30 [years]. | Region of its middle: 15 [years?]. | Region of its feet: 7 years, 6 [months].’

— BM 32339+ rev. vi'-vii' 1'-4'

Genre: zodiacal astrology
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 400-300 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2018. BM 32339+32407+32645: New Evidence for Late Babylonian Astrology. In: Mesopotamian Medicine and Magic. Studies in Honor of Markham J. Geller, eds. Strahil V. Panayotov and Ludêk Vacín. Leiden and Boston: Brill: 401-420. 406
