ἑῷα ἀνατολή
heōia anatolē

Greek noun

Last edited: 1/23/2024, 1:22:36 PM
Primary meaning morning rising
Literal translation morning rising
Variants
ἑῷα ἀνατολή heōia anatolē
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
morning rising astronomical event

Ἀφροδείτης ἐν Τοξότῃ μοι[ρ]ῶν θʹ λεπτο̑ν νδʹ· ἑῷα ἀνατολάς

‘Venus in Sagittarius at 9 degrees, 54 minutes—morning rising’

— P.Paris 19 20–21

Genre: horoscope

(GH 137a)

See also

〈ἑῷα φάσις〉 heōia phasis ‘morning visibility’
