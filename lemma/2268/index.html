ἀστρολόγος
astrologos

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:08:12 AM
Primary meaning astronomer
Literal translation astronomer
Variants
ἀστρολόγος astrologos
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
astronomer practitioner

Ἀλλὰ μὴ γεωμέτρης ἐπιθυμεῖς, ἔφη, γενέσθαι ἀγαθός, ὥσπερ ὁ Θεόδωρος; Οὐδὲ γεωμέτρης, ἔφη. Ἀλλὰ μὴ ἀστρολόγος, ἔφη, βούλει γενέσθαι; ὡς δὲ καὶ τοῦτο ἠρνεῖτο, Ἀλλὰ μὴ ῥαψῳδός; ἔφη· καὶ γὰρ τὰ Ὁμήρου σέ φασιν ἔπη πάντα κεκτῆσθαι.

‘“Well, perhaps you want to be a good mathematician, like Theodorus?”1 “No, not that either.” “Well, perhaps you want to be an astronomer?” And as he again said no, “Perhaps a rhapsodist, then? They tell me you have a complete copy of Homer.”’

— Xenophon, Memorabilia 4.2.10

Genre: philosophy
astrologer practitioner

ἀστρολόγος· γενεθλιολόγος

‘astrologos: birth expert.’

— Hesychius α 7912

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2018. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 1 (A - Delta), ed. I.C. Cunningham. Berlin, Boston: Walter de Gruyter. 361
