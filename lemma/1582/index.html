pectus gemini

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:41:03 AM
Primary meaning the chest of the Twin
Literal translation chest of the twin
Variants
pectus gemini
gemini pectus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the chest of the northern Twin animal part

8°–10° Gemini

VIII. et IX. et X. in pectore

‘[the degrees] 8 and 9 and 10 are in the chest [of the northern Twin]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
