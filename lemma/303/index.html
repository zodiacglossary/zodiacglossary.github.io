grḥ

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:01:39 PM
Primary meaning night
Literal translation night
Variants
grḥ
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
night time interval

Used for nocturnal births

ḥsb.t 5.t n Ꜣntwnyns | ỉbd-3 (n) šmw sw ꜥrḳy n pꜣ Wynyn pꜣ ṯꜣy-wnw.t 3.t n grḥ

‘Regnal year 5 of Antoninus (Pius) | month 3 of Shemu, day 30 according to the Greeks, 3rd hour of the night’

— Griffith MSS 3.59 1-2

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes (?)
Date: 2nd cent. CE

A. Winkler, A Starry Summer Night in AD 142: A Theban Horoscope (Griffith MSS 3.59) in Context, in K. Donker van Heel, F.A.J. Hoogelijk, and C.J. Martin (eds), Hieratic, Demotic and Greek Studies and Text Editions: Of Making Many Books There Is No End. Festschrift for Sven P. Vleeming (Leiden:Brill), 298-308. 301
