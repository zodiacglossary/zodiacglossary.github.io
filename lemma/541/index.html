ὡροσκόπος
hōroskopos

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/3/2024, 3:33:04 PM
Primary meaning ascendant
Literal translation hour-watcher
Variants
ὡροσκόπος hōroskopos
ὡρ hōr abbreviation (e.g. P. Oxy. LXI 4263)
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
ascendant cardinal point, astrological concept

Ἥλιος, Ἀφροδίτη Ὑδροχόῳ, Σελήνη, Ζεὺς Τοξότῃ, Κρόνος Λέοντι, Ἑρμῆς Αἰγοκέρωτι, Ἄρης, ὡροσκόπος Ζυγῷ.

‘The Sun, Venus (were) in Aquarius; the Moon, Jupiter in Sagittarius; Saturn in Leo; Mercury in Capricorn; Mars, the Ascendant in Libra.’

— Vettius Valens 5.6.112

Genre: horoscope
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 122,I,22)

Παῦνι ιγʹ εἰς ιδʹ, ὥρᾳ γʹ νυκτός. Ὥρα Ἐγόκερῳ. // (...) Ὡροσκόπος Αἰγόκαιρω. (...)

‘Pauni 13 till 14, hour 3 of night. Ascendant in Capricorn. // (...) Ascendant in Capricorn. (...)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4257 1, 4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 279 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 267 + II: 394-395
