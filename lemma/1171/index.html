ina IGI
ina pāni

Akkadian none

Last edited: 10/30/2023, 1:31:02 PM
Primary meaning in front of
Literal translation in front of
Variants
ina IGI ina pāni
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
in front of expression of relative position

ZIZ2 14 dil-bat ina NIM ina IGI GU ŠU2 ŠE DIRI

‘The 14th of month XI, Venus's last visibility in the east in front (west) of Aquarius. (The year had) an intercalary month XII.’

— AO 17649 (BH 1) obv. 6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Nippur
Date: 5th-4th century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: Darius II., Year 13, X.24 = -409 Jan. 12/13)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 51
