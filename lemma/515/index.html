IZI.ŠUB
miqit(ti) išâti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:34:45 PM
Primary meaning lightning strike
Literal translation fall of fire
Variants
IZI.ŠUB miqit(ti) išâti
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
lightning strike meteorological phenomenon

⸢ITU BI U₄⸣ 13 IZI.ŠUB ina KI-ti₃ TIN.TIR.⸢KI⸣

‘That month, (on day) 13, a fall of fire in the district of Tintir.’

— BM 34938+ (ADRT II No. -197C) rev. 5

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 198 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 248-249
