dorsus piscis

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/31/2025, 3:32:04 PM
Primary meaning the back of the Fish
Literal translation back of the fish
Variants
dorsus piscis
piscis dorsus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the back of the southern Fish animal part

13°–15° Pisces

sequentes vero tres per ordinem eiusdem Piscis dorsus accipiet.

‘however, the back of the same Fish will take up the following three [degrees] in order’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.12

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.293
