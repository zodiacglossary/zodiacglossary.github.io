קָדִים
qād̲îm

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:46:11 AM
Primary meaning east
Literal translation east
Variants
קָדִים qād̲îm
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
east cardinal direction

וְאֵ֖לֶּה שְׁמ֣וֹת הַשְּׁבָטִ֑ים מִקְצֵ֣ה צָפ֡וֹנָה אֶל־יַ֣ד דֶּֽרֶךְ־חֶתְלֹ֣ן ׀ לְֽבוֹא־חֲמָ֡ת חֲצַ֣ר עֵינָן֩ גְּב֨וּל דַּמֶּ֤שֶׂק צָפ֙וֹנָה֙ אֶל־יַ֣ד חֲמָ֔ת וְהָיוּ־ל֧וֹ פְאַת־קָדִ֛ים הַיָּ֖ם דָּ֥ן אֶחָֽד׃

wəʾēlleh šəmôt̲ haššəb̲āṭîm miqṣēh ṣāp̲ônāh ʾel-yad̲ derek̲-ḥet̲lōn  ləb̲ôʾ-ḥămāt̲ ḥăṣar ʿênān gəb̲ûl dammeśeq ṣāp̲ônāh ʾel-yad̲ ḥămāt̲ wəhāyû-lô p̲əʾat̲-qād̲îm hayyām dān ʾeḥād̲.

‘Now these are the names of the tribes: from the north end, beside the way of Hethlon to the entrance of Hamath, Hazar-enan, at the border of Damascus, northward, beside Hamath; and they shall have their sides east and west: Dan, one portion.’

— Ezekiel 48 1

Genre: religious text
