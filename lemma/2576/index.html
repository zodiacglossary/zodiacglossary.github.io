מְבוֹא הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ
məb̲ôʾ haššemeš

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:39:54 AM
Primary meaning west
Literal translation setting of the sun
Variants
מְבוֹא הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ məb̲ôʾ haššemeš
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
west cardinal direction

הֲלֹא־הֵ֜מָּה בְּעֵ֣בֶר הַיַּרְדֵּ֗ן אַֽחֲרֵי֙ דֶּ֚רֶךְ מְב֣וֹא הַשֶּׁ֔מֶשׁ בְּאֶ֙רֶץ֙ הַֽכְּנַעֲנִ֔י הַיֹּשֵׁ֖ב בָּעֲרָבָ֑ה מ֚וּל הַגִּלְגָּ֔ל אֵ֖צֶל אֵלוֹנֵ֥י מֹרֶֽה׃

hălōʾ-hēmmāh bəʿēb̲er hayyardēn ʾaḥărê derek̲ məb̲ôʾ haššemeš bəʾereṣ hakkənaʿănî hayyōšēb̲ bāʿărāb̲āh mûl haggilgāl ʾēṣel ʾēlônê mōreh.

‘Both are on the other side of the Jordan, beyond the west road that is in the land of the Canaanites who dwell in the Arabah—near Gilgal, by the terebinths of Moreh.’

— Deuteronomy 11 30

Genre: religious text
