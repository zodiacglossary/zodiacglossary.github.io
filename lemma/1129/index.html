BAR
mišlu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/24/2024, 7:38:47 AM
Primary meaning half
Literal translation half
Variants
BAR mišlu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
half temporal expression

iti.ŠE GE6 1[4 AN.GE6 30] | ina RIN2 TIL-tim GAR-a[n] | U4.28 AN.[GE6 20] | ina ḪUN BAR DIB

‘Month XII, night of the 1[4th a lunar eclipse,] | in Libra totality occurr[ed]. | On the 28th day an ecl[ipse of the sun] | in Aries, one-half (month) having passed (since the previous lunar eclipse).’

— BM 36620 (BH 14) 3-6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 3rd century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: 92 S.E. VII.12(?) = -219 Oct. 21)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 93

midpoint (midday, midnight, midyear) temporal expression
middle spatial expression
half mathematical quantity
