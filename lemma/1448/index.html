a-ru-u₂
arû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 3/22/2024, 3:02:08 PM
Primary meaning multiplication
Literal translation multiplication
Variants
a-ru-u₂ arû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
multiplication mathematical operation, arithmetical operation, multiplicative operation

Only in mathematical corpus

computation mathematical expression

Meaning attested once in colophon of computed lunar table. This rare meaning is not mentioned in CAD (but see CAD arû A 2)

(21′) a-ru-u₂ ne₂-me-qi₂ ᵈdiš-u₂-tu₂ ad.ḫal dingir.[meš gal.meš] (22') mi₂.uru₃ um-man-nu zu-u₂ ana lu₂ [zu-u₂] (23′) li-kal-lim la lu₂.zu-u₂ nu [igi-mar nig₂.gig] (24′) ᵈa-nim ᵈen-lil₂ u₃ [ᵈe-a dingir.meš gal.meš]

‘(21') Computation, wisdom of Anûtu, secret of the [great] gods, (22') guarded knowledge of the scholar. One who knows (23')may reveal it to one who knows, one who does not know may not see it, it is a taboo of (24') Anu, Enlil, [Ea, and the great gods]’

— AO 6485 (ACT No. 135) rev. 21'-24'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: synodic table
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Neugebauer, Otto, 1955, Astronomical Cuneiform Texts
