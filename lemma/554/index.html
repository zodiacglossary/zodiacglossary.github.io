umāmu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:15:43 AM
Primary meaning animal
Variants
u2-ma-mu umāmu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
animal common noun
animal astrological concept

The astral animal maybe represents the conceptual forerunner of the term zodiac sign (ζῴδιον, zōidion). It is so far only attested in one text (BM 35402 = LBAT 1593, see quotation below).

u2-ma-mu ša2 13 ana IGI-ka 1 1 1 13 u2-ma-mu ša2 NE ša2 BAR DAB-bat 1 2 1 26 u2-ma-mu ša2 ZIZ2 ša2 BAR DAB-bat | u2-ma-mu ša2 iṣ-ṣi ša2 giš.GIŠIMMAR KUR.GI.mušen giš.GIŠIMMAR ana u2-ma-mu ša2 13 DU3-uš giš.ZU ša2 DU6 ša2 BAR ša2 AB | ana u2-ma-mu ša2 4.37 giš.GIŠIMMAR KUR.GI.mušen giš.GIŠIMMAR TA UGU mul2.ŠU.GI TA x ŠE SAR NA4 U2 u GIŠ | u2-ma-mu ša2 13 u3 4.37 KI a-ha-meš DAB-bat NA4 U2 u GIŠ ana LU2.GIG ŠEŠ2 GU7-šu2 u tu-qat-tar-šu2 | bi-ib-lu ša2 BAR TA 1 EN 30 (...)

‘The animal of 13 is in front of you. (Month) 1, (day) 1, (zodiac sign) 1, 13°. The animal of month-5-of-month-1 you take. (Month) 1, (day) 2, (zodiac sign) 1, 26°. The animal of month-11-of-month-1 you take. | The animal of the wood of the date palm, the goose (of) date palm for the animal of 13 you make. The writing board of month-7-of-month-1 of month 10, | for the animal of 277, date palm, the goose (of) date palm, from the top of the Old-Man-star ..., stone, plant, and wood | The animal of 13 and 277 together you take. Stone, plant, and wood for the sick person. You anoint, feed, and fumigate him. | Almanac for month 1, from (day) 1 to (day) 30 (...)’

— BM 35402 (LBAT 1593) obv. 15'-19'

Genre: horoscopic astrology
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 4th-2nd century BCE

Reiner, E. 2000. Early Zodiologia and Related Matters. In: Wisdom, Gods and Literature, Studies in Assyriology in Honour of W. G. Lambert, eds. Andrew R. George and I. L. Finkel. Winona Lake, Indiana: Eisenbauns: 421-427. 422
