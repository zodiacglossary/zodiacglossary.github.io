cauda arietis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:32:04 AM
Primary meaning the tail of the Ram
Literal translation tail of the ram
Variants
cauda arietis
arietis cauda
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the tail of the Ram animal part

30° Aries

in cauda (habet) XXX. partem

‘[Aries] has the 30th degree in its tail’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.288
