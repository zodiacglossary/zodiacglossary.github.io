pyrois

Latin noun
quoted from Greek

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:33:30 PM
Primary meaning Mars
Variants
pyrois
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Mars planet

qui a nobis Mars, ab illis Pyrois dicitur

‘Mars to us is Pyrois to them’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.2.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.42
