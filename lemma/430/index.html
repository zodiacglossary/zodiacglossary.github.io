MUL₂ e ša₂ SAG GIR₂.TAB
kakkabu elû ša rēš zuqaqīpi

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:17:50 PM
Primary meaning β Scorpii
Literal translation the upper star of the head of the scorpion
Variants
MUL₂ e ša₂ SAG GIR₂.TAB kakkabu elû ša rēš zuqaqīpi
MUL₂ AN.TA ša₂ SAG GIR₂.TAB kakkabu elû ša rēš zuqaqīpi
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
β Scorpii star: normal star

Jones (2004) c23

⸢GI₆⸣ 26 ŠU₂.ŠU₂ ina ZALAG₂ sin ina IGI MUL₂ e ša₂ SAG GIR₂ 1 1/2 KUŠ₃

‘Night of (day) 26, very overcast; last part of the night, Sin was 1 1/2 cubits in front of the upper star of the head of the scorpion.’

— BM 33837 (ADRT II No. -232) obv. 21

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 233 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 106-107
