ὕπαυγος
hupaugos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/3/2024, 11:48:24 AM
Primary meaning invisible
Literal translation under the rays
Variants
ὕπαυγος hupaugos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
invisible term of visual perception

ἄδικος δὲ μὴ κατηγορούμενος διὰ τὴν τοῦ Ἄρεως καὶ Ἑρμοῦ στάσιν ἐπὶ τοῦ μεσουρανήματος, ἀμφοτέρων ὄντων ὑπαύγων

‘(The native was) unjust (but) not accused on account of the position of Mars and Mercury in Midheaven, both of them being under the rays (sc. of the Sun, i.e., invisible)’

— Antigonos of Nikaia F2 §56

Genre: astrological treatise

(GH L 40)
