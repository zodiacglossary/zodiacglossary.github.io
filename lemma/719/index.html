DU₃
epēšu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/24/2023, 7:22:23 PM
Primary meaning to compute
Literal translation to make
Variants
DU₃ epēšu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to compute mathematical operation

U₄.1.KAM ana DU₃-ka

‘In order for you to compute day 1.’

— BM 36722 (BMAPT No. 52) obv. i 39

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: Seleucid

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer

to construct mathematical operation
