aurem coniungo

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:11:10 AM
Primary meaning to hear
Literal translation to join the ear
Variants
aurem coniungo
coniungo aurem
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to hear astrological concept

relation between zodiac signs

at Leo cum Geminis aciem coniungit et aurem Centauro ferus et Capricorni diligit astrum.

‘But the Lion joins his gaze to that of the Twins and his hearing—for he too is a fierce star—to that of the Centaur and loves the sign of Capricorn.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.497 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 120-121
