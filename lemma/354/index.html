AN
zunnu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:46:26 PM
Primary meaning rain
Literal translation rain
Variants
AN zunnu abbreviation of ŠEG₃ used in astronomical diaries and related texts
ŠEG₃ zunnu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
rain meteorological phenomenon

GE₆ 5 SAG GE₆ ŠU₂.ŠU₂ IM ŠAR₂ GIR₂ GU₃ U AN kab-bar PISAN DIB

‘Night of (day) 5, beginning of the night, very overcast, gusty wind, lightning, thunder, thick rain, PISAN DIB.’

— BM 32889+ (ADRT II No. -246) obv. 2

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 247 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 58-59
