BIR-(star) constellation

24 BIR ina KUR IGI

‘(Day) 24, BIR appears in the east.’

— MLC 1885 (ADRT VII No. 57) obv. 11'

Genre: astronomical diaries and related texts: normal star almanac
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 180 BCE

Hunger, H. 2014. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. VII: Almanacs and Normal Star Almanacs. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 112-113
