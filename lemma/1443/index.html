ἐναντίος
enantios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/24/2023, 9:49:31 AM
Primary meaning in opposition
Literal translation opposite
Variants
ἐναντίος enantios
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
in opposition astrological concept

οὗτοι γὰρ ἐὰν ἐναντίοι γένωνται, τοὺς κλιμακτῆρας καὶ τοὺς θανάτους σημαίνουσιν.

‘For these (sc. the Sun, Moon, and Ascendant), when they are in opposition, signify (medical) crises (klimakteres) and death.’

— Vettius Valens 3.6.1

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 61,X)
