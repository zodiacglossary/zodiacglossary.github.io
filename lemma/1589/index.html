fauces cancri

Latin noun

Last edited: 5/27/2024, 7:55:08 AM
Primary meaning the throat of the Crab
Literal translation throat of the crab
Variants
fauces cancri
cancri fauces
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the throat of the Crab animal part

6°–7° Cancer

in faucibus vero VI. et VII.

‘in the throat [of the Crab] however [are the degrees] 6 and 7’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
