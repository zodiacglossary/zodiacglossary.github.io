ἐργαστικός
ergastikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:02:07 AM
Primary meaning industrious
Literal translation associated with working
Variants
ἐργαστικός ergastikos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
industrious zodiac sign quality

ἔστι δὲ τὸ ζῴδιον (…) ἐργαστικόν

‘The zodiac sign (Taurus) is (…) industrious’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.14

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
