καλοποιός
kalopoios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 3/10/2025, 1:38:09 AM
Primary meaning benefic
Literal translation doing good
Variants
καλοποιός kalopoios
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
benefic astrological concept

χαίρει δὲ ὁ Ἑρμῆς ἀεὶ συνὼν κακοποιοῖς καὶ καλοποιοῖς

‘Mercury always rejoices when with malefics and benefics’

— Anon., On Cardinal Points §82

Genre: astrological treatise

Pingree, D. (1976) Dorothei Sidonii Carmen Astrologicum 367
