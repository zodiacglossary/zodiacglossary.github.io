βασιλικός
basilikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:08:33 AM
Primary meaning royal
Literal translation royal
Variants
βασιλικός basilikos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
royal zodiac sign quality

attribute of Leo

[ἐν] ζῳδίῳ σ]τερεῷ ἀρρε[ν]ικῷ βασιλικ[ῷ] Λ[έο]ντι

‘in the solid, masculine, royal sign Leo’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 i. 20-21

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 420-421

See also
