locus filiorum

Latin noun

Last edited: 10/27/2023, 3:15:42 PM
Primary meaning place of children
Literal translation place of children
Variants
locus filiorum
filiorum locus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
place of children astrological place

5th astrological house

filiorum in quinto

‘the [place] of the children [is] in the fifth [sign]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.14.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.58

lot of children astrological lot

Locum nobis filiorum Venus et Mercurius semper ostendunt.

‘Venus and Mercury always show us the lot of children.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.33

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.184
