LA2
naḫāsu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 3/22/2024, 3:09:31 PM
Primary meaning to subtract
Literal translation to subtract
Variants
LA2 naḫāsu used with preposition ultu(TA) = from
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to subtract mathematical operation, arithmetical operation, subtractive operation

(15') ki-i DIŠ.UD ša₂ SIG al-⸢la BE⸣ BAR.NUN u 2 ḪAB-rat DIRI-at BE BAR.NUN u 2 ḪAB-rat (16') TA lib₃-bi-šu₂ LA₂ ana ⸢GI₆ E-bi⸣

‘If the DIŠ.UD of height exceeds the elongation, the ṣiliptu-coefficient, and the 2 for the disk you subtract the elongation, the ṣiliptu-coefficient, and the 2 for the disk from it and you call it GI₆.’

— MMA 86.11.363 (BMAPT No. 61G) obv. 15'-16'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-100 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
