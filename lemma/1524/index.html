pes quartus scorpionis

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:40:10 AM
Primary meaning the fourth leg of Scorpio
Literal translation fourth foot
Variants
pes quartus scorpionis
quartus pes scorpionis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the fourth leg of Scorpio animal part

Qui in quarto pede habuerint horoscopum, erunt cinaedi, mulieres vero prostituto pudore meretrices.

‘Whoever has the ascendant in the fourth leg [of Scorpio] will be homosexual, women however will be prostitutes.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.26.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.337
