procyon

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 9/23/2024, 8:44:46 AM
Primary meaning the Fore-dog
Literal translation fore-dog
Variants
procyon
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Fore-dog constellation

probably modern Canis minor

In Cancri parte ΧΧ<VII>. oritur Procyon.

‘At 27° Cancer rises the Fore-dog.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.9.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.302
