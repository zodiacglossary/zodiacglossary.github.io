BAR
aḫû

Akkadian adjective

Last edited: 12/3/2024, 11:48:32 AM
Primary meaning invisible
Literal translation extraneous
Variants
BAR aḫû
Edited by
Alessia Pilloni
extraneous adjective

GE₆ 14 27 GE₆ GIN AN.MI sin BAR DIB

‘Night of the 14th, 7° after sunset, lunar eclipse, „extraneous“, will be omitted.’

— BM 32847 (= 77-2-22, 9) + 41842 (= 81-6-25, 462) obv. 1

Genre: normal-star almanac
Provenance: Babylon
Date: SE 55

Hunger, H. 2014. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. VII: Almanacs and Normal Star Almanacs. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 18-19

"F. Rochberg14 translates BAR = aḫû by „invisible“ because „this is the effective meaning“, quoting Steele approvingly. I agree with this argument but nevertheless use the literal translation „extraneous“ in this edition." (Hunger 2014: XV).
invisible term of visual perception

in eclipse statements
