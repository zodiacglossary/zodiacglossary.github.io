φωσφόρος
phōsphoros

Greek noun

Last edited: 6/15/2025, 3:13:17 PM
Primary meaning Venus
Literal translation light-bearer
Variants
φωσφόρος phōsphoros

φωσφώρος ὁ τῆς Ἀφροδίτηι̣ ἐ̣ν̣ Αἰγ̣όγερωι

‘The light-bearer, the (star) of Aphrodite (Venus) (was) in Capricorn …’

— P.Oxy. 307 13–14

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 46 CE
