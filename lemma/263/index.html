ꜥ.wy šy

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 6:42:26 PM
Primary meaning House of Daimon
Literal translation House of Daimon
Variants
šy
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
House of Daimon astrological house

5th astrological place

pꜣy-ms=w r pꜣ-nṯr-t(w)ꜣ n ꜥ.wy n. šy | r pꜣ rmṯ n rn=f r ỉr nfr r šy

‘The one who was born when Venus was in the House of Daimon, the mentioned man will be happy as to fate.’

— P.Berlin 8345

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: 1st cent. CE to 2nd cent. CE

Quack, J. F., 2008, Texte aus Ägypten: Demotische, magische und divinatorische Texte. In B. Janowski and B. Wilhelm (eds), Omina, Orakel, Rituale und Beschwörungen. Texte aus der Umwelt des Alten Testaments, Neue Folge 4. Gütersloh: Gütersloher Verlagshaus.

pr.t nṯr.t pꜣ gnḥḏ | pꜣ šy pꜣ mꜣy

‘The Domain of the goddess: Cancer | The Daimon: Leo’

— O.Neugeb. 3 21-22

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117
