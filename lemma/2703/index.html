νυχθήμερον
nuchthēmeron

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:19:10 AM
Primary meaning day-and-night
Literal translation night-day
Variants
νυχθήμερον nuchthēmeron
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
day-and-night time interval

μετα- [βάλλειν εἰς τὰ ὁμαλ]ὰ νυχθήμερα ...

‘to change ... into uniform day-and-nights’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 Fr. 3, col. i, l. 16-17

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 34-35

See also

〈νύξ〉 nux ‘night’

〈ἡμέρα〉 hēmera ‘day’
