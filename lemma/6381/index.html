द्रेकाण
drekāṇa

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 11/22/2025, 7:35:19 PM
Primary meaning Decan
Literal translation division
Variants
द्रेक्क drekka
द्रेष्काण dreshkāņa
दृकाण dṛkāṇa
Edited by
Poorvaa Malhotra
Originating from Egypt, drekāṇas or D3 chart is 1/3 of a zodiac sign equal to 10 degrees (face)

होरा प्रसिद्धाः । द्रेकाणसंज्ञाः प्रथितास्तथात्र चिन्ताकृतिप्राप्तिप्रकीर्तिताथ ॥३४॥

horā prasiddhāḥ | drekāṇasaṃjñāḥ prathitāstathātra cintākṛtiprāptiprakīrtitātha ||34||

‘The horās are well-known, and so the drekāṇas are named here; likewise the forms of considerations and attainment are also described’

— Yavanajātaka v.34

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Pingree, David. The Yavanajātaka of Sphujidhvaja. Harvard Oriental Series, vol. 48. Cambridge: Harvard University Press 49
