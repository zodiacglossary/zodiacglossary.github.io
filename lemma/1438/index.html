numero

Latin verb

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:23:00 PM
Primary meaning to count
Literal translation to count
Variants
numero
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to count mathematical operation

In genitura viri a Saturno numera ad Venerem, si sit diurna, et tantundem ab horoscope

‘In the nativity of a man count from Saturn to Venus, if it is a day-chart, and [add] this amount to the ascendant’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 7.12.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.239
