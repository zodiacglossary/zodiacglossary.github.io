liberatus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:59:35 AM
Primary meaning not in aspect
Literal translation freed
Variants
liberatus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
not in aspect astrological concept

especially w.r.t. the sun

<facit> divites potentes imperiosos magnos [...], praesertim si liberata a Solis radiis Luna in horoscopi signo fuerit inventa

‘it makes [the native] rich and powerful, mighty, and great, especially when the Moon is found in the sign of the ascendant, not aspected by the Sun’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.14.18

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.230
