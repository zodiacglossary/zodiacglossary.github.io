DUR
markasu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 12/11/2024, 9:35:48 AM
Primary meaning band
Literal translation band
Variants
DUR markasu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
band spatial concept

This is the conventional translation but perhaps "course" is more appropriate (maprasu form of rakasu)

(1) [NIM u SIG ša₂] ⸢MUL₂.BABBAR⸣ 2/3 KUŠ₃ e DUR MURUB₄ NIM (2) [2/3 KUŠ₃] SIG DUR MURUB₄ SIG (3) ki-i ina mul₂.GIR₂.TAB NIM ina zib.ME BUR₃ (4) a-na 1 DANNA 8 u NIM u SIG

‘(1) [Height and depth of] Jupiter: 2/3 cubit above the middle band it is ‘high’, (2) [2/3 cubit] below the middle band it is ‘low’. (3) If it is ‘high’ in Scorpio it is ‘low’ in Pisces. (4) For 1 bēru it ascends and descends 8 fingers.’

— BM 37266 (BMAPT No. 47) obv. 1-4

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 334
