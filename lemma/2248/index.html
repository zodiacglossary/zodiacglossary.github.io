αὐξομείωσις
auxomeiōsis

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/29/2024, 9:48:21 AM
Primary meaning difference
Literal translation increase-decrease
Variants
αὐξομείωσις auxomeiōsis
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
difference arithmetical operation, mathematical quantity

γίνεται γ γʹ ὅ ἐστιν αὐξομείωσις ἑκάστου ζῳδίου· τῷ αὐτῷ τρόπῳ καὶ ἐπὶ τῶν ἄλλων ζῳδίων γνώσει

‘The result is 3 1/3, which is the difference (between rising-times) for each zodiac sign; by the same method you will also know (the values) for the other zodiac signs.’

— Rhetorius CCAG I p. 163 §50

Genre: astrological treatise

CCAG I
