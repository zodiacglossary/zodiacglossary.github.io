x ana y LA₂
x ana y muṭṭu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:46:01 PM
Primary meaning to diminish y by x
Literal translation to diminish y by x
Variants
x ana y LA₂ x ana y muṭṭu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to diminish y by x mathematical operation

ki NIM ana BE LA₂

‘If it (the Moon) is high you diminish the elongation by it (the "DIŠ.UD of height").’

— BM 35399 (BMAPT No. 61A) obv. 11

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
