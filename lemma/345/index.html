ỉmy-wnw.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 6/30/2023, 10:55:29 AM
Primary meaning astronomer
Literal translation who is in the hour
Variants
ỉmy-wnw.t
wnm-wnw.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
astronomer title

st wbꜣ=t m-ḳd nꜣ ỉmy-wnw.wt [r] spd.t ỉw=w ⌈...⌉ n krḥ

‘They are opposite you just like the astronomers are (behind) Sothis, when they ⌈...⌉ in the night.’

— P.Leid. 384 x 16

Genre: literary text
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 2nd cent. CE

W. Spiegelberg, 2017, Der Ägyptische Mythus vom Sonnenauge, der Papyrus der Tierfabeln, Kufi. Nach dem Leidener demotischen Papyrus I 384 (Strasbourg)
