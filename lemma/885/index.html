retrogradus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:59:49 AM
Primary meaning retrograde
Literal translation going backwards
Variants
retrogradus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
retrograde astronomical term

praedicimus etiam quando retrogradus ad stationem pristinam revertatur

‘We even predict when he will retrograde and when return to his former station’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.4.6

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.12

See also

〈LA₂〉 naḫāsu ‘to move in retrograde direction’

〈reverto〉 ‘to return’
