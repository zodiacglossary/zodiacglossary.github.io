AN.UR₃
išid šamê

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/21/2025, 7:42:34 AM
Primary meaning horizon
Literal translation heaven's base
Variants
AN.UR₃ išid šamê
Edited by
Alessia Pilloni
horizon cosmological concept

KI.ŠE₃ AN.KI TEŠ₂.BI LA₂.A.TA AN.UR₃.TA UM.TA.E₃.NA.Z[U.Š]E ana e-ma ša-mu-u₂ u KI-tum DIŠ-niš na-an-du-ru iš-tu i-šid AN-e ina <a-še-ka>

‘When you (Sun-god) rise at the place where heaven and earth embrace, from the horizon.’

— K 4872 4

Genre: ritual
Provenance: Nineveh
Date: neo-assyrian

Borger, R. (1967). Das dritte “Haus” der Serie bīt rimki (VR 50-51, Schollmeyer HGŠ NR. 1). Journal of Cuneiform Studies, 21, 1–17. https://doi.org/10.2307/1359353 3
