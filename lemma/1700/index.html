sol conceptionalis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:43:01 PM
Primary meaning the Sun at the time of conception
Literal translation the Sun pertaining to conception
Variants
conceptionalis sol
sol conceptionalis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Sun at the time of conception astrological concept

si una malivola in conceptionali Sole fuerit inventa, is qui natus fuerit exponetur.

‘if a malefic [influence] is found with respect to the Sun at the time of conception, he who is born will be exposed’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 7.2.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.210
