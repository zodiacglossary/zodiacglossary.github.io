λεπτόν
lepton

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 11:20:39 AM
Primary meaning minute
Literal translation small quantity
Variants
λεπτόν lepton
λ l abbreviation
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
minute unit of celestial position

[οὗ τὸ δωδεκατημόριον ἐν Σ]κορπίωι [μοιρ]ῶν ηʹ λεπτῶν νβʹ

‘Its dodekatemorion is in Scorpio, at 8 degrees, 52 minutes’

— P.Lond. 98 i 27–28

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 95 CE

GH 95

πρ(οσθετικὸς) (Ἄρης) Αἰγοκέρωτι μο(ίρᾳ) ζʹ λ(επτοῖς) θʹ

‘Additive Mars in Capricorn 7° 9'’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4275 8

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 508 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 281-282 + II: 416-417
