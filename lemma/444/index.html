MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ SUḪUR.MAŠ₂
kakkabu arkû ša suḫurmāši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:11:06 PM
Primary meaning δ Capricorni
Literal translation the rear star of the goat-fish
Variants
MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ SUḪUR.MAŠ₂ kakkabu arkû ša suḫurmāši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
δ Capricorni star: normal star

Jones (2004) c28

GI₆ 10 SAG GI₆ sin ar₂ MUL₂ ar₂ ša₂ SUḪUR.MAŠ₂ 1 1/2 KUŠ₃

‘Night of the 10th, beginning of the night, the moon was 1 1/2 cubits behind the rear star of the goat-fish.’

— BM 37231 (ADRT I No. -333) rev. 11'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 334 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 166-167
