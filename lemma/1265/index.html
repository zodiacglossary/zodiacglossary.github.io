KIN.KIN
šite'û

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 10/30/2023, 1:32:51 PM
Primary meaning to investigate
Literal translation to investigate
Variants
KIN.KIN šite'û
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
to investigate verb of visual perception

šum4-ma EŠ.BAR 3.20 ana IGI-ka ša2 d.UDU.IDIM.MEŠ ina lu-maš KIN.KIN-ma

‘In order for you to see an ominous decision about the king, you investigate the planets within the (zodiacal) constellations.’

— AO 6455 (TCL VI 11 = TU 11) rev. 37

Genre: collection of astrological and astronomical rules
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Brack-Bernsen, Lis, and Hunger, Hermann. 2002. TU 11. A Collection of Rules for the Prediction of Lunar Phases and of Month Lengths. SCIAMVS 3: 3–90. 12
