ἀκούω
akouō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/12/2024, 2:43:49 PM
Primary meaning to hear
Literal translation to hear
Variants
ἀκούω akouō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to hear astrological concept

[κα]ὶ Λέ̣ων καὶ Τοξ[ό]της ἀ[κο]ύου[σ]ιν ἀλλήλων κα[ὶ] Ζυγὸς καὶ Κρειός.

‘Leo and Sagittarius also hear each other, and Libra and Aries.’

— P.Mich. III 149 xii, 26–27

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st-2nd century CE
