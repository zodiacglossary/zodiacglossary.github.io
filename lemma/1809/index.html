mul.NUN.ki
Eridu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:19:43 AM
Primary meaning Eridu
Literal translation Eridu (star)
Variants
mul.NUN.ki Eridu
NUN.ki Eridu
mul₂.NUN.ki Eridu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Eridu constellation, star

Star or constellation corresponding to Canopus (= α Carinae) and/or a part of Puppis. See Hunger ADRT VII, XV.

29 mul₂.NUN.ki ina NIM ⸢IGI⸣

‘(Day) 29, the Eridu (star) appears in the east.’

— BM 41900 (ADRT VII No. 196) obv. 12

Genre: astronomical diaries and related texts: almanac
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 70 BCE

Hunger, H. 2014. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. VII: Almanacs and Normal Star Almanacs. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 388-389
