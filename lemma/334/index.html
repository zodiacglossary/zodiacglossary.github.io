mul.ŠU.PA

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/9/2024, 1:13:35 PM
Primary meaning ŠU.PA-star
Variants
mul.ŠU.PA
mul2.ŠU.PA
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
ŠU.PA-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to Boötes.

iti.DU6 2.40 mul2.NIN.MAḪ 1.30 mul2.UGA.mušen 45 mul2.[Š]U.PA

‘Month VII: 2,40 Ninmaḫ, 1,30 'The Raven', 45 [Š]U.PA.’

— BM 34713 (LBAT 1499) obv. 7

Genre: schematic astronomy: 'three stars each' + rising times
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 4th-2nd cent BCE

Horowitz, W., 2014. The Three Stars Each: The Astrolabes and Related Texts (Archiv für Orientforschung, Beiheft 33). Horn/Wien: Berger & Söhne/Institut für Orientalistik der Universität Wien. 126

10 ŠU.PA ina KUR IGI

‘(Day) 10, ŠU.PA appears in the east.’

— MLC 1885 (ADRT VII No. 57) obv. 12'

Genre: astronomical diaries and related texts: normal star almanac
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 180 BCE

Hunger, H. 2014. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. VII: Almanacs and Normal Star Almanacs. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 114-115

α Boötis (Arcturus)

In astronomical diaries and related texts
