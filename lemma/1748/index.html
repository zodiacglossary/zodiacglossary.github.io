κλῆρος ἔρωτος
klēros erōtos

Greek none

Last edited: 1/19/2024, 6:08:39 AM
Primary meaning lot of desire
Literal translation lot of desire
Variants
κλῆρος ἔρωτος klēros erōtos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
lot of desire astrological lot

[ὁ κλῆρος ἔ]ρωτος εὑρέθη Ταύρῳ μοιρῶν δʹ οἴκῳ καὶ ὁρίοις Ἀ[φροδίτης· οὖτος σημαίν]ει τὸν περὶ φ̣ιλίας καὶ συστάσεως

‘The lot of desire was found in Taurus at 4 degrees, house and terms of Venus: this signifies the (qualities) relating to affection and association.’

— P.Princ. II 75 6–7

Genre: horoscope
Date: 138–161 CE

(GH 138/161)
