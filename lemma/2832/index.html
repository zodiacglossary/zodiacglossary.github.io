Κόρη
Korē

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/4/2025, 3:33:04 AM
Primary meaning Virgo
Literal translation girl
Variants
Κόρη Korē
Κούρη Kourē poetic
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Virgo zodiac sign

Κύπρις δ’ ἐν Ταύρῳ γάνυται νόον, ἐν δέ νυ Κούρῃ Ἑρμείας

‘The mind of the Cyprian (Venus) rejoices in Taurus, that of Mercury in the Girl (Virgo)’

— Dorotheus 1.1.9

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
