मीन
mīna

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 9/30/2025, 2:58:15 PM
Primary meaning Pisces
Literal translation fish
Edited by
Pisces zodiac sign

जले तु मीनद्वयमन्त्यराशिः कालस्य पादौ कथितौ वरिष्ठैः।

jale tu mīnadvayam antyarāśiḥ kālasya pādau kathitau variṣṭhaiḥ|

‘The last sign is a pair of fish in water. They are said by the excellent [sages] to be the feet of Time.’

— Vṛddhayavanajātaka 1.15ab

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: India
Date: ca. 300–325 CE

Mak, Bill M. 2018. “The First Two Chapters of Mīnarāja’s Vṛddhayavanajātaka.” Zinbun 48: 1–31.
