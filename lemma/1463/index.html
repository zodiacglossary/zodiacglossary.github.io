auriga

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/1/2023, 4:06:05 PM
Primary meaning the Charioteer
Literal translation charioteer
Variants
auriga
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Charioteer constellation

In Arietis parte XV. ad Aquilonem versus exoritur Auriga.

‘At 15° Aries turned to the north rises the Charioteer.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.6.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.296
