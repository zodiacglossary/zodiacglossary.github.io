mul.SIM.MAḪ
šinūnūtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/2/2024, 4:50:39 PM
Primary meaning Swallow-star
Literal translation Swallow-star
Variants
mul.SIM.MAḪ šinūnūtu
mul.SIM.MAḪ.mušen šinūnūtu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Swallow-star constellation

Approx. corresponding ε, ζ, θ Pegasi, α Equulei, and the western fish of Pisces.

DIŠ MUL ša2 ina IGI-et mul.AŠ.IKU GUB-zu mul.Ši-nu-nu-tu4

‘¶ The star which stands opposite the Field: the Swallow.’

— BM 86378 i 41

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 38

See also

〈DUR SIM.MAḪ〉 rikis šinūnūti ‘δεζ Piscium’
