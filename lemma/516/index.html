EN.NUN
maṣṣartu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:24:20 PM
Primary meaning watch
Literal translation watch
Variants
EN.NUN maṣṣartu may also be read naṣāru, with the same meaning
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
watch observational practice

EN.NUN ša₂ gi-ne₂-e ša₂ TA ITU.⸢NE⸣ [EN TIL] ⸢ITU⸣.APIN ša₂ MU 1 ME 43.⸢KAM₂⸣ [m.an-ti-ʾu]-uk-su LUGAL

‘Regular watch from month V [to the end of] month VIII of year 1 hundred 43, king Antiochus.’

— BM 41581 (ADRT II No. -168A) rev. 22'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 169 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 476-477

watch unit of time
