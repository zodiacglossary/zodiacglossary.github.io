pedes sinistri cancri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:29:04 PM
Primary meaning the left legs of the Crab
Literal translation left feet of the crab
Variants
pedes sinistri cancri
cancri pedes sinistri
sinistri pedes cancri
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the left legs of the Crab animal part

26°–27° Cancer

septem autem sequentes in dextris Cancri pedibus collocantur, XXVI. autem et XXVII. in sinistris Cancri sunt pedibus

‘the seven following degrees however are located in the right legs of the Crab, but [the degrees] 26 and 27 are in the left legs of the Crab’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289
