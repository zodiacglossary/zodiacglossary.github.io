UŠ ša₂ ŠU₂
nēmettu ša rabê

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 6:38:55 PM
Primary meaning Evening Station
Literal translation western station
Variants
UŠ ša₂ ŠU₂ nēmettu ša rabê
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Evening Station synodic phenomenon

of Mercury or Venus

(7) ⸢U₄.MEŠ ša₂ UŠ ša₂ ŠU₂ ana UŠ ša₂ ŠU₂⸣ (8) ⸢GU zib.ME 9.51⸣ TAB (9) [ḪUN] ⸢MUL₂ MAŠ 10.1 TAB⸣ (10) [ALLA A] ⸢9.59 TAB⸣ (11) [ABSIN RIN₂] ⸢GIR₂.TAB 9.46 TAB⸣ (12) [PA] MAŠ₂ 9.49 TAB

‘(7) The "days" for western station to western station: (8) Aquarius, Pisces you add 9,51, (9) [Aries], Taurus, Gemini you add 10,1, (10) [Cancer, Leo] you add 9,59, (11) [Virgo, Libra], Scorpio you add 9,46, (12) [Sagittarius], Capricorn you add 9,49.’

— BM 35495 (BMAPT No. 7) rev. i 7-12

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
