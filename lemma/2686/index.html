ἡμίφωνος
hēmiphōnos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 11/28/2024, 1:47:14 PM
Primary meaning semivocal
Literal translation half-voiced
Variants
ἡμίφωνος hēmiphōnos
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
semivocal zodiac sign quality

Κριός ἐστιν (…) ζῴδιον (…) ἐλεύθερον

‘Aries is a (…) semivocal zodiac sign’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.1

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
