Πτιαῦ
Ptiau

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/12/2025, 6:32:25 AM
Primary meaning Decan 1 of Aquarius
Variants
Πτιαῦ Ptiau reading of Ep. 4 (only)
Πιαύ Piau variant reading in MS P
Πτιάν Ptian variant reading in MS A
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 1 of Aquarius decan

οἱ δὲ τρεῖς αὐτοῦ δεκανοί· Πτιαῦ ὁ πρῶτος, Ἀεὺ ὁ δεύτερος, Πτηβυοὺ ὁ τρίτος

‘The three decans of (Aquarius) are: Ptiau, the first; Aeu, the second, Ptēbuou, the third’

— Hephaestion 1.1.205

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
