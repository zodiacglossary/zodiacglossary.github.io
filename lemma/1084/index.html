GI
šullumu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:37:11 AM
Primary meaning to complete
Variants
GI šullumu D stem of šalāmu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to complete astronomical term

said of time intervals

TA 1 ME GI ⸢EN⸣ 1 ME 1.30

‘After completing 1,(0) days, until 1,(0) days, (0);1,30.’

— BM 40054 obv. 4

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon?
Date: ca. 300-50 BCE

M. Ossendrijver, 2017, New results on a Babylonian scheme for Jupiter's motion along the zodiac, Journal of Near Eastern Studies 76, 231–247 236
