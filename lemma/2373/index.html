augeo

Latin verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:10:38 AM
Primary meaning to increase
Literal translation to grow
Variants
augeo
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to increase mathematical quantity

his usque ad Chelas horarum partibus aucta per totidem e Libra decrescent sidera partes.

‘With these fractions of hours the signs increase as far as the Claws; with the same gradations they will decrease after Libra.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 3.413 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 194-195

to add mathematical operation, arithmetical operation

Sed, cum bis denas augebit septima partes Lanigeri, surgent Hyades.

‘The Hyades will rise when twenty degrees of the Ram are augmented by a seventh.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 5.118 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 308-309
