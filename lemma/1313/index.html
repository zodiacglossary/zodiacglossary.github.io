splendidus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:43:40 PM
Primary meaning bright
Literal translation bright
Variants
splendidus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
bright adjective

Sed antequam ad explicationem mixturarum sermo noster accedat, pauca tibi de splendidarum stellarum dicam

‘But before we come to speak about configurations of planets, I want to tell you something about the bright stars’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.1.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.69
