GI
šalāmu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:38:02 AM
Primary meaning to be/become intact
Literal translation to be/become intact
Variants
GI šalāmu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to be/become intact astronomical term

used in connection with periodic phenomena

ina 8 MU.MEŠ ŠU₂ u₃ IGI GI

‘In 8 years the setting and the appearance (of Venus) become ‘intact’.’

— BM 33552 (BMAPT No. 9) rev. 18

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 400-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 224

See also

〈GI〉 šullumu ‘to complete’
