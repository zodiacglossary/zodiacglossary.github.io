DIRI.ŠE
addaru arkû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:37:23 PM
Primary meaning Second Addaru (month XII.2)
Variants
DIRI.ŠE addaru arkû
iti.DIRI.ŠE addaru arkû
iti.DIRI.ŠE.KIN.KU₅ addaru arkû
Edited by
Alessia Pilloni
Second Addaru (month XII.2) month, month name, time interval

¶ mul.dil-bat ina iti.Š[E i]ti.DIRI.ŠE d.30 mul.ŠU.PA

‘When Venus in Month XII, intercalary month XII Sin ŠU.PA’

— K 42 rev. 1

Genre: celestial divination: enūma anu enlil
Provenance: Nineveh

Reiner, E., Pingree, D. 1998. Babylonian Planetary Omens: Part Three. 194-195

¶ ina iti.DIRI.ŠE.KIN.KU₅ BAL-e MAR.TU-i

‘¶ an intercalary Month XII (belongs to) the Amorite reign’

— VAT 9412+11279 ii 19 ́

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: Assur
Date: 687 BC

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 91
