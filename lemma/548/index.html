ꜥ.wy (n) hby

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 9/18/2023, 6:55:00 PM
Primary meaning depression
Literal translation house of depression
Variants
ꜥ.wy n hby
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
depression astrological concept

Point 180° opposite the exaltation

[ḥr-pꜣ-šty tꜣ] qnḥy.(t) mtw⸗f ṯsy ẖn⸗s | pꜣ gnhḏ n swsw [1]5 pꜣy⸗f ꜥ.wy n hby pꜣ-ḥr-ꜥnḫ [n swsw 15 pꜣ ỉsw pꜣ mꜣy pꜣ-nty-ꜣtḥ]

‘[Jupiter, the] dignity in which it exalts: Cancer in 15 degrees, its depression Capricorn [in 15 degrees; Aries, Leo, Sagittarius]’

— P.Carlsberg 81 Frag. 31 ii 17-18

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

A. Winkler, 2016, Some Astrologers and Their Handbooks in Demotic Egyptian, in J.M. Steele (ed.), The Circulation of Astronomical Knowledge in the Ancient World (Leiden: Brill), 245-286 257
