βλάπτω
blaptō

Greek verb

Last edited: 2/24/2025, 6:51:09 AM
Primary meaning to injure
Literal translation to harm
Variants
βλάπτω blaptō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to injure astrological concept

Ἠέλιος φαυλοῖσι βεβλαμμένος, Ἀφρογενής τε (…) ἀνέρι πῆμα φέρουσιν

‘The Sun, when injured by bad ones (malefics), and the Foam-born (Venus) (…) bring pain to a husband’

— Dorotheus 5.16.1–40

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
See also

〈noceo〉 ‘to be harmful’
