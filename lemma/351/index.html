term astrological concept

Standard term for the terms (ὅριον).

(pꜣ)-nṯr-(dwꜣ) rpy.t 12 tꜣy=f tny.t ḥꜥ=f wꜣḥ

‘Venus: Virgo 12°, its own terms. (It) adds.’

— ANAshm.Mus.D.O. 641 7

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Author
Date: 48 BCE

A. Winkler, 2022, The First Zodiac Sign and the Daimon: The Advent of an Astrological Tradition and Seven Elaborate Horoscopes, Studien our Alt'ägyptischen Culture 51: 267-319 302
