daemonium

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 10/21/2024, 7:54:08 AM
Primary meaning fourth astrological place
Literal translation little Daimon
Variants
daemonium
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
fourth astrological place astrological place

only in Manilius

titulus, quem Graecia fecit, Daemonium signat dignas pro nomine vire.

‘Daemonium is the name the Greeks have given it, denoting influences fitting the name.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.935a+937b

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 156-157
