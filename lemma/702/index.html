nothing mathematical quantity

representation of the number zero in some early Seleucid texts

(8) 5 TAB ša₂ zib (9) 5 TAB ša₂ ḪUN bi-rit TAB ana TAB ia-a-nu

‘(8) The addition for Pisces is 5, (9) the addition for Aries is 5, the difference from addition to addition is nothing.’

— BM 36722 (BMAPT No. 52) rev. ii 8-9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: Seleucid

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer 348-349
