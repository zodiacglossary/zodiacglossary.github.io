γένεσις
genesis

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/26/2023, 10:26:35 AM
Primary meaning nativity
Literal translation birth
Variants
γένεσις genesis
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
nativity astrological concept

γένεσις Φιλόης ιγ (ἔτους) Ἀντωνίνο[υ] Καίσαρος τοῦ κυρίου Φαμε(νὼθ) ιεʹ εἰς ιϛʹ ὥρας δʹ νυκτός

‘Nativity of Philoe, in the 13th year of the lord Antoninus Caesar, (month of) Phamenoth, the 15th into the 16th, 4th hour of the night’

— P.Oslo II 6 1–3

Date: 150 CE

(GH 150)
