E
qabû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:42:02 PM
Primary meaning to predict
Literal translation to say
Variants
E.A qabû
E qabû
DU₁₁.GA qabû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to predict mathematical operation

(37) 4.30 IB₂.TAG₄ (38) U₄.28.KAM 4.30 ⸢ME⸣ NIM.A ana KUR E.A GAR-an

‘(37) 4,30 is the remainder (38) of day 28. You put down 4,30 as the time (from New Moon) to sunrise in order to predict KUR.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. ii 37-38

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 368-369

(11) ki-i sin [ma]-la a-ma-ri la ma-ṣu-u₂ (12) ZI ᵈsin ša₂ 1-en u₄-mu KI-šu₂ TAB-ma ana kun-nu E-bi

‘(11) If Sin is insufficient(ly bright) for being visible (12) you add Sin’s displacement for 1 day to it and you predict a full month.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. ii 11-12

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 364-365
