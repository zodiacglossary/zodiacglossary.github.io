Second Ulūlu (month VI.2) month, time interval

Intercalary month VI

⸢ina⸣ ⸢iti⸣KIN.2.KAM UD.8.KAM ina dUTU.E₃ IGI ŠA₂ KUR ŠA₂?-ab

‘in the intercalary month VI, day 8, at sunset morning first the middle’

— K 2321 r. 9'

Genre: celestial divination: enūma anu enlil
Provenance: Nineveh

Reiner, E., Pingree, D. (1975). Babylonian Planetary Omens: Part One. Enūma Anu Enlil Tablet 63: The Venus Tablet of Ammiṣaduqa. Undena. 29-62
