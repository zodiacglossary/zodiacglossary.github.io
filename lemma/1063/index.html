malignus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:07:51 PM
Primary meaning malefic
Literal translation malign
Variants
malignus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
malefic astrological concept

[...], praesertim si in malignae stellae vel signo vel loco vel finibus fuerit inventus.

‘[...] especially if he is in the house or terms of a malefic planet.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 3.2.21

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.103
