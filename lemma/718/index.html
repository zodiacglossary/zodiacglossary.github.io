IB₂.ME.A
qību (?)

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 9/13/2023, 9:35:26 AM

Literal translation prediction

prediction

2 u₄-mu [TA] IGI 2 U₄ [x x x x x x x x x x x x] IB₂.ME.A GAR-an [x x]

‘2 days "from the appearence, 2 days [...] 30 20 you put down as a prediction’

— BM 76488 obv. 10

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon or Sippar?
Date: Seleucid
