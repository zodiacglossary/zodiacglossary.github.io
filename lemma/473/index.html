DU₃-šu₂
epūšu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/24/2023, 8:02:59 PM
Primary meaning procedure
Literal translation procedure
Variants
DU₃-šu₂ epūšu
DU₃-uš epūšu
DU₃.DU₃ epūšu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
procedure mathematical operation

DU₃-šu₂ ša₂ 2.13.20 ša₂ U₄.15.[KAM]

‘Procedure for 2;13,20 of day 15.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) obv. i 7

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.

(4') DU₃.DU₃.BI ša₂ SAG.KI GU₄ SAG-⸢su⸣ [xx] (5') ana muḫ-ḫi a-ḫa-miš₂ TAB-ma ½-šu₂ GIŠ-⸢ma⸣

‘(4') Its procedure: the [...] width(s) of the trapezoid (5') you add (“accumulate”) together, you compute half of it, and ...’

— BM 34757 side Y 4'-5'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon?
Date: ca. 350-100 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2016, Ancient Babylonian astronomers calculated Jupiters position from the area under a time-velocity graph, Science, 351, 482-484
