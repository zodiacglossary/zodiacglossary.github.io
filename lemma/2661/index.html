μείς
meis

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/3/2024, 3:25:09 PM
Primary meaning month
Literal translation month
Variants
μείς meis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
month time interval

ἡ προγενομένη πανσέληνος ἐγένετο τῷ αὐτῷ μηνὶ Παῦνι κεʹ εἰς κϛʹ ὥρ(ᾳ) βʹ νυκ(τός)

‘The preceding full moon took place in the same month, Pauni 25 till 26, hour 2 of night’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4282 1-6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 430-431
