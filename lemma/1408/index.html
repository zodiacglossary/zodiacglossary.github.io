mul.ŠUDUN.ANŠE EGIR
nattullu arkû

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/24/2024, 7:30:29 AM
Primary meaning Rear Harness star
Literal translation Rear Harness star
Variants
mul2.na-at-tul-lum ar2-ku-u2 nattullu arkû
mul.na-tul-lu EGIR nattullu arkû
mul.ŠUDUN.ANŠE EGIR-ti nattullu arkīti
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Rear Harness star star: ziqpu star

ε Boötis

2 MA.NA KI.LA₂ 12 UŠ i-na qaq-qa-ri 21 lim 6 me ⸢DANNA⸣ [ina AN-e] | TA mul.ŠUDUN.ANŠE EGIR-ti EN ⸢mul⸣.[GUR₂-ti]

‘2 minas weight, 12 UŠ on the ground, 21 thousand 6 hundred bēru [in heaven], from the Rear Harness to the [Circle].’

— AO 6478 (TCL VI 21 = TU 21) obv. 7'-8'

Genre: star list
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Thureau-Dangin 1913, 'Distances entres étoiles fixes d'après une tablette de l'époque Séleucides', RA 10, 215-225 218
