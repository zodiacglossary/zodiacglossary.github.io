urna

Latin noun

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:53:12 AM
Primary meaning Aquarius
Literal translation urn
Variants
urna
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Aquarius zodiac sign

Lanigero genitis bellum est cum Virgine natis et Libra Geminisque et eis quos protulit Urna.

‘The Ram’s children are at war with the offspring of the Virgin, the Balance, and the Twins, and with those whom the Urn has brought forth.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.541 f

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 126-127
