sky common noun

r dỉ.t ḫpr=f ꜥn n ỉr pꜣ nty pḥ n wš nb r-ẖ(.t) tꜣ ẖ.t nty-ỉw tꜣ p.t smn ḥr-ꜣt.ṱ={w}⟨=s⟩ n pꜣ hrw

‘... in order to allow it to also happen as is fitting at all times according to the manner which the sky is established today’

— Canopus Decree, Tanis 39

Genre: inscription
Provenance: Tanis
Date: 3rd century BCE

S. Pfeiffer, 2008, Das Dekret von Kanopos (238 v. Chr.): Kommentar und historische Auswertung eines dreisprachigen Synodaldekretes der ägyptischen Priester zu Ehren Ptolemaios' III. und seiner Familie.
