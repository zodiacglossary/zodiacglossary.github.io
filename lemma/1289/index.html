TIL qītu
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
end spatial concept

Said of zodiac signs and constellations.

ina si-man-šu2 30 ina TIL MAŠ2 ina 18 | 20 ina TIL ḪUN ina 30

‘In his hour (of birth), the moon was at the end of Capricorn in 18°, | the sun at the end of Aries in 30°.’

— BM 38104 (BH 27) obv. 3-4

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 1st century BCE (date of birth in horoscope: 243 S.E. I.20 = -68 Apr.16)

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 137
