species nixa genibus

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/31/2025, 3:16:39 PM
Primary meaning the Kneeling one
Literal translation a figure on bended knee
Variants
species nixa genibus
species nixa genu Manilius 5.645
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the Kneeling one constellation

nixa venit species genibus, sibi conscia causae.

‘Next [to the chill Bears and the frozen north] comes a figure on bended knee, the reason for whose posture is known to none but him.’

— Manilius, Astronomica 1.315

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 28-29
