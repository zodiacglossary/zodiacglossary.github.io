δρόμος
dromos

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 12:09:22 PM
Primary meaning path
Literal translation course
Variants
δρόμος dromos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
path planetary motion, astronomical concept, spatial concept

[ἕ- βδο]μον σελίδιν δρόμος σε- λήν]ης οὗ ὅρος δʹ κʹεʹ κʹζʹ [εʹ

‘Seventh column, course of the moon, if which limit 4;25,27,5’

— PSI XV 1491 17-19

Genre: astronomical treatise
Provenance: Egypt
Date: II CE

Jones, A., 2002. Babylonian Lunar Theory in Roman Egypt: Two New Texts. In: J. M. Steele and A. Imhausen, eds. Under One Sky: Astronomy and Mathematics in the Ancient Near East, Alter Orient und Altes Testament, 297. Münster: Ugarit-Verlag, 167–174.
