MUL₂ TUR ša₂ 2 1/2 KUŠ₃ ár MÚL ar₂ ša₂ SUHUR.MAŠ₂
kakkabu ṣeḫru ša 2 1/2 ammatu arki kakkabi arkî ša suḫurmāši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:22:44 PM
Primary meaning ι Aquarii
Literal translation The small star which is 2 1/2 cubits behind the rear ̆ star of the goat-fish
Variants
MUL₂ TUR ša₂ 2 1/2 KUŠ₃ ár MÚL ar₂ ša₂ SUHUR.MAŠ₂ kakkabu ṣeḫru ša 2 1/2 ammatu arki kakkabi arkî ša suḫurmāši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
ι Aquarii star: normal star

Jones (2004) a10

[... GU₄].UD ŠU₂ <ina> ZALAG₂-ru GENNA E-a IB₂.TAG₄ dUDU.IDIM.MEŠ NU GUB.MEŠ 3 KUŠ₃ ⸢ar₂⸣ MUL₂ TUR ša₂ 2 1/2 KUŠ₃ ⸢ar₂⸣ [MUL₂-ar₂-ša₂-SUḪUR-MAŠ₂ a-dir ...]

‘[... Mer]cury set; <during> clearing, Saturn came out; the remainder of the planets did not stand there; 3 cubits behind the small star which is 2 1/2 cubits behind [the rear star of the goat-fish it was eclipsed ...]’

— BM 33044+33047 (ADRT III No. -122D) obv. 10'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 123 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften.
