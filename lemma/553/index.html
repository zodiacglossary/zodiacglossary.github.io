twtw nty hby

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 10/21/2024, 7:52:52 AM
Primary meaning apoklima
Literal translation zodiac sign which is depressed/desce
Variants
twtw nty hby
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
apoklima (?) astrological concept

possible meaning

[ỉw=f ḫpr r wn] sw bꜣn ṯsy | ẖn tꜣ mỉ.t 2.t r wn sw nfr ẖn nꜣ twtw.w nty hby [...

‘[If a] maleficent star exalts | in the 2 paths when a beneficent star is inside the zodiac signs which are depressed’

— P.Carlsberg 81 Frag. 31 ii 3-4

Genre: astrological treatise
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd cent. CE

Unpublished
