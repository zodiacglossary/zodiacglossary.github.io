? astronomical event

An eclipse-related astronomical phenomenon. Rarely attested. Maybe derived from the verb parādu "to be fearful, disturbed, restless" (D-stem "to frighten, disturb"; "to become confused").

pi-ir-du ša2 AN.MI

‘The pirdu of an eclipse.’

— AO 6455 (TCL VI 11 = TU 11) obv. 13

Genre: collection of astrological and astronomical rules
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Brack-Bernsen, Lis, and Hunger, Hermann. 2002. TU 11. A Collection of Rules for the Prediction of Lunar Phases and of Month Lengths. SCIAMVS 3. 8
