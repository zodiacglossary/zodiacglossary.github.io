cepheus

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:33:05 AM
Primary meaning Cepheus
Literal translation Cepheus
Variants
cepheus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Cepheus constellation

In XV. parte Capricorni oritur Cepheus.

‘At 15° Capricorn rises Cepheus.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.15.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.310
