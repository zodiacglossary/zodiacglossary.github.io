ἀντιμεσουράνημα
antimesouranēma

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/19/2025, 7:09:52 AM
Primary meaning Lower Midheaven
Literal translation opposite Midheaven
Variants
ἀντιμεσουράνημα antimesouranēma
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Lower Midheaven cardinal point

τὸ δὲ ὑπὸ γῆν καὶ ἀντιμεσουράνημα, ὃ καὶ αὐτὸ μεσουράνημά ἐστιν

‘Lower Midheaven and Opposite Midheaven, which itself is also a midheaven’

— Sextus Empiricus 5.12
