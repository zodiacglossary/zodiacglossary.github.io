διαφορός
diaphoros

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:21:50 AM
Primary meaning of difference
Literal translation different
Variants
διαφορός diaphoros
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
of difference subtractive quantity

προσθήσομ(εν) ὡς ἔ- φην [τ]ὰ δ(ια)φορὰ ἑξηκοστὰ τοῖς ἀν[ωμ]αλοῖς

‘we add as I said(?) the sixtieths of difference to the non-uniform (day-and-nights)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4142 Fr. 3, col. i, l. 6-8

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 32-33
