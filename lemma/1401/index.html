mensis

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:18:39 PM
Primary meaning month
Literal translation month
Variants
mensis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
month unit of time

Si Saturnus temporum dominus effectus Marti deputaverit tempora, deputat quidem [et] ex decennio suo menses XV.

‘If Saturn becomes the time-lord and allots its time to Mars, he allots 15 months of his ten-year period’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.33.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.191
