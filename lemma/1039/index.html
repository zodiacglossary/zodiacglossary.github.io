ὄργανον
organon

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/11/2025, 4:11:41 PM
Primary meaning table
Literal translation instrument
Variants
ὄργανον organon
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
table astronomical genre, artefact of astral science

οἷον ἐπὶ τοῦ Κρόνου τῶν μοιρῶν τὰς διαμετρούσας θεωρεῖν τίνος εἰσὶ θεοῦ, καθὼς ἐν τῷ ὀργάνῳ πρόκειται

‘Such as, in the case of the degrees of Saturn, one is to observe the (degrees) in opposition, which god’s they are, as is set out in the instrument (i.e., table).’

— Vettius Valens 3.6.2

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 61,X)
