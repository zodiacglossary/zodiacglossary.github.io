zib
zibbātu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 8/30/2023, 9:16:37 AM
Primary meaning Pisces
Literal translation tails
Variants

Pisces zodiac sign

3.8 ZIZ₂ 12 9.40 zib.ME UŠ

‘(Year) 3,8, (month) XI, (day) 12, 9;40 Pisces, it (Venus) is stationary.’

— BM 42799+45777 obv. ii(ES) 4'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: synodic table
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 140-120 BCE

Neugebauer ACT No. 421a

(12) TA 26 RIN2 EN 10 zib 1.46.40 TAB 10 (13) zib DIB-iq GAM 6 DU LA2

‘From 26 Libra until 10 Pisces you add 1;46,40, (if) it passes 10 Pisces you multiply by 0;6, subtract.’

— AO 6477 obv. 12-13

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 250-150 BCE

Ossendrijver 2012 No. 42

Tails constellation

(14') SAG.UŠ in AB.⸢SIN₂⸣ dil-bat ina KUN.ME ⸢GUB.ME-ma⸣ (15') A.AN u A.KAL ⸢MAḪ⸣

‘Saturn stands in the Furrow (Virgo?), Venus in the Tails (Pisces?), then strong rain and flood.’

— BM 36353+36647 obv. ii' 14'-15'

Genre: astrology
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE
