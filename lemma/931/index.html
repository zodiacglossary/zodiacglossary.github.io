stilbon

Latin noun
quoted from Greek

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:44:28 PM
Primary meaning Mercury
Literal translation Mercury
Variants
stilbon
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Mercury planet

quem nos Mercurium dicimus, illi Stilbontem vocant

‘the one we call Mercury they call Stilbon’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.2.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.42
