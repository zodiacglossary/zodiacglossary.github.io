facies scorpii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:47:45 AM
Primary meaning the face of the Scorpion
Literal translation face of the scorpion
Variants
facies scorpii
scorpii facies
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the face of the Scorpion animal part

6°–7° Scorpio

VI. et VII. in facie

‘[degrees] 6 and 7 are in the face [of the Scorpion]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.8

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
