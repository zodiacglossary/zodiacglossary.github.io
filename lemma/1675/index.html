manus sinistra aquarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 8:00:45 AM
Primary meaning the left hand of the Waterbearer
Literal translation left hand of the waterbearer
Variants
manus sinistra aquarii
sinistra manus aquarii
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the left hand of the Waterbearer animal part, part of zodiac sign

16°–17° Aquarius

XVI. et XVII. in sinistra ponuntur manu

‘[degrees] 16 and 17 are placed in the left hand [of Aquarius]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.11

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
