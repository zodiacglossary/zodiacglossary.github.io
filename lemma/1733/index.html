section of a rainbow meteorological phenomenon

A rainbow that is not a complete arch.

⸢22⸣ ina KIN.SIG bu-tuq TIR.AN id SI u KUR GAL₂-ši

‘The 22nd, in the afternoon, there was a section of a rainbow in the direction of north and east.’

— BM 45927 (ADRT I No. -284) obv. 10'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: -284

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 294
