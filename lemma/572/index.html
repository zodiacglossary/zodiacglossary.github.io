MUL₂ 4 ša₂ GABA-šu₂
kakkabu 4 ša irat nēši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 2/5/2025, 2:44:24 PM
Primary meaning α+η+γ+ζ Leonis?
Literal translation four stars of the breast of the lion
Variants
MUL₂ 4 ša₂ GABA-šu₂ kakkabu 4 ša irtišu
4 MUL.MEŠ ša₂ GABA mul.UR.A kakkabu 4 ša irat nēši

α+η+γ+ζ Leonis? star: ziqpu star

MUL₂ 4 ša₂ GABA-šu₂ [...]

‘The four stars of his breast: [...]’

— BM 36628+ obv. iii 9'

Genre: astral compendium
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 450-50 BCE

Steele, J. 2015, A Late Babylonian Compendium of Calendrical and Stellar Astrology, Journal of Cuneiform Studies 67, 187-215

4 MUL.MEŠ ša₂ GABA mul.UR.A KIMIN GIR₃.2 15 IGI-tu₄ ša₂ mul.UR.A MURUB₄ mul.MUŠ u mul.NUN.KI GU

‘The four stars of the breast of the lion or the front right foot of the lion, the middle of the snake and the Nunki(-star): a string.’

— BM 78161 (GU text) obv. 11-12

Genre: astronomical list
Provenance: Babylon or Sippar?
Date: ca. 7th-5th century BCE

Pingree, D. and C. Walker 1988, A Babylonian Star-Catalogue: BM 78161, Sachs Mem. Vol. 313-322
