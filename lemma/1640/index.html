pedes sinistri scorpii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:29:43 PM
Primary meaning the left feet of the Scorpion
Literal translation left feet of the scorpion
Variants
pedes sinistri scorpii
sinistri pedes scorpii
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the left feet of the Scorpion animal part

23°–25° Scorpio

in pedibus vero dextris duae sequentes ordinantur, in sinistris pedibus tres

‘in the right legs the following two [degrees] are counted, in the left legs [the following] three’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.8

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
