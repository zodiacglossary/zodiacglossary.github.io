SAG A
rēš nēši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:30:40 PM
Primary meaning ε Leonis
Literal translation the head of the lion
Variants
SAG A rēš nēši
SAG UR.A rēš nēši mainly in astronomical diaries 350-300 BCE
SAG UR rēš nēši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
ε Leonis star: normal star

Jones (2004) c14

⸢ina ZALAG₂ dil-bat SIG⸣ SAG A 4 KUŠ₃

‘Last part of the night, Venus was 4 cubits below the head of the lion.’

— BM 33850 (ADRT III No. -162) obv. 7

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 163 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 24-25
