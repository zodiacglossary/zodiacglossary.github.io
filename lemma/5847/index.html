ὁ διεστὼς αὐτῶν πρὸς μεσημβρίαν μοναχός
ho diestōs autōn pros mesēmbrian monachos

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:09:49 AM
Primary meaning 94 Aquarii
Literal translation The lone star at some distance from these [two] towards the south
Variants

ὁ διεστὼς αὐτῶν πρὸς μεσημβρίαν μοναχός

‘The lone star at some distance from these [two] towards the south [94 Aquarii]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 8

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
