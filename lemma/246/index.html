MUL₂ SIG ša₂ SAG GIR₂.TAB
kakkabu šaplû ša rēš zuqaqīpi

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:22:12 PM
Primary meaning π Scorpii
Literal translation the lower star of the head of the scorpion
Variants
MUL₂ SIG ša₂ SAG GIR₂.TAB kakkabu šaplû ša rēš zuqaqīpi
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
π Scorpii star: normal star

Jones (2004) a7

(2) [GI₆ 5 SAG GI₆ sin] (3) ina IGI MUL₂ SIG sa SAG GIR₂.TAB 2 2/3 KUŠ₃

‘(2) [Night of (day) 5, beginning of the night, Sin] (3) was 2 2/3 cubits in front of the lower star of the head of the scorpion.’

— BM 45808 (ADRT I No. -262) obv 3-4

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 263 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 366-367
