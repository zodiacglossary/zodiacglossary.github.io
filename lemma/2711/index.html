σελίδιον
selidion

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 9:31:00 AM
Primary meaning column
Literal translation little page
Variants
σελίδιον selidion
σελίδιν selidin late
Edited by

column textual layout

of an astronomical table

τρίτον] σελίδιν ζῳδίω[ν

‘Third] column is for the zodiacal signs.’

— PSI XV 1491 6

Genre: astronomical treatise
Provenance: Egypt
Date: II CE

Jones, A., 2002. Babylonian Lunar Theory in Roman Egypt: Two New Texts. In: J. M. Steele and A. Imhausen, eds. Under One Sky: Astronomy and Mathematics in the Ancient Near East, Alter Orient und Altes Testament, 297. Münster: Ugarit-Verlag, 167–174. 171
