udazallû-coefficient astronomical quantity

Coefficient related to time intervals. Also transliterated uddazallû. Attested mainly in schematic astronomy, rarely in mathematical astronomy

(13') BE-ma UD.DA.ZAL-e u₄-mi ⌈ITI⌉ u MU.AN.NA ana IGI-[ka] (14') 1.40 UD.DA.ZAL.LA₂-e u₄-me ana ITI IL₂-ma (15') 50 UD.DA.ZAL.LA₂-e ITI IGI

‘(13') In order for you to see the udazallu-coefficient for day, [month], and year: (14') you multiply 1.40, the udazallu-coefficient for a day, by a month, and (15') you find 50, the udazallu-coefficient for a month.’

— VAT 9412+11279 (Mul.Apin HH) ii 13'-15' (Mul.Apin Tablet II ii 13-15)

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: Assur
Date: 687 BC

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge.
