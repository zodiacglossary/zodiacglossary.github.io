E₁₁
šūlû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:34:21 AM
Primary meaning to subtract
Literal translation to deduct
Variants
E₁₁ šūlû
NIM šūlû
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to subtract mathematical operation, subtractive operation, arithmetical operation

(16) a-na tar-ṣi 2.12.8.53.20 LA₂-u₂ 2.42.57.46.40 GAR-an (17) [m]im-ma ša₂ al-la 2.12.8.53.20 LA₂-u₂ i-maṭ-ṭu-u₂ (18) [E]N 2.11.51.6.40 LA₂-u₂ TA 2.12.8.53.20 (19) ⸢E₁₁⸣-ma ša₂ re-ḫi GAM 5 DU-ma KI 2.42.57.46.40 (20) [TAB]-ma GAR-an

‘(16) Opposite 2,12;8,53,20, decreasing, you put down 2,42;57,46,40. (17) That which is less than 2,12;8,53,20, decreasing, (18) until 2,11;51,6,40, decreasing, you deduct from 2,12;8,53,20, (19) and what remains you multiply by 5, and you [add] it with 2,42;57,46,40 and (20) you put it down.’

— MLC 2205 (BMAPT No. 67) obv. 16-20

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer 434
