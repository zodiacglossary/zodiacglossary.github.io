E₃
šūṣû

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 10/19/2023, 8:55:08 AM
Primary meaning to compute
Literal translation to let appear
Variants
E₃ šūṣû + ina (from): to compute from
E₃ šūṣû + ana (to): to transform into
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to compute mathematical operation

to compute Q2 from Q1

[NIM u SIG TA] ⸢ḪAB-rat⸣.MEŠ ana šu-ṣu-u₂

‘In order to compute [the ‘height and depth’ from] the ‘disks’.’

— BM 34079 (BMAPT No. 65) obv. 15'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 426-427

to transform mathematical operation

to transform Q1 into Q2

(9) 2.13.20 ša₂ U₄.1.KAM ana U₄.14.KAM ana šu-ṣu-u₂ 20.⸢3⸣[9.48.53.20] (10) ⸢TAB u⸣ LA₂ ša₂ al 2.17.⸢4⸣.48.53.20 DIRI [TA 2.17.4.48] (11) ⸢5⸣[3.20] LA₂ ša₂ ⸢al⸣ 1.57.47.⸢5⸣[7.4]6.[40 LA₂ KI 1.57] (12) 4⸢7.5⸣[7.4]6.40 TAB-ma GAR-an

‘(9) In order to transform 2,13;20 for day 1 (= Φ_nm) into (that for) day 14 (= Φ_fm) (10) you add and subtract 2[0;39,48,53,20]. (The amount) by which it exceeds 2,17;[4],48,53,20 you subtract [from 2,17;4,48],5[3,20]. (11) (The amount) by which it [is less] than 1,57;47,[57,4]6,[40] (12) you add [with 1,57];4[7,57,4]6,40 and put down.’

— BM 34079 (BMAPT No. 65) rev. 9

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 426-427
