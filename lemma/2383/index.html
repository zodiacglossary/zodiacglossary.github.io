adicio

Latin verb

Last edited: 12/2/2024, 9:03:54 AM
Primary meaning to add
Literal translation to throw near
Variants
adicio
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to add mathematical operation, arithmetical operation

atque hunc numerum revocabis in ipsum multiplicans decies, adiectis insuper eidem quinque tamen summis

‘and [you must] repeatedly heap this number on itself by multiplying it ten times, adding to the amount, however, a further five times’

— Manilius, Astronomica 3.486 ff

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 200-201
