עֶרֶב
ʿereb̲

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:44:19 AM
Primary meaning evening
Literal translation evening
Variants
עֶרֶב ʿereb̲
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
evening time interval

וַיִּקְרָ֨א אֱלֹהִ֤ים ׀ לָאוֹר֙ י֔וֹם וְלַחֹ֖שֶׁךְ קָ֣רָא לָ֑יְלָה וַֽיְהִי־עֶ֥רֶב וַֽיְהִי־בֹ֖קֶר י֥וֹם אֶחָֽד׃ {פ}

wayyiqrāʾ ʾĕlōhîm  lāʾôr yôm wəlaḥōšek̲ qārāʾ lāyəlāh wayhî-ʿereb̲ wayhî-b̲ōqer yôm ʾeḥād̲.

‘God called the light Day and called the darkness Night. And there was evening and there was morning, a first day.’

— Genesis 1 5

Genre: religious text
