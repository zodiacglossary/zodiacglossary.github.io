Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:09:42 AM
Primary meaning Ewe-star
Literal translation Ewe-star
Variants

Edited by

Ewe-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to the northeastern part of Boötes (?)

DIŠ ⌈MUL ša2⌉ ina ⌈SAG⌉.KI mul.MAR.GID2.DA GUB-zu mul.U8 d.A-a

‘¶ The star which stands in the front part of the Wagon: the Ewe, Aja.’

— BM 86378 i 18

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 33
