Ἀφρογενής
Aphrogenēs

Greek noun

Last edited: 2/24/2025, 6:53:36 AM
Primary meaning Venus
Literal translation Foam-born
Variants
Ἀφρογενής Aphrogenēs
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Venus planet

Ἠέλιος φαυλοῖσι βεβλαμμένος, Ἀφρογενής τε (…) ἀνέρι πῆμα φέρουσιν

‘The Sun, when injured by bad ones (malefics), and the Foam-born (Venus) (…) bring pain to a husband’

— Dorotheus 5.16.1–40

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
