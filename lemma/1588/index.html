collum cancri

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:34:19 AM
Primary meaning the neck of the Crab
Literal translation neck of the crab
Variants
collum cancri
cancri collum
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the neck of the Crab animal part

3°–5° Cancer

III. et IV. et V. in collo

‘[the degrees] 3 and 4 and 5 [are located] in the neck [of the Crab]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.4

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.289

See also

〈collum scorpii〉 ‘the neck of the Scorpion’

〈collum piscis〉 ‘the neck of the Fish’
