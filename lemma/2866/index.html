Ῥηουώ
Rhēouō

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/7/2025, 7:37:26 AM
Primary meaning Decan 1 of Sagittarius
Variants
Ῥηουώ Rhēouō
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 1 of Sagittarius decan

οἱ δὲ τρεῖς αὐτοὐ δεκανοί· ὁ πρῶτος Ῥηουώ, ὁ δεύτερος Σεσμέ, ὁ τρίτος Κομμέ

‘The three decans of (Sagittarius) are: the first, Rhēouō; the second, Sesme; the third, Komme’

— Hephaestion 1.1.167

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
