MUL₂ IGI ša₂ SUḪUR.MAŠ₂
kakkabu maḫrû ša suḫurmāši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:20:18 PM
Primary meaning γ Capricorni
Literal translation the front star of the goat-fish
Variants
MUL₂ IGI ša₂ SUḪUR.MAŠ₂ kakkabu maḫrû ša suḫurmāši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
γ Capricorni star: normal star

Jones (2004) c27

[GE₆ 6 SAG GE₆] ⸢sin e⸣ MUL₂ IGI ša₂ ⸢SUḪUR⸣.MAŠ₂ ⸢2 1/2⸣ KUŠ₃ SI GIN

‘[Night of (day) 6, beginning of the night,] Sin was 2 1/2 cubits above the front star of the goat-fish; the north wind blew.’

— BM 41462+ (ADRT III No. -163B) obv. 4

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 165 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 10-11
