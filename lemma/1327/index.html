salutaris

Latin adjective

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:39:30 PM
Primary meaning benefic
Literal translation healthful
Variants
salutaris
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
benefic astrological concept

Quodsi ab his benivolarum stellarum recesserit radius, et ab omni salutarium siderum fuerint testimonio destituti, tunc matrem inhonesta corporis turpitudo dedecorat

‘If the of the benefic recedes from them and they are unaspected by all benefic planets, then shameful ugliness disgraces the mother's body’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.15.20

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.106
