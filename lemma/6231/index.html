παρθενική
parthenikē

Greek noun

Last edited: 10/7/2025, 8:30:27 AM
Primary meaning Virgo
Literal translation Virginal one
Variants
παρθενική parthenikē
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Virgo zodiac sign

ἐν δέ νυ Παρθενικῇ Μαίης προσλάμβανε κοῦρον.

‘And in [Virgo] it got as well the child of Maia.’

— Dorotheus Hephaestion 1.6

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
