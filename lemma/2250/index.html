σαύη
sauē

Greek noun
loaned from Akkadian

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:17:25 AM
Primary meaning cosmos
Literal translation cosmos
Variants
σαύη sauē restored reading
σάνη sanē rare
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
cosmos cosmological concept, spatial concept

σαυή· ὁ κόσμος. Βαβυλώνιοι

‘sauē: the cosmos. Babylonians.’

— Hesychius σ 260

Genre: lexicon
Provenance: Alexandria
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Hesychius Alexandrinus. 2005. Hesychii Alexandrini Lexicon, Vol. 3 (Pi - Sigma), ed. P.A. Hansen. Berlin, New York: Walter de Gruyter. 270

In the manuscript, only the reading σάνη is attested.
See also

〈AN〉 šamû ‘sky’
