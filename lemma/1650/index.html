venter equinus sagittarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:49:09 AM
Primary meaning the horse-belly of the Archer
Literal translation horse-belly of the archer
Variants
venter equinus sagittarii
equinus venter sagittarii
sagittarii venter equinus
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the horse-belly of the Archer animal part

21°–22° Sagittarius

in ventre equino II

‘in the horse-belly [are located the following] 2 [degrees]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
