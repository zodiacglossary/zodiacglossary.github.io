ḪA.LA
zittu

Akkadian noun

micro-sign astrological concept

Portion of a zodiacal sign

[3]0 U4.MEŠ 1 DANNA šu-u2 2 ½ u4-mu a-na 1-en ḪA.LA ŠID-ma 12 UZU.MEŠ ḪA.LA ša2 mul2.lu2.ḪUN.GA2 ip-pal-ka

‘30 days are 1 bēru (= 30°); you count 2 1/2 days for one portion, and 12 'limbs', portion(s) of Aries, it gives you.’

— AO 6483 (TCL 6 14 = TU 14) 10'-11'

Genre: horoscopic astrology
Provenance: Uruk
Date: 250-150 BCE

Sachs, A., 1952. Babylonian Horoscopes, Journal of Cuneiform Studies 6: 49-75.
