ἄνεμος
anemos

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/17/2024, 4:13:32 PM
Primary meaning wind
Literal translation wind
Variants
ἄνεμος anemos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
wind meteorological phenomenon

βαθμῷ ϛʹ ἀνέμῳ Λ[ι]β[εί, τ]όπῳ ζ[ῳδια]κῷ Αἰγόκερῳ, (...)

‘Step 6, wind Lips, zodiacal place Capricorn, (...)’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 fr. 1, i, 13-14

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd-3rd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 284-286 + II: 420-427

Jones translates Αἰγόκερῳ as 'Sagittarius'.
direction planetary motion

(...) καὶ ἐὰν μὲ[ν τυγχάνωσι οἱ ἄνεμοι [ὁ]μογεν[εῖς, ..... προσθέν- τες ἑκάτερα πρ[ὸς ...

‘(...) And if ... directions are of the same kind ... adding both to [each other (?) ...]’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4143 9-11

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 4th century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 105-108 + II: 36-39
