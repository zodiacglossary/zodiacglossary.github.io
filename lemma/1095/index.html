humanus

Latin adjective

Last edited: 10/17/2024, 8:31:22 AM
Primary meaning human
Literal translation human
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
human astrological concept, zodiac sign quality

the signs Gemini, Virgo, Libra, Aquarius

in humanis vero signis constitutus gladio facit interire.

‘in human signs he is killed by a sword.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 3.4.20

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.120
