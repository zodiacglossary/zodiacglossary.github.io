KI niṣirti
ašar niṣirti

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 2:58:24 PM
Primary meaning exaltation
Literal translation place of secret
Variants
KI ni-ṣir-tu2 ašar niṣirti
KI ni-ṣir-ti3 ašar niṣirti
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
exaltation astrological concept, astrological position, planetary position

KI ni-ṣir-tu4 ša2 d.UTU mul.lu2.ḪUN.GA2 d.Du[mu-zi . . .]

‘The place of secret of the Sun is the Hired Man constellation (Aries), Du[muzi …].’

— BM 35407+ i 30'

Genre: calendar treatise
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 4th-2nd century BCE

Reynolds, F., 2019. A Babylon Calendar Treatise: Scholars and Invaders in the Late First Millennium. Oxford: Oxford University Press. 194
