iuppiter

Latin noun

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:27:07 AM
Primary meaning Jupiter
Literal translation Jupiter
Variants
iuppiter
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Jupiter planet

facit [...] Iuppiter maturos, bonos, benignos ac modestos

‘Jupiter [makes men] mature, kindly, generous, temperate’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 1.2.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.6
