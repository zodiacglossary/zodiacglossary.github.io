Ἀφροδίτη
Aphroditē

Greek noun

Last edited: 11/4/2024, 11:50:21 AM
Primary meaning Venus
Literal translation Aphrodite
Variants
Ἀφροδίτη Aphroditē
Ἀφροδε Aphrode abbreviation (papyri)
Ἀφροδείτη Aphrodeitē papyri
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Venus planet

Ἥλιος, Ἀφροδίτη Ὑδροχόῳ, Σελήνη, Ζεὺς Τοξότῃ, Κρόνος Λέοντι, Ἑρμῆς Αἰγοκέρωτι, Ἄρης, ὡροσκόπος Ζυγῷ.

‘The Sun, Venus (were) in Aquarius; the Moon, Jupiter in Sagittarius; Saturn in Leo; Mercury in Capricorn; Mars, the Ascendant in Libra.’

— Vettius Valens 5.6.112

Genre: horoscope
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 122,I,22)
