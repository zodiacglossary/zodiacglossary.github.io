ŠU₂ ša₂ ŠU₂
rabû ša rabê

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:43:51 PM
Primary meaning Evening Last
Literal translation western setting
Variants
ŠU₂ ša₂ ŠU₂ rabû ša rabê
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Evening Last synodic phenomenon

[M]U ana MU ŠU₂ ša₂ ŠU₂ u KUR ša₂ NIM

‘Year by year, western setting and eastern rising.’

— BM 36321 (BMAPT No. 5) obv. 4

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer.
