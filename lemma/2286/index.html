TAB u LA₂
ṭīpu u mīṭu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/29/2024, 9:47:49 AM
Primary meaning additive or subtractive difference
Literal translation addition and subtraction
Variants
ṭīpu u mīṭu TAB u LA₂
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
additive or subtractive difference mathematical quantity, arithmetical quantity

(8') [TA 13 R]IN₂ EN 27 zib.ME ITI ana I[TI] (9') [2.6.15.4]2 TAB u LA₂-ka

‘(8’) [From 13 Li]b until 27 Psc, month by m[onth] (9’) your addition and subtraction is [2,6;15,4]2.’

— BM 34148 (BMAPT No. 58) BM 34148 (BMAPT No. 58)

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer
