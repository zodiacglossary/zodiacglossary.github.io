locus spei

Latin noun

Last edited: 9/19/2023, 2:11:02 PM
Primary meaning place of hope
Literal translation place of hope
Variants
spei locus
locus spei
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
place of hope astrological place

2nd astrological place

spei vel pecuniae in secundo horoscopi signo

‘of Expectation of Inheritace and Wealth in the second sign from the ascendant’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.14.3

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.58

See also

〈—〉 ꜥ.wy sḥn-ꜥnḫ ‘House of the provisions of life’

〈locus pecuniae〉 ‘place of money’

〈inferna porta〉 ‘place of the gate of hell’

〈anafora〉 ‘rising time’
