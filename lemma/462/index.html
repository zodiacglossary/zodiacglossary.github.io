qup-pu ar₂ ša₂ GU
quppu arkû ša Gula

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:29:43 PM
Primary meaning a group of stars in Aquarius or Pisces
Literal translation the rear container of the great one
Variants
qup-pu ar₂ ša₂ GU quppu arkû ša Gula
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
a group of stars in Aquarius or Pisces star: normal star

Jones (2004) a12. Very rarely used; identification (Jones 2004) 29, 27, 33, 30 Psc uncertain

[...] KUŠ₃ ar₂ qup-pu ar₂ ša₂ GU a-dir

‘[...] cubits behind the rear container of the great one it was eclipsed.’

— BM 32149+ (ADRT I No. -366B) rev. 6

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 367 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1988. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. I: Diaries from 652 B.C. to 262 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 134-135
