Κυθηρία
Kuthēria

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/14/2025, 7:42:18 AM
Primary meaning Venus
Literal translation From Cythera
Variants
Κυθηρία Kuthēria
Κυθερείη Kuthereiē poetic form
Κυθέρεια Kuthereia poetic form used by Dorotheus
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Venus planet

κόρην ἂν φράσσαιο καὶ ἠυκόμου Κυθερείης

‘You would declare (the prospects for) a wife (lit. “girl”) (from the Descendant) and the fair-haired one from Cythera (Venus)’

— Dorotheus 5.16.1–40

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 1st cent. CE?
