UŠ
nēmettu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:31:05 AM
Primary meaning station
Literal translation station
Variants
UŠ nēmettu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
station synodic phenomenon, astronomical event

(15') TA IGI ana UŠ 16.15 KI DU (16') UŠ

‘(15') From appearance (FA) to station (S1) the position proceeds 16;15, (16’) (then) it (Jupiter) becomes stationary.’

— BM 34081+ (BMAPT No. 18) rev. ii 15'-16'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 250-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2016, Ancient Babylonian astronomers calculated Jupiters position from the area under a time-velocity graph, Science, 351, 482-484
