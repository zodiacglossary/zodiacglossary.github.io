taš-pil-tu₂
tašpiltu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:44:44 PM
Primary meaning difference
Literal translation difference
Variants
taš-pil-tu₂ tašpiltu
taš tašpiltu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
difference mathematical quantity

(2') 17.46.40 taš-pil-tu₂ (3') ⸢ša₂ 18⸣ MU.MEŠ

‘(2') (0);17,46,40, the difference (3’) for 18 years.’

— BM 36722 (BMAPT No. 82) obv. 2'-3'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 470-471

(5) ša₂ GENNA 14.4.42.30 LA₂ 11.14.2.⸢30⸣ [SIG] (6) 12 taš an-na-a KI.⸢MEŠ⸣

‘(5) For Saturn: the ‘height’ is 14;4,42,30, [the ‘depth’ is] 11;14,2, 30, (6) the difference is (0);12; thus the positions.’

— A 3418 (BMAPT No. 41) rev. 5-6

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 170-200 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer 310
