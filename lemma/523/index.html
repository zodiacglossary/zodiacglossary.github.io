rnp.t ḫm

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:20:07 PM
Primary meaning small year
Literal translation small year
Variants
rnp.t ḫm restored
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
small year calendrical concept

Lunar year of 12 synodic months

ḥsb.t 19.t n ỉꜥḥ [rnp.t ḫm tꜣy]

‘Year 19 of the Moon [is a small year]’

— P.Carlsberg 9 iii 20

Genre: astronomy
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: Second cent. CE

Neugebauer, O. & Parker, R.A. EAT 3 225
