שָׁנָה
šānāh

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:40:58 AM
Primary meaning year
Literal translation year
Variants
שָׁנָה šānāh
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
year calendrical concept, time interval, unit of time

בִּשְׁנַת־מוֹת֙ הַמֶּ֣לֶךְ עֻזִּיָּ֔הוּ וָאֶרְאֶ֧ה אֶת־אֲדֹנָ֛י יֹשֵׁ֥ב עַל־כִּסֵּ֖א רָ֣ם וְנִשָּׂ֑א וְשׁוּלָ֖יו מְלֵאִ֥ים אֶת־הַהֵיכָֽל׃

bišnat̲-môt̲ hammelek̲ ʿuzziyyāhû wāʾerʾeh ʾet̲-ʾăd̲ōnāi yōšēb̲ ʿal-kissēʾ rām wəniśśāʾ wəšûlāyw məlēʾîm ʾet̲-hahêk̲āl.

‘In the year that King Uzziah died, I beheld my Sovereign seated on a high and lofty throne; and the skirts of God’s robe filled the temple.’

— Isaiah 6 1

Genre: religious text
