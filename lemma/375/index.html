παρανατέλλω
paranatellō

Greek verb

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 10:35:00 AM
Primary meaning to rise besides
Literal translation to rise
Variants
παρανατέλλω paranatellō
παραντέλλω parantellō
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
to rise besides astronomical event

Παρανατέλλει δὲ νοτόθεν καὶ τοῦ Ποταμοῦ τὸ λοιπὸν καὶ Ὠρίων.

‘And it (i.e. Gemini) rises besides the remaining part of Eridanus (= Ποταμός), and so does Orion.’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.28

Genre: astrology
Date: 2nd century CE

Vettius Valens. 1986. Vettii Valentis Antiocheni Anthologarium Libri Novem, ed. D. Pingree. Leipzig: B.G. Teubner. 8
