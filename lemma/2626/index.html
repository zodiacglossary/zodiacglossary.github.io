πληρόω
plēroō

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 12:02:35 PM
Primary meaning to complete
Literal translation to fill
Variants
πληρόω plēroō
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
to complete verb of time

e.g. an hour

ὥρ(ᾳ) [vac.?] εʹ πληρωμέν[ῃ

‘hour 5 completed. [’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4256 2

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 269-275 CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 266 + II: 394-395
