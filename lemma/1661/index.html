cor capricorni

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:37:36 AM
Primary meaning the heart of Capricorn
Literal translation heart of the goat-horned
Variants
cor capricorni
capricorni cor
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the heart of Capricorn animal part

11° Capricorn

XI. in corde Capricorni collocatur

‘[degree] 11 is located in the heart of Capricorn’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.10

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.292
