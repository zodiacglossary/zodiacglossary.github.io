τῶν ἐν ἄκρῳ τῷ κρανίῳ βʹ ὁ νότιος
tōn en akrōi tōi kraniōi 2 ho notios

Greek noun

Last edited: 5/28/2025, 8:23:53 AM
Primary meaning ν Virginis
Literal translation The southernmost of the 2 stars in the top of the skull
Variants

‘The southernmost of the 2 stars in the top of the skull [ν Virginis]’

— Ptolemy, Almagest 7

Genre: astronomical treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
