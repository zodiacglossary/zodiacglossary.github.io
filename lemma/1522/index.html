pes tertius scorpionis

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:37:27 AM
Primary meaning third leg of Scorpio
Literal translation third foot
Variants
pes tertius scorpionis
tertius pes scorpionis
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
third leg of Scorpio animal part

Qui in tertio pede habuerint horoscopum, erunt hortulani.

‘Whoever has the ascendant in the third leg [of Scorpio] will be a gardener.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.26.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.337
