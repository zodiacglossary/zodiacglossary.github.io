GIŠ.KUN A
rapašti nēši

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/27/2024, 4:30:21 PM
Primary meaning θ Leonis
Literal translation the rump of the lion
Variants
GIŠ.KUN A rapašti nēši
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
θ Leonis star: normal star

Jones (2004) c17

GI₆ 12 ⸢SI GIN⸣ [ina] ⸢ZALAG₂⸣ MUL₂.BABBAR SIG GIŠ.KUN A 4 1/2 KUŠ₃

‘Night of (day) 12, the north wind blew; last part of the night, Jupiter was 4 1/2 cubits below the rump of the lion.’

— BM 34609 (ADRT III No. -144) rev. 4

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 145 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 96-97
