GE6 ana ZALAG2
mūšu ana namāri

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 10/31/2023, 3:41:44 PM
Primary meaning time until sunrise
Literal translation night until dawn
Variants
GE6 ana ZALAG2 mūšu ana namāri rare in mathematical astronomy
ana ZALAG2 ? abbreviation of GE6 ana ZALAG2
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
Marvin Schreiber
time until sunrise time interval

Interval that starts between midnight and sunrise and ends at sunrise. It is a rare synonym of napāḫ Šamaš.

MU.1.ME.1,34.KAM | ZIZ2 GE6 10 30 DANNA | GE6 ana ZALAG2 lu2.TUR a-lid

‘Year 194 (S.E.), | month XI, night of the 10th, 30 (UŠ, i.e.) one bēru | remaining until sunrise, the child was born.’

— BM 34567 (BH 32) 4-6

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 2nd century

Rochberg, F. 1998. Babylonian Horoscopes. Transactions of the American Philosophical Society, 88. 147

GAN 25 45 ZALAG2

‘Month IX, (day) 25, 45 (UŠ until) sunrise’

— BM 36731 obv. ii' 6'

Genre: mathematical astronomy: table of solstices, equinoxes, sirius phenomena
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 450-50 BCE

Neugebauer, O., Sachs, A. 1967, Atypical Astronomical Cuneiform Texts I, Journal of Cuneiform Studies 21, 183-218
