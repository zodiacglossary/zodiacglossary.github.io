ἐνιαυσιαῖος
eniausiaios

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 2:47:56 PM
Primary meaning of a year
Literal translation of a year
Variants
ἐνιαυσιαῖος eniausiaios
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
of a year attribute of time, calendrical concept

Καὶ ἡ μὲν μοῖρα τξʹον μέρος ἐστὶ τοῦ ζῳδιακοῦ κύκλου, ἡ δὲ ἡμέρα τξεʹον καὶ δʹον μέρος ὡς ἔγγιστα τοῦ ἐνιαυσιαίου χρόνου.

‘Also, the degree is a 360th part of the zodiac, and the day as close as possible to a 365th and a 4th part of the time of a year.’

— Geminus 1.8

Genre: astronomy
Date: 1st century BCE

Geminus. 1975. Géminos, Introduction aux Phénomènes, ed. G. Aujac. Paris: Les Belles Lettres. 2
