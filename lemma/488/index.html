MUL₂.na-aṣ-ra-pu
naṣrapu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/26/2023, 4:39:00 PM
Primary meaning naṣrapu-star
Literal translation ?
Variants
MUL₂.na-aṣ-ra-pu naṣrapu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
naṣrapu-star star: ziqpu star

attested in diaries to mark time, identified with b (,c?) Persei (Hunger & Pingree 1999, 274).

[GI₆ 5 SAG GI₆ MUL₂].⸢na-aṣ-ra⸣-pu ziq-pi [GENNA ana] ⸢SI⸣ sin ša₂ ULU₃ KU₄

‘[Night of (day) 5] Naṣrapu-star (in) culmination, [Saturn] entered the southern [horn of] Sin.’

— BM 45693+ (ADRT III -124B) obv. 24'

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 125 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1996. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. III. Diaries from 164 B.C. to 61 B.C. Vienna, Austria: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 276-277
