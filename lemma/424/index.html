sšr

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 1/11/2024, 2:46:40 PM
Primary meaning Lot of Evil Daimon
Literal translation damned one
Variants
sšr
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
Lot of Evil Daimon astrological concept

Lot 180 degrees opposite the Lot of Daimon

(rꜥ)-ḫꜥ mw 22 (rꜥ)-ḥtp mꜣy 22 (šy-n-tꜣ)-p.t ḏlꜣ.t 20 |(šy-n-tꜣ)-dwꜣ.t kꜣ 20 šꜣw tbt(.w) {30} sšr rpy.t 30 špšy.t | ḥr-ꜥnḫ 14 wrꜣ(.t) gnhd ⌈14⌉ ꜥnḫ pꜣ-nty-ꜣtḥ | mwt ḥtr(.w) ḥꜥ(.w) rpy.t (y)ꜥb(.t) tbt(.w)

‘Ascendant: Aquarius 22°; Descendant: Leo 22°; (Lake of the) Sky: Scorpio 20°| (Lake of the) Netherworld: Taurus 20° (Lot of the) Daimon: Pisces {30}; (Lot of the) Evil Daimon: Virgo 30°; (Lot of) Fortune: |Capricorn 14°; (Lot of) Misfortune: Cancer ⌈14°⌉; (Lot of) Life: Sagittarius’

— ANAshm.Mus.D.O. 641

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Athribis
Date: 48 BCE

A. Winkler, 2022, The First Zodiac Sign and the Daimon: The Advent of an Astrological Tradition and Seven Elaborate Horoscopes, Studien our Alt'ägyptischen Culture 51: 267-319 302
