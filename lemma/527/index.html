ἀντίκειμαι
antikeimai

Greek verb

Last edited: 12/17/2024, 4:10:53 PM
Primary meaning to be in opposition
Literal translation to be opposite
Variants
ἀντίκειμαι antikeimai
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
to be in opposition astrological concept

ἐχθροὶ γάρ εἰσιν οἱ τῶν ἀντικειμένων μοιρῶν ὁρικοὶ κύριοι.

‘For the masters of the terms of the degrees that are in opposition are hostile.’

— Vettius Valens 3.5.3

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
