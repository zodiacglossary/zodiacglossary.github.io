κατωφερής
katōpherēs

Greek adjective

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 9:06:29 AM
Primary meaning downward-inclined
Literal translation downward-inclined
Variants
κατωφερής katōpherēs
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
downward-inclined zodiac sign quality

ἔστι δὲ τὸ ζῴδιον (…) κατωφερές

‘The zodiac sign (Taurus) is … downward-inclined (or, prone to lewdness)’

— Vettius Valens 1.2.14

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
