locus gloriae

Latin noun

Last edited: 1/5/2024, 8:34:31 AM
Primary meaning lot of fame
Literal translation fame
Variants
gloria
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of fame astrological lot

Gloriae nocte a Venere ad Iovem, die contra.

‘[The lot of] Fame [can be calculated by adding to the ascendant the amount of degrees] from Venus until Jupiter, in a day-chart vice versa.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.56

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.189
