Andromeda constellation

Si Iuppiter <in iis Piscium partibus> fuerit, in quibus est Andromeda constituta [...]

‘If Jupiter stands in those degrees of Pisces in which Andromeda is located [...]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.31.59

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.164

In Piscium parte XII. oritur Andromeda.

‘At 12° Pisces rises Andromeda.’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.17.1

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.312
