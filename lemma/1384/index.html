daemon

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:39:32 AM
Primary meaning lot of the daemon
Literal translation daemon
Variants
daemon
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
lot of the daemon astrological lot

In nocturna vero <a> Fortuna usque ad Daemonem simillimis rationibus conputabis

‘In a night-chart count from the lot of fortune until the lot of the daemon in a most similar way’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 6.32.45

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.187
