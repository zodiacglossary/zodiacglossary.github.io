The-Demon-with-the-gaping-mouth-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to Cygnus, Lacerta, and parts of Cassiopeia and Cepheus.

DIŠ mul.UD.KA.DUḪ.A d.U.GUR

‘¶ 'The Demon with the Gaping Mouth', Nergal.’

— BM 86378 i 28

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 35
