wḥm

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 6/30/2023, 10:30:24 AM
Primary meaning section of zodiac sign
Literal translation repetition
Variants
wḥm
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
section of zodiac sign astrological concept

Refers to the section of 10 degrees occupied by a decan in each zodiac sign.

[pꜣy-ms=w] r tꜣ wḥm mḥ-2.t (n) tꜣ ḏlꜥ ṯꜣy swsw 11 šꜥ swsw 20 r pꜣ ỉꜥḥ mtry r sšm

‘[He who was born] when the second stage of Scorpio, from 11 degrees to 20 degrees, while the moon was in agreement with the (decan) Seshm.’

— P.Lille vi x+23

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Tebtunis
Date: 2nd century CE

M. Chauveau, 1992, "Un traité d'astrologie en écriture démotique", Cahiers de Recherches de l'Institut de Papyrologie et d'Égyptologie de Lille 14, 101-105 104
