MUL.MEŠ šu-ut d.A-nim
kakkabū šūt Anim

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/3/2023, 10:08:57 AM
Primary meaning Stars of Anu
Literal translation Stars of Anu
Variants
MUL.MEŠ šu-ut d.A-nim kakkabū šūt Anim
Edited by
Marvin Schreiber
Stars of Anu group of stars and constellations

One of the three stellar divisions of the sky.

23 MUL.MEŠ šu-ut d.A-nim

‘23 stars of Anu.’

— BM 86378 ii 18

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: ?
Date: ca. 626-539 BCE
