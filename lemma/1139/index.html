dominium temporum

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 11:42:19 AM
Primary meaning rulership of time
Literal translation rulership of time
Variants
dominium temporum
temporum dominium
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
rulership of time astrological concept

nam in nocturnis genituris initium ac dominium ipsa suscipit temporum, in diurnis vero genituris Sol;

‘for in night charts the beginning and rulership of time she [the Moon] possesses herself, in day charts, however, the Sun does’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.1.8

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.199
