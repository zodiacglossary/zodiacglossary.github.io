δωδεκάτροπος
dōdekatropos

Greek noun

Last edited: 3/17/2025, 9:03:40 AM
Primary meaning dodekatropos
Literal translation twelve traits
Variants

dodekatropos astrological concept

περὶ τῶν ὀνομασιῶν τῶν ιβʹ τόπων καὶ περὶ τῆς δωδεκατρόπου

‘On the names of the 12 places and on the dodekatropos’

— Vettius Valens 4.12

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE
