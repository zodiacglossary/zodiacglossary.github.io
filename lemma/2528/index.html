יָרֵחַ
yārēaḥ

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 10/21/2024, 7:28:41 AM
Primary meaning moon
Literal translation moon
Variants
יָרֵחַ yārēaḥ
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
moon luminary

especially poet.

אִם־אֶרְאֶ֣ה א֭וֹר כִּ֣י יָהֵ֑ל וְ֝יָרֵ֗חַ יָקָ֥ר הֹלֵֽךְ׃

ʾim-ʾerʾeh ʾôr kî yāhēl wəyārēaḥ yāqār hōlēk̲.

‘If ever I saw the light shining, The moon on its course in full glory,’

— Job 31 26

Genre: religious text
