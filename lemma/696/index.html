tūʾamū ṣeḫrūtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 6/3/2024, 7:54:08 AM
Primary meaning Small-Twins-star

Variants

Small-Twins-star constellation

Approx. corresponding to ζ and λ Geminorum.

DIŠ mul.MAŠ.[TA]B.BA.⌈TUR⌉.TUR d.MUŠ3.LAL3 d.Nin- E[ZEN×GUD]

‘¶ The Small Twins, Alammuš and Nin-gublaga.’

— VAT 9429 i 5

Genre: schematic astronomy: mulapin
Provenance: Assur
Date: 8th-7th cent. BCE

Hunger, H. and Steele, J., 2019. The Babylonian Astronomical Compendium MUL.APIN. London and New York: Routledge. 30-31
