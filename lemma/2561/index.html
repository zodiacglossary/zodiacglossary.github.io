תֵּימָן
têmān

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 11/25/2024, 9:48:02 AM
Primary meaning south
Literal translation south
Variants
תֵּימָן têmān
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
south cardinal direction

chiefly poetic

הֲֽ֭מִבִּינָ֣תְךָ יַאֲבֶר־נֵ֑ץ יִפְרֹ֖שׂ כְּנָפָ֣ו לְתֵימָֽן׃

hămibbînāt̲ək̲ā yaʾăb̲er-nēṣ yip̲rōś kənāp̲āw lət̲êmān.

‘Is it by your wisdom that the hawk grows pinions, Spreads his wings to the south?’

— Job 39 26

Genre: religious text
