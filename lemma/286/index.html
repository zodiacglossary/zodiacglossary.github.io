tny.t sḫny

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/14/2023, 3:09:20 PM
Primary meaning part of conjunction
Literal translation part of conjunction
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
part of conjunction astrological concept

7th astrological place; equivalent to the Descendant

tꜣ tny.t ḫny nꜣ tbt.w | tꜣ tny.t sḫny pꜣ ỉsw

‘The part of mayhem: Pisces | The part of conjunction: Aries.’

— O.Neugeb. 3 17-18

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent. CE

Neugebauer, O., 1943. Demotic Horoscopes, Journal of the American Oriental Society 63: 116-117 116-117
