IB₂.TAG₄
rīḫtu

Akkadian noun

Last edited: 7/15/2024, 7:54:05 AM
Primary meaning remainder
Literal translation remainder
Variants
IB₂.TAG₄ rīḫtu
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
remainder mathematical quantity

(37) 1.30 ana KUR šamaš₂ re-ḫi 1.30 ina 6 12 DANNA E₁₁-ma 4.30 IB₂.TAG₄ (38) U₄.28.KAM

‘(37) 1,30 remains until sunrise. You deduct 1,30 from 6,0 (UŠ, i.e.) 12 bēru, 4,30 is the remainder (38) of day 28.’

— BM 32167+ (BMAPT No. 53) rev. ii 37-38

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-250 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 368-369
