κλῆρος ἀνάγκης
klēros anankēs

Greek noun

Last edited: 7/11/2023, 2:54:49 PM
Primary meaning lot of necessity
Literal translation lot of necessity
Variants
κλῆρος ἀνάγκης klēros anankēs
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
lot of necessity astrological lot

[ὁ κλῆρος] τῆς ἀνάγκης εὑρέθη Κριῷ μοιρῶν ϛʹ οἴκῳ Ἄρεως [ὁρίοις Διός· οὗ]τος σημαίνει τὸν περὶ ἐκχθρῶν καὶ παν̣τ̣ο̣δα̣π̣οῦ [δυστυχήμα]τος

‘The lot of necessity was found in 6 degrees of Aries, the house of Mars, terms of Jupiter. This signifies the (character) concerning enemies and misfortune of all kinds’

— P.Princ. II 75 8–10

Genre: horoscope
Date: 138–161 CE

(GH 138/161)

κλ(ῆρος) ἀνάγκης Τ̣[αύρῳ] μυρ(ῶν) ιʹ λε(πτῶν) θ̣ʹ

‘The lot of necessity: in 10 degrees, 9 minutes of Taurus’

— PSI I 23a 16

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Antinoopolis
Date: 338 CE

(GH 338)
