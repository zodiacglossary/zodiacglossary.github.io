ἐπιπροσθετικός
epiprosthetikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 12/9/2024, 9:26:42 AM
Primary meaning additive
Literal translation additive
Variants
ἐπιπροσθετικός epiprosthetikos
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
additive mathematical property

ταῦτα ἐπ[εὶ ....] ἐν τοῖς ἐ]πιπροσθετικ[οῖς] ....ς

‘Since these [were found(?)] in the additive ones, ...’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4143 Verso, l. 1-2

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. II: 38-39

See also

〈προστίθημι〉 prostithēmi ‘to add’
