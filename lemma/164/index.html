ZI
nasāḫu

Akkadian verb

Last edited: 11/9/2023, 2:54:56 PM
Primary meaning to move
Literal translation to move
Variants
ZI nasāḫu ZI is used for "to move"
ZAL nasāḫu ZAL is used for "to pass"
Edited by
Mathieu Ossendrijver
to move verb of motion

30 ME ina IGI-at ŠU₂-šu₂ ša₂ ME 6 ZI-ma ŠU₂

‘30 days before its setting it (Saturn) moves (0);6 per day, then it sets.’

— AO 6477 (BMAPT No. 42) rev. 12

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer 312-315

to pass astronomical term

said of time
