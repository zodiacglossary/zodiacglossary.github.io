atebenus

12°–19° Libra decan

numbers are corrupted and may be off 1°

In Librae signo quae sint partes plenae, quae vacuae, hoc demonstratur exemplo. Sunt autem partes plenae XIX, vacuae XII [sic!]. I locus partes V Seuichut [idest apoyc] II locus partes VI vacuus III locus partes VIII Sepisent [idest atebenus] IV locus partes III vacuus V locus partes VI Senta [idest atepiten] VI locus partes III vacuus.

‘In the sign of Libra there are some empty degrees and some full ones. This will be shown by example. There are 19° full and 12° empty [sic!]. 1. 5° Seuichut [i.e. Apoyc] 2. 6° empty 3. 8° Sepisent [i.e. Atebenus] 4. 3° empty 5. 6° Senta [i.e. Atepiten] 6. 3° empty’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 4.22.14

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.268-269
