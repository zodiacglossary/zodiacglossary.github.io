ἑξάγωνος πλευρά
hexagōnos pleura

Greek noun

Last edited: 3/24/2024, 10:32:47 PM
Primary meaning side of hexagon
Literal translation hexagonal side
Variants
ἑξάγωνος πλευρά hexagōnos pleura
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
side of hexagon astrological concept

ἐχρημάτισε γὰρ ἡ ἀναφορὰ τοῦ Ταύρου κγʹ καὶ Ἄρεως περίοδος ιεʹ· γίνονται ληʹ· ἀλλὰ καὶ ἑξάγωνος πλευρὰ Κρόνου καὶ Ἀφροδίτης ληʹ.

‘For the rising(-time) of Taurus was active, 23 (years), and the period of Mars, 15, total 38, but also the hexagonal side of Saturn and of Venus (i.e., one side of a hexagon along the zodiacal circle that coincides with their positions), (total) 38.’

— Vettius Valens 7.3.31–32

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 2nd century CE

(GH L 117,VI)
