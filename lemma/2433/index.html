nepa

Latin noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 4:19:31 PM
Primary meaning Scorpio
Literal translation scorpion
Variants
nepa
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
Scorpio zodiac sign

haerentisque decem partes Nepa vindicat ipsi

‘whilst the adjoining ten degrees are claimed by the Scorpion’

— Manilius, Astronomica 4.366

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 250-251
