rhwy

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 12/15/2023, 2:35:39 PM
Primary meaning night
Literal translation evening
Variants
rhwy
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
night time interval

Term for night in Theban horoscopes

ḥsb.t 4.t ỉbd-2 ꜣḫ.t sw 9 ṯꜣy-wnw.t 11 rhwy

‘Regnal year 4, month 2 of Akhet, day 9, 11th hour of the night.’

— O.Neugeb. 2 1

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Thebes
Date: 1st cent.CE

O. Neugebauer, “Demotic Horoscopes”, JAOS 63 (1943), 115–127. 116-117
