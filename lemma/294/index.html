time from sunset to first moonrise after sunset astronomical quantity: lunar six interval

GI₆ ana DU₃-ka

‘In order for you to construct GI₆.’

— BM 35399 (BMAPT No. 61A) rev. 13

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Babylon
Date: ca. 350-50 BCE

Ossendrijver, M. 2012. Babylonian Mathematical Astronomy: Procedure Texts. New York: Springer. 408-409

GI₆ 15 16.20 GI₆ muš

‘Night of (day) 15, GI₆ = 16;20, measured.’

— BM 41616+ (ADRT II No. -255) obv. 17

Genre: astronomical diary
Provenance: Babylon
Date: 256 BCE

Sachs, A. J., Hunger, H. 1989. Astronomical Diaries and Related Texts from Babylonia, Vol. II: Diaries from 261 B.C. to 165 B.C. Vienna: Verlag der Österreichischen Akademie der Wissenschaften. 18-19

40 ša₂ ME u GI₆ TA GI₆ ZI-aḫ

‘(0);40 of ME and GI₆ you subtract from GI₆.’

— AO 6455 (TU 11) obv. 38

Genre: astrology and goal-year astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Uruk
Date: ca. 200 BCE

Brack-Bernsen, Lis, and Hunger, Hermann. 2002. TU 11. A Collection of Rules for the Prediction of Lunar Phases and of Month Lengths. SCIAMVS 3: 3–90.
