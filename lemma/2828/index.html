Χώου
Chōou

Greek noun
loaned from Egyptian

Last edited: 2/3/2025, 10:58:00 AM
Primary meaning Decan 1 of Taurus
Variants
Χώου Chōou
Edited by
Michael Zellmann-Rohrer
Decan 1 of Taurus decan

οἱ δὲ δεκανοὶ αὐτοῦ τρεῖς· Χώου ὁ πρῶτος, Ἔρω ὁ δεύτερος, Ῥομβρόμαρε ὁ τρίτος

‘The decans of (Taurus) are three: Chōou is the first, Erō the second, Rhombromare the third’

— Hephaestion 1.1.31

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 5th century CE
