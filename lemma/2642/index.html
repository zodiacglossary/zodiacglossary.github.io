ἀνωμαλία
anōmalia

Greek noun

Last edited: 12/17/2024, 4:16:15 PM
Primary meaning anomaly
Literal translation irregularity
Variants
ἀνωμαλία anōmalia
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
anomaly astronomical concept

(...) ἐ[π’] ἀναποδισμοῦ ἀνωμαλίας δευτ[έ]ρῳ, δ[ε]καν[ῷ] αʹ.

‘(...) at the second change of direction of anomaly, decan 1.’

— P. Oxy. LXI 4277 fr. 1, i, 14-15

Genre: horoscope
Provenance: Oxyrhynchus
Date: 2nd-3rd century CE

Jones, A. (ed.), 1999. Astronomical papyri from Oxyrhynchus (P. Oxy. 4133-4300a), Volumes I and II. Philadelphia: American Philosophical Society. I: 284-286 + II: 420-427
