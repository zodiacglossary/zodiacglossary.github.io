तिथि
tithi

Sanskrit noun

Last edited: 11/19/2024, 4:13:42 PM
Primary meaning tithi
Literal translation tithi
Variants
तिथि tithi
Edited by
Thomas Peeters
tithi unit of time

1 (mean) "tithi" = 63/64 of a day. This corresponds to 1/30 of a mean synodic month.

दिनं चतुःषष्टिलवोनमाहुस्तिथिं युशब्दान्त्यमहस्तु सर्वम् । त्रिषष्टिभागेनयुतं सहस्रं युगेवमानामपसप्तषट्कम् ॥५॥

dinaṃ catuḥṣaṣṭi-lavônam āhus tithiṃ yuśabdāntyam ahas tu sarvam | triṣaṣṭibhāgena yutaṃ sahasraṃ yuge 'vamānām apasaptaṣaṭkam |5|

‘They say that a day minus 1/64th is a tithi whereas a whole (day) is (a tithi) plus 1/63rd. The omitted (tithis) in a yuga are 1.000 minus 7 x 6. |5|’

— Yavanajātaka 79.5

Genre: astrological treatise
Date: 4th-6th century CE

Mak, B., 2013. The date and nature of Sphujidhvaja’s Yavanajātaka reconsidered in the light of some newly discovered materials, History of Science in South Asia 1: 1-20. 8-9
