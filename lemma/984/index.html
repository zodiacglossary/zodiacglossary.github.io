medium caelum

Latin noun

Last edited: 6/10/2024, 7:36:51 AM
Primary meaning midheaven
Literal translation midheaven
Variants
medium caelum
MC
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
upper midheaven astrological place, astrological concept, cardinal point

10th astrological place

Medium vero caelum est ab horoscopo X. signum

‘The midheaven is the tenth sign from the ascendant’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 2.15.2

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.58

midspace of the sky astronomical term

any line that cuts the sky in two

nam sua quadratis veniunt, sua iura trigonis et quae per senos decurrit virgula tractus quaeque secat medium transverso limite caelum;

‘For special properties accrue to squares, to trigons, to the line which traverses six chords, and to that which cuts the midspace of the sky with diametric track;’

— Manilius, Astronomica 2.646 ff

Genre: astrological treatise

Manilius. Astronomica. Edited and translated by G. P. Goold. Loeb Classical Library 469. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1977. 132-133
