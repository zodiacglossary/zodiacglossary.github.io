catafora

Latin noun
loaned from Greek

Last edited: 11/11/2024, 9:18:01 AM
Primary meaning the sign after a cardinal point
Literal translation setting
Variants
catafora
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the sign after a cardinal point astrological house, zodiac sign quality

= the place preceding a cardinal place

solus in cardinibus geniturae fuerit inventus vel in cataforis vel in anaforis eorum

‘alone on an angle or on the anafora or catafora of an angle [= the sign before or after]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 3.5.5

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 1.128
