כּוֹכָב
kôk̲āb̲

Hebrew noun

Last edited: 5/11/2025, 3:48:39 PM
Primary meaning star
Literal translation star
Variants
כּוֹכָב kôk̲āb̲
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
star celestial object

הָאֹמֵ֣ר לַ֭חֶרֶס וְלֹ֣א יִזְרָ֑ח וּבְעַ֖ד כּוֹכָבִ֣ים יַחְתֹּֽם׃

hāʾōmēr laḥeres wəlōʾ yizrāḥ ûb̲əʿad̲ kôk̲āb̲îm yaḥtōm.

‘Who commands the sun not to shine; Who seals up the stars;’

— Job 9 7
