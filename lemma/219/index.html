rnp.t ꜥꜣ.t

Egyptian noun

Last edited: 8/25/2025, 7:59:51 AM
Primary meaning cycle
Literal translation great year
Variants
rnp.t ꜥꜣ.t
Edited by
Andreas Winkler
cycle astronomical term

Sbk | pꜣy=f ḫꜥ ỉmnṱ 1513 | tꜣy=f rnp.t ꜥꜣ.t 480

‘Mercury | its evening rising | 1514 | its great year 480’

— ANAShm.Mus.D.O. 483 1-3

Genre: mathematical astronomy: procedure text
Provenance: Egypt
Date: 3rd cent. BCE to 3rd cent. CE

Ossendrijver, M. and A. Winkler, 2018. Chaldeans on the Nile: Two Egyptian Procedure Texts with Babylonian System A1 and A2 for Mercury, in Scaffolding of Our Thoughts: Essays on Assyriology and the History of Science in Honor of Francesca Rochberg, eds. J. Crisostomo et al. (Leiden: Brill, 2018), 282-418

Lunar year of 13 synodic months calendrical concept

ḥsb.t 23.t n ỉꜥḥ rnp.t ꜥꜣ.t tꜣy

‘Numbering year 23 of the Moon is a great year.’

— P.Carlsberg 9 iii 17

Genre: astronomy
Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos
Date: Second cent. CE

Neugebauer, O. & Parker, R.A. EAT 3 225
