χρηματιστικός
chrēmatistikos

Greek adjective

Last edited: 7/11/2023, 6:59:31 AM
Primary meaning active
Literal translation money-making
Variants
χρηματιστικός chrēmatistikos

χρηματιστικὰ ζῴδια λέγεται τὰ δʹ κέντρα καὶ αἱ τούτων ἐπαναφοραί

‘Active zodiac signs are called the 4 cardinal points (kentra) and their following places (epanaphorai)’

— Sarapion of Alexandria, Various Names for Configurations of the Stars

Genre: astrology
