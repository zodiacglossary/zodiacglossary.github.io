iulius

Latin noun

Last edited: 11/3/2024, 3:33:19 PM
Primary meaning July
Literal translation July
Variants
iulius
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
July month, time interval

Iulio enim mense iam despoliata sunt omnia aut certe admodum pauca iacent adhuc sub nive.

‘For in the month of July everything is clear of snow, or at least very few places still lie under snow.’

— Seneca, Natural Questions 5.11.2

Genre: philosophy
Date: 62/63 CE

Seneca. Natural Questions, Volume II: Books 4-7. Translated by Thomas H. Corcoran. Loeb Classical Library 457. Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 1972. 92-93
