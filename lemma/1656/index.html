manus dextra sagitarii

Latin noun

Last edited: 12/12/2023, 12:08:24 PM
Primary meaning the right hand of the Archer
Literal translation right hand of the archer
Variants
manus dextra sagitarii
dextra manus sagitarii
Edited by
Kierán Meinhardt
the right hand of the Archer animal part

28°–29° Sagittarius

sequentes vero duae in dextra ponuntur manu

‘the following two [degrees] are placed in the right hand [of the Archer]’

— Firmicus Maternus, Mathesis 8.4.9

Genre: astrological compendium
Provenance: Sicily
Date: ca. 334 CE

Firmicus Maternus. 1897. Ivlii Firmici Materni Matheseos Libri VIII, ed. W. Kroll, F. Skutsch. Stuttgart: B. G. Teubner. 2.291
