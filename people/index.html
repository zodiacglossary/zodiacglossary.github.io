Contributors

The ZODIAC Glossary is made possible through the contributions of a dedicated team of scholars, researchers, and editors with expertise in linguistics, history, and the study of ancient astral sciences. Each contributor participates in the careful documentation, translation, and annotation of lemmata, meanings, and textual sources, ensuring the accuracy and scholarly rigor of the database. Their work enables precise cross-cultural comparisons and supports advanced research into the transmission of astronomical and zodiacal knowledge in antiquity.
