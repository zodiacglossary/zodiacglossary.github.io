# The ZODIAC Glossary

Welcome to the [ZODIAC Glossary](/about), a scholarly resource dedicated to the systematic study of ancient astral science. This project brings together lexical data from Akkadian, Aramaic, Egyptian, Greek, Hebrew, Latin, Sanskrit, and other languages, tracing the transmission and transformation of zodiacal and celestial concepts across cultures.

For an overview of the team contributing to this research, see our [contributors page](/about). To explore the glossary in depth, access the [advanced search](/search) to query lemmata, meanings, categories, and associated textual sources, facilitating precise comparative study and research.
